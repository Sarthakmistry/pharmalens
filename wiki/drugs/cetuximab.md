### Clinical evidence
A network meta-analysis published in 2025, involving 18 RCTs, found that cetuximab showed higher serious adverse event (SAE) risks (OR 1.95) when used in advanced biliary tract cancer compared to chemotherapy alone. The study was a network meta-analysis with chemotherapy alone as the comparator, focusing on overall survival (OS).
