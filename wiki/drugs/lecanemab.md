### Clinical evidence
A randomized controlled trial (RCT) involving 856 participants compared lecanemab to placebo. The primary outcome measures were Core Study Phase: Change From Baseline in Alzheimer's Disease Composite Score (ADCOMS) at Month 12 | Core Study Phase: Number of Participants With All Treatment Emergent Adverse Events (TEAEs) and Serious Adverse Events (SAEs) | OLE Phase: Number of Participants With All TEAEs and SAEs. Safety was assessed by monitoring the number of participants with all treatment emergent adverse events (TEAEs) and serious adverse events (SAEs). No primary results or conclusions were reported in this document.
