**Company:** [[pfizer]] | **Class:** CDK4/6 inhibitor | **Status:** approved

### Approved indications

### Pipeline
- glioblastoma — Phase 2, NCT03158389, Results reported (did not meet primary endpoint)

### Management sentiment
The management sentiment for palbociclib is neutral, as its subtrial in the N2M2 umbrella study for glioblastoma did not meet its primary endpoint for efficacy.

### Clinical evidence
In the N2M2 umbrella trial (NCT03158389), a randomized controlled trial involving 228 patients with newly diagnosed glioblastoma, the palbociclib subtrial did not meet its primary endpoints for efficacy. The study evaluated targeted treatments, and while the [[temsirolimus]] subtrial met its primary endpoint, the palbociclib subtrial did not.
