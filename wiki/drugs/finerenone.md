### Management sentiment

### Clinical evidence
A systematic review of medications for type 2 diabetes, published in BMJ in 2025, found that finerenone significantly increases the risk of severe hyperkalaemia (OR 5.92, 95% CI 3.02 to 11.62) with high certainty.
