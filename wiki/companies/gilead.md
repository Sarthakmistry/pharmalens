### Pipeline
- Tenofovir Alafenamide (TAF) (Phase 2) for Chronic Hepatitis B. The trial (NCT02932150) is currently recruiting, with primary completion expected in September 2026.
- Brexucabtagene Autoleucel (KTE-X19) (Phase 1/2) for Relapsed/Refractory B-precursor Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia and Relapsed/Refractory B-Cell Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma. The study is active, not recruiting for ALL patients as of October 2022, but open for NHL patients. Primary completion is expected in February 2026.
- Bictegravir/Emtricitabine/Tenofovir Alafenamide Fixed Dose Combination (Phase 2/3) for HIV-1 Infection (Trial NCT02881320, active not recruiting, primary completion 2025-03-24, completion 2026-11).
- Magrolimab (Phase 1/2) for Relapsed/Refractory B-cell Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma (Trial NCT02953509 terminated on 2026-04-07).
