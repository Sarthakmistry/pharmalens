### Earnings intelligence
From Regeneron's Q3 2025 earnings call (October 28, 2025):
The collaboration with Bayer on Aflibercept (EYLEA) outside the U.S. is impacted by the performance of the drug. While EYLEA HD total net sales increased 31.35% to $711.0 million, EYLEA total net sales decreased 34.93% to $1,254.0 million in the third quarter 2025. This indicates a mixed performance for the Aflibercept franchise, with the newer HD formulation showing strong growth while the original EYLEA faces significant decline.

From Regeneron's Q1 2025 earnings call (April 29, 2025):
The collaboration with Bayer on Aflibercept (EYLEA) outside the U.S. is impacted by the performance of the drug. While Regeneron reported strong Dupixent sales, EYLEA U.S. net sales decreased, though EYLEA HD showed significant growth. Total EYLEA HD and EYLEA U.S. net sales decreased 26% to $1.04 billion, indicating ongoing challenges for the EYLEA franchise despite the success of the HD formulation.

From Regeneron's Q4 2025 earnings call (January 30, 2026):
The collaboration with Bayer on Aflibercept (EYLEA) outside the U.S. is impacted by the performance of the drug. EYLEA HD U.S. net sales showed strong growth, increasing 66% to $506 million in Q4 2025, and received FDA approval for macular edema following retinal vein occlusion (RVO). This indicates a positive outlook for the high-dose formulation. Conversely, total EYLEA sales (including EYLEA HD) decreased 34% to $5.3 billion in 2025, primarily due to competition and the ongoing transition to EYLEA HD, suggesting challenges for the standard dose.

From Regeneron's Q2 2025 earnings call (August 1, 2025):
The collaboration with Bayer on Aflibercept (EYLEA) outside the U.S. is impacted by the performance of the drug. Total EYLEA HD and EYLEA U.S. net sales decreased 25% to $1.15 billion, indicating a significant decline for EYLEA.
