## Sanofi's Vrvg-2 Rabies Vaccine Phase 3 Trial Demonstrates Non-Inferiority

**Date:** 2020-12-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
A Phase 3 randomized controlled trial (NCT03965962) for Sanofi's Vrvg-2 rabies vaccine has completed, demonstrating non-inferiority compared to current standard-of-care vaccines. The study, involving 640 participants, showed comparable immunogenicity and safety for Vrvg-2 when administered with Human Rabies Immune Globulin (HRIG). This outcome supports the vaccine's potential as an effective option for rabies post-exposure prophylaxis.

### Market implication
This successful trial completion strengthens Sanofi's vaccine pipeline and positions Vrvg-2 as a promising new candidate for rabies prevention, potentially expanding the company's market share in the vaccine segment.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 640
- **Comparator:** PVRV + HRIG and HDCV + HRIG
- **Primary outcome:** proportion of participants achieving RVNA titers ≥0.5 IU/mL
- **Primary result:** Non-inferiority of PVRV-NG2 + HRIG compared with PVRV + HRIG and HDCV + HRIG was demonstrated. Nearly all participants (99.6%, PVRV-NG2 + HRIG; 100%, PVRV + HRIG; 98.7%, HDCV + HRIG; 100%, PVRV-NG2 alone) achieved RVNA titers ≥0.5 IU/mL at D28.
- **Secondary results:** Geometric mean titers were similar between groups with concomitant HRIG administration at all time points.
- **Safety:** Safety profiles were similar between PVRV-NG2 and comparator vaccines.
- **Conclusions:** In a simulated PEP setting, PVRV-NG2 + HRIG showed comparable immunogenicity and safety to current standard-of-care vaccines.
- **Journal:** Clin Infect Dis, 2024
- **Industry sponsored:** True
