## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial for Lasmiditan (NCT04396574) terminated

**Date:** 2025-12-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04396574) for Lasmiditan. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 566 participants and was originally scheduled for primary completion on December 4, 2025, was stopped early. This termination indicates a significant halt in the development pathway for Lasmiditan.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 3 trial represents a setback for Eli Lilly's development of Lasmiditan, potentially impacting its future market prospects.
