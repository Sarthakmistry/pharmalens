## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04396574) for Lasmiditan Terminated

**Date:** 2025-12-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04396574) for the drug Lasmiditan, sponsored by Eli Lilly, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 566 participants and was originally projected to reach primary completion by December 4, 2025. The termination indicates that the trial will not proceed as planned.
