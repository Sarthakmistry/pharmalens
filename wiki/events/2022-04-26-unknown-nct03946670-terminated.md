## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT03946670) for investigational drug MBG453

**Date:** 2022-04-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03946670) for the investigational drug MBG453, which was being studied in combination with hypomethylating agents. The trial, which enrolled 127 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2022, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for MBG453 represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline in this specific indication, potentially impacting future development plans for the compound.
