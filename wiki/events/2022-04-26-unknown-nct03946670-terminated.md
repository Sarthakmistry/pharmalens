## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial NCT03946670

**Date:** 2022-04-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03946670) investigating Mbg453 in combination with Hypomethylating agents. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 127 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2022, and has results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, potentially removing a future asset and indicating a negative outcome for the investigational drug.
