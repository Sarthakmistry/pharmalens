## Pfizer Announces Results of 2026 Annual Meeting of Shareholders

**Date:** 2026-04-27 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer Inc. reported the results of its Annual Meeting of Shareholders held on April 23, 2026. Key outcomes included the election of all nominated directors, the ratification of KPMG LLP as the independent registered public accounting firm for 2026, and the advisory approval of executive compensation. A shareholder proposal advocating for an independent chair policy was not approved.
