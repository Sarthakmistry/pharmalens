## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial of Isatuximab sar650984, Carfilzomib, and Dexamethasone Completed

**Date:** 2022-01-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03275285) sponsored by [[sanofi]], investigating Isatuximab sar650984 in combination with Carfilzomib and Dexamethasone, reached its primary completion date on January 14, 2022. The trial enrolled 302 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial makes the data available for analysis, which will inform Sanofi's strategic decisions regarding the future development and potential regulatory submissions for Isatuximab sar650984 in this indication.
