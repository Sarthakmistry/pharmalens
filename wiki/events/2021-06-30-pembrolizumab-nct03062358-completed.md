## Clinical trial NCT03062358 for Pembrolizumab in NSCLC reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2021-06-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03062358, evaluating [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda) by [[merck]] for advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion on June 30, 2021. This Phase 3 study enrolled 453 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides further data for [[merck]]'s [[pembrolizumab]] in the non-small cell lung cancer market, with results already available.
