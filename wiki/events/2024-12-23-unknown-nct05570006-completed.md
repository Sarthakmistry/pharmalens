## AbbVie's ABBV-668 Phase 2 Trial for Ulcerative Colitis Completed

**Date:** 2024-12-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05570006) evaluating ABBV-668 for moderate to severe ulcerative colitis reached primary completion on December 23, 2024. The trial enrolled 30 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as specific results are not yet available to assess the drug's efficacy or safety.
