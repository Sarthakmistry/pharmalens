## Nivolumab Phase 3 Trial NCT02998528 for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2021-09-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02998528) investigating [[nivolumab]] for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached primary completion on September 8, 2021. The study enrolled 505 participants and has reported results, marking a significant step in the drug's development for this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial with available results provides a positive outlook for nivolumab's potential in the NSCLC market, pending the release and interpretation of the data.
