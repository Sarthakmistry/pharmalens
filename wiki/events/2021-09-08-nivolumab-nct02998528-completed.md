## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Phase 3 Trial NCT02998528 for NSCLC Completes Primary Study

**Date:** 2021-09-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02998528) investigating nivolumab for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on September 8, 2021. The study, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, enrolled 505 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for nivolumab in this indication.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial allows for the analysis and potential publication of results, which could inform future treatment guidelines or label expansions for nivolumab.
