## Avelumab, Axitinib, and Sunitinib Renal Cell Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2024-06-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 JAVELIN Renal 101 study, evaluating avelumab with axitinib versus sunitinib in advanced renal cell cancer, has completed with results available. This trial aimed to assess the efficacy and safety of the combination therapy as a first-line treatment.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides clinical data for avelumab and axitinib in advanced renal cell cancer, but without specific results, the market implication remains neutral.

### Clinical evidence
- Study design: RCT
- Sample size: 886 patients
- Comparator: sunitinib monotherapy
- Primary outcome: Progression Free Survival (PFS) as Assessed by Blinded Independent Central Review (BICR) in Programmed Death-Ligand 1 (PD-L1) Positive Participants | Overall Survival (OS) in PD-L1 Positive Participants
