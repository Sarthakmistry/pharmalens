## Merck Reports Q2 2025 Earnings with Mixed Performance and Strategic Acquisitions

**Date:** 2025-06-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck reported a 2% decline in total revenue for the first six months of 2025, primarily due to lower sales in vaccines, immunology, and virology, which offset growth in its oncology and cardiovascular franchises. The company also announced significant business development activities, including the acquisition of Verona Pharma for $10 billion to gain Ohtuvayre for COPD, and new licensing agreements for pipeline assets, while facing ongoing challenges from U.S. drug pricing legislation.

### Market implication
Merck's stock performance is likely to remain neutral as growth in key oncology and cardiovascular assets is balanced by declines in other segments and the financial impact of new acquisitions and U.S. drug pricing pressures.
