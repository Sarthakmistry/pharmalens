## Regeneron Reports Strong Q4 and Full-Year 2025 Results Driven by Dupixent and EYLEA HD Growth

**Date:** 2026-01-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. reported its fourth quarter and full year 2025 financial and operating results, highlighting strong growth for Dupixent and EYLEA HD. Dupixent global net sales increased 34% to $4.9 billion in Q4 2025 and 26% to $17.8 billion for the full year. EYLEA HD U.S. net sales grew 66% to $506 million in Q4 2025 and 36% to $1.6 billion for the full year, also receiving FDA approval for the treatment of macular edema following retinal vein occlusion (RVO). Overall, Regeneron's Q4 2025 revenues increased 3% to $3.9 billion, and full year 2025 revenues increased 1% to $14.3 billion. Libtayo global net sales also increased 16% to $425.4 million in Q4 2025.

### Market implication
The strong performance of key growth drivers like Dupixent and EYLEA HD, coupled with overall revenue growth and a new FDA approval, indicates a bullish outlook for Regeneron's financial performance.
