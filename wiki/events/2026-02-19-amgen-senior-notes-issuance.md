## Amgen Issues $4 Billion in Senior Notes

**Date:** 2026-02-19 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Amgen Inc. issued and sold $4,000,000,000 aggregate principal amount of senior notes across four tranches with varying interest rates and maturity dates. The notes include 4.200% Senior Notes due 2031, 4.850% Senior Notes due 2036, 5.500% Senior Notes due 2046, and 5.650% Senior Notes due 2056.

### Market implication
The issuance of senior notes is a routine financing activity for a company of Amgen's size, indicating capital raising rather than a specific positive or negative operational event.
