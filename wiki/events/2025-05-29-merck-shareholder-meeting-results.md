## Merck & Co., Inc. held its Annual Meeting of Shareholders on May 27, 2025, where shareholders voted on the election of directors, executive compensation, auditor ratification, and several shareholder proposals.

**Date:** 2025-05-29 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck & Co., Inc. held its Annual Meeting of Shareholders on May 27, 2025, where shareholders voted on the election of directors, executive compensation, auditor ratification, and several shareholder proposals. Shareholders elected twelve nominees to the Board, approved executive compensation, and ratified the independent registered public accounting firm. Several shareholder proposals, including those related to human rights, tax transparency, DEI goals, and civil liberties in advertising, were voted against by the majority.

### Market implication
This event is a routine corporate governance update and is not expected to have a direct impact on Merck's stock or valuation.
