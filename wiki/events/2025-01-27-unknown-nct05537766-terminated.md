## Gilead terminates Phase 2 trial NCT05537766

**Date:** 2025-01-27 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Gilead has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial NCT05537766, which was investigating Brexucabtagene autoleucel, Cyclophosphamide, and Fludarabine. The trial had an actual enrollment of 19 participants and reached its primary completion on January 27, 2025, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Gilead's pipeline in this specific area.
