## Gilead Terminates Phase 2 Trial NCT05537766

**Date:** 2025-01-27 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Gilead Sciences has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05537766, which investigated Brexucabtagene autoleucel, Cyclophosphamide, and Fludarabine. The trial reached its primary completion date on January 27, 2025, with an actual enrollment of 19 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial may lead to a re-evaluation of the development strategy for the involved compounds within Gilead's pipeline.
