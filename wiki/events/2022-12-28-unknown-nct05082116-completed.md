## Novo Nordisk's Turoctocog alfa pegol (n8-gp) Phase 3 trial NCT05082116 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2022-12-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 trial (NCT05082116) for Turoctocog alfa pegol (n8-gp) on December 28, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 36 participants, has reached its primary completion, indicating that data collection for the primary outcome measure is finished.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Novo Nordisk's Turoctocog alfa pegol (n8-gp), moving it closer to potential regulatory review.
