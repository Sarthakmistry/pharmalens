**Date:** 2026-05-07 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company held its annual meeting of shareholders on May 4, 2026, where directors were elected, executive compensation and auditor appointment were approved. Several corporate governance proposals, including amending the Articles of Incorporation to eliminate the classified board structure and supermajority voting provisions, and shareholder proposals requesting an independent board chair and an annual lobbying report, failed to pass.
