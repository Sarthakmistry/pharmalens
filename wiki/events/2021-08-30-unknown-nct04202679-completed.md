## Sanofi's Phase 3 trial NCT04202679 completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2021-08-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04202679, reached its primary completion date on August 30, 2021. The study enrolled 160 participants and investigated Dupilumab sar231893 in combination with standard therapies like moisturizers, low to medium potent topical corticosteroids, and topical calcineurin inhibitors. The trial has results available, though specific outcomes are not detailed in this update.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Sanofi, with market impact dependent on future data release.
