## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2 Trial NCT04165096 for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-04-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04165096) sponsored by Merck, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with other agents for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on April 30, 2025. The trial enrolled 128 participants and has completed its primary data collection for the main outcome measures.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step, and its market implication is neutral until results are released and analyzed.
