## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide (Wegovy) Trial NCT04165096 for Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-04-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04165096) investigating semaglutide for obesity has reached its primary completion date on April 30, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 128 participants, has results available. This event signifies the conclusion of the primary data collection phase for the study.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial is a standard procedural step and does not immediately impact Novo Nordisk's valuation, pending the release and interpretation of the study results.
