## AstraZeneca's Ceralasertib Phase 2 Trial for Alzheimer's Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-02-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04564027) sponsored by AstraZeneca, investigating Ceralasertib for Alzheimer's disease, has reached its primary completion date on February 18, 2025. The trial enrolled 54 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on AstraZeneca's pipeline in Alzheimer's disease, with results now available for evaluation.
