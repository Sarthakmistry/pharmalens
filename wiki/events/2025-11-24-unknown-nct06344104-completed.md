## AstraZeneca's Baxdrostat Phase 3 trial NCT06344104 for heart failure and hypertension completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-11-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT06344104, investigating Baxdrostat for indications related to heart failure and hypertension, reached its primary completion date on November 24, 2025. Sponsored by AstraZeneca, this trial enrolled 326 participants. The trial status is now completed, but results have not yet been published.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for AstraZeneca's Baxdrostat, with market impact pending the release of trial results.
