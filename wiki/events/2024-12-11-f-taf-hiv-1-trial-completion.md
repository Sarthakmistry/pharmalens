## Trial Completion for Emtricitabine/Tenofovir Alafenamide (F/TAF) in HIV-1 Infected Children and Adolescents

A Phase 2/3 study evaluating Emtricitabine/Tenofovir Alafenamide (F/TAF) in HIV-1 infected children and adolescents has completed with results available. The trial, NCT02285114, was sponsored by Gilead Sciences.
