## Vertex Completes BLA Submission for Povetacicept in IgA Nephropathy, Expects Expedited Review

**Date:** 2026-03-31 | **Type:** pipeline_update | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals has completed the submission of its rolling Biologics Licensing Application (BLA) for potential accelerated approval of povetacicept for the treatment of immunoglobulin A nephropathy (IgAN) in adults. The company utilized a priority review voucher, which is expected to expedite the FDA review process to six months from the date of BLA acceptance.

### Market implication
The completion of the BLA submission with a priority review voucher suggests a potentially faster path to market for povetacicept, which could positively impact Vertex's future revenue streams.
