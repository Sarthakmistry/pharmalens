## Merck Q2 2025 Earnings: Keytruda Drives Oncology Growth Amidst Vaccine Declines and Strategic Pipeline Investments

**Date:** 2025-08-05 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck reported a 2% decline in worldwide sales for Q2 and H1 2025, driven by lower vaccine and virology sales, partially offset by strong growth in oncology (Keytruda, Welireg, Lynparza) and cardiovascular (Winrevair). The company engaged in significant business development activities, including the acquisition of Verona Pharma for Ohtuvayre (ensifentrine) and licensing agreements for pipeline assets, while also facing challenges from the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) impacting future pricing of key drugs like Januvia, Janumet, and Keytruda.

### Market implication
Worldwide sales declined by 2% in H1 2025 due to lower sales in vaccines, immunology, and virology, partially offset by growth in oncology and cardiovascular, while significant R&D expenses were incurred for business development, leading to a neutral overall market signal.
