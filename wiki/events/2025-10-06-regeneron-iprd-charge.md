## Regeneron anticipates $83 million pre-tax IPR&D charge in Q3 2025 due to Hansoh Pharmaceuticals upfront payment

**Date:** 2025-10-06 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. announced an expected pre-tax acquired in-process research and development (IPR&D) charge of approximately $83 million for the third quarter of 2025. This charge primarily stems from an $80 million upfront payment made to Hansoh Pharmaceuticals Group Company Limited under a 2025 license agreement. Regeneron does not typically forecast such charges due to their uncertain nature.

### Market implication
This charge is expected to negatively impact Regeneron's GAAP and non-GAAP net income per diluted share for Q3 2025 by approximately $0.68, indicating a bearish financial signal for the quarter.
