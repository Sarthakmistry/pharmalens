## Regeneron expects $83 million IPR&D charge in Q3 2025

**Date:** 2025-10-06 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals expects an acquired in-process research and development (IPR&D) charge of approximately $83 million pre-tax for the third quarter of 2025. This charge primarily relates to an $80 million up-front payment made to Hansoh Pharmaceuticals Group Company Limited under a 2025 license agreement. This is anticipated to negatively impact diluted EPS by about $0.68.

### Market implication
The expected $83 million IPR&D charge will negatively impact Regeneron's GAAP and non-GAAP net income per diluted share for Q3 2025, indicating a bearish financial signal for the company.
