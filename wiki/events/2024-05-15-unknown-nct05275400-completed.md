## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial for Insulin efsitora alfa in Type 2 Diabetes completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-05-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05275400) evaluating Insulin efsitora alfa for Type 2 Diabetes has completed its primary endpoint on May 15, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 986 participants, compared Insulin efsitora alfa to Insulin degludec. The trial status is COMPLETED and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step for Eli Lilly in its diabetes pipeline, with results now available for evaluation.
