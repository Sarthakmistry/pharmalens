## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial (NCT05275400) for Insulin efsitora alfa in Type 2 Diabetes completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-05-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05275400) for Insulin efsitora alfa, an investigational once-weekly basal insulin, in patients with Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 986 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 15, 2024, and results are now available. The study compared Insulin efsitora alfa to Insulin degludec.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential regulatory submission for Eli Lilly's Insulin efsitora alfa, indicating progress in its diabetes pipeline.
