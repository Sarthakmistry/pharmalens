## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial (NCT05872620) for GLP-1 Obesity and Type 2 Diabetes Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05872620) for Orforglipron, an investigational drug for GLP-1 obesity and type 2 diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on August 8, 2025. The trial successfully enrolled 1613 participants. Results from this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a procedural step in the drug development process for Orforglipron, with market implications dependent on the future release and interpretation of its clinical results.
