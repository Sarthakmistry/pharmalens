## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 trial (NCT05872620) for GLP-1 obesity and type 2 diabetes completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial, NCT05872620, investigating Orforglipron for GLP-1 obesity and type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on August 8, 2025. The trial enrolled 1613 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The market implication is neutral pending the release of trial results, which will determine the drug's future potential.
