## Sanofi's Phase 3 Meningococcal Vaccine Trial NCT04084769 Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2020-09-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On September 14, 2020, Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04084769 reached its primary completion date. The study evaluated Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine menacyw conjugate vaccine, Meningococcal group b vaccine (trumenba®), and Meningococcal group b vaccine (bexsero®). The trial enrolled 570 participants and has reported results, marking a key procedural milestone in the development of these vaccines.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for Sanofi's vaccine development, with neutral market implications given the lack of specific outcome data in the event summary.
