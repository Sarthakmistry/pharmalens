## Sanofi's Phase 3 trial NCT03368664 terminated

**Date:** 2021-05-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03368664, was officially terminated on May 4, 2021. The trial had an actual enrollment of 16 participants and reached its primary completion on the same date. This termination means the study will not continue as initially planned, impacting the development path for the investigated drug, Alemtuzumab gz402673.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial is generally a bearish signal for Sanofi, as it indicates a setback in its pipeline development and potential future revenue streams for the drug involved.
