## Sanofi's Trial NCT044498832 for Influenza Vaccines Completed

**Date:** 2021-01-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04498832, on January 14, 2021. This trial investigated High-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccine and Local standard-dose inactivated quadrivalent influenza vaccine. The study enrolled 2100 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard operational milestone for Sanofi, with no immediate market impact without specific results.
