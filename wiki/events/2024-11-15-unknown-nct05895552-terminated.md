## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial NCT05895552 for Rta 901

**Date:** 2024-11-15 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05895552) for the drug Rta 901. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 209 participants, reached its primary completion on November 15, 2024, and was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for an untracked drug is unlikely to have a significant market impact on Eli Lilly.
