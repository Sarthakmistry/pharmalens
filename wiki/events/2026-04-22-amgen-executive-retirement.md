## Amgen Announces Executive VP and CTO David M. Reese's Retirement

**Date:** 2026-04-22 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Amgen Inc. announced the retirement of David M. Reese, Executive Vice President and Chief Technology Officer, effective June 30, 2026. His responsibilities will be redistributed among James E. Bradner, Murdo Gordon, and Peter H. Griffith, effective June 1, 2026, as part of an internal restructuring to integrate science, technology, and data.
