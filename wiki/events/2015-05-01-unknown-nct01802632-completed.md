## AstraZeneca's Azd9291 trial NCT01802632 completes primary phase

**Date:** 2015-05-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's clinical trial NCT01802632, investigating the drug Azd9291 for indications including Alzheimer's disease and non-small cell lung cancer, reached its primary completion on May 1, 2015. This Phase 1/2 study enrolled 603 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides further data for AstraZeneca's pipeline, with market implications dependent on the eventual disclosure of results.
