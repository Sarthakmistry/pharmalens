## AstraZeneca's Azd9291 Phase 1/2 Trial for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2015-05-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT01802632) investigating Azd9291 for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on May 1, 2015. The study enrolled 603 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
This marks a routine progression in AstraZeneca's oncology pipeline, with the completion of a key development milestone for Azd9291.
