## Johnson & Johnson Q1 2025 Earnings Show Mixed Performance with Strong Oncology and MedTech Growth Offset by STELARA Decline

**Date:** 2025-04-23 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported Q1 2025 worldwide sales of $21.9 billion, a 2.4% increase (4.2% operational), driven by strong growth in its Oncology and MedTech segments. However, these gains were significantly impacted by a substantial decline in STELARA sales due to biosimilar competition and the effects of Medicare Part D redesign.

### Market implication
The neutral signal reflects the balance between strong operational growth in key segments like Oncology and MedTech, particularly Cardiovascular, and the significant headwinds from STELARA's biosimilar entry and the broader impact of Medicare Part D on several products, leading to a mixed financial outlook.
