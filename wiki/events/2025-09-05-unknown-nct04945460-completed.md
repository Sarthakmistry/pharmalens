## Clinical Trial NCT04945460 for Sotatercept Reaches Primary Completion Date

**Date:** 2025-09-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04945460, investigating Sotatercept (0.3 mg/kg escalating to 0.7 mg/kg) for heart failure and hypertension, has reached its primary completion date on September 5, 2025. This Phase 2 study, sponsored by Eli Lilly, enrolled 164 participants. Results for the primary endpoint are not yet available.

### Market implication
As results are not yet available for this trial, the market implication for Eli Lilly is neutral.
