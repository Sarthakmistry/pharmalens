## Eli Lilly's Trial NCT04945460 for Sotatercept in HF-HTN completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04945460, sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating Sotatercept (0.3 mg/kg escalating to 0.7 mg/kg) for heart failure with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF), reached its primary completion date on September 5, 2025. The Phase 2 study enrolled 164 participants and is now completed, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a standard progression in the drug development pipeline for Eli Lilly, with market implications remaining neutral until results are disclosed.
