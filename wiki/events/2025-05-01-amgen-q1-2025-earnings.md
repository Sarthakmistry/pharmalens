## Amgen Reports Strong Q1 2025 Operating Cash Flow and Free Cash Flow, Records Otezla Impairment

**Date:** 2025-05-01 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Amgen Inc. announced its first quarter 2025 financial results, reporting significant increases in net cash provided by operating activities to $1,391 million and free cash flow to $980 million, compared to the prior year. The company also recorded an impairment charge for the Otezla® intangible asset during the quarter, while providing full-year GAAP diluted EPS guidance of $12.21 to $13.46 and non-GAAP diluted EPS guidance of $20.00 to $21.20.

### Market implication
The substantial increase in operating cash flow and free cash flow indicates a strong financial performance, suggesting a bullish outlook for Amgen's valuation despite the Otezla impairment.
