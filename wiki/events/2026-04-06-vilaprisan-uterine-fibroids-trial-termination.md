## Vilaprisan Uterine Fibroids Trial Termination

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03194646) assessing Vilaprisan for Uterine Fibroids, sponsored by Bayer, was terminated. This study aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of Vilaprisan compared to standard of care in subjects with uterine fibroids.

### Market implication
The termination of the Phase 3 trial for Vilaprisan in Uterine Fibroids indicates a halt in its development for this indication, representing a negative signal for Bayer's pipeline in this area.
