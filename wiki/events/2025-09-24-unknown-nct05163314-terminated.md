## Takeda terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05163314 for Soticlestat

**Date:** 2025-09-24 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05163314, which was investigating Soticlestat. The trial had an actual enrollment of 352 participants and was originally expected to reach primary completion on September 24, 2025. The termination of a late-stage trial indicates a significant setback for the development of Soticlestat.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Soticlestat is a bearish signal for Takeda, as it represents the discontinuation of a late-stage asset and potential loss of future revenue.
