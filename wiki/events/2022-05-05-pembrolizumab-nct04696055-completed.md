## Phase 2 Trial of Pembrolizumab and Regorafenib in Advanced Solid Tumors Completes

**Date:** 2022-05-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04696055, investigating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]] and Regorafenib in patients with advanced solid tumors, reached its primary completion on May 5, 2022. The study enrolled 95 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step and does not immediately convey market-moving information without the release of detailed results.
