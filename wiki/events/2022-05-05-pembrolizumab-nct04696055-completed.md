## Pembrolizumab trial NCT04696055 completes Phase 2 study in advanced solid tumors

**Date:** 2022-05-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04696055, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Regorafenib for advanced solid tumors, reached its primary completion date on May 5, 2022. The trial, sponsored by [[bayer]], enrolled 95 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant step in the development of [[pembrolizumab]] for broader oncology indications.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides further data for [[pembrolizumab]]'s potential in advanced solid tumors, offering a neutral signal as results are now available for evaluation.
