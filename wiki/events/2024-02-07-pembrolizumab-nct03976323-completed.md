## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial KEYNOTE-826 for NSCLC Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-02-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, KEYNOTE-826 (NCT03976323), investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with platinum-doublet chemotherapy for metastatic non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), has reached its primary completion date on February 7, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 1003 participants, has reported that results are available, indicating the study has concluded its main data collection phase.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone for [[pembrolizumab]] in [[oncology-nsclc]], with market implications pending the release and analysis of the trial's full results.
