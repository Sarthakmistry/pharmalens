## Pfizer's Tofacitinib Phase 3 Trial for Systemic Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis Completes

**Date:** 2024-03-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 randomized withdrawal study of Tofacitinib in pediatric patients with Systemic Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis (sJIA) has completed. The trial, sponsored by Pfizer, enrolled 100 patients and evaluated the time to sJIA disease flare. While the trial is complete, specific outcomes were not provided in this document.
