## AbbVie's Phase 4 Trial for OnabotulinumtoxinA, Juvéderm, Coolsculpting, SkinMedica, and Kybella Completed

**Date:** 2026-02-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie's Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT06783621), investigating OnabotulinumtoxinA, Juvéderm, Coolsculpting elite system, Skinmedica products, and Kybella, reached primary completion on February 26, 2026. The trial enrolled 130 participants and is now completed, though results have not yet been released.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides an update on AbbVie's clinical pipeline for its aesthetic and therapeutic products, with further implications awaiting the release of study results.
