## Eli Lilly's Tirzepatide Phase 3 Trial NCT04184622 for Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2022-04-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04184622) for [[tirzepatide]] in the [[glp1-obesity/_index]] indication reached its primary completion date on April 1, 2022. The trial enrolled 2539 participants and has reported results, indicating the conclusion of the primary data collection phase.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial paves the way for the release of clinical data, which will be crucial for assessing tirzepatide's efficacy and safety profile in obesity, potentially impacting its market position.
