## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial NCT06531538 for 20-valent Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-02-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06531538, evaluating a 20-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (20vPnC) in healthy adults, has reached its primary completion date on February 9, 2026. The study, which enrolled 225 participants, is designed to assess the safety and immunogenicity of the vaccine. No results have been posted yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step, with market implications awaiting the release of clinical data.
