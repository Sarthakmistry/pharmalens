## Pfizer's Phase 2 trial NCT06531538 for Pg4 and 20-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (20vPNC) completes primary endpoint

Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06531538, evaluating Pg4 and 20-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (20vPNC), reached its primary completion date on February 9, 2026. The study enrolled 225 participants. No results from this trial have been released yet.
