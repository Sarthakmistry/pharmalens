## Atezolizumab trial NCT05450562 terminated

**Date:** 2025-12-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05450562) investigating atezolizumab, sponsored by Sanofi, has been terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 33 participants, was originally set to complete its primary phase on December 22, 2025. The termination indicates a halt in the development of this specific study.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial, especially in early phases, is a bearish signal for the drug's development pipeline and the sponsoring company, as it removes a potential future revenue stream.
