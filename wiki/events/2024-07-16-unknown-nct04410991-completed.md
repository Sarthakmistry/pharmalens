## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial NCT04410991 for Tolebrutinib and Teriflunomide hmr1726 Completed

Sanofi announced the completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04410991, investigating Tolebrutinib and Teriflunomide hmr1726. The trial, which enrolled 899 participants, reached its primary completion date on July 16, 2024.
