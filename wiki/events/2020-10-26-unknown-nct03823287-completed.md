## Roche's NCT03823287 trial involving Faricimab and Aflibercept completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2020-10-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's clinical trial NCT03823287, investigating Faricimab and Aflibercept, reached its primary completion date on October 26, 2020. This Phase 3 study enrolled 671 participants and has reported results. The trial's completion marks a significant milestone in the development of these therapies.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step and is considered a neutral event for Roche's market valuation until detailed results are publicly disclosed.
