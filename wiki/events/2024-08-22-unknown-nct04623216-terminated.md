## Novartis terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04623216 for Sabatolimab and Azacitidine

**Date:** 2024-08-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04623216) investigating Sabatolimab in combination with Azacitidine. The study, which had an actual enrollment of 24 participants, reached its primary completion on August 22, 2024, and has results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this early-stage Phase 1/2 trial for Sabatolimab and Azacitidine represents a minor setback for Novartis's oncology pipeline, potentially indicating issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic reprioritization.
