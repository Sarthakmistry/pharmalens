## Novartis terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT04623216) for Sabatolimab and Azacitidine

**Date:** 2024-08-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04623216) investigating Sabatolimab and Azacitidine. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 24 participants, reached its primary completion on August 22, 2024, and has results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial for Sabatolimab and Azacitidine could be seen as a bearish signal for [[novartis]], potentially indicating issues with efficacy or safety, or a strategic reprioritization of its pipeline.
