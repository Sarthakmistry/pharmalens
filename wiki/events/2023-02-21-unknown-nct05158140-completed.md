## Merck's Phase 3 Trial NCT05158140 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-02-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05158140, sponsored by Merck, reached its primary completion on February 21, 2023. The trial enrolled 850 participants and has reported that results are available. This marks a significant procedural milestone in the development of the investigational products V110, V114, and Mrna-1273.
