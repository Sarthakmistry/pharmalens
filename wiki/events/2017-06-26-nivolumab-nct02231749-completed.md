## Nivolumab Trial NCT02231749 Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2017-06-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02231749) evaluating [[nivolumab]] as a monotherapy or in combination with Ipilimumab versus Sunitinib for previously untreated advanced or metastatic Renal Cell Carcinoma reached its primary completion date on June 26, 2017. The trial enrolled 1096 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant development milestone for Bristol-Myers Squibb's nivolumab, providing data for further analysis and potential regulatory submissions.
