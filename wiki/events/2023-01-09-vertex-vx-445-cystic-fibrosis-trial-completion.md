## Vertex Completes Phase 3 Trial for VX-445 in Cystic Fibrosis

**Date:** 2023-01-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated completed a Phase 3 study (NCT03525574) evaluating the long-term safety and efficacy of VX-445 combination therapy for Cystic Fibrosis. The trial enrolled 507 participants and focused on assessing safety and tolerability as its primary outcome.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for VX-445 in Cystic Fibrosis represents a procedural milestone for Vertex, with no immediate market impact as results are not yet available.
