## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 VZV vaccine trial NCT05703607

**Date:** 2025-09-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05703607) for Varicella Zoster Virus (VZV) vaccine candidates (pf-07915234, pf-07921188, pf-07921186) on September 23, 2025. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 484 participants, was investigating multiple vaccine candidates. The termination indicates a halt in the development of these specific candidates.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial for VZV vaccine candidates represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline in this area, potentially impacting future revenue projections from these specific assets.
