## AbbVie Reports Strong Q2 2025 Earnings with Increased EPS and Revenue Growth

**Date:** 2025-07-31 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported robust second-quarter 2025 financial results, with adjusted diluted EPS increasing by 12.1% to $2.97 and net revenues growing by 6.6% to $15.423 billion. This strong performance was primarily driven by significant growth in the Immunology and Neuroscience portfolios, with key products like Skyrizi and Rinvoq contributing substantially to revenue.

### Market implication
The strong financial results and growth across key therapeutic areas suggest a positive outlook for AbbVie, likely leading to increased investor confidence and a bullish market response for the company's stock.
