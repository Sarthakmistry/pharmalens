## Eli Lilly's Trial NCT06370715 for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT06370715, investigating Insulin lispro-aabc and Insulin glargine for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion on September 2, 2025. The trial enrolled 150 participants. Results from this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 4 trial marks a step forward in the development process, with market implications pending the release of results.
