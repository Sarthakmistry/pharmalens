## Eli Lilly's Trial NCT06370715 for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT06370715, investigating Insulin lispro-aabc and Insulin glargine for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on September 2, 2025. The study enrolled 150 participants and has been marked as completed, though results are not yet available. This milestone indicates the conclusion of data collection for the primary outcome measure.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial marks a procedural milestone for Eli Lilly in its Type 2 Diabetes research, with market implications awaiting the release of study results.
