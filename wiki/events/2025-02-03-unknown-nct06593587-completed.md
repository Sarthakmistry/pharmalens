## Pfizer's RSVpref Vaccine Phase 3 Trial (NCT06593587) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-02-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06593587) investigating Pfizer's RSVpref vaccine has reached its primary completion date on February 3, 2025. The trial successfully enrolled 378 participants and has reported that results are available. This milestone indicates the conclusion of the primary data collection period for the study.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and does not immediately imply a market impact without the release and analysis of the trial results.
