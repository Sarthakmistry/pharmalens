## D.R. Horton Amends Credit Agreements, Increasing Commitments and Extending Maturity

**Date:** 2026-03-31 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
D.R. Horton, Inc. and its subsidiary DRH Rental, Inc. amended their credit agreements, increasing D.R. Horton's Aggregate Revolving Credit Commitment to $3.295 billion and Aggregate Credit Facility Limit to $4.0 billion. The amendments also extended DRH Rental's credit facility termination date to March 27, 2030, and modified applicable interest rate margins and refreshed extension options for both agreements.

### Market implication
The amendments provide increased financial flexibility through higher credit commitments and extended maturity dates, which are generally positive, but it's a routine financial update.
