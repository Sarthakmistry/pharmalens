## Sanofi's Phase 3 influenza vaccine trial NCT04137887 terminated after primary completion

**Date:** 2020-05-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04137887, evaluating Quadrivalent influenza vaccine (split virion, inactivated) high-dose (qiv-hd) and Standard-dose inactivated influenza vaccine quadrivalent, northern hemisphere strains (qiv-sd), was terminated on May 31, 2020. The trial had reached its primary completion on the same date and results are available, indicating the study concluded its main objectives before formal termination.

### Market implication
The termination of this trial, following its primary completion, is a neutral event for Sanofi as the study's objectives appear to have been met and results are available.
