## Regeneron terminates Phase 2 trial of Regn5381 for heart failure

**Date:** 2025-09-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06237309) investigating the drug Regn5381 for heart failure. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 89 participants, reached its primary completion date on September 17, 2025, but was subsequently terminated. This termination represents a setback for Regn5381's development in the heart failure indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Regn5381 could be viewed negatively by the market, indicating a potential halt in the development of this asset for heart failure.
