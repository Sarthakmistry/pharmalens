## Regeneron terminates Phase 2 trial NCT06237309 for investigational drug Regn5381 in heart failure

**Date:** 2025-09-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06237309) for the investigational drug Regn5381, which was studying its efficacy in heart failure. The trial had an actual enrollment of 89 participants and was originally expected to complete its primary endpoint on September 17, 2025. The reason for termination was not specified in the available information.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Regn5381 represents a setback for Regeneron's pipeline in the heart failure indication, potentially impacting future revenue streams from this program.
