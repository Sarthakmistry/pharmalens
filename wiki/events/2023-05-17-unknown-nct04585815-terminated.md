## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04585815 for Sasanlimab, Encorafenib, Binimetinib, Axitinib, and Sea-tgt in oncology-nsclc

**Date:** 2023-05-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04585815) investigating Sasanlimab, Encorafenib, Binimetinib, Axitinib, and Sea-tgt for non-small cell lung cancer (oncology-nsclc). The trial had an actual enrollment of 34 participants and reached its primary completion date on May 17, 2023. The termination of an early-phase trial can be due to various reasons including futility, safety concerns, or strategic business decisions.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a minor negative signal for [[pfizer]]'s oncology pipeline, as it removes a potential treatment option in oncology-nsclc, though the impact is likely limited given the early stage of development and the non-tracked status of the drugs involved.
