## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT04585815) for untracked oncology drugs in NSCLC

**Date:** 2023-05-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04585815) investigating Sasanlimab, Encorafenib, Binimetinib, Axitinib, and Sea-tgt for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 34 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 17, 2023, and results are available. The termination indicates a halt in the development of these specific drug combinations for this indication by Pfizer.

### Market implication
This trial termination represents a minor setback for Pfizer's early-stage oncology pipeline, removing a Phase 1/2 asset, though the impact on the company's overall valuation is likely limited given the untracked status of the specific drugs involved.
