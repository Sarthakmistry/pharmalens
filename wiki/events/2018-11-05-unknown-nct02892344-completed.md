## Novartis's Phase 3 Trial NCT02892344 Completed

**Date:** 2018-11-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT02892344, reached its primary completion date on November 5, 2018. The study, which enrolled 802 participants, has reported results. The trial investigated Qmf149 150/80 µg and Mf 200 µg.
