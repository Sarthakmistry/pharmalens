## Novartis completes Phase 3 trial NCT02892344

**Date:** 2018-11-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis completed its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT02892344 on November 5, 2018. The trial, which enrolled 802 participants, has reported results. The study investigated Qmf149 150/80 µg and Mf 200 µg.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides data for further evaluation of the studied compounds.
