## Johnson & Johnson Reports Q3 2025 Earnings with Strong Oncology Growth Amidst STELARA Biosimilar Impact

**Date:** 2025-10-22 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported worldwide sales of $69.6 billion for the fiscal nine months of 2025, an operational increase of 4.7%, driven by strong performance in Innovative Medicine, particularly in Oncology. This growth was significantly offset by a 6.1% negative impact from declining STELARA sales due to biosimilar competition and Medicare Part D redesign.

### Market implication
The neutral signal reflects the balanced performance with strong growth in key oncology products and acquisitions being largely counteracted by the substantial decline of STELARA due to biosimilar entry and other competitive pressures.
