## Roche's Atezolizumab and Bevacizumab Trial NCT05908786 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-08-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05908786, sponsored by Roche and investigating [[atezolizumab]] and [[bevacizumab]] in combination with Tiragolumab and Tobemstomig for oncology-nsclc, reached its primary completion date on August 27, 2025. This Phase 1/2 study enrolled 62 participants and is now completed, with results pending.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial is a standard procedural step and does not immediately impact the market, pending future data release.
