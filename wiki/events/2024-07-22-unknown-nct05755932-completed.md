## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 Trial NCT05755932 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-07-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05755932, reached its primary completion date on July 22, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 35 participants, has completed its primary data collection, though results are not yet available. The study involved the investigational drugs Hfo-1234ze(e) and Hfa-134a.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step, and without results, it carries a neutral market implication for AstraZeneca.
