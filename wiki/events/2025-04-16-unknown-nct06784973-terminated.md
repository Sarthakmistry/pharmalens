## Gilead's Phase 2 Trial (NCT06784973) for Obeldesivir Terminated

**Date:** 2025-04-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT06784973, investigating Obeldesivir and sponsored by Gilead, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 4 participants and was originally expected to reach primary completion on April 16, 2025. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Obeldesivir within this specific trial.
