## Gilead's Phase 2 Trial for Obeldesivir (NCT06784973) Terminated

**Date:** 2025-04-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Gilead Sciences has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06784973) for the drug Obeldesivir, which was investigating its efficacy. The trial, which had a primary completion date of April 16, 2025, was terminated early after enrolling only 4 actual participants. The reason for termination was not specified, but early termination with low enrollment typically indicates issues such as futility, safety concerns, or business decisions.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Obeldesivir represents a setback for Gilead's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue streams from this specific asset.
