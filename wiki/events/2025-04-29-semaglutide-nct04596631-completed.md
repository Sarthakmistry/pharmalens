## Semaglutide (NCT04596631) Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04596631) investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on April 29, 2025. The trial, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, enrolled 132 participants and has now concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for semaglutide is a neutral event, as no new efficacy or safety data has been released, but it marks progress in the drug's development lifecycle.
