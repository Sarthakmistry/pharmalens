## Novo Nordisk completes Phase 3 trial NCT04596631 for semaglutide in Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk has announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04596631, for semaglutide in patients with Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 132 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 29, 2025, marking a significant milestone in the development pathway for the drug. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as it signifies progress in the clinical development of semaglutide, with market impact awaiting the release and analysis of the study's results.
