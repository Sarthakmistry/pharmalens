## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 Benralizumab trial (NCT05251909) terminated

**Date:** 2024-02-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05251909, investigating Benralizumab, was officially terminated on February 13, 2024. The trial had an actual enrollment of 12 participants and reached its primary completion on the same date.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even with low enrollment, represents a negative development for Benralizumab and AstraZeneca's pipeline, potentially indicating issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic priorities.
