## Eli Lilly Reports Strong Q4 and Full Year 2025 Financial Results

**Date:** 2026-02-04 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company reported strong financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2025. The company saw significant increases in net income and diluted earnings per share compared to the prior year, with Q4 2025 Reported Net Income reaching $6,636 million and Full Year 2025 Reported Net Income at $20,640 million. The results also excluded charges related to intangible asset impairment for Vitrakvi for the three months ended December 31, 2024.

### Market implication
The reported Net Income for Q4 2025 increased by approximately 50% and for Full Year 2025 by approximately 95% compared to the respective prior periods, with similar strong growth in diluted EPS, indicating a bullish outlook for the company's valuation.
