## Novartis Completes Phase 1/2 Trial of Branaplam (LMI070) for Type 1 Spinal Muscular Atrophy

**Date:** 2022-12-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis Pharmaceuticals completed an open-label, multi-part, first-in-human Phase 1/2 study (NCT02268552) of branaplam (LMI070) in infants with Type 1 Spinal Muscular Atrophy. The trial aimed to assess the safety and preliminary efficacy of the investigational drug.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides initial safety data for branaplam in Type 1 SMA, with no immediate significant market impact.
