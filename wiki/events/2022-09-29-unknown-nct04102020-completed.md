## Phase 3 Clinical Trial NCT04102020 for Venetoclax and Azacitidine Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-09-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04102020) sponsored by AbbVie, investigating Venetoclax in combination with Azacitidine, completed its primary endpoint on September 29, 2022. The trial enrolled 112 participants and has reported results, marking a key milestone in its development.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial with available results indicates progress in the development of the Venetoclax and Azacitidine combination, paving the way for potential data dissemination and further regulatory steps.
