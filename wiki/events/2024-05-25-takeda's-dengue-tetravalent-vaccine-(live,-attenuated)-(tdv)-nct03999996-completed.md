## Takeda's Dengue Tetravalent Vaccine (TAK-003) Trial NCT03999996 Completed; Post-hoc Analysis Shows No Increased Adverse Pregnancy Outcomes

**Date:** 2024-05-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 3 trial (NCT03999996) for its dengue tetravalent vaccine (TAK-003) has completed. A post-hoc analysis of 557 pregnancies found no evidence of increased adverse pregnancy outcomes following unintentional TAK-003 vaccination compared with placebo, suggesting a favorable safety profile regarding pregnancy.

### Market implication
This positive safety data regarding pregnancy outcomes for Takeda's dengue vaccine (TAK-003) could support broader use and market acceptance, representing a bullish signal for the company.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Takeda's dengue tetravalent vaccine (live, attenuated) (tdv)
- Company: [[takeda]]

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 557 pregnancies
- **Comparator:** placebo
- **Primary outcome:** pregnancy outcomes and neonatal adverse events (AEs)
- **Primary result:** Of the spontaneous abortions, there was no significant difference between TAK-003 and placebo recipients, or between those occurring within or outside the exposure window.
- **Secondary results:**
    - 38 pregnancies (TAK-003, n=28/375; placebo, n=10/182) occurred inside the exposure window.
    - 28 live births (TAK-003, n=23; placebo, n=5) from pregnancies inside the exposure window.
    - 4 elective terminations (TAK-003, n=2; placebo, n=2) from pregnancies inside the exposure window.
    - 5 spontaneous abortions (TAK-003, n=3; placebo, n=2) from pregnancies inside the exposure window.
    - 6 participants who received TAK-003 in the exposure window and 2 neonates experienced serious AEs.
- **Safety:** Serious AEs were none considered TAK-003 related.
- **Conclusions:** This post-hoc analysis found no evidence of increased adverse pregnancy outcomes following unintentional TAK-003 vaccination occurring inside the exposure window compared with placebo.
- **Journal:** Expert Rev Vaccines, 2025
- **Industry sponsored:** True
