## Novartis' Phase 2 Trial NCT07235033 for Investigational Drugs Completed

**Date:** 2025-03-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis' Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT07235033, investigating the drugs Kae609, Soc (coartem), and Klu156, reached its primary completion date on March 5, 2025. The study enrolled 60 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as the market awaits the release of the study's results to assess the potential efficacy and safety profile of the investigational drugs.
