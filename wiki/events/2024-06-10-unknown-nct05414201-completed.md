## AbbVie's Adalimumab Phase 4 Trial (NCT05414201) completes

**Date:** 2024-06-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05414201) sponsored by AbbVie, investigating Adalimumab, reached its primary completion date on June 10, 2024. The trial enrolled 87 participants and has not yet posted results.
