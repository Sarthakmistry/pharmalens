## Eli Lilly's Tirzepatide Phase 3 Trial (NCT06047548) for GLP-1 Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06047548) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating [[tirzepatide]] for GLP-1 obesity, reached its primary completion date on January 20, 2026. The study enrolled 441 participants and has not yet reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the trial's progression towards potential data readout.
