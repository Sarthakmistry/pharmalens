## AstraZeneca's Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (szc) Phase 4 Trial NCT05347693 Completes Primary Completion

AstraZeneca's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05347693, investigating Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (szc), reached its primary completion date on December 10, 2024. The trial enrolled 186 participants and has reported that results are available.
