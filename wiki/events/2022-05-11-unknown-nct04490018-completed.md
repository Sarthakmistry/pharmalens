## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial NCT04490018 for Meningococcal Vaccines Completes Primary Study

**Date:** 2022-05-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04490018), evaluating meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups A, C, Y, and W) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine alongside other vaccines, reached its primary completion on May 11, 2022. The study, which enrolled 463 participants, has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard step in the drug development process and is neutral for Sanofi's valuation at this stage, awaiting detailed results.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine (MenACWY conjugate vaccine), Meningococcal group a, c, w-135, and y conjugate vaccine, Human papillomavirus 9-valent vaccine (9vHPV), Tetanus toxoid, reduced diphtheria toxoid and acellular pertussis vaccine adsorbed combined with inactivated poliomyelitis vaccine (Tdap-IPV)
