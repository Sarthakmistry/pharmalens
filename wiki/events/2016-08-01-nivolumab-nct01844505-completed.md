## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Phase 3 Trial NCT01844505 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2016-08-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT01844505) for [[nivolumab]] reached its primary completion date on August 1, 2016. The trial, which enrolled 945 participants, has results available, indicating the completion of its main data collection phase.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this large Phase 3 trial for nivolumab marks a procedural milestone for Bristol-Myers Squibb, with further analysis of results expected to inform future development or regulatory submissions.
