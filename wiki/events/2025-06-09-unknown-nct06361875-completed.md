## Sanofi's Quadrivalent Influenza mRNA Vaccine Trial (NCT06361875) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-06-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT06361875) sponsored by Sanofi, evaluating several quadrivalent influenza mRNA vaccine candidates (mrt5421, mrt5424, mrt5429) against standard and high-dose influenza vaccines, has reached its primary completion date. The study enrolled 910 participants to assess the safety and immunogenicity of the investigational vaccines. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and is neutral for Sanofi's valuation until results are announced.
