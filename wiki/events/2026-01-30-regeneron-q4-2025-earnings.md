## Regeneron Reports Strong Q4 and Full-Year 2025 Results Driven by Dupixent and EYLEA HD Growth, with EYLEA HD Receiving New FDA Approval

**Date:** 2026-01-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron announced its Q4 and full year 2025 financial results, reporting a 3% increase in Q4 revenues to $3.9 billion and a 1% increase in full-year revenues to $14.3 billion. Key growth drivers included Dupixent, with global net sales up 26% to $17.8 billion for the full year, and EYLEA HD, which saw U.S. net sales increase 78% to $2.57 billion. EYLEA HD also received FDA approval for the treatment of macular edema following retinal vein occlusion (RVO) and for monthly dosing flexibility.

### Market implication
The strong performance of Dupixent and EYLEA HD, coupled with a new FDA approval for EYLEA HD, indicates robust growth in Regeneron's key product portfolio, which is a bullish signal for the company's valuation.
