## Regeneron Reports Strong Q4 2025 Earnings Driven by Dupixent and EYLEA HD Growth, EYLEA HD Receives New FDA Approvals

**Date:** 2026-01-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals reported strong financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2025. The growth was primarily driven by significant sales increases for Dupixent and EYLEA HD, with EYLEA HD also receiving new FDA approvals for macular edema following retinal vein occlusion (RVO) and for monthly dosing flexibility. Overall revenues for Q4 2025 increased by 3% to $3.9 billion.

### Market implication
This strong financial performance, coupled with key regulatory approvals for EYLEA HD, is expected to have a bullish impact on Regeneron's valuation and future growth prospects.
