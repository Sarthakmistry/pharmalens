## Novartis terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05678959 for Ligelizumab

**Date:** 2025-03-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05678959, which was investigating Ligelizumab 120 mg and Ligelizumab 240 mg. The trial had an actual enrollment of 163 participants and reached its primary completion date on March 6, 2025, before being terminated. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Ligelizumab within the scope of this specific trial.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Ligelizumab represents a negative development for Novartis, potentially impacting the drug's future prospects and the company's pipeline in the relevant therapeutic area.
