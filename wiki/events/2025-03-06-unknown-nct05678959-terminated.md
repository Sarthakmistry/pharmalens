## Novartis terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05678959 for Ligelizumab

**Date:** 2025-03-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05678959) which was investigating Ligelizumab 120 mg and Ligelizumab 240 mg. The trial had an actual enrollment of 163 participants and reached its primary completion on March 6, 2025, prior to its termination. The specific reason for the termination was not provided in the source document.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue expectations for Ligelizumab and signaling a negative development for the program.
