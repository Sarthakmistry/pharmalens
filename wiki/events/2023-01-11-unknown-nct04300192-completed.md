## Sanofi's Phase 4 trial NCT04300192 for a Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis, and Polio vaccine completed its primary phase.

**Date:** 2023-01-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT04300192, which investigated a Tetanus toxoid, reduced diphtheria toxoid and acellular pertussis vaccine adsorbed combined with inactivated poliomyelitis vaccine, reached its primary completion date on January 11, 2023. The trial enrolled 273 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial for a vaccine is a standard operational update for Sanofi and is expected to have a neutral market impact, particularly as results have not yet been published.
