## Eli Lilly's Phase 2 Trial for Ly3556050 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

This marks a significant milestone in the drug's development, moving it closer to potential data readout.
