## AstraZeneca Terminates Phase 3 Trial (NCT04157335) for Benralizumab

**Date:** 2024-08-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AstraZeneca announced the termination of its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04157335) for Benralizumab 30 mg on August 6, 2024. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 295 participants, had already reached its primary completion date. While the specific reasons for termination were not immediately disclosed, the trial's results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even if results are available, typically represents a setback for the specific drug and indication, potentially leading to a bearish market reaction for AstraZeneca's related pipeline assets.
