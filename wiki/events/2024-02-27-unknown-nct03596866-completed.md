## Takeda's Phase 3 Trial NCT03596866 for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2024-02-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03596866, investigating Brigatinib and Alectinib for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on February 27, 2024. The trial enrolled 248 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Takeda's pipeline in NSCLC, with market impact pending the release of trial results.
