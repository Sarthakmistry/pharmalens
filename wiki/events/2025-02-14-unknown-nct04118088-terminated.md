## Takeda terminates Phase 4 trial NCT04118088 for Darvadstrocel

**Date:** 2025-02-14 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04118088) for Darvadstrocel. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 53 participants, reached its primary completion date on February 14, 2025, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 4 trial, even with results available, could suggest a re-evaluation of Darvadstrocel's post-market strategy, safety profile, or commercial viability, potentially having a negative impact on Takeda.
