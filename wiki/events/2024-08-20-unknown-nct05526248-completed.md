## Bayer's Phase 2 Trial (NCT05526248) for Darolutamide and Enzalutamide Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2024-08-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05526248, investigating Darolutamide and Enzalutamide, reached its primary completion on August 20, 2024. The study enrolled 28 participants, and results are now available.

The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a procedural milestone for Bayer's oncology pipeline, with the availability of results allowing for further evaluation of the compounds.
