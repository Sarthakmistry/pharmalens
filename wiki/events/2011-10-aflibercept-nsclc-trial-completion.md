### Summary
A Phase 3 study (NCT00532155) sponsored by Sanofi, with Regeneron Pharmaceuticals as a collaborator, has reached primary completion. The trial investigated the combination of aflibercept and docetaxel against docetaxel and placebo in 913 participants with locally advanced or metastatic non-small cell lung cancer, with Overall Survival (OS) as the primary outcome.
