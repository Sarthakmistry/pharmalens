## Amgen's Erenumab Trial NCT03971071 for Alzheimer's Disease Completed

**Date:** 2022-12-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT03971071) sponsored by [[amgen]], investigating Erenumab (70 mg and 140 mg) for [[alzheimers]] disease, reached its primary completion on December 1, 2022. The trial enrolled 620 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides further data on Erenumab's profile, with potential implications for its use in Alzheimer's disease.
