## Bristol Myers Squibb Terminates Phase 3 Trial of Nivolumab (NCT04149574) for Bladder Cancer

**Date:** 2023-10-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04149574) investigating [[nivolumab]] in combination with Bacillus Calmette-Guerin (BCG) for patients with high-risk non-muscle invasive bladder cancer unresponsive to BCG. The trial reached primary completion on October 31, 2023, with an actual enrollment of 13 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 3 trial for [[nivolumab]] represents a minor setback for [[bristol-myers-squibb]]'s oncology pipeline, specifically impacting its potential in the bladder cancer indication.
