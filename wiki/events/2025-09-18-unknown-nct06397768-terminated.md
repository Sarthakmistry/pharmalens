## Sanofi's Phase 3 RSV vaccine trial (NCT06397768) terminated

**Date:** 2025-09-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06397768) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating an RSV vaccine, was terminated on September 18, 2025. The trial had an actual enrollment of 2226 participants and was originally expected to complete its primary endpoint on the termination date. The reason for termination is not specified in the available data.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for an RSV vaccine represents a setback for Sanofi's pipeline in this indication, potentially impacting future market share in the respiratory vaccine space.
