## Sanofi terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT06508489) involving Sar443579, Venetoclax, and Azacitidine.

**Date:** 2025-08-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial, NCT06508489, which was investigating the combination of Sar443579 with Venetoclax and Azacitidine. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 7 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 8, 2025, but was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of an early-phase clinical trial is generally a bearish signal for the involved company, as it indicates a halt in the development of a potential new therapeutic agent or combination.
