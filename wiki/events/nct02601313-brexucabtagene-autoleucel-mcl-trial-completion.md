## Trial Completion for Brexucabtagene Autoleucel (KTE-X19) in Mantle Cell Lymphoma

**Date:** 2023-09-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical study of brexucabtagene autoleucel (KTE-X19) in participants with relapsed/refractory mantle cell lymphoma (NCT02601313) has completed. The study, sponsored by Kite, a Gilead Company, enrolled 105 participants to evaluate objective response rates.

### Market implication
This trial completion provides an update on the development timeline for brexucabtagene autoleucel, with neutral immediate market implications.
