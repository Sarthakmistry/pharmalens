## Sanofi's Phase 3 Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT04544267) Terminated

**Date:** 2021-06-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04544267), investigating Quadrivalent influenza vaccine, high-dose and Quadrivalent influenza vaccine, standard dose, was terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 100 participants and reached its primary completion date on June 2, 2021. The termination indicates the trial will not proceed as originally planned.

### Market implication
This termination of a vaccine trial for non-tracked drugs is unlikely to have a significant direct market impact on Sanofi's core pharmaceutical portfolio.
