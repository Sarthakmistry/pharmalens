## Sanofi completes Phase 3 trial NCT04537234 for high-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccine

**Date:** 2021-02-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On February 9, 2021, Sanofi announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04537234). The study, which enrolled 165 participants, compared a high-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccine against a standard-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccine. The trial has reported results.
