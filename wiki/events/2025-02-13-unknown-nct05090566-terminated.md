## Pfizer terminates Phase 2 trial NCT05090566 for Elranatamab combinations

**Date:** 2025-02-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05090566) investigating Elranatamab in combination with nirogacestat or lenalidomide + dexamethasone. The study, which had an actual enrollment of 46 participants, reached its primary completion date on February 13, 2025, and has reported results. The termination indicates a halt in the development of these specific combination therapies.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Elranatamab combinations represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline in this area, potentially impacting future revenue projections for these specific regimens.
