## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial NCT05090566 for Elranatamab Combinations Terminated

**Date:** 2025-02-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05090566), investigating combinations of Elranatamab with nirogacestat or lenalidomide plus dexamethasone, was officially terminated on February 13, 2025. The trial had an actual enrollment of 46 participants and had reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for untracked drug combinations is a factual event with a neutral market implication, as no specific reason for termination or impact on Pfizer's broader pipeline has been disclosed.
