## Regeneron Reports Strong Q3 2025 Earnings with Libtayo FDA Approval and Pipeline Progress

**Date:** 2025-10-28 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. announced its third quarter 2025 financial and operating results, reporting a 1% increase in total revenues to $3.75 billion. The company also highlighted the FDA approval of cemiplimab (Libtayo) for high-risk adjuvant cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (CSCC) and positive Phase 3 trial results across multiple therapeutic areas.

### Market implication
The positive financial results, coupled with a significant FDA approval for cemiplimab (Libtayo) and promising pipeline advancements, suggest a bullish outlook for Regeneron's valuation.
