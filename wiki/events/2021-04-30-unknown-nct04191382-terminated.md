## Sanofi's Amcenestrant (SAR439859) Trial NCT04191382 Terminated

**Date:** 2021-04-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04191382) sponsored by [[sanofi]] investigating Amcenestrant (SAR439859) in combination with Letrozole for oncology-breast was terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 105 participants and reached its primary completion on April 30, 2021, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Amcenestrant (SAR439859) by Sanofi is a neutral signal, as the drug is not a tracked entity and the reason for termination is not specified as safety or futility.
