## Clinical Trial NCT04605549 for Cin-107 Completed

**Date:** 2024-10-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04605549, a Phase 2 study sponsored by AstraZeneca, reached its primary completion on October 28, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 15 participants, investigated different dosing regimens of Cin-107 (2 mg, 4 mg, and 8 mg). Results from this study are now available.

### Market implication
As no specific results or further details regarding the trial's outcome have been disclosed, the market impact is currently neutral.
