## Merck's Pembrolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT04662710) for Colorectal Cancer Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2024-10-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04662710, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with lenvatinib and chemotherapy for advanced or metastatic colorectal cancer, reached its primary completion date on October 29, 2024. The study enrolled 895 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone for [[merck]]'s [[pembrolizumab]] in colorectal cancer, with upcoming data expected to inform its market potential.
