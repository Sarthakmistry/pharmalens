## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial (NCT04662710) for Colorectal Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04662710, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Lenvatinib and chemotherapy for advanced colorectal cancer, has reached its primary completion date on October 29, 2024. The trial, titled "A Study of Pembrolizumab Plus Lenvatinib and Chemotherapy in Participants With Advanced Colorectal Cancer (LEAP-017)," enrolled 895 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential new treatment options for colorectal cancer, with results expected to inform future regulatory submissions for [[merck]].
