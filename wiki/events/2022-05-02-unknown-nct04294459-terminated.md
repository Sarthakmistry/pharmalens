## Sanofi terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04294459

**Date:** 2022-05-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial, NCT04294459, which was investigating Isatuximab sar650984. The trial reached primary completion on May 2, 2022, with 23 participants enrolled, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this early-phase trial for an untracked drug is unlikely to have a significant market impact on Sanofi.
