## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 trial NCT05573464 completes primary completion

**Date:** 2024-03-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05573464, which enrolled 559 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 26, 2024. The trial investigated Bgf mdi hfo 320/14.4/9.6 µg and Bgf mdi hfa 320/14.4/9.6 µg. This procedural milestone indicates the completion of data collection for the primary outcome.
