## Sanofi's Phase 3 trial NCT05413369 for Type 2 Diabetes completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2023-10-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05413369, investigating Insulin glargine/lixisenatide, Idegasp, Metformin, and Sglt2 inhibitor for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on October 20, 2023. The study enrolled 582 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial allows Sanofi to proceed with data analysis, which is a standard and neutral step in the drug development process for its Type 2 Diabetes pipeline.
