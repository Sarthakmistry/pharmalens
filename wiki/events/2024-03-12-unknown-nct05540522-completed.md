## Pfizer's Phase 3 Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT05540522) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-03-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05540522) for a Quadrivalent influenza vaccine has reached its primary completion date on March 12, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 45,789 participants, has reported having collected primary endpoint data.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential regulatory submission for Pfizer's influenza vaccine, with future market implications dependent on the disclosed results.
