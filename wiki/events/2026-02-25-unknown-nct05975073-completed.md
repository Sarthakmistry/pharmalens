## Amgen's Phase 1/2 trial (NCT05975073) of Anvumetostat and Ide397 for NSCLC completes primary phase

Amgen's Phase 1/2 clinical trial, NCT05975073, investigating Anvumetostat and Ide397 for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on February 25, 2026. The trial enrolled 53 participants and has not yet posted results.
