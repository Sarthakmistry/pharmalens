## Pfizer Netherlands International Finance B.V. completes €3.3 billion notes offering guaranteed by Pfizer Inc.

**Date:** 2025-05-19 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer Inc.'s wholly-owned subsidiary, Pfizer Netherlands International Finance B.V., completed a public offering of €3.3 billion aggregate principal amount of notes. These notes are fully and unconditionally guaranteed by Pfizer Inc., representing a standard corporate financing activity.

### Market implication
