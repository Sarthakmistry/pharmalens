## Sanofi terminates Phase 2 trial of Amcenestrant (NCT04059484) for breast cancer

**Date:** 2022-02-15 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04059484) investigating Amcenestrant for the treatment of breast cancer. The trial, which enrolled 367 participants, reached its primary completion on February 15, 2022, before being terminated. This decision indicates a halt in the development of Amcenestrant for this indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Amcenestrant represents a setback for Sanofi's oncology pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue expectations from this asset.
