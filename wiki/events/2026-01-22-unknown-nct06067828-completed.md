## AstraZeneca's NCT06067828 Trial Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-01-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's clinical trial NCT06067828, investigating treatments involving budesonide, glycopyrronium, and formoterol fumarate, and budesonide and formoterol fumarate, reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2026. The Phase 3 study enrolled 171 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial is a neutral event as no results have been released, providing no new information to assess the potential success or failure of the treatments.
