## Lenvatinib and Pembrolizumab Endometrial Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2022-03-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03517449, studying lenvatinib in combination with pembrolizumab for advanced endometrial cancer, has been completed. The trial aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of the combination therapy against physician's choice of treatment.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a neutral event as no efficacy or safety results have been released. Further information will be needed to assess market impact.

### Affected entities
- Drug: lenvatinib, [[pembrolizumab]], doxorubicin, paclitaxel
- Company: [[eisai]], [[merck]]
- Indication: endometrial cancer

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 827 participants
- **Comparator:** treatment of physician's choice (doxorubicin or paclitaxel)
- **Primary outcome:** Progression Free Survival (PFS) Per Response Evaluation Criteria in Solid Tumors Version 1.1 (RECIST 1.1) Based on Blinded Independent Central Review (BICR) in Mismatch Repair Proficient (pMMR) Participants | PFS Per Response Evaluation Criteria in Solid Tumors Version 1.1 (RECIST 1.1) Based on BICR in All-comer Participants | Overall Survival (OS) in pMMR Participants | OS in All-comer Participants
