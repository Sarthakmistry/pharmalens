## Bayer's Phase 3 trial (NCT05098938) for Acyclovir reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2023-07-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05098938) investigating Acyclovir 50mg buccal/topical tablet reached its primary completion date on July 19, 2023. The trial enrolled 2020 participants and has results available. This marks a significant milestone in the drug's development.

### Market implication
The primary completion of a Phase 3 trial is a standard development milestone; market impact will depend on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
