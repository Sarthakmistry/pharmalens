## AbbVie Reports Strong Q1 2026 Financial Results with Significant Growth in Immunology and Neuroscience Portfolios

**Date:** 2026-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie announced robust first-quarter 2026 financial results, with adjusted diluted EPS increasing by 7.7 percent to $2.65 and net revenues growing by 12.4 percent to $15.002 billion. This growth was primarily fueled by strong performances in its Immunology and Neuroscience portfolios, with key drugs like Skyrizi (risankizumab), Rinvoq (upadacitinib), Vraylar (cariprazine), and Botox Therapeutic (onabotulinumtoxinA) contributing significantly, while Humira (adalimumab) revenues continued to decline.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, particularly the significant growth in key immunology and neuroscience assets, indicates a positive outlook for AbbVie's revenue diversification strategy post-Humira exclusivity challenges, likely leading to a bullish market response.
