## Takeda's Phase 1/2 Trial NCT04844073 for Tak-186 Terminated

**Date:** 2025-06-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04844073) investigating Tak-186, a drug targeting indications such as Alzheimer's, NSCLC, and CRC, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 95 participants and reached its primary completion date on June 17, 2025, before being terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial for Tak-186 represents a setback for Takeda's pipeline in oncology and Alzheimer's, potentially impacting future revenue projections for the company.
