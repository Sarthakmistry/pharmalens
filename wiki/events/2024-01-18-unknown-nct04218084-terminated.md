## Pfizer terminates Phase 3 trial NCT04218084 for Voxelotor

**Date:** 2024-01-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04218084) for the drug Voxelotor. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 236 participants, reached its primary completion on January 18, 2024, and was subsequently terminated.
