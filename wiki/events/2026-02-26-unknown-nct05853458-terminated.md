## Novartis's Phase 4 Trial NCT05853458 for Hydroxyurea Terminated

**Date:** 2026-02-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 4 clinical trial NCT05853458, investigating Hydroxyurea, reached its primary completion on February 26, 2026, and was subsequently terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 76 participants and did not report results at the time of termination.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 4 trial, following its primary completion, is a neutral event for Novartis as no negative reasons for termination were disclosed.
