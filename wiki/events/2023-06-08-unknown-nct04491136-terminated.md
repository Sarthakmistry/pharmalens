## Novartis terminates Phase 4 trial NCT04491136 for heart failure, studying Acei/arb and Arni.

**Date:** 2023-06-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT04491136, which was investigating Acei/arb and Arni for the treatment of heart failure. The trial had an actual enrollment of 201 participants and reached its primary completion date on June 8, 2023, before being terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 4 trial, even after primary completion, could indicate a strategic shift or unexpected findings, potentially leading to a slightly bearish sentiment for Novartis regarding its heart failure pipeline.
