## Novartis terminates Phase 4 trial (NCT04491136) for heart failure and hypertension

**Date:** 2023-06-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT04491136, which was investigating Acei/arb and Arni for heart failure and hypertension. The trial had an actual enrollment of 201 participants and reached its primary completion date on June 8, 2023, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 4 trial, despite reaching primary completion, could signal a re-evaluation of the therapeutic approach or an unfavorable outcome, potentially impacting Novartis's future strategy in the heart failure and hypertension market.
