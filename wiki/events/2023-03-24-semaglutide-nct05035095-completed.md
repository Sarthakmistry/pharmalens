## Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT05035095) for Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2023-03-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05035095, investigating [[semaglutide]] for obesity, reached its primary completion date on March 24, 2023. This trial, sponsored by [[novo-nordisk]], enrolled 667 participants to evaluate the drug's efficacy and safety in the [[glp1-obesity/_index]] indication. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development of [[semaglutide]] for obesity, with results anticipated to inform future regulatory submissions and market positioning.
