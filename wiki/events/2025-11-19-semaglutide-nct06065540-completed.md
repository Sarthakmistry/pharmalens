## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT06065540) for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06065540) evaluating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on November 19, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 2734 participants, is now moving towards data analysis, with results not yet publicly available. This marks a significant procedural step in the development of semaglutide for this indication.

The completion of this large Phase 3 trial is a procedural milestone for semaglutide, but market implications will depend on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
