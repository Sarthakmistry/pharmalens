## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Nivolumab Phase 1/2 Trial (NCT04349267) for Alzheimer's Disease Completed

**Date:** 2024-08-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04349267) evaluating [[nivolumab]], sponsored by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], has reached primary completion on August 22, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 44 participants, investigated nivolumab for [[alzheimers]] disease and has reported results. The completion of this early-phase trial provides initial data for the drug in this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial is a neutral event, as results are available but their impact on future development is yet to be determined.
