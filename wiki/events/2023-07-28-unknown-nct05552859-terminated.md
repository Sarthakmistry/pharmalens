## Sanofi terminates Phase 4 trial NCT05552859 for Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2023-07-28 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05552859, which investigated Insulin glargine 300 u/ml and Insulin degludec 100 u/ml for Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 62 participants, reached its primary completion date on July 28, 2023, before being terminated. The specific reason for the termination was not disclosed in the available information.

### Market implication
The market implication is neutral, as the reason for the trial's termination is not specified, preventing a clear assessment of its impact on Sanofi's pipeline or market position.
