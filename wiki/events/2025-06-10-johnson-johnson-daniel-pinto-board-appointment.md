## Johnson & Johnson Announces Election of Daniel Pinto to Board of Directors

**Date:** 2025-06-10 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the election of Daniel Pinto to its Board of Directors, effective July 1, 2025. Mr. Pinto, currently President of JPMorganChase, will also become Vice Chairman of JPMorganChase from July 1, 2025, through his retirement at the end of 2026. He will serve on J&J's Audit Committee and Compensation & Benefits Committee.

### Market implication
This board appointment is a routine corporate governance matter and is not expected to have a direct impact on Johnson & Johnson's market valuation.
