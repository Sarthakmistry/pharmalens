## Sanofi terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT04394624) of pembrolizumab combination in NSCLC

**Date:** 2023-03-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04394624, sponsored by Sanofi and investigating [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda) in combination with SAR408701 and ramucirumab for non-small cell lung cancer, was terminated on March 6, 2023. The Phase 2 study had enrolled 31 participants and reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a minor setback for [[sanofi]]'s pipeline in NSCLC and removes a potential combination therapy for Merck's [[pembrolizumab]].
