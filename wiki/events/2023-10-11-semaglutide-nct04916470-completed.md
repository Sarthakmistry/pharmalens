## Semaglutide (NCT04916470) Phase 3 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2023-10-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04916470) for semaglutide, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, reached its primary completion date on October 11, 2023. The trial, which enrolled 617 participants, investigated semaglutide for indications related to type 2 diabetes and heart failure/hypertension. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural step forward for semaglutide, with market impact awaiting the release and analysis of the trial's results.
