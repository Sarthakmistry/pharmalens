## Bristol-Myers Squibb Reports Bearish Q2 2025 Earnings with Decreased EPS

**Date:** 2025-07-31 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company reported its second quarter 2025 financial results on July 31, 2025. The company's GAAP diluted earnings per share decreased to $0.64 in Q2 2025 from $0.83 in Q2 2024, while Non-GAAP diluted EPS also fell from $2.07 to $1.46. This represents a notable decline in profitability compared to the prior year.

### Market implication
The significant decrease in both GAAP and Non-GAAP diluted EPS is likely to be perceived negatively by the market, potentially impacting investor confidence and the company's stock valuation.
