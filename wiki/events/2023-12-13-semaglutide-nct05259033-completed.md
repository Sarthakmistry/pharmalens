## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05259033) for Semaglutide in Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2023-12-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05259033) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on December 13, 2023. The trial enrolled 683 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for semaglutide in Type 2 Diabetes marks a procedural step forward in its development, with further market implications dependent on the eventual publication and nature of the trial results.
