## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide (NCT05259033) Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2023-12-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05259033) for Novo Nordisk's semaglutide, investigating its use in type 2 diabetes, reached primary completion on December 13, 2023. The trial, which enrolled 683 participants, has reported that results are available, moving the drug closer to potential regulatory milestones.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard step in drug development, providing data that will inform future regulatory submissions for semaglutide in type 2 diabetes.
