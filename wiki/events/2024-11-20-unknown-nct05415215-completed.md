## Roche's Phase 3 trial NCT05415215 for breast cancer completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-11-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05415215), investigating a fixed-dose combination of pertuzumab and trastuzumab for subcutaneous use in breast cancer, has completed its primary endpoint on November 20, 2024. The study enrolled 346 participants and evaluated the treatment in combination with investigator's choice of chemotherapy and radiotherapy.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development process for Roche's breast cancer treatment, with market implications pending the release of results.
