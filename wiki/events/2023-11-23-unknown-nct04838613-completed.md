## Novartis' Phase 3 Trial NCT04838613 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-11-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis' Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04838613, investigating [18F]CTT1057 and [68Ga]Ga-PSMA-11, reached its primary completion date on November 23, 2023. The study enrolled 190 participants and has reported results, indicating the conclusion of its primary data collection phase.
