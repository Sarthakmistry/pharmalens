## AbbVie's Phase 3 Trial of Upadacitinib for Psoriatic Arthritis Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie's Phase 3 study (NCT03104374) comparing upadacitinib to placebo in participants with active Psoriatic Arthritis has completed. This trial, involving 642 participants, aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of upadacitinib, with its primary outcome measuring the percentage of participants achieving an ACR20 response at Week 12.
