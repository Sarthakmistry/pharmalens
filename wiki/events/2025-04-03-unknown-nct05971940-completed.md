## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial (NCT05971940) for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-04-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05971940, investigating Orforglipron for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on April 3, 2025. The trial enrolled 559 participants and has indicated that results are available, marking a significant step towards potential regulatory submission for the drug.

### Market implication
The completion of this pivotal Phase 3 trial for Orforglipron positions Eli Lilly closer to potentially expanding its diabetes portfolio, with future market reaction dependent on the eventual disclosure of trial results.
