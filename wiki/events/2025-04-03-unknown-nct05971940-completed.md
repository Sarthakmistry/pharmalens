## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-04-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05971940) investigating Orforglipron for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes has reached its primary completion date on April 3, 2025. The study enrolled 559 participants and has indicated that results are available, though not yet published.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard development step for Orforglipron, with market reaction awaiting the release and analysis of the trial's results.
