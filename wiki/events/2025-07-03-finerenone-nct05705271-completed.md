## Bayer's Phase 4 trial of finerenone (NCT05705271) for type 2 diabetes reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer has announced the primary completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05705271) for finerenone in patients with type 2 diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 200 participants, reached its primary completion date on July 3, 2025. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a procedural step; market implications are neutral until trial results are released and analyzed.
