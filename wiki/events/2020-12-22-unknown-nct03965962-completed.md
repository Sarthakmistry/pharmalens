## Sanofi's Phase 3 Rabies Vaccine Trial (NCT03965962) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2020-12-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03965962, investigating rabies vaccines (including Vrvg-2, Purified inactivated rabies vaccine, Human diploid cell vaccine (hdcv), and Rabies immune globulin (human)), reached its primary completion date on December 22, 2020. The study enrolled 640 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial's primary endpoint is a standard procedural step and does not immediately convey specific market implications without the release of detailed results.
