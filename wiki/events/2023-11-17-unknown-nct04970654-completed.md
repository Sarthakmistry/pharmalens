## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial for Somapacitan (NCT04970654) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2023-11-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04970654), which investigated Somapacitan, has completed its primary endpoint on November 17, 2023. The trial enrolled 110 participants, and results are now available. Somapacitan is a long-acting growth hormone, and Norditropin® was also mentioned in the trial context.
