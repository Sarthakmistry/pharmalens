## AstraZeneca's Azd5004 Phase 2 Trial for Obesity Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2025-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06579092) for Azd5004, investigating its efficacy in glp1-obesity, has completed its primary data collection on November 21, 2025. The trial enrolled 310 participants and has not yet posted results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released to indicate the drug's potential efficacy or safety.
