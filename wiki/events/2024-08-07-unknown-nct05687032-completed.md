## Pfizer's Phase 4 Trial NCT05687032 Completed

**Date:** 2024-08-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05687032) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating Inotuzumab ozogamicin, has reached its primary completion on August 7, 2024. The study enrolled 44 participants and has reported that results are available.
