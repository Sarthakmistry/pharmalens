## Regeneron's Aflibercept Trial (NCT04429503) for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04429503) sponsored by Regeneron, investigating Aflibercept and High-dose aflibercept for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion on May 30, 2022. The trial enrolled 660 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides data for Regeneron's Aflibercept program in Type 2 Diabetes, which could inform future development decisions.
