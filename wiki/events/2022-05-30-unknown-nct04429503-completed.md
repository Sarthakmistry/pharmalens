## Regeneron's Aflibercept Trial (NCT04429503) in Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2022-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04429503) investigating Aflibercept for Type 2 Diabetes reached its primary completion on May 30, 2022. The study enrolled 660 participants and has results available.
