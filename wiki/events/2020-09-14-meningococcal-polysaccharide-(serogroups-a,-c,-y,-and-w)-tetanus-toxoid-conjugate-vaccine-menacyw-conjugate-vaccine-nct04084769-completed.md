## Sanofi's Meningococcal ACYW-TT Vaccine Trial NCT04084769 Completes with Positive Results

**Date:** 2020-09-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04084769) for Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine (MenACYW-TT) has completed. The trial results demonstrated robust immunogenicity against all serogroups and an acceptable safety profile, regardless of the primary vaccine received. This positive outcome supports the potential efficacy and safety of the MenACYW-TT vaccine.

### Market implication
The positive trial results could support future regulatory submissions and market potential for Sanofi's MenACYW-TT vaccine.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** null
- **Comparator:** MCV4-CRM
- **Primary outcome:** vaccine seroresponse (post-vaccination titers ≥1:16 if pre-vaccination titers <1:8; or a ≥4-fold increase if pre-vaccination titers ≥1:8) 30 days post booster
- **Primary result:** Seroresponse after MenACYW-TT booster was high regardless of priming vaccine (serogroup A: 94.8% vs 93.2%; C: 97.1% vs 98.9%; W: 97.7% vs 98.9%; and Y; 98.9% vs 100% for MenACWY-TT-primed and MCV4-CRM-primed groups, respectively)
- **Secondary results:** The persistence of the immune response following primary vaccination with MenACYW-TT was demonstrated; Co-administration with MenB vaccines did not affect MenACWY-TT immunogenicity
- **Safety:** No vaccine-related serious adverse events were reported
- **Conclusions:** MenACYW-TT booster induced robust immunogenicity against all serogroups, regardless of the primary vaccine received, and had an acceptable safety profile.
- **Journal:** Pediatr Res., 2023
- **Industry sponsored:** True
