## AbbVie Reports Full-Year 2025 Earnings

**Date:** 2026-02-04 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie reported full-year 2025 net revenues of $61.160 billion, an increase of 8.6 percent on a reported basis. Adjusted diluted EPS for the full year was $10.00, representing a decrease of 1.2 percent. Key revenue contributions included Skyrizi with $17.562 billion, Rinvoq with $8.304 billion, and Humira with $4.540 billion. The Immunology portfolio saw net revenues of $30.406 billion, up 14.0%, while Neuroscience net revenues were $10.767 billion, up 19.6%.

### Market implication
The mixed financial results, with revenue growth offset by a decrease in adjusted diluted EPS, suggest a neutral market reaction for AbbVie.
