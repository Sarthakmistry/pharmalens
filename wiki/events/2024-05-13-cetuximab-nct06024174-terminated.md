## Cetuximab Trial NCT06024174 Terminated by Bristol-Myers Squibb

**Date:** 2024-05-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The clinical trial NCT06024174, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb and investigating cetuximab for Alzheimer's disease, was terminated on May 13, 2024. This Phase 1/2 study had an actual enrollment of 5 participants and reached primary completion on the same date.

### Market implication
The termination of this exploratory trial for cetuximab in Alzheimer's disease removes a potential development pathway for the drug in this indication, representing a minor negative for Bristol-Myers Squibb as the sponsor.
