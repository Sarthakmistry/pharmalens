## Phase 2/3 Trial NCT04619797 for Pembrolizumab and Atezolizumab in NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-04-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2/3 clinical trial NCT04619797, evaluating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]] and [[atezolizumab]] with chemotherapy in non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on April 19, 2024. The study, sponsored by [[roche]], enrolled 542 participants and has not yet reported results.
