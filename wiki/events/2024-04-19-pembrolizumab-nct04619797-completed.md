## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2/3 Trial NCT04619797 in NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-04-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04619797) investigating Merck's [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy and [[atezolizumab]] for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on April 19, 2024. The trial enrolled 542 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial marks a procedural milestone; market impact will depend on the eventual release and outcome of the study results.
