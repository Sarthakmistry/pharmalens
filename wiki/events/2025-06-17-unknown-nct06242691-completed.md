## Merck's Mk-1200 trial (NCT06242691) for Alzheimer's completes Phase 1/2

**Date:** 2025-06-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT06242691) sponsored by Merck, investigating Mk-1200 and an antiemetic for Alzheimer's disease, reached its primary completion date on June 17, 2025. The study enrolled 13 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial is a standard procedural step and does not immediately impact Merck's valuation, as results are not yet available.
