## AbbVie announces patent settlement for RINVOQ, extending U.S. exclusivity until at least April 2037

**Date:** 2025-09-11 | **Type:** patent_event | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie announced a settlement of litigation with all generic manufacturers that filed abbreviated new drug applications with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration for generic versions of upadacitinib tablets, which AbbVie markets as RINVOQ. This settlement extends the market exclusivity for RINVOQ in the U.S. until at least April 2037, subject to standard acceleration provisions and the granting of pediatric exclusivity.

### Market implication
This settlement provides long-term market exclusivity for RINVOQ, securing a significant revenue stream for AbbVie well into the future.
