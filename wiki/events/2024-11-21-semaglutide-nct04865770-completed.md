## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial (NCT04865770) for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2024-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04865770) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on November 21, 2024. The trial enrolled 106 participants and is now marked as completed with results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for semaglutide in type 2 diabetes is a standard procedural step, and its market implication is neutral pending the release and analysis of the trial results.
