## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial NCT04865770 for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2024-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04865770) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on November 21, 2024. The study enrolled 106 participants and now has results available, indicating the conclusion of primary data collection.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development of semaglutide for type 2 diabetes, with market attention now shifting to the anticipated release of its results.
