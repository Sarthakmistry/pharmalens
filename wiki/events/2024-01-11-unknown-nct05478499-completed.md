## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Deucravacitinib trial NCT05478499 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-01-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb announced the primary completion of its Phase 4 trial (NCT05478499) for Deucravacitinib on January 11, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 154 participants, has reached its primary completion date, indicating that primary outcome data collection is finished and results are available.
