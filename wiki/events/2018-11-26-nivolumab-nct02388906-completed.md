## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Phase 3 Trial NCT02388906 Completed

**Date:** 2018-11-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02388906) investigating nivolumab, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, reached its primary completion date on November 26, 2018. The trial enrolled 906 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone for nivolumab, with results now available for evaluation by the market.
