## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Nivolumab Trial NCT03446040 Terminated

**Date:** 2024-08-29 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT03446040, investigating nivolumab (Opdivo) in combination with Bms-986258 and Rhuph20 for Alzheimer's disease, has been terminated. The trial, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, had an actual enrollment of 92 participants and reached its primary completion date on August 29, 2024.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial for nivolumab in Alzheimer's disease represents a setback for Bristol-Myers Squibb's pipeline efforts in this indication.
