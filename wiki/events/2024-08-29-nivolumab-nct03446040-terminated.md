## Bristol-Myers Squibb Terminates Nivolumab Trial NCT03446040 in Alzheimer's Disease

**Date:** 2024-08-29 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03446040) investigating nivolumab in Alzheimer's disease. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 92 participants, reached its primary completion on August 29, 2024, and results are available.

The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial for [[nivolumab]] in Alzheimer's disease represents a setback for Bristol-Myers Squibb's pipeline in this indication, potentially reducing future revenue opportunities in the neurology space.
