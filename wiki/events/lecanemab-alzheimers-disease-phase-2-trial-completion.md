## Lecanemab Phase 2 Trial for Alzheimer's Disease Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 study evaluating Lecanemab for early Alzheimer's Disease has completed. The trial, NCT01767311, was a multinational, multicenter, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-group study.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 study provides additional clinical data for lecanemab, marking a step forward in its development for Alzheimer's Disease.
