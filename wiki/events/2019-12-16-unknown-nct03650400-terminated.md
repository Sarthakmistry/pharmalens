## Novartis terminates Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03650400) for Fevipiprant

**Date:** 2019-12-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03650400) investigating Fevipiprant. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 11 participants, reached its primary completion date on December 16, 2019, and its status was subsequently updated to 'TERMINATED'.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Fevipiprant by Novartis suggests potential issues with the drug's development, which could be viewed negatively for the company's pipeline in this area.
