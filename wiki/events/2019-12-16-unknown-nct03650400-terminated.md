## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT03650400) for Fevipiprant

**Date:** 2019-12-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03650400) for Fevipiprant on December 16, 2019. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 11 participants, reached its primary completion on the same date. This termination indicates that further development of Fevipiprant in this specific trial context has ceased.

This termination for an untracked drug has a neutral market implication for Novartis's overall valuation, as Fevipiprant was not a core asset.
