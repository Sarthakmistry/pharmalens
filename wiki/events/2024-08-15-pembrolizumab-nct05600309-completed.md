## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial (NCT05600309) for Colorectal Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-08-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05600309) evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with Regorafenib and Tas-102 for metastatic colorectal cancer has reached its primary completion date on August 15, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Merck, enrolled 94 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step; the market impact will depend on the eventual disclosure and significance of the trial results.
