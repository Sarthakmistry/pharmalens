## Pfizer's Phase 3 Pneumococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT05512819) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05512819), evaluating a 20-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine against a 13-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine, reached its primary completion date on September 20, 2025. The study enrolled 541 participants, and results have not yet been released.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for Pfizer's vaccine pipeline; market impact will be determined upon the release and analysis of the trial's results.
