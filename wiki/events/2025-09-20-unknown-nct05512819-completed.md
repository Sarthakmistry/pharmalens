## Pfizer's Phase 3 Pneumococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT05512819) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05512819) sponsored by Pfizer, evaluating a 20-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine against a 13-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine, reached its primary completion date on September 20, 2025. This milestone indicates that data collection for the primary outcome has concluded, moving the trial closer to potential results and subsequent regulatory review. The trial enrolled 541 participants.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step, with market implications awaiting the release of trial results.
