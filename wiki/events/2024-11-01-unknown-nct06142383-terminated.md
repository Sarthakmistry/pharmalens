## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT06142383) for investigational drug XXB750 in heart failure and hypertension

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06142383) investigating the investigational drug XXB750 for heart failure and hypertension.
