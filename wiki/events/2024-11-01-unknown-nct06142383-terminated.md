## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT06142383) for investigational drug XXB750 in heart failure

**Date:** 2024-11-01 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06142383) investigating the investigational drug XXB750 for heart failure (HF-HTN). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 136 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 1, 2024, before being terminated. The study compared XXB750 at various doses against placebo and [[sacubitril-valsartan]].

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an investigational asset represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline in heart failure, indicating potential issues with the drug's efficacy, safety, or strategic reprioritization.
