## Sanofi's Phase 1/2 trial NCT04537208 for COVID-19 vaccine candidates completes primary data collection.

**Date:** 2021-11-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04537208), which investigated several COVID-19 vaccine candidates including Cov2 pres dtm-af03 (low-dose and high-dose), Cov2 pres dtm-as03 (low-dose and high-dose), and Cov2 pres dtm (high-dose) without adjuvant, completed its primary data collection on November 19, 2021. The trial enrolled 441 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial is a standard procedural step for Sanofi, with no immediate market impact without disclosed results for these untracked vaccine candidates.
