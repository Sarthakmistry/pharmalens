## Sanofi's Phase 4 trial NCT04998604 for Dupilumab and Omalizumab completes primary phase

**Date:** 2024-10-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT04998604, investigating Dupilumab and Omalizumab, has reached its primary completion date on October 16, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 360 participants, is now complete and has results available.

The completion of this Phase 4 trial marks a significant operational milestone for Sanofi, moving Dupilumab and Omalizumab closer to potential data publication, which could inform future clinical use or label expansions.
