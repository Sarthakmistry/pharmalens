## AbbVie Reports Strong Q1 2025 Earnings and Key Pipeline Advancements

**Date:** 2025-05-09 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported Q1 2025 net revenues of $13.3 billion, an 8.4% increase, driven by strong growth in Skyrizi (risankizumab) and Rinvoq (upadacitinib). The company also announced significant pipeline progress, including FDA approvals for Rinvoq in giant cell arteritis (GCA) and Emblaveo (aztreonam and avibactam) for complicated intra-abdominal infections, a Biologics License Application (BLA) submission for trenibotulinumtoxinE (BoNT/E) for glabellar lines, and a Phase 3 trial initiation for Qulipta (atogepant) in menstrual migraine.

### Market implication
The robust performance of key growth drivers and strategic pipeline advancements position AbbVie for continued financial success and market leadership, despite biosimilar competition for Humira (adalimumab).
