## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial (NCT02504372) for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2023-01-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02504372) for [[pembrolizumab]] in non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion on January 24, 2023. The trial, which enrolled 1177 participants, has results available, indicating the completion of its primary endpoint assessment.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for an established drug like pembrolizumab is a standard procedural update and is not expected to have a significant immediate market impact.
