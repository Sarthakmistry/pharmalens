## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05890976) for Semaglutide in Obesity Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-01-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05890976) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for the treatment of glp1-obesity, has reached its primary completion date on January 16, 2025. The study enrolled 200 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for semaglutide in obesity, with market impact pending the release and analysis of the study's results.
