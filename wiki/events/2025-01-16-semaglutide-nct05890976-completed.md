## Semaglutide (NCT05890976) Phase 3 Trial for Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2025-01-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05890976) investigating semaglutide for the treatment of obesity has reached its primary completion date on January 16, 2025. The study, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, enrolled 200 participants and is a significant milestone in the drug's development pathway for this indication. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and is considered a neutral event for Novo Nordisk and semaglutide, with market impact contingent upon the eventual release and interpretation of the trial's results.
