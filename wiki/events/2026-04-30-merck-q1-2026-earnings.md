## Merck Reports Strong Q1 2026 Results Driven by Keytruda and New Launches

**Date:** 2026-04-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Merck & Co., Inc. announced its first-quarter 2026 financial results, reporting total worldwide sales of $16.3 billion, a 5% increase (3% ex-FX). The growth was primarily driven by the continued strong performance of [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda/Keytruda Qlex), which saw sales of $8.0 billion, and increasing contributions from new launches like Winrevair. Animal Health products also contributed significantly to the positive results.

### Market implication
The strong sales growth, particularly from its oncology blockbuster [[pembrolizumab]] and new product launches, indicates robust commercial execution and a positive outlook for Merck's revenue and valuation.
