## Sanofi's Dupilumab Sar231893 Phase 3 Trial Shows Positive Results in Chronic Spontaneous Urticaria

**Date:** 2024-08-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
The Phase 3 trial NCT04180488 for dupilumab sar231893, sponsored by [[sanofi]], has completed with positive results for Chronic Spontaneous Urticaria (CSU). The study demonstrated that dupilumab sar231893 significantly reduced urticaria activity, including itch and hives severity, in anti-IgE-naive patients with CSU who remained symptomatic despite H1-antihistamine use.

### Market implication
These positive Phase 3 results for dupilumab sar231893 could expand [[sanofi]]'s market presence in immunology, potentially leading to increased revenue if approved for Chronic Spontaneous Urticaria.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 289 patients
- **Comparator:** placebo
- **Primary outcome:** change from baseline at week 24 in Itch Severity Score over 7 days (ISS7) or Urticaria Activity Score over 7 days (UAS7)
- **Primary result:** For ISS7, least squares mean (SE) changes were -8.64 (1.41) for dupilumab vs -6.10 (1.40) for placebo (difference: -2.54 points [95% CI, -4.65 to -0.43]; P = .02). For UAS7, least squares mean (SE) changes were -15.86 (2.66) for dupilumab vs -11.21 (2.65) for placebo (difference: -4.65 points [95% CI, -8.65 to -0.65]; P = .02).
- **Secondary results:** CUPID-A and -C combined data (289 participants) demonstrated greater improvements in UAS7 and ISS7.
- **Safety:** Safety outcomes were generally consistent with the known dupilumab safety profile; 77 patients (53.5%) taking dupilumab vs 81 patients (55.9%) taking placebo reported treatment-emergent adverse events in the pooled analysis.
- **Conclusions:** The CUPID-C randomized clinical trial confirmed CUPID-A findings, and pooled data demonstrated dupilumab significantly reduced urticaria activity by reducing itch and hives severity in anti-IgE-naive patients with CSU who remained symptomatic despite the use of H1-AH.
- **Journal:** JAMA Dermatol, 2026
- **Industry sponsored:** None
