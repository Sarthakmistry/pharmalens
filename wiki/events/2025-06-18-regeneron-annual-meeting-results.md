## Regeneron Pharmaceuticals Announces 2025 Annual Meeting Voting Results

**Date:** 2025-06-18 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. announced the voting results from its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Key outcomes included the election of Class I directors Bonnie L. Bassler, Michael S. Brown, Leonard S. Schleifer, and George D. Yancopoulos, ratification of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the independent registered public accounting firm for 2025, and approval of executive compensation. Shareholders also approved an amendment to the Company’s Certificate of Incorporation to declassify the board of directors, while proposals related to mandatory conversion of Class A Stock and director removal for cause were not approved.

### Market implication
The reported voting results are factual and do not indicate a direct positive or negative impact on Regeneron's operational or financial outlook.
