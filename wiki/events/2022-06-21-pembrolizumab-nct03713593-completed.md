## Merck's KEYNOTE-775 Trial for Pembrolizumab and Lenvatinib Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-06-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial KEYNOTE-775 (NCT03713593), sponsored by Merck, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Lenvatinib for advanced endometrial carcinoma, reached its primary completion date on June 21, 2022. The trial enrolled 794 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step in the development of [[pembrolizumab]] for advanced endometrial carcinoma, moving it closer to potential regulatory review, pending the full release and analysis of its results.
