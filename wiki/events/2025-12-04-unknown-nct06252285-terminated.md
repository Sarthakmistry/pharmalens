## Sanofi's Phase 3 RSV Vaccine Trial (NCT06252285) Terminated

**Date:** 2025-12-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06252285) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating an Rsvt vaccine, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 6300 participants and was originally expected to complete its primary endpoint by December 4, 2025.

### Market implication
The termination of a large Phase 3 trial for an Rsvt vaccine represents a setback for Sanofi's pipeline in this therapeutic area, potentially impacting future revenue projections.
