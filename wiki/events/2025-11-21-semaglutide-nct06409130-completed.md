## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial NCT06409130 Completes Primary Phase 2 Study

**Date:** 2025-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06409130, investigating semaglutide, has reached its primary completion date on November 21, 2025. The study enrolled an actual total of 270 participants. This trial is evaluating semaglutide, Nnc0194-0499, Cagrilintide + semaglutide, and Cagrilintide.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a procedural step in the development of semaglutide, with market impact dependent on future data readouts.
