## Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 Trial NCT06409130 for Semaglutide Completed

**Date:** 2025-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06409130, investigating semaglutide, has reached its primary completion date on November 21, 2025. The study, which enrolled 270 participants, has concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures. Results are not yet available.
