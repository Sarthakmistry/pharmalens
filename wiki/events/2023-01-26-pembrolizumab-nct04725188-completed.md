## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2 Trial NCT04725188 for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2023-01-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04725188) evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with docetaxel for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on January 26, 2023. The trial, sponsored by Merck, enrolled 255 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides new data for pembrolizumab in NSCLC, which will be analyzed for potential future development or label expansion, with market impact dependent on the published results.
