## Merck's Phase 2 trial NCT04725188 for pembrolizumab in NSCLC reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2023-01-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck has announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04725188) investigating pembrolizumab for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial, which enrolled 255 participants, reached its primary completion date on January 26, 2023, and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides further data for pembrolizumab in NSCLC, with potential implications for future development and market positioning once results are fully analyzed and published.
