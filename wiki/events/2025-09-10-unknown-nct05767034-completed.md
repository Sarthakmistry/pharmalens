## Novartis's Secukinumab trial NCT05767034 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05767034, investigating Secukinumab 300 mg and Secukinumab 150 mg, reached its primary completion date on September 10, 2025. The trial enrolled 381 participants. Results from this trial have not yet been reported.

### Market implication
As results are not yet available, the market implication for Novartis remains neutral following the primary completion of this trial.
