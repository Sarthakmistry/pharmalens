## Atezolizumab Trial NCT05251948 for Advanced Solid Tumors Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05251948) investigating atezolizumab in combination with capecitabine, oxaliplatin, and tiragolumab for advanced solid tumors has reached its primary completion date on September 4, 2025. The study enrolled 40 participants, and results have not yet been reported.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 1/2 trial for atezolizumab is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication as no results have been released to assess efficacy or safety.
