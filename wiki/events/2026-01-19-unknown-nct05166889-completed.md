## AstraZeneca's Tozorakimab Phase 3 Trial NCT05166889 Reaches Primary Completion

AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05166889, investigating the drug Tozorakimab, reached its primary completion date on January 19, 2026. The trial successfully enrolled 1132 participants. This milestone indicates that the final data for the primary outcome measure has been collected.
