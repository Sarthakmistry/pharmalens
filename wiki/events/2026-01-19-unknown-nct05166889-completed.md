## AstraZeneca's Tozorakimab Phase 3 Trial (NCT05166889) Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2026-01-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05166889, investigating Tozorakimab, reached its primary completion date on January 19, 2026. The trial enrolled 1132 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as no efficacy or safety data has been released to inform market expectations for Tozorakimab.
