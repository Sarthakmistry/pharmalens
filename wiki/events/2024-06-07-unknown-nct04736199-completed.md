## Bayer's Phase 3 Trial NCT04736199 for Darolutamide Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-06-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04736199, investigating Darolutamide (nubeqa, bay1841788) in combination with Androgen Deprivation Therapy (ADT) for prostate cancer, reached its primary completion date on June 7, 2024. The trial enrolled 669 participants and has reported that results are available. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for Darolutamide.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as specific efficacy or safety results have not yet been disclosed, but it progresses Darolutamide's clinical development.
