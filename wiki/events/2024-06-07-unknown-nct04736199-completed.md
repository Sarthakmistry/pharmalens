## Bayer's Phase 3 Trial NCT04736199 for Darolutamide Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-06-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04736199, investigating Darolutamide (Nubeqa) in combination with Androgen Deprivation Therapy (ADT) for prostate cancer, has reached its primary completion date on June 7, 2024. The study, which enrolled 669 participants, is now complete and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural update and does not immediately impact Bayer's valuation without specific results being released.
