## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT05179603 Terminated

**Date:** 2023-09-14 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05179603) investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Thor-707, sponsored by Sanofi, was terminated on September 14, 2023. The trial had enrolled 14 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day. The reason for termination was not specified in the provided information.

### Market implication
The termination of this small Phase 2 trial, without disclosed reasons, is likely to have a neutral market implication for [[pembrolizumab]] or its primary developer, Merck.
