## Roche terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT04338269) of atezolizumab in combination with Cabozantinib for advanced solid tumors

**Date:** 2023-01-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04338269) evaluating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with Cabozantinib for participants with advanced solid tumors has been terminated. The trial had enrolled 522 participants and reached its primary completion date on January 3, 2023, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for [[atezolizumab]] represents a setback for [[roche]]'s oncology pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue projections for the drug in broader solid tumor indications.
