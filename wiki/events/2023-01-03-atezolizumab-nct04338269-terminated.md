## Roche terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT04338269) for atezolizumab

**Date:** 2023-01-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
On January 3, 2023, Roche terminated the Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04338269, which was investigating atezolizumab. The trial had enrolled 522 participants and had reached its primary completion date. The trial also involved Cabozantinib.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for atezolizumab could negatively impact Roche's pipeline and future revenue expectations for the drug in the specific indication targeted by the trial.
