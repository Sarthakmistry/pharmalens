## Novartis completes Phase 2 trial NCT03568461

**Date:** 2020-11-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT03568461, on November 24, 2020. This trial, which investigated Tisagenlecleucel and enrolled 98 participants, has reached its primary completion date, signifying that data collection for the primary outcome measure is finished. The trial status is now completed, and results are expected to be published.

### Market implication
This event is neutral for Novartis as it represents a standard progression in the clinical development pipeline, with results pending.
