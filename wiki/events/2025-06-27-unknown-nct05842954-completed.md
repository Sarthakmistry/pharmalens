## Novartis's NCT05842954 trial for Klu156 and Coartem completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-06-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05842954, investigating Klu156 and Coartem, reached its primary completion date on June 27, 2025. The trial enrolled 1720 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
