## Pfizer's Phase 1/2 Trial NCT03519178 for Untracked Drug in Breast Cancer Terminated

**Date:** 2023-04-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03519178) investigating Pf-06873600 in combination with endocrine therapies for advanced breast cancer was terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 155 participants, reached its primary completion on April 5, 2023, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial for an untracked drug by Pfizer is a neutral event, as the drug's market impact is not significant at this early stage.
