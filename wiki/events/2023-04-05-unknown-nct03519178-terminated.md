## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT03519178 for an investigational breast cancer drug

**Date:** 2023-04-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03519178) investigating Pf-06873600 in combination with endocrine therapies 1 and 2 for advanced or metastatic breast cancer. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 155 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 5, 2023, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this early-phase trial for an investigational drug is a neutral event, as it is common for compounds not to advance through the pipeline.
