## Sanofi Completes Phase 3 Rabies Vaccine Trial NCT04478084

**Date:** 2022-03-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04478084, investigating various rabies vaccines and immunoglobulins, reached its primary completion on March 19, 2022. The study enrolled 403 participants and is now completed, though results have not yet been published. The trial evaluated Purified Vero Rabies Vaccine - Serum Free VRVG-2, Purified Inactivated Rabies Vaccine, Equine Rabies Immunoglobulins (ERIG), and Human Rabies Immunoglobulins (HRIG).

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a routine procedural milestone for Sanofi and carries no immediate market implications without the disclosure of study results.
