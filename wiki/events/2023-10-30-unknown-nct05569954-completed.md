## Merck's V116 Phase 3 Trial (NCT05569954) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-10-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05569954) evaluating V116, a 21-valent pneumococcal conjugate vaccine, has reached its primary completion date on October 30, 2023. The study, which enrolled 1484 participants, investigated the safety and immunogenicity of V116 compared to Ppsv23 in adults. Results for this trial are indicated as available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural step forward for Merck's vaccine pipeline; market implications will depend on the eventual disclosure and interpretation of the trial's results.
