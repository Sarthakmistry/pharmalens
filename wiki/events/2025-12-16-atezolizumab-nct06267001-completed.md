## Atezolizumab (Tecentriq) Phase 3 trial NCT06267001 for NSCLC reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2025-12-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06267001) evaluating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with Tiragolumab for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on December 16, 2025. The trial, sponsored by [[roche]], enrolled 56 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural milestone and does not immediately impact market valuation without the release of clinical data.
