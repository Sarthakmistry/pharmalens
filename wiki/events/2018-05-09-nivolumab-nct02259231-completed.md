## Nivolumab Trial NCT02259231 Completed

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT02259231) investigating nivolumab reached its primary completion on May 9, 2018. The trial, which enrolled 41 participants, has reported results, contributing further data to the understanding of nivolumab's profile.

The completion of this trial provides additional data for nivolumab, which could inform its ongoing development and use in various oncology indications.
