## Sanofi's Caplacizumab Trial NCT05468320 Completes

**Date:** 2024-12-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's clinical trial NCT05468320, investigating Caplacizumab for the treatment of thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura, has reached its primary completion date on December 26, 2024. This Phase 3 study enrolled 51 participants and has reported having results, which are expected to be published in 2025 in Hematology Am Soc Hematol Educ Program.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Sanofi's Caplacizumab, with future publication of results anticipated to provide further insights.
