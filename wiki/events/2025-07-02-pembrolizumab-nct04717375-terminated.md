## Pembrolizumab and Cetuximab Trial NCT04717375 Terminated

**Date:** 2025-07-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04717375, sponsored by Sanofi and investigating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]] (Merck) and [[cetuximab]] (Eli Lilly) with SAR444881, carboplatin, and pemetrexed, has been terminated. The Phase 1/2 study had an actual enrollment of 125 participants and reached its primary completion date on July 2, 2025.

### Market implication
The termination of this trial represents a setback for the development programs involving [[pembrolizumab]] and [[cetuximab]], potentially impacting future pipeline and revenue expectations for [[merck]], [[eli-lilly]], and [[sanofi]].
