## Regeneron terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT04916002) for Vidutolimod and Cemiplimab in solid tumors.

**Date:** 2024-10-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04916002) investigating Vidutolimod in combination with Cemiplimab for advanced solid tumors, including breast cancer and non-small cell lung cancer. The trial, which had enrolled 77 participants, reached its primary completion on October 31, 2024, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Regeneron's pipeline in oncology, removing a potential treatment option for solid tumors.
