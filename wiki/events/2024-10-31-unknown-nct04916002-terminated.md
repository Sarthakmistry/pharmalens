## Regeneron terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT04916002) for Vidutolimod and Cemiplimab in solid tumors

**Date:** 2024-10-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04916002), investigating Vidutolimod in combination with Cemiplimab for advanced solid tumors, including breast cancer and non-small cell lung cancer, has been terminated. The trial enrolled 77 participants and reached its primary completion date on October 31, 2024.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial suggests potential challenges with the drug combination or a strategic reprioritization, which is a bearish signal for Regeneron's pipeline in these oncology indications.
