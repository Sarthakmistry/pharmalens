## AstraZeneca terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05056727 for Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (SZC)

**Date:** 2024-02-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AstraZeneca has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05056727) which was investigating Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (SZC). The trial had enrolled 1112 participants and reached its primary completion date on February 7, 2024, with results now available. The termination of a late-stage clinical trial typically signals a strategic reassessment or issues identified with the drug's profile.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for AstraZeneca's pipeline, indicating a discontinued development effort for Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (SZC) and potentially impacting future revenue projections for this asset.
