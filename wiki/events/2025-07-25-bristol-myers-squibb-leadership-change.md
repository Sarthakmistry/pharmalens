## Bristol Myers Squibb Announces Leadership Change for Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development

**Date:** 2025-07-25 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced a leadership change with Dr. Samit Hirawat stepping down as Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development, and Dr. Cristian Massacesi appointed as his successor. Dr. Hirawat will transition from his current role on August 1, 2025, to become an advisor until November 1, 2025, at which point he will leave the company.

### Market implication
The leadership transition is expected to be neutral for the company's market valuation as a replacement with extensive industry experience has been appointed.
