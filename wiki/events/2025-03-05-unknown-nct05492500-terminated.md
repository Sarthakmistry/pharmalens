## Pfizer's Ponsegromab Phase 2 Trial (NCT05492500) for Heart Failure Terminated

**Date:** 2025-03-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05492500) investigating ponsegromab for heart failure was terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 455 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 5, 2025, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for ponsegromab represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline in the heart failure indication.
