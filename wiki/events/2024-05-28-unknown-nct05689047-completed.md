## Merck's Phase 2 trial NCT05689047 for M5717 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-05-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05689047) investigating M5717 (330 mg, 500 mg, 660 mg) and Pyronaridine (360 mg, 540 mg, 720 mg) has reached its primary completion date on May 28, 2024. The trial enrolled 38 participants, and results are now available.

The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides new data for Merck's pipeline, which could inform future development decisions for M5717.
