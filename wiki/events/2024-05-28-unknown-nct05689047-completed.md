## Merck's Phase 2 trial NCT05689047 for M5717 completes with results available

Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05689047) investigating M5717, an untracked drug, has reached primary completion on May 28, 2024, with results now available. The trial, which enrolled 38 participants, studied various dosages of M5717 and Pyronaridine.
