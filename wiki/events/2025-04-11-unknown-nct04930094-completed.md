## Novartis's NCT04930094 trial for Secukinumab completed primary completion

**Date:** 2025-04-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On April 11, 2025, Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04930094, investigating Secukinumab 300 mg and Secukinumab 150 mg, reached its primary completion. The trial enrolled 354 participants and currently has no results posted.
