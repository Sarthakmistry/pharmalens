## Novartis Trial NCT04930094 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-04-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04930094, which investigated Secukinumab 300 mg and Secukinumab 150 mg, reached its primary completion date on April 11, 2025. The trial enrolled 354 participants and is currently listed as completed, with no results yet available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial is a standard procedural milestone; market impact is neutral pending the release of results.
