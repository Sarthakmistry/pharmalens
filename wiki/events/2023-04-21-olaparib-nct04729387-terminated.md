## Olaparib Phase 3 Trial (NCT04729387) Terminated by Novartis

**Date:** 2023-04-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04729387) for [[olaparib]], sponsored by [[novartis]], was officially terminated on April 21, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 358 participants and reached its primary completion on the same date. This termination represents a discontinuation of further development for olaparib within the context of this specific study.
