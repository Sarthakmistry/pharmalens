## Regeneron's Phase 3 Trial NCT05074433 for Casirivimab+imdevimab Terminated

**Date:** 2022-05-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05074433) investigating Casirivimab+imdevimab was officially terminated on May 18, 2022. The trial had an actual enrollment of 66 participants and reached its primary completion on the same date.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial is a negative signal for Regeneron, indicating a halt in the development of Casirivimab+imdevimab for the studied indication.
