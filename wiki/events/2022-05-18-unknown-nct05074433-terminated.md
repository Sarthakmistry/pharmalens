## Regeneron terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05074433 for Casirivimab+imdevimab

**Date:** 2022-05-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05074433) investigating Casirivimab+imdevimab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 66 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 18, 2022, prior to its termination.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Casirivimab+imdevimab is a bearish signal for Regeneron, indicating a potential setback for the drug's development.
