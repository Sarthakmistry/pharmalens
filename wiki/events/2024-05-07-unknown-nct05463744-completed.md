## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial NCT05463744 for Insulin Efsitora Alfa Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-05-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05463744), which investigated Insulin efsitora alfa and Insulin degludec, reached its primary completion date on May 7, 2024. The study enrolled 692 participants and has reported results.
