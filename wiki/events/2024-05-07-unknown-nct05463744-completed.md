## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial NCT05463744 for Insulin efsitora alfa and Insulin degludec completes primary phase

**Date:** 2024-05-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05463744, evaluating Insulin efsitora alfa and Insulin degludec, has completed its primary phase on May 7, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 692 participants, has reached a significant milestone, indicating the completion of data collection for its primary endpoints. Results are now available, and their analysis will determine the next steps for these investigational drugs.
