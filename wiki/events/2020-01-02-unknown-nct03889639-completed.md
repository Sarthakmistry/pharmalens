## Sanofi's Phase 2 Trial NCT03889639 Completed

**Date:** 2020-01-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT03889639, reached its primary completion date on January 2, 2020. The study, which enrolled 130 participants, has been marked as completed and results are available. The trial investigated Sar442168 and a locally approved intravenous contrast medium for contrast-enhanced magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Sanofi's pipeline, with the availability of results potentially informing future development decisions for Sar442168.
