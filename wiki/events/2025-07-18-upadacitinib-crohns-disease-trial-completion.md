## Upadacitinib Crohn's Disease Trial Completion

**Date:** 2025-07-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A long-term open-label extension study evaluating the efficacy, safety, and tolerability of Upadacitinib (ABT-494) in participants with Crohn's Disease has been completed. This study, NCT02782663, sponsored by AbbVie, focused on long-term outcomes.

### Market implication
The completion of this long-term study provides further data on Upadacitinib for Crohn's Disease, with potential implications for future regulatory submissions or label updates.
