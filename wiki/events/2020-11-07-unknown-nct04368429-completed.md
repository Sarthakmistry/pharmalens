## Sanofi's Phase 3 Meningococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT04368429) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-11-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04368429, investigating a Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine, reached its primary completion date on November 7, 2020. The study, which enrolled 360 participants, aimed to evaluate the safety and immunogenicity of the vaccine. Results for this trial are available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of a Phase 3 trial is a standard milestone in the drug development process, indicating progress in Sanofi's vaccine pipeline.
