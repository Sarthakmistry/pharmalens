## Merck Reports Q1 2025 Earnings, Driven by Keytruda and New Product Winrevair Despite Overall Sales Dip

**Date:** 2025-04-24 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck reported total worldwide sales of $15.5 billion for Q1 2025, representing a 2% decrease from Q1 2024, but a 1% increase when excluding the impact of foreign exchange. Growth was primarily driven by strong performance from [[pembrolizumab]] (KEYTRUDA) and the successful launch of WINREVAIR, which generated $280 million in sales. However, this growth was partially offset by declines in other products, including GARDASIL/GARDASIL 9.

### Market implication
The mixed sales performance, with growth in key products offsetting declines elsewhere, suggests a neutral short-term market reaction for Merck.
