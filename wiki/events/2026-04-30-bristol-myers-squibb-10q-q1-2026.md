## Bristol Myers Squibb Reports Q1 2026 Financial Results

**Date:** 2026-04-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb reported Q1 2026 net product sales of $11.168 billion and total revenue of $11.489 billion. Eliquis was the top-selling product with $4.137 billion in sales, followed by Opdivo ([[nivolumab]]) with $2.146 billion. The company also reported a net income of $2.677 billion for the quarter.
