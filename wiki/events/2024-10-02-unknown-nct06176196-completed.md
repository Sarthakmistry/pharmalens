## Vertex's Phase 2 Trial NCT06176196 for Vx-548 in Alzheimer's Disease Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals' Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06176196, investigating Vx-548 for Alzheimer's disease, has reached its primary completion date on October 2, 2024. The trial enrolled 218 participants and has completed its primary data collection, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of the primary phase of this trial is a neutral event, as the market awaits the release of clinical data to assess the potential efficacy and safety of Vx-548 in Alzheimer's disease.
