## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04191096 for Metastatic Hormone-Sensitive Prostate Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2022-10-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04191096, evaluating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Enzalutamide and Androgen Deprivation Therapy (ADT) for metastatic hormone-sensitive prostate cancer, completed its primary endpoint on October 31, 2022. The study, sponsored by [[merck]], enrolled 1251 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a standard progression in the development of [[pembrolizumab]] for prostate cancer, with market implications awaiting the release of study results.
