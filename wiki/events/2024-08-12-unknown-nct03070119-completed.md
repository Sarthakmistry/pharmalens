## Biogen's Phase 3 Trial for Tofersen (NCT03070119) Completed

**Date:** 2024-08-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03070119) investigating Tofersen for the treatment of Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) has reached its primary completion date on August 12, 2024. The trial enrolled 139 participants and now has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as market reaction will depend on the eventual disclosure and interpretation of the trial's results.
