## AbbVie Reports Strong Q1 2025 Earnings Driven by Immunology and Neuroscience Growth

**Date:** 2025-04-25 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported first-quarter 2025 financial results with adjusted diluted EPS increasing by 6.5% to $2.46. Net revenues grew by 9.8% operationally to $13.343 billion, driven by strong performance in its Immunology and Neuroscience portfolios, which saw operational growth of 18.1% and 17.0% respectively. Key products like Skyrizi and Rinvoq contributed significantly to this growth.

### Market implication
The strong operational revenue growth and increased adjusted EPS suggest a positive outlook for AbbVie's valuation, particularly driven by its key growth products.
