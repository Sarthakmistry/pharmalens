### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05478252) for [[semaglutide]] in type 2 diabetes reached its primary completion date on August 9, 2023. The trial, which enrolled 388 participants, has now concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures. This milestone indicates the successful execution of the trial and moves the drug closer to potential regulatory submissions or further data analysis.
