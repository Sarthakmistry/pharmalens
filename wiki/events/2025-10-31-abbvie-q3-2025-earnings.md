## AbbVie Q3 2025 Earnings Show Revenue Growth Amidst EPS Decline

**Date:** 2025-10-31 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie reported third-quarter 2025 financial results, with diluted EPS of $0.10 (GAAP) and $1.86 (adjusted), and net revenues of $15.776 billion, an increase of 9.1%. Key products like Skyrizi, Rinvoq, Vraylar, and Botox Therapeutic showed strong revenue growth, while Humira's revenue continued to decline. The oncology portfolio also saw a slight decrease in net revenues.

### Market implication
The mixed financial results, with declining EPS but increasing net revenues, suggest a neutral short-term market reaction for AbbVie.
