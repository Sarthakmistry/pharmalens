## Merck Reports Strong Q4 and Full-Year 2025 Results, Driven by Keytruda and New Product Contributions

**Date:** 2026-02-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Merck & Co., Inc. announced strong financial results for the fourth quarter and full-year 2025. The company reported worldwide sales of $16.4 billion for Q4 2025, a 5% increase, and full-year sales of $65.0 billion. Growth was primarily driven by its oncology portfolio, particularly [[pembrolizumab]] (KEYTRUDA), and strong performance in Animal Health, with increasing contributions from new products like WINREVAIR and CAPVAXIVE. The company also noted an acquisition of sole global rights for pipeline candidate MK-8690.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, particularly in key growth areas like oncology and new product launches, suggests a positive outlook for Merck's stock and valuation.
