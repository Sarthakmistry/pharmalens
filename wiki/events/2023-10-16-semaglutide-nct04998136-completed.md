## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04998136) for Semaglutide in Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2023-10-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04998136) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for obesity, reached its primary completion date on October 16, 2023. The trial enrolled 150 participants and has reported that results are available, marking a significant milestone in the drug's development for this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial with results available for semaglutide in obesity provides further data supporting its efficacy and safety, potentially strengthening Novo Nordisk's market leadership in the GLP-1 obesity space.
