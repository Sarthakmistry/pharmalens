Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05132088) investigating semaglutide for obesity completed its primary data collection on July 11, 2023. The trial enrolled 201 participants and has not yet published its results. This marks a significant milestone in the trial's progression, moving it closer to potential data analysis and reporting.
