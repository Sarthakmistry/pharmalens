## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT05132088) for Obesity Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2023-07-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05132088) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for glp1-obesity, reached its primary completion date on July 11, 2023. The trial enrolled 201 participants and has completed its primary data collection, though results have not yet been published.

### Market implication
The completion of the primary phase for this semaglutide trial is a standard procedural update and does not carry immediate market implications until results are disclosed.
