## Phase 2 trial (NCT05061420) of pembrolizumab and cetuximab in head and neck cancer terminated

**Date:** 2023-07-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05061420) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]], [[cetuximab]], and SAR444245 for recurrent or metastatic head and neck squamous cell carcinoma, was terminated on July 21, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 59 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for the combination therapy, potentially impacting the development strategy for [[pembrolizumab]] and [[cetuximab]] in this specific oncology indication.
