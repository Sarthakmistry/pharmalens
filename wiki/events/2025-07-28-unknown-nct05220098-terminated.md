## Takeda's Phase 1/2 Trial of Tak-280 for Alzheimer's Disease Terminated

**Date:** 2025-07-28 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05220098) investigating Tak-280 for Alzheimer's disease. The trial had an actual enrollment of 69 participants and was originally expected to reach primary completion on July 28, 2025. The termination indicates a halt in the development of Tak-280 for this indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial for Tak-280 represents a setback for Takeda's Alzheimer's disease pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue prospects in this therapeutic area.
