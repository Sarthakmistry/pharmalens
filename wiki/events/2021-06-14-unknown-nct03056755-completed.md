## Novartis completes Phase 2 trial NCT03056755 for breast cancer

**Date:** 2021-06-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03056755) sponsored by Novartis, investigating Alpelisib, Fulvestrant, Letrozole, Goserelin, and Leuprolide for breast cancer, reached its primary completion on June 14, 2021. The trial enrolled 383 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a standard progression in Novartis's clinical development pipeline for breast cancer, with no immediate market impact indicated without specific results.
