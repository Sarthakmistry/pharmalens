## Novartis Completes Phase 2 Trial (NCT03056755) in Breast Cancer

**Date:** 2021-06-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has announced the completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT03056755, which investigated treatments for breast cancer. The trial, which enrolled 383 participants, reached its primary completion date on June 14, 2021, and results are available. The study involved drugs such as Alpelisib, Fulvestrant, Letrozole, Goserelin, and Leuprolide.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a step forward in Novartis's breast cancer pipeline, with results now available for analysis.
