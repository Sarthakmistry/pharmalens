## Sanofi terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT04524689) of Sar408701 with Merck's Pembrolizumab in NSCLC

**Date:** 2024-03-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04524689) investigating Sar408701 (tusamitamab ravtansine) in combination with Merck's [[pembrolizumab]] for the treatment of non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 57 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 12, 2024, with a status of "Terminated."

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for Sanofi's pipeline in NSCLC and could be seen as a bearish signal for the combination therapy, though the impact on Merck's established [[pembrolizumab]] is likely limited.
