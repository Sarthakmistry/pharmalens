## Pembrolizumab (NCT04381650) Phase 1/2 Trial Completed

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04381650) investigating pembrolizumab, sponsored by Takeda, reached its primary completion on October 29, 2024. The trial enrolled 161 participants and has results available. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for the drug.
