## Sanofi's Phase 2 Alzheimer's Trial for Sar444656 (kt-474) Terminated

**Date:** 2025-10-09 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06028230) investigating the drug Sar444656 (kt-474) for Alzheimer's disease was terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 70 participants and was originally expected to complete primary data collection by October 9, 2025.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Sanofi's Alzheimer's pipeline and the development of Sar444656 (kt-474), potentially impacting future revenue projections in this indication.
