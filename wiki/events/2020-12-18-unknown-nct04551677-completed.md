## Sanofi's Phase 4 trial NCT04551677 for Fluzone vaccines completed primary data collection

**Date:** 2020-12-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04551677) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating "Fluzone quadrivalent vaccine, no preservative (0.5-ml dose), 2020-2021 formulation" and "Fluzone high-dose quadrivalent vaccine (0.7-ml dose), 2020-2021 formulation", reached its primary completion date on December 18, 2020. The trial, which enrolled 90 participants, has been completed and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial for Sanofi's Fluzone vaccines is a standard procedural update and is considered neutral for the company's market valuation.
