## Johnson & Johnson Reports Strong Q2 2025 Results and Raises Full-Year Outlook

**Date:** 2025-07-16 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced its second-quarter 2025 financial results, reporting sales of $23.7 billion with 5.8% growth and adjusted EPS of $2.77. The company also raised its full-year 2025 outlook, reflecting strong performance across its segments. Key product highlights included significant growth for CARVYKTI, DARZALEX, and TREMFYA, alongside the approval of IMAAVY and priority review for TAR-200.

### Market implication
The strong Q2 2025 sales and earnings, coupled with an increased full-year outlook, indicate positive financial performance and future expectations for Johnson & Johnson, likely leading to a bullish market response.
