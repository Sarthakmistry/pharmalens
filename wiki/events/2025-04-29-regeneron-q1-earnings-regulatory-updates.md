## Regeneron Reports Strong Q1 2025 Earnings with Dupixent and EYLEA HD Growth, Key Regulatory Approvals

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron reported first quarter 2025 revenues of $3.0 billion, driven by strong sales growth for Dupixent and EYLEA HD. The company also announced significant regulatory milestones, including the approval of linvoseltamab in the EU for multiple myeloma and new approvals for Dupixent in chronic spontaneous urticaria in the U.S. and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease in Japan.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, particularly the growth of key products and positive regulatory developments, indicates a bullish outlook for Regeneron's valuation and future revenue streams.
