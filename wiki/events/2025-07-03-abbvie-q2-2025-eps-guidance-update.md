## AbbVie Updates Q2 and Full-Year 2025 EPS Guidance Due to IPR&D Expense

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. updated its 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance to reflect an $823 million acquired IPR&D and milestones expense incurred in the second quarter of 2025, resulting in an unfavorable impact of $0.42 per share. This adjustment lowers both the second quarter and full-year EPS guidance ranges previously provided by the company.

### Market implication
The reduction in AbbVie's EPS guidance due to the acquired IPR&D and milestones expense is a bearish signal, as it directly lowers the company's projected profitability for the quarter and the full year.
