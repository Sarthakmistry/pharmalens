## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial (NCT02684292) for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-01-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02684292) evaluating pembrolizumab in participants with advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on January 14, 2026. The study enrolled 304 participants and is now moving towards data analysis, with results not yet available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural milestone for pembrolizumab, with market impact awaiting the release and interpretation of the study's results.
