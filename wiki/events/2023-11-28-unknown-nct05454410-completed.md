## Novartis completes Phase 2 trial for investigational drug Mij821 (NCT05454410)

**Date:** 2023-11-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has announced the completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05454410) for the investigational drug Mij821, administered via subcutaneous injection in 1 mg, 4 mg, and 10 mg doses. The trial enrolled 60 participants and reached its primary completion date on November 28, 2023, with results now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as specific results or future development plans for Mij821 have not yet been disclosed.
