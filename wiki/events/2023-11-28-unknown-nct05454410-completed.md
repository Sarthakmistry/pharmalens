## Novartis Completes Phase 2 Trial for Mij821 (NCT05454410)

**Date:** 2023-11-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed a Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05454410) for its investigational drug, Mij821, administered via subcutaneous injection in 1 mg, 4 mg, and 10 mg doses. The trial, which enrolled 60 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 28, 2023, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial for an investigational drug by Novartis is a standard pipeline progression and has a neutral market implication at this stage, awaiting the release and interpretation of the trial results.
