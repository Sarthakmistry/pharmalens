## Sanofi's Sar442970 Phase 2 Trial for Alzheimer's Disease Completed

**Date:** 2024-08-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05849922) for Sar442970, an investigational drug for Alzheimer's disease. The trial, which enrolled 86 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 22, 2024, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial allows Sanofi to analyze data for potential progression to later stages, which could influence future pipeline valuation.
