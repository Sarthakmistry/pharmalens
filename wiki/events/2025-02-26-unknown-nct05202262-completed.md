## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 trial NCT05202262 for respiratory drugs reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2025-02-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05202262), investigating respiratory drugs including Bff mdi 320/9.6 µg, Bff mdi 160/9.6 µg, Bd mdi 320 µg, and Open-label symbicort tbh 320/9 µg, has reached its primary completion date on February 26, 2025. The trial successfully enrolled 645 participants and has indicated that results are available, though specific findings have not yet been disclosed.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for AstraZeneca's respiratory pipeline, with market implications awaiting the release of trial results.
