## Novartis completes Phase 2 trial (NCT04546633) for malaria drugs

**Date:** 2024-08-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis announced the completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04546633) for the investigational malaria drugs Kaf156, Lum-sdf, and Coartem. The trial, which enrolled 295 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 13, 2024, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event for Novartis, as specific results have not yet been disclosed.
