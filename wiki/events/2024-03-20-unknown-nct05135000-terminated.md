## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05135000) for Ltp001 in Heart Failure/Hypertension

**Date:** 2024-03-20 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05135000) for the investigational drug Ltp001, which was being studied for heart failure and hypertension. The trial, which enrolled 47 participants, reached its primary completion on March 20, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Ltp001 represents a bearish signal for Novartis's specific pipeline asset in the HF/HTN indication.
