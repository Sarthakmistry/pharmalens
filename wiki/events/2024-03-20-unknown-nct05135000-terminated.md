## Novartis's Trial NCT05135000 for Ltp001 in HF-HTN Terminated

**Date:** 2024-03-20 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05135000) investigating Ltp001 for heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN) has been terminated. The trial, which enrolled 47 participants, reached its primary completion on March 20, 2024, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial typically represents a setback for the developing company, potentially indicating issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic priorities, which could negatively impact Novartis's pipeline valuation.
