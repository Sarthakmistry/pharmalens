## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT01772472 for Breast Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2018-07-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT01772472, investigating a treatment for breast cancer, reached its primary completion date on July 25, 2018. The trial enrolled 1486 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone in the development process for Roche's breast cancer program, moving closer to potential regulatory submission.
