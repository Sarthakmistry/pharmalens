## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial for Quadrivalent Recombinant Influenza Vaccine (NCT05513053) Completed

**Date:** 2023-10-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi announced the completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05513053, for a Quadrivalent Recombinant Influenza Vaccine. The trial, which enrolled 1308 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 27, 2023, and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a standard progression in Sanofi's vaccine development pipeline, with market implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
