## Amgen Reports Q4 and Full Year 2025 Financial Results, Provides 2026 Guidance

**Date:** 2026-02-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Amgen Inc. reported its Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 financial results and provided 2026 guidance. For the full year 2025, GAAP Net Income was $7,711 million and EBITDA was $16,898 million. Free Cash Flow for 2025 decreased to $8,100 million from $10,394 million in 2024. The company issued 2026 GAAP diluted EPS guidance of $15.45 to $16.94 and Non-GAAP diluted EPS guidance of $21.60 to $23.00, with a debt leverage ratio of 3.2 as of December 31, 2025.

### Market implication
