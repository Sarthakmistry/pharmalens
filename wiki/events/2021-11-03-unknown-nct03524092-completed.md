## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 trial (NCT03524092) for Mirikizumab completed primary endpoint.

**Date:** 2021-11-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03524092) investigating Mirikizumab (both SC and IV formulations) reached its primary completion date on November 3, 2021. The trial, which enrolled 1177 participants, has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial with results provides key data for Eli Lilly's Mirikizumab program, moving it closer to potential regulatory submissions.
