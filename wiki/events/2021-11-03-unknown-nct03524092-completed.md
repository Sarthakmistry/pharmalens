## Eli Lilly's Mirikizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT03524092) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-11-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03524092, investigating Mirikizumab (both SC and IV formulations) reached its primary completion date on November 3, 2021. The trial enrolled 1328 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Eli Lilly, with further implications dependent on the disclosed results.
