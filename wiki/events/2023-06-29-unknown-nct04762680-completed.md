## Sanofi's Phase 2/3 COVID-19 Vaccine Trial (NCT04762680) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-06-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04762680) sponsored by Sanofi, evaluating various SARS-CoV-2 recombinant protein vaccine formulations, reached its primary completion on June 29, 2023. The study enrolled 3159 participants and has reported results. This trial assessed multiple vaccine formulations, including monovalent and bivalent options, in combination with AS03 adjuvant.

### Market implication
The completion of this COVID-19 vaccine trial by Sanofi is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication given the current landscape of COVID-19 vaccines and the lack of specific efficacy or safety data in this event.
