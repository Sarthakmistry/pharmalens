## Merck Reports Q1 2025 Earnings with Strong Keytruda Growth Amidst Vaccine Declines and IRA Impact

**Date:** 2025-05-02 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Q1 2025 total revenue decreased 2% to $15.53 billion, though it increased 1% excluding foreign exchange impacts. This performance was driven by strong oncology sales from Keytruda and Welireg, and growth in the cardiovascular franchise with Winrevair, but was offset by declines in vaccines (primarily Gardasil/Gardasil 9) and virology (Lagevrio), alongside significant pricing pressures from the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) and tariffs.

### Market implication
Merck's Q1 2025 results present a mixed picture, with robust performance from key oncology and cardiovascular assets balancing declines in other segments and the financial impact of the IRA and tariffs, leading to a neutral market signal.
