## Sanofi's Quadrivalent Recombinant Influenza Vaccine (RIV4) Phase 3 Trial (NCT05513053) Demonstrates Non-Inferior Immune Response

**Date:** 2023-10-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
The Phase 3 trial (NCT05513053) for Sanofi's Quadrivalent recombinant influenza vaccine (RIV4) season/2022-2023/nh reached primary completion on October 27, 2023. The study, which enrolled 1308 patients, demonstrated non-inferior immune responses in participants aged 9-17 years compared to adults aged 18-49 years, with no reported safety concerns.

### Market implication
The positive trial results could support future regulatory submissions and expand the vaccine's target population, potentially boosting Sanofi's market position in influenza vaccines.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** null
- **Sample size:** 1308 patients
- **Comparator:** adults aged 18-49 years
- **Primary outcome:** the non-inferiority of immune response at day 29 as assessed by HAI titre ratios and differences in seroconversion rates between the two age groups
- **Primary result:** The HAI GMT ratio between the paediatric and adult groups at day 29 in the per-protocol analysis set was 2·0 (95% CI 1·7 to 2·3), 3·3 (2·8 to 3·9), 1·6 (1·4 to 1·8), and 1·2 (1·1 to 1·4) for A/H1N1, H3N2, B/Victoria, and B/Yamagata, respectively. Differences in seroconversion between the groups were 1·9 (-2·8 to 6·6), -0·6 (-4·4 to 3·2), 3·3 (-1·6 to 8·1), and 14·3 (9·2 to 19·3) for each of the four strains, respectively.
- **Safety:** The safety profile was comparable in both age groups, except for solicited reactions within 7 days post-vaccination that were fewer in children and adolescents (274 [44%] of 618; 95% CI 40 to 48) than in adults (336 [53%] of 635; 49 to 57). No related serious adverse events, deaths, or adverse events of special interest were reported.
- **Conclusions:** RIV4 induced non-inferior immune responses in the participants aged 9-17 years compared with those aged 18-49 years. No safety concerns were reported.
- **Journal:** Lancet Infect Dis, 2025
- **Industry sponsored:** True
