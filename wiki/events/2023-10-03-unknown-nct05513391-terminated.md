## Sanofi's Phase 3 Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT05513391) Terminated

**Date:** 2023-10-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05513391) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating recombinant influenza vaccine (riv4) and quadrivalent influenza vaccine (iiv4), was terminated on October 3, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 366 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day.

### Market implication
The termination of this influenza vaccine trial by Sanofi has a neutral market implication given the non-tracked nature of the specific drugs and the lack of further context regarding the termination reason.
