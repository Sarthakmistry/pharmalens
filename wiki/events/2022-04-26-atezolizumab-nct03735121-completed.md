## Roche's Atezolizumab Trial NCT03735121 Completes for NSCLC

**Date:** 2022-04-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03735121) sponsored by Roche, investigating atezolizumab in combination with Rhuph20 for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2022. The trial enrolled 438 participants and has reported having results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as specific results are not yet available to assess its impact on atezolizumab's market position or Roche's valuation.
