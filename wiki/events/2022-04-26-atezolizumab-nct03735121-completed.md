## Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT03735121) for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-04-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03735121) evaluating atezolizumab in combination with Rhuph20 for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2022. The study, sponsored by Roche, enrolled 438 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for atezolizumab in NSCLC is a neutral event, as the results are not yet publicly available to assess the potential impact on Roche's pipeline or market position.
