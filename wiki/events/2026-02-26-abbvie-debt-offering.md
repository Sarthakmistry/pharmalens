## AbbVie Announces $7.5 Billion Senior Notes Offering

**Date:** 2026-02-26 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. announced an underwriting agreement to issue and sell $7.5 billion aggregate principal amount of senior notes across multiple series. The offering includes both floating rate and fixed-rate notes with maturities ranging from 2028 to 2066, with the closing expected on March 4, 2026.

### Market implication
This debt offering provides AbbVie with capital for general corporate purposes, including potential acquisitions, debt repayment, or share repurchases, with a neutral immediate market impact.
