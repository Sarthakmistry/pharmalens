## Eli Lilly's Retatrutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT06354660) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-01-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT06354660, investigating retatrutide for glp1-obesity and type2-diabetes, reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2026. The trial enrolled 537 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Eli Lilly's retatrutide, with market impact awaiting the release of clinical data.
