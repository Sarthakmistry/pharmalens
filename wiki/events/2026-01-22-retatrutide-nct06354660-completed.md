## Eli Lilly's Retatrutide Phase 3 Trial NCT06354660 for Obesity and Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-01-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06354660) for retatrutide, investigating its efficacy in obesity and type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2026. The trial enrolled 537 participants and has not yet released its results. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for the drug.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a procedural step towards potential regulatory submission for retatrutide, but there is no immediate market impact as the results have not yet been disclosed.
