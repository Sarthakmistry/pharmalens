## Takeda's Phase 3 trial NCT06108544 for Tak-279 and Apremilast reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2024-12-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT06108544, investigating Tak-279 and Apremilast, has reached its primary completion date on December 6, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 1108 participants, is now completed, indicating that data collection for the primary outcome measures has concluded. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been disclosed to provide insight into the efficacy or safety of the investigated drugs.
