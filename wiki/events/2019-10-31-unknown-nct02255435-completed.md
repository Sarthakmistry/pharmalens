## Biogen's Omaveloxolone Phase 2 Trial (NCT02255435) Completes

**Date:** 2019-10-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02255435) for Omaveloxolone capsules, investigating various dosages (2.5 mg to 300 mg), reached its primary completion date on October 31, 2019. The study enrolled 172 participants and has reported results.
