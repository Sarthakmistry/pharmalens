## Merck's Pembrolizumab trial NCT04934722 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2022-10-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04934722) investigating Merck's pembrolizumab in combination with Enzalutamide and Androgen Deprivation Therapy (ADT) for prostate cancer reached its primary completion date on October 31, 2022. The trial enrolled 186 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial marks a step towards potential data readout, but has no immediate market implication without disclosed results.
