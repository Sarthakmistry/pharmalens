## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04934722 for Prostate Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2022-10-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04934722) investigating pembrolizumab in combination with Enzalutamide and Androgen Deprivation Therapy (ADT) for metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer has reached its primary completion. The trial, sponsored by Merck, completed on October 31, 2022, and results are expected to be published.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step towards potential new data for pembrolizumab in prostate cancer, with market impact dependent on future results.
