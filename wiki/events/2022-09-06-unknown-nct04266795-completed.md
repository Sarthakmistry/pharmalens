## Takeda's Phase 2 trial NCT04266795 completed its primary endpoint

**Date:** 2022-09-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04266795, investigating Pevonedistat, Venetoclax, and Azacitidine, reached its primary completion date on September 6, 2022. The trial enrolled 164 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
This event represents a standard progression in the clinical development pipeline for Takeda, with no immediate market impact without further disclosure of the trial results.
