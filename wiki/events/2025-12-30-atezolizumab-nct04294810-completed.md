## Roche's Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT04294810) for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-12-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04294810) investigating [[atezolizumab]] for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on December 30, 2025. The study, which enrolled 620 participants, evaluated atezolizumab, also known as Tecentriq, in combination with Tiragolumab.
