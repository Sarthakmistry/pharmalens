## Roche's Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT04294810) for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-12-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04294810) sponsored by Roche, investigating atezolizumab in combination with Tiragolumab for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on December 30, 2025. The study enrolled 620 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone, with market impact awaiting the release and analysis of the study's results.
