## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial (NCT03036488) for Breast Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-10-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03036488, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy for early-stage triple-negative breast cancer, reached its primary completion date on October 14, 2025. The study enrolled 1174 participants and aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of the treatment regimen. This milestone indicates that data collection for the primary outcome measures is complete.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step, moving the drug closer to potential data readout and subsequent regulatory filings, which could impact Merck's oncology portfolio.
