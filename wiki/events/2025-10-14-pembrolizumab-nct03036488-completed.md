## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT03036488 for Breast Cancer Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-10-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03036488) investigating pembrolizumab in combination with chemotherapy for breast cancer has reached its primary completion date on October 14, 2025. The study, which enrolled 1174 participants, evaluated the efficacy and safety of the regimen. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a significant step in the development of pembrolizumab for breast cancer, indicating progress towards potential future data readouts, though there is no immediate market impact without results.
