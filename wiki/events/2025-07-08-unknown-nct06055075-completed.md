## Roche's trial NCT06055075 for Forimtamig, Carfilzomib, and Daratumumab completes primary phase

**Date:** 2025-07-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT06055075, sponsored by [[roche]], has reached its primary completion date on July 8, 2025. This Phase 1/2 study investigated Forimtamig in combination with Carfilzomib and Daratumumab. The trial enrolled 19 participants and is now completed, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released to indicate efficacy or safety, which would influence market perception.
