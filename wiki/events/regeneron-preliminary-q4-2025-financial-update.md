## Regeneron Provides Preliminary Q4 and Full Year 2025 Financial Update with Negative Charges

**Date:** 2026-01-12 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals provided a corporate update at the J.P. Morgan Healthcare Conference, including preliminary unaudited financial information for Q4 and full year 2025. The company expects an approximate $19 million pre-tax acquired IPR&D charge and a $60 million charge for its matching contribution to Good Days' Retinal Vascular and Neovascular Disease Fund, both negatively impacting Q4 2025 financial results. These preliminary results are unaudited and subject to finalization.

### Market implication
The reported charges are expected to negatively impact Regeneron's diluted EPS for Q4 2025, indicating a bearish signal for the company's short-term financial performance.
