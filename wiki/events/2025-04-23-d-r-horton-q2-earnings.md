## D.R. Horton Reports Significant Q2 2025 Declines in Revenue and Net Income Due to Slower Home Demand

**Date:** 2025-04-23 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
D.R. Horton reported significant declines in revenues, net income, and EPS for both the three and six months ended March 31, 2025, attributing the downturn to slower new home demand, affordability constraints, and increased sales incentives. Consolidated revenues for the three months decreased 15% to $7.7 billion, with net income falling 31% to $810.4 million. The home sales gross margin also decreased to 21.8% due to these increased incentives.

### Market implication
The reported declines in key financial metrics and the expectation of continued high sales incentives indicate a bearish outlook for D.R. Horton's near-term financial performance and market valuation.
