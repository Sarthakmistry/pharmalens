## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04889118 Completed

**Date:** 2023-01-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04889118) sponsored by Merck, investigating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]] with Lenvatinib in participants with advanced unresectable or metastatic melanoma, reached its primary completion on January 18, 2023. The study enrolled 131 participants, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step in the drug development process for [[pembrolizumab]]. Without specific data from the trial results, the market implication is neutral.
