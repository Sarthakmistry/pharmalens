## Trial NCT05070247 involving Pembrolizumab terminated

**Date:** 2025-01-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05070247, a Phase 1/2 study investigating pembrolizumab in combination with TAK-500, was officially terminated on January 6, 2025. The trial, sponsored by Takeda, had an actual enrollment of 61 participants and had reached its primary completion. This termination affects the ongoing development of pembrolizumab within this specific combination therapy.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a bearish signal for the specific combination therapy involving pembrolizumab and TAK-500, indicating a halt in its development path.
