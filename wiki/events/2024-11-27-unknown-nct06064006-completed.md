## Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 Trial for Nnc0487-0111 in Obesity Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk has announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06064006) for the drug Nnc0487-0111, targeting glp1-obesity. The trial, which enrolled 125 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 27, 2024, indicating that data collection for the primary outcome measures is finished. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event for Novo Nordisk, as no efficacy or safety data has been released, and it represents a standard progression in the drug development pipeline.
