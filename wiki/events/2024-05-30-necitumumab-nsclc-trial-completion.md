## Necitumumab NSCLC Trial Completion

**Date:** 2024-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial investigating necitumumab (IMC-11F8) in combination with gemcitabine-cisplatin for the first-line treatment of Stage IV squamous non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has completed. The study, sponsored by Eli Lilly and Company, enrolled 1093 participants and focused on Overall Survival Time (OS) as its primary outcome.

### Market implication
This trial completion is a neutral signal as no results or further details regarding the outcome of the study have been released.
