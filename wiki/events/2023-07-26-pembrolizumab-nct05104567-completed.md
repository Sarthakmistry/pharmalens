## Phase 2 Trial of Pembrolizumab and Cetuximab in Colorectal Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2023-07-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05104567) evaluating the combination of [[pembrolizumab]] and [[cetuximab]] for metastatic colorectal cancer reached its primary completion date on July 26, 2023. The trial, sponsored by [[sanofi]], enrolled 138 participants and has reported having results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on the development timeline for the combination therapy, with further insights pending the release of the study results.
