## Trial for Pembrolizumab (NCT04420884) in Advanced Solid Tumors Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-03-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT04420884, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Dazostinag and chemotherapy (Platinum, 5-fluorouracil) for advanced solid tumors, reached its primary completion date on March 30, 2026. This trial, sponsored by Takeda, enrolled 248 participants and marks a significant milestone in its development, with results pending.

### Market implication
The market implication is neutral as the trial has completed, but no results have been released yet to assess efficacy or safety.
