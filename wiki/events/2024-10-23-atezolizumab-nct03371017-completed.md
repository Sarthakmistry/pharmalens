## Atezolizumab (Tecentriq) trial NCT03371017 for breast cancer completes primary study

**Date:** 2024-10-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03371017, sponsored by Roche and investigating atezolizumab (Tecentriq) in combination with chemotherapy (Gemcitabine and Carboplatin) for breast cancer, completed its primary study on October 23, 2024. The trial enrolled 595 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial for atezolizumab in breast cancer, with results now available, represents a key data point for Roche's oncology pipeline and could influence future market positioning depending on the outcomes.
