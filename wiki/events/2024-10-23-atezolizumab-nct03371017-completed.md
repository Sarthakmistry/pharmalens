## Atezolizumab (Tecentriq) Phase 3 Trial NCT03371017 for Breast Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03371017) investigating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy (Gemcitabine and Carboplatin, or Capecitabine) for breast cancer has reached its primary completion date on October 23, 2024. The study enrolled 595 participants and has reported that results are available. This milestone indicates the completion of the primary data collection for the trial.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as the market impact will depend on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial results.
