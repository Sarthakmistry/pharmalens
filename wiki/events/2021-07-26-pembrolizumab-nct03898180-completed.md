## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide (Wegovy) Phase 3 trial for obesity completed.

**Date:** 2021-07-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03898180) investigating semaglutide (Wegovy) for obesity reached its primary completion date on July 26, 2021. The trial, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, enrolled 505 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as results have been reported and are already factored into market expectations for semaglutide in obesity.
