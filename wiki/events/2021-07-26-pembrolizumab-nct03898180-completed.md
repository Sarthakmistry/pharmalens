## Merck's Phase 3 trial NCT03898180 for pembrolizumab completes primary phase

**Date:** 2021-07-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03898180, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda) in combination with Lenvatinib, completed its primary phase on July 26, 2021. Sponsored by [[merck]], the trial enrolled 505 participants and has reported that results are available. This completion signifies the end of primary data collection for the study.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for [[pembrolizumab]] by [[merck]] allows for the analysis and potential release of results, which could impact future market expectations for the drug.
