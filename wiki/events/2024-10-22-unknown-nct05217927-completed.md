## Pfizer's Phase 4 Trial NCT05217927 for Rimegepant Completed

**Date:** 2024-10-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer announced the completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05217927, investigating Rimegepant 75mg (daily and every other day dosing) for migraine. The trial enrolled 1415 participants and reached its primary completion date on October 22, 2024, with results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides further data on Rimegepant, potentially reinforcing its market position for migraine treatment.
