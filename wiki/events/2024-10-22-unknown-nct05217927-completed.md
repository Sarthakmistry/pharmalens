## Pfizer's Phase 4 Trial NCT05217927 for Rimegepant Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05217927, investigating Rimegepant (75mg daily dosing and 75mg every other day dosing), reached its primary completion date on October 22, 2024. The trial enrolled 1415 participants and has results available, indicating the study has concluded its main data collection phase.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial marks a procedural milestone for Pfizer, with results now available for evaluation.
