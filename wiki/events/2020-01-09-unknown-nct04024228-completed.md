## Sanofi completes Phase 3 trial for influenza vaccines (NCT04024228)

**Date:** 2020-01-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has completed a Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04024228) investigating the immunogenicity and safety of standard-dose and high-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccines. The trial, which enrolled 1539 participants, reached its primary completion date on January 9, 2020, and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard operational milestone for Sanofi's vaccine development, with a neutral market implication as no specific results or significant new information impacting valuation have been released.
