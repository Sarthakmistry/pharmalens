## Regeneron's Phase 2/3 Trial (NCT04206553) for Dupilumab Completed

**Date:** 2024-07-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04206553), investigating Dupilumab in combination with oral corticosteroids (ocs), reached its primary completion on July 12, 2024. The trial enrolled 106 participants, and results are reported to be available.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a standard procedural step; market impact will depend on the eventual release and interpretation of the study results.
