## Regeneron's Phase 2/3 Trial (NCT04206553) for Dupilumab Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-07-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04206553), investigating Dupilumab in combination with oral corticosteroids (ocs) for Eosinophilic Esophagitis, reached its primary completion date on July 12, 2024. The trial enrolled 106 participants, and results are reported as available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial represents a development milestone for Regeneron, with market impact dependent on the eventual disclosure of the study results.
