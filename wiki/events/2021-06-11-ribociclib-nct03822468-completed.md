## Novartis' Ribociclib Trial NCT03822468 for Breast Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2021-06-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis' Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03822468) investigating [[ribociclib]] in combination with anastrozole or letrozole and goserelin for breast cancer reached its primary completion date on June 11, 2021. The trial enrolled 376 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides further data for [[ribociclib]] in breast cancer, with potential implications depending on the published results.
