## Novartis's Ribociclib (Kisqali) Trial NCT03822468 Completed for Breast Cancer

**Date:** 2021-06-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03822468) sponsored by Novartis, investigating [[ribociclib]] in combination with Anastrozole or Letrozole and Goserelin for breast cancer, reached its primary completion date on June 11, 2021. The trial enrolled 376 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides further data for [[ribociclib]] in breast cancer, with potential implications for future development and market positioning.
