## Novo Nordisk's semaglutide trial NCT04251156 for obesity and type 2 diabetes completes

**Date:** 2022-08-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04251156) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for obesity and type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on August 23, 2022. The trial enrolled 375 participants and has reported that results are available. This marks a significant step in the development pathway for semaglutide in these indications.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for semaglutide provides further data for Novo Nordisk's established drug, potentially supporting future regulatory submissions or label expansions.
