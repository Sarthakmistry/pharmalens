## Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial NCT04251156 for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2022-08-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04251156, investigating [[semaglutide]] for type 2 diabetes and obesity, reached its primary completion date on August 23, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 375 participants, has now concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures, and results are available. This marks a significant step in the development pathway for semaglutide in these indications.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial represents a standard progression in the drug development lifecycle for [[semaglutide]], providing further data for its use in [[type2-diabetes/_index]] and [[glp1-obesity/_index]].
