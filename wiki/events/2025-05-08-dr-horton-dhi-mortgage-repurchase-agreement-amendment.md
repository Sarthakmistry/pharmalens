## D.R. Horton's DHI Mortgage Amends Repurchase Agreement, Increasing Commitment to $1.4 Billion

**Date:** 2025-05-08 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
D.R. Horton's wholly-owned subsidiary, DHI Mortgage Company, Ltd., entered into a Fourth Amendment to its Master Repurchase Agreement, increasing the maximum aggregate commitment to $1.4 billion with an accordion feature up to $2.0 billion. This agreement is with U.S. Bank National Association and other buyers.

### Market implication
This amendment provides enhanced financing and liquidity to DHI Mortgage by increasing the maximum aggregate commitment of its repurchase facility, which is a positive signal for its operations.
