## Sanofi completes Phase 2 trial NCT04969276 for influenza and COVID-19 vaccines

**Date:** 2022-02-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has completed its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04969276) investigating the co-administration of a quadrivalent inactivated influenza high-dose vaccine and a COVID-19 mRNA vaccine. The trial, which enrolled 306 participants, reached its primary completion date on February 8, 2022, and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a standard progression in Sanofi's vaccine development pipeline, providing data for potential future development.
