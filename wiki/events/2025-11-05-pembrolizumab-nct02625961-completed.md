## Merck's Phase 2 trial (NCT02625961) for pembrolizumab in Alzheimer's disease completed

**Date:** 2025-11-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02625961) investigating pembrolizumab for Alzheimer's disease has reached its primary completion date on November 5, 2025. The trial enrolled 296 participants and is now completed, though results have not yet been posted.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released to indicate the efficacy or safety profile of pembrolizumab in Alzheimer's disease.
