## Bristol Myers Squibb's Phase 2 Nivolumab Trial (NCT05136677) for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb's Phase 2 trial (NCT05136677), investigating nivolumab in combination with ipilimumab and chemotherapy for advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on November 12, 2025. The study enrolled 102 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on the development timeline for nivolumab in NSCLC, with market impact pending future data release.
