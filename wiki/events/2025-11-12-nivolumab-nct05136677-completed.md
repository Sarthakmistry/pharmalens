## Bristol Myers Squibb's Phase 2 Nivolumab Trial for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05136677) for [[nivolumab]] in combination with Ipilimumab or chemotherapy (Pemetrexed, Cisplatin, Carboplatin) for advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) on November 12, 2025. The study enrolled 102 participants, and results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a procedural step, with market implications contingent upon the eventual release and interpretation of the clinical data.
