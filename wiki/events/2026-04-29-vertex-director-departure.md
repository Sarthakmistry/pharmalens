## Vertex Director Suketu Upadhyay Not Standing for Re-election Due to Scheduling Conflicts

**Date:** 2026-04-29 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Suketu Upadhyay informed the board of directors of Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated of his decision not to stand for re-election as a director at the 2026 annual meeting of shareholders. This decision is attributed to scheduling conflicts arising from his new role as Chief Financial Officer of Incyte Corporation. Mr. Upadhyay will continue to serve on the Board until the 2026 Annual Meeting, after which the Board intends to reduce its size to ten members.

### Market implication
This event is considered neutral as Mr. Upadhyay's departure was due to scheduling conflicts and not any disagreement with Vertex's operations, policies, or practices.
