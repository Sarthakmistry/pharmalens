## AbbVie terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05513703) of Telisotuzumab vedotin for NSCLC

**Date:** 2024-10-28 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT05513703, sponsored by [[abbvie]], investigating Telisotuzumab vedotin for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), was terminated on October 28, 2024. The trial had enrolled 9 participants and reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for [[abbvie]]'s pipeline in [[oncology-nsclc/_index]] and for Telisotuzumab vedotin's development.
