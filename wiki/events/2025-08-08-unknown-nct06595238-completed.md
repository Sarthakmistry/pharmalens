## AstraZeneca's Azd6234 Phase 2 Trial for GLP-1 Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06595238) for Azd6234, targeting GLP-1 obesity, reached its primary completion date on August 8, 2025. The trial enrolled 262 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
This event is neutral as the trial has completed its primary endpoint, but results are not yet available to assess efficacy or safety.
