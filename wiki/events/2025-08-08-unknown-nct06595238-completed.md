## AstraZeneca's Azd6234 Phase 2 trial for GLP-1 obesity completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06595238) investigating Azd6234 for GLP-1 obesity has reached its primary completion date. The trial enrolled 262 participants and focused on evaluating the drug's efficacy and safety in this indication. This milestone signifies the conclusion of the primary data collection phase for the study.

The completion of this Phase 2 trial indicates progress for AstraZeneca's pipeline in the GLP-1 obesity space, with future data readouts anticipated to inform further development.
