## AbbVie Reports Strong Q2 2025 Earnings Driven by Skyrizi and Rinvoq Growth

**Date:** 2025-07-31 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported second-quarter 2025 financial results, with adjusted diluted EPS increasing by 12.1% to $2.97 and net revenues growing by 6.6% to $15.423 billion. This growth was primarily driven by strong performance in its Immunology and Neuroscience portfolios, with key products like Skyrizi and Rinvoq showing significant revenue increases.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, particularly the double-digit growth in adjusted EPS and key product revenues, indicates a positive outlook for AbbVie's valuation and stock performance.
