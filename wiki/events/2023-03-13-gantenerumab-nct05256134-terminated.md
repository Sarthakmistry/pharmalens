## Roche terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT05256134) for Gantenerumab

**Date:** 2023-03-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Roche terminated its Phase 3 trial (NCT05256134) for Gantenerumab on March 13, 2023. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 25 participants, reached its primary completion on the same day it was terminated. The source document also included clinical findings from an observational study, published in Alzheimers Dement. in 2026, which evaluated the accuracy of a pTau217 plasma immunoassay in detecting amyloid pathology.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 3 trial for Gantenerumab represents a negative development for Roche's Alzheimer's disease pipeline, suggesting potential challenges in the drug's development path.

### Clinical evidence
An observational study, published in Alzheimers Dement. in 2026, investigated the detection of amyloid pathology using a pTau217 plasma immunoassay.
- **Study design:** observational
- **Sample size:** 2148
- **Comparator:** amyloid positron emission tomography
- **Primary outcome:** detection of amyloid pathology using pTau217 plasma immunoassay
- **Primary result:** Area under the curve was 0.878 (95% confidence interval [CI] 0.840, 0.915; impaired) and 0.907 (95% CI 0.885, 0.929; unimpaired).
- **Secondary results:**
    - For cognitively impaired, mean plasma pTau217 levels for amyloid-positive (A+) individuals were higher (n = 394; 0.835 pg/mL) than amyloid-negative (A-) individuals (n = 144; 0.361 pg/mL).
    - For cognitively unimpaired, A+ (n = 224; 0.516 pg/mL) and A- individuals (n = 1386; 0.220 pg/mL).
    - A cutoff < 0.189 pg/mL reliably ruled out individuals without amyloid pathology.
    - High negative predictive values (92.51% [impaired]; 98.60% [unimpaired]) were observed with sensitivity/specificity of 98.98%/29.17% and 95.54%/50.72%, respectively.
- **Safety:** None
- **Conclusions:** The pTau217 plasma immunoassay accurately detects amyloid pathology, irrespective of cognitive status.
- **Industry sponsored:** True
