## Trial NCT03786081 for Pembrolizumab Terminated

**Date:** 2026-03-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03786081) investigating pembrolizumab in combination with tisotumab vedotin and carboplatin for locally advanced or metastatic solid tumors was terminated on March 19, 2026. The trial, sponsored by Eli Lilly and Company, had an actual enrollment of 214 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this trial represents a minor setback for the development pipeline of pembrolizumab in combination therapies for solid tumors, potentially impacting future revenue projections in this specific indication area.
