## Trial NCT03786081 for Pembrolizumab in Cervical Cancer Terminated

**Date:** 2026-03-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT03786081, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with [[bevacizumab]], Tisotumab vedotin, and Carboplatin for recurrent or metastatic cervical cancer, has been terminated. The trial, sponsored by [[merck]], reached its primary completion date on March 19, 2026, with an actual enrollment of 214 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a setback for the specific combination therapy in cervical cancer, potentially impacting [[merck]]'s pipeline for this indication.
