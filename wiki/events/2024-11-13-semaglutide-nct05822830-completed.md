## Eli Lilly's GLP-1 Obesity Trial (NCT05822830) Involving Semaglutide and Tirzepatide Reaches Primary Completion

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05822830) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating treatments for GLP-1 obesity, has reached its primary completion date on November 13, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 751 participants, involved drugs such as [[semaglutide]] and [[tirzepatide]].
