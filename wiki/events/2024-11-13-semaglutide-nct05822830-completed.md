## Eli Lilly's GLP-1 Obesity Trial (NCT05822830) Involving Semaglutide Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2024-11-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05822830, sponsored by Eli Lilly and investigating treatments for GLP-1 obesity, completed its primary phase on November 13, 2024. The trial, which involved semaglutide and tirzepatide, enrolled 751 participants, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a standard procedural step, and its market impact will depend on the forthcoming results.
