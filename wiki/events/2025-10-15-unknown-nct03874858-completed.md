## Novartis completes Phase 2 trial NCT03874858 for Nilotinib and Asciminib

**Date:** 2025-10-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed its Phase 2 clinical trial NCT03874858, which investigated Nilotinib and Asciminib. The trial enrolled 124 participants and reached its primary completion date on October 15, 2025. This marks a procedural milestone in the development of these compounds.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication until results are released.
