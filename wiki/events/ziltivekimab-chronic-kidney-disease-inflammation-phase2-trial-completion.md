## Novo Nordisk Completes Phase 2 Trial of Ziltivekimab for Chronic Kidney Disease and Inflammation

**Date:** 2026-04-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk A/S has completed a Phase 2 trial (NCT03926117) evaluating Ziltivekimab for the reduction of inflammation in patients with advanced chronic renal disease. This interventional study enrolled 264 participants and investigated doses of 7.5 mg, 15 mg, and 30 mg subcutaneous monthly compared to placebo over six months, with the primary outcome measuring the percent change from baseline in High-sensitivity C-reactive Protein (Hs-CRP) levels.
