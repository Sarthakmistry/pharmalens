## Sanofi's Phase 3 Hectorol Trial Terminated

**Date:** 2025-07-29 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02859896) for Hectorol in pediatric patients with chronic kidney disease and secondary hyperparathyroidism was terminated. This trial aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of Hectorol compared to Rocaltrol (calcitriol).

### Market implication
The termination of the Phase 3 trial for Hectorol represents a setback for Sanofi in the treatment of secondary hyperparathyroidism in pediatric patients.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Hectorol
- Company: [[sanofi]]
- Indication: Secondary Hyperparathyroidism-Chronic Kidney Disease

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** INTERVENTIONAL
- **Sample size:** 21 participants
- **Comparator:** Rocaltrol (calcitriol)
- **Primary outcome:** Percentage of participants achieving two consecutive ≥30% reductions in iPTH
- **Primary result:** None
