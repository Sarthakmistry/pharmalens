## Bayer's Larotrectinib (Vitrakvi) Phase 2 Trial for NTRK-Fusion Positive Solid Tumors Completes

**Date:** 2025-09-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 study (NCT02576431) of larotrectinib (Vitrakvi) for NTRK-fusion positive solid tumors, sponsored by Bayer, has completed. The trial enrolled 215 participants and evaluated the best overall response as its primary outcome. This study aimed to assess the efficacy of larotrectinib in adults and children with NTRK-fusion positive solid tumors.

### Market implication
This event marks the completion of a clinical trial, providing an update on Bayer's pipeline, with a neutral market implication as no results are yet reported.
