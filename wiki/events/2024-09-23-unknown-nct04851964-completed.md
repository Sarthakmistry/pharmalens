## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 trial for tezepelumab in chronic rhinosinusitis with nasal polyps reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2024-09-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04851964) investigating tezepelumab for the treatment of chronic rhinosinusitis with nasal polyps has reached its primary completion date on September 23, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 416 participants, has now completed data collection for its primary endpoint.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant operational milestone for AstraZeneca, moving tezepelumab closer to potential regulatory submission for this indication, pending the analysis and announcement of results.
