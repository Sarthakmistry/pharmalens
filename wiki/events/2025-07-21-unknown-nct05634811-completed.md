## Pfizer's Phase 3 Trial NCT05634811 for Vla15 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-07-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05634811, investigating Vla15 against Normal saline, reached its primary completion date on July 21, 2025. The study enrolled 3549 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released to indicate the efficacy or safety profile of Vla15.
