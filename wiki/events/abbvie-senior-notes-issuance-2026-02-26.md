## AbbVie Inc. Announces Issuance of $8 Billion Senior Notes

**Date:** 2026-02-26 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. announced the issuance and sale of $8 billion aggregate principal amount of senior notes across multiple tranches. The notes include floating rate notes due 2028 and fixed-rate senior notes due 2028, 2031, 2033, 2036, 2056, and 2066. The closing of the sale is expected to occur on March 4, 2026, with J.P. Morgan Securities LLC, BofA Securities, Inc., Morgan Stanley & Co. LLC, and Wells Fargo Securities, LLC acting as representatives of the underwriters. This financial move is for general corporate purposes.

### Market implication
This is a standard financial transaction for AbbVie, providing capital for general corporate purposes without immediate direct impact on drug-specific valuation.
