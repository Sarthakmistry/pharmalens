## Regeneron Reports Q1 2026 Earnings with Revenue Growth but Net Income Decline

**Date:** 2026-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron reported Q1 2026 total revenue of $3,605.4 million, an increase from the prior year, but net income and EPS declined, partly due to a temporary manufacturing interruption. Key products mentioned include EYLEA HD, EYLEA, Libtayo, Praluent, Evkeeza, and Lynozyfic.

### Market implication
Total revenue increased year-over-year, but net income and diluted EPS decreased, and the company experienced a temporary manufacturing interruption impacting gross margin, though it also authorized a new share repurchase program.
