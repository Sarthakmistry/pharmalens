## Novartis completes Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04209205

**Date:** 2022-05-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis completed a Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04209205) on May 17, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 381 participants, has reached its primary completion and reported results. The study investigated the compounds Ain457 6 mg/kg i.v. and Ain457 3 mg/kg.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial represents a standard progression in Novartis's pipeline, with market implications dependent on the eventual disclosure of trial results.
