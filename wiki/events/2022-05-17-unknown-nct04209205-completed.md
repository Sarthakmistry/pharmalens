## Novartis completes Phase 3 trial NCT04209205

**Date:** 2022-05-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04209205 on May 17, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 381 participants, has reached its primary completion date. The study investigated Ain457 6 mg/kg i.v. and Ain457 3 mg/kg.

### Market implication
The completion of a clinical trial is a standard procedural step; market impact will depend on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
