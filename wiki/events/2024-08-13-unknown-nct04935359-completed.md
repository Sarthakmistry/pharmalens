## Novartis's Nis793 Phase 3 Trial for Alzheimer's Disease Completes Primary Study

**Date:** 2024-08-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04935359) sponsored by Novartis, investigating Nis793 in combination with Nab-paclitaxel and Gemcitabine for Alzheimer's disease, completed its primary study on August 13, 2024. The trial enrolled 511 participants and has reported that results are available. The completion of the primary study indicates that the main data collection for the trial's primary endpoints has concluded.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as the results are now available for analysis but have not yet been publicly disclosed, making the outcome for Novartis's Nis793 uncertain.
