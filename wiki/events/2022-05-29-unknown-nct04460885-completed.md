## Novo Nordisk's NCT04460885 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2022-05-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04460885, investigating Insulin icodec and Insulin glargine for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion on May 29, 2022. The trial enrolled 984 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Novo Nordisk's diabetes pipeline, with market implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
