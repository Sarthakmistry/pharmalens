## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04460885) for Insulin Icodec in Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-05-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04460885) sponsored by [[novo-nordisk]], investigating Insulin icodec for the treatment of [[type2-diabetes]] compared to Insulin glargine, completed its primary endpoint on May 29, 2022. The trial successfully enrolled 984 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development of Insulin icodec, with potential future implications for Novo Nordisk's diabetes portfolio.
