## Pfizer's Phase 2/3 trial NCT04960202 for Pf-07321332 and Ritonavir completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2021-12-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04960202) investigating Pf-07321332 in combination with Ritonavir reached its primary completion date on December 9, 2021. The study enrolled 2091 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides data for Pfizer's investigational drug, which will inform future development and regulatory decisions.
