## Amgen Reports Strong Q2 2025 Earnings and Raises Non-GAAP EPS Guidance

**Date:** 2025-08-05 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Amgen Inc. reported its Second Quarter 2025 financial results and provided updated GAAP and non-GAAP guidance for the full year 2025. The company provided strong non-GAAP diluted EPS guidance for 2025 ($20.20 - $21.30), significantly exceeding GAAP guidance, and reported positive Free Cash Flow of $1,911 million for Q2 2025.
