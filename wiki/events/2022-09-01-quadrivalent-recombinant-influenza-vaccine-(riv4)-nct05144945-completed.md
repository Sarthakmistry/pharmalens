## Sanofi's Quadrivalent Recombinant Influenza Vaccine (RIV4) Phase 3 Trial (NCT05144945) Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi announced the completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05144945) for Quadrivalent recombinant influenza vaccine (RIV4) on September 1, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 301 participants, investigated the immunogenicity and safety of RIV4 compared to Quadrivalent inactivated influenza vaccine (IIV4). The trial has reported having results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been publicly disclosed yet to indicate efficacy or safety outcomes.
