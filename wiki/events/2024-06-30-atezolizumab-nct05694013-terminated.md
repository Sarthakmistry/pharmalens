## Roche terminates Phase 2/3 trial (NCT05694013) for atezolizumab in advanced solid tumors

**Date:** 2024-06-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Roche has terminated the Phase 2/3 clinical trial NCT05694013, which was evaluating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with DPM Module and local SOC support for patients with advanced solid tumors. The trial, which enrolled 49 participants, reached its primary completion on June 30, 2024, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of this trial represents a minor bearish signal for [[roche]]'s [[atezolizumab]] development, as it removes a potential future indication, although the impact is mitigated by the trial's early phase and broad, non-specific oncology focus.
