## Roche's Atezolizumab Trial NCT05694013 Terminated

**Date:** 2024-06-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The clinical trial NCT05694013, investigating the impact of digital patient monitoring (DPM) on health outcomes and healthcare resource usage in patients receiving systemic anticancer treatment (SACT), has been terminated. The trial, sponsored by Roche and involving the drug [[atezolizumab]], had enrolled 49 patients and reached its primary completion date on June 30, 2024. The termination means the study will not yield its intended results regarding the impact of DPM on health outcomes and HRU.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2/3 trial represents a minor bearish signal for Roche, as it halts research into digital patient monitoring for oncology patients receiving SACT, potentially delaying insights into this area.
