## Sanofi's Insulin Glargine (u300) Phase 4 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2022-12-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04980027) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating Insulin glargine (u300) for Type 2 Diabetes, completed its primary data collection on December 23, 2022. The trial enrolled 228 participants and has not yet released its results. This marks a procedural milestone in the trial's progress.

### Market implication
The completion of primary data collection for this Phase 4 trial is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication until results are released.
