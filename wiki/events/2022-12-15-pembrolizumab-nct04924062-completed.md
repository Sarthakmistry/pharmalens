## Merck's Pembrolizumab (KEYNOTE-B69) Phase 3 Trial for Urothelial Carcinoma Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-12-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, KEYNOTE-B69 (NCT04924062), investigating [[pembrolizumab]] with or without Gemcitabine and Cisplatin in participants with locally advanced or metastatic urothelial carcinoma, reached its primary completion date on December 15, 2022. The trial enrolled 158 participants and has reported having results, though specific findings are not detailed in this update. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for [[pembrolizumab]] in this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial moves [[pembrolizumab]] closer to potential regulatory filings or label expansions for urothelial carcinoma, pending the release and analysis of its results.
