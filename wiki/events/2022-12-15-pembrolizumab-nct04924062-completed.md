## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04924062 for Urothelial Carcinoma Completed

**Date:** 2022-12-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04924062, a Phase 3 study investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Gemcitabine and Cisplatin for locally advanced or metastatic urothelial carcinoma, reached its primary completion on December 15, 2022. The trial, sponsored by [[merck]], enrolled 158 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for [[pembrolizumab]] marks a step forward in its development for urothelial carcinoma, with results now available for evaluation.
