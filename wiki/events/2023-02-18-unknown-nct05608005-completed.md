## Sanofi's Phase 1/2 Trial NCT05608005 Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-02-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05608005), investigating an influenza vaccine candidate, reached its primary completion on February 18, 2023. The study enrolled 581 participants and has reported results. The trial evaluated different formulations including Panblok + mf59 (dose 1 and dose 2) and unadjuvanted Panblok (dose 3).

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides an update on Sanofi's vaccine pipeline, with results now available for further evaluation.
