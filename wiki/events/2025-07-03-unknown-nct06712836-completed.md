## Pfizer Completes Primary Completion for Phase 2 Met097 Trial in GLP-1 Obesity

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer has announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06712836) for Met097, an investigational drug targeting GLP-1 obesity. The trial, which enrolled 239 participants, reached its primary completion date on July 3, 2025. No results have been reported yet.
