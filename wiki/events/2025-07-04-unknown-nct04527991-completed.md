## Gilead's Phase 3 trial NCT04527991 for Alzheimer's disease reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2025-07-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04527991, has reached its primary completion date on July 4, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 712 participants, is investigating treatments for Alzheimer's disease. The study involved drugs such as Sacituzumab govitecan-hziy, Paclitaxel, Docetaxel, and Vinflunine. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
As no results have been released, the market implication is neutral pending data readout.
