## Gilead's Phase 3 Trial NCT04527991 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-07-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04527991, has reached its primary completion date of July 4, 2025. This trial, which enrolled 712 participants, was investigating treatments for Alzheimer's disease. The completion marks a significant milestone in the trial's progression, moving it closer to potential data readout.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral development, as no results have been released to indicate the efficacy or safety profile of the investigated treatments.
