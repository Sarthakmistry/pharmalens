## Pfizer Completes Phase 2 Trial (NCT03909971) for Lorlatinib in NSCLC

**Date:** 2020-08-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On August 10, 2020, Pfizer completed its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03909971) investigating Lorlatinib for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial enrolled 109 participants and has reported results, contributing to the understanding of the drug's efficacy and safety in this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides further data on Lorlatinib's profile in NSCLC, contributing to the drug's overall development and market positioning.
