## Semaglutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT04969939) Completed for Obesity

**Date:** 2022-12-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04969939) investigating semaglutide for obesity, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, reached its primary completion on December 6, 2022. The trial enrolled 120 participants and has reported results. This trial assessed Semaglutide 2.4 mg and nnc0165-1875 1.0 mg.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides additional data for semaglutide in obesity, which could inform future development and market positioning for Novo Nordisk.
