## Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 Trial (NCT04969939) for Semaglutide in Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2022-12-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04969939) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for obesity, reached its primary completion date on December 6, 2022. The trial enrolled 120 participants and has results available, indicating the study has concluded its data collection for the primary outcome.
