## Novo Nordisk's NNC0195-0092 Growth Hormone Deficiency Trial Completes

**Date:** 2024-09-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT02616562, investigating the efficacy and safety of once-weekly NNC0195-0092 compared to daily Norditropin® FlexPro® in pre-pubertal children with growth hormone deficiency, has been completed. The trial focused on growth hormone treatment naive pre-pubertal children with growth hormone deficiency and enrolled 76 participants.

### Market implication
The completion of this clinical trial for NNC0195-0092 is a neutral event as no results have been disclosed, providing no immediate impact on Novo Nordisk's valuation.

### Affected entities
- Drug: NNC0195-0092
- Company: [[novo-nordisk]]
- Indication: Growth Hormone Deficiency

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 76 participants
- **Comparator:** Norditropin® FlexPro®
- **Primary outcome:** Height Velocity (HV) (cm/Year) During the First 26 Weeks of Treatment, Measured as Standing Height With Stadiometer | Cohort II and Cohort III - Adverse Events Rate, Including Injection Site Reactions in Children With GHD.
