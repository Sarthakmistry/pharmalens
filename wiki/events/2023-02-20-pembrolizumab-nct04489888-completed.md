## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04489888 Completed

**Date:** 2023-02-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT04489888, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Carboplatin and Paclitaxel, reached its primary completion on February 20, 2023. The trial enrolled 101 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the ongoing study of [[pembrolizumab]].

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides additional data on [[pembrolizumab]]'s profile, which could inform clinical practice. Given no specific results are detailed in this completion notice, the market implication is neutral.
