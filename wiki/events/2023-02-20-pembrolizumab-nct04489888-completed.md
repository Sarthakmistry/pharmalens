## Merck's Pembrolizumab Phase 4 NSCLC Trial NCT04489888 Completed

**Date:** 2023-02-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck announced the completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04489888) evaluating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy (Carboplatin and Paclitaxel) for patients with advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial enrolled 101 participants and reached its primary completion date on February 20, 2023, with results now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides further data for [[pembrolizumab]] in NSCLC, reinforcing its established market position.
