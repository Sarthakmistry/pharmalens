## Imbruvica (ibrutinib) Phase 4 Post-Marketing Safety Study in CLL/MCL Completed

**Date:** 2025-05-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 post-marketing safety study of Imbruvica (ibrutinib) in Indian participants with Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia or Mantle Cell Lymphoma has completed, with results available. The study, sponsored by Johnson & Johnson Private Limited, aimed to evaluate the post-marketing safety of Imbruvica (ibrutinib capsule 140 mg) under actual conditions of use, focusing on the incidence of adverse events. It enrolled 75 participants.

### Market implication
The completion of this post-marketing safety study for Imbruvica (ibrutinib) is a neutral event for Johnson & Johnson, as no specific safety concerns or new efficacy data were highlighted in the completion notice.
