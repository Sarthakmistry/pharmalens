## Bayer's Aflibercept trial NCT04423718 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2022-07-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04423718, sponsored by Bayer, reached its primary completion date on July 27, 2022. This trial, which enrolled 1011 participants, investigated Aflibercept high dose vegf trap-eye (bay86-5321) and Aflibercept vegf trap-eye (eylea, bay86-5321). Results for this completed trial are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for Aflibercept by Bayer is a neutral development, as specific results or their implications are not detailed.
