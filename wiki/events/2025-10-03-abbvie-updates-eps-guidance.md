## AbbVie Updates 2025 EPS Guidance Due to Significant IPR&D Expense

**Date:** 2025-10-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie updated its third quarter and full-year 2025 adjusted diluted earnings per share guidance, reflecting an unfavorable impact of $1.50 per share due to $2.68 billion in acquired IPR&D and milestones expense. This revision leads to a lower EPS range for both the quarter and the full year compared to previous projections.

### Market implication
The downward revision of AbbVie's EPS guidance for Q3 and full-year 2025, driven by substantial acquired IPR&D and milestones expense, is likely to be viewed negatively by investors, potentially impacting the company's stock valuation.
