## Biogen's Phase 3 Trial NCT03749447 for Bardoxolone Methyl Terminated

**Date:** 2023-08-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03749447), which was investigating Bardoxolone methyl, reached its primary completion on August 23, 2023, and has since been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 270 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial represents a bearish signal for Biogen, indicating a significant setback for the development of Bardoxolone methyl within its pipeline.
