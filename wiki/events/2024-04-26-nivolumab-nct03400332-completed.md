## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Phase 1/2 Trial NCT03400332 for Solid Tumors Completes Primary Study

**Date:** 2024-04-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03400332) evaluating [[nivolumab]] (Opdivo) in combination with BMS-986253 and/or Ipilimumab for advanced solid tumors has completed its primary study. The trial, sponsored by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], enrolled 281 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial paves the way for data analysis, which could inform future development strategies for [[nivolumab]] in solid tumors, potentially impacting its long-term market position.
