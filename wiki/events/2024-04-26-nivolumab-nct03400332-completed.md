## Bristol Myers Squibb's Phase 1/2 trial (NCT03400332) for nivolumab completes primary phase

**Date:** 2024-04-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03400332) sponsored by Bristol Myers Squibb, investigating nivolumab in combination with BMS-986253 and/or ipilimumab, has reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2024. The study enrolled 281 participants and has reported that results are available.
