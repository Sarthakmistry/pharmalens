## Nilotinib Trial for Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumors Completes Phase 4 Study

**Date:** 2026-04-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT00756509) for Nilotinib in patients with metastatic or unresectable Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumors has been completed. The study, sponsored by Novartis Pharmaceuticals, enrolled 34 participants and focused on the proportion of participants with best overall response at Month 6.

As no specific results or outcomes have been released, the market implication for Nilotinib and Novartis Pharmaceuticals remains neutral at this time.
