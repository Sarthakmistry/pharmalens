## Sanofi's Prv-015 Phase 2 Trial (NCT04424927) Completed

**Date:** 2024-07-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 trial for Prv-015 (NCT04424927), which enrolled 388 participants, reached primary completion on July 30, 2024. The trial has reported results, which include conclusions proposing a three-axis model for exhaustion-resistant CD8(+) T cells in Ankylosing Spondylitis, published in Immunology in 2025.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on [[sanofi]]'s pipeline development for Prv-015, with initial findings published.
