## Bristol Myers Squibb terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05556265) for Deucravacitinib

**Date:** 2024-01-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05556265) for the drug Deucravacitinib. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 94 participants, reached its primary completion date on January 6, 2024, and results are available. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Deucravacitinib for the specific condition studied in this trial.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial is generally a bearish signal for the drug's development and the company's pipeline in that area, suggesting potential efficacy or safety concerns, or a strategic reprioritization.
