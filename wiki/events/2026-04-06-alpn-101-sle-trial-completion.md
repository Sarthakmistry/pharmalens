## ALPN-101 (Acazicolcept) SLE Trial Completion

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 trial for ALPN-101 (Acazicolcept) in Systemic Lupus Erythematosus has been completed by Alpine Immune Sciences Inc, a subsidiary of Vertex.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial indicates progress in the development of ALPN-101 (Acazicolcept) for Systemic Lupus Erythematosus.
