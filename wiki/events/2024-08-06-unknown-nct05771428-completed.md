## AbbVie's Phase 2 Alzheimer's Trial (NCT05771428) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-08-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05771428) for the investigational drug Abbv-552, targeting Alzheimer's disease, reached its primary completion date on August 6, 2024. The study successfully enrolled 263 participants and results are now available. This marks a significant procedural milestone in the drug's development.
