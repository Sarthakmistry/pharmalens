## D.R. Horton Reports Q4 2025 Earnings with Revenue and Net Income Decline

**Date:** 2026-01-22 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
During the three months ended December 31, 2025, D.R. Horton's consolidated revenues decreased 10% to $6.9 billion, net income attributable to D.R. Horton decreased 30% to $594.8 million, and diluted earnings per share were $2.03, down 22%. Homebuilding revenues also saw a 9% decrease, with homes closed down 7% and the average closing price down 3%.

### Market implication
The significant decline in consolidated revenues and net income indicates a bearish outlook for D.R. Horton's financial performance, reflecting challenges in the homebuilding market.
