## Novartis terminates Phase 3 trial NCT04737330 for Secukinumab

**Date:** 2023-05-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

Novartis has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04737330, which involved the drug Secukinumab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 28 participants, reached its primary completion on May 16, 2023, the same day it was terminated. Results for the trial are available.
