## Novo Nordisk completes Phase 3 trial for turoctocog alfa pegol in Haemophilia A

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 trial investigating the safety and efficacy of turoctocog alfa pegol (N8-GP) in previously untreated patients with Haemophilia A has been completed by Novo Nordisk A/S. The trial aimed to assess the formation of inhibitory antibodies against Coagulation Factor VIII (FVIII).

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a neutral event as specific outcomes are not yet available to determine its impact on the drug's potential or Novo Nordisk's valuation.
