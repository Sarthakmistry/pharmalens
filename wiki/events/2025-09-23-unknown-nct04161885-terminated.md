## AbbVie Terminates Phase 3 Clinical Trial NCT04161885

**Date:** 2025-09-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04161885, which had an actual enrollment of 465 participants. The trial was originally expected to reach primary completion on September 23, 2025. The termination suggests a halt in the development of the investigational treatment associated with this study.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial represents a setback for AbbVie, indicating a loss of investment and potential future revenue from the undisclosed therapeutic candidate.
