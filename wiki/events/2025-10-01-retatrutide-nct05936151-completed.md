## Eli Lilly's Retatrutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT05936151) for Obesity and Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05936151) evaluating retatrutide for the treatment of GLP-1 obesity and type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on October 1, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 146 participants, is now completed, with results pending.
