## Eli Lilly's retatrutide Phase 2 trial NCT05936151 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-10-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05936151) for retatrutide, investigating its use for glp1-obesity and type2-diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on October 1, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 146 participants, has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a step forward in the development of retatrutide, with future data readouts anticipated to provide insights into its efficacy and safety profile.
