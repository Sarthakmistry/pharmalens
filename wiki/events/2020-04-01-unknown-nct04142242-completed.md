## Sanofi Completes Phase 3 Trial NCT04142242

**Date:** 2020-04-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi announced the completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04142242 on April 1, 2020. This trial, which enrolled 471 participants, investigated a Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine. The trial has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event for Sanofi, as specific efficacy or safety results are not detailed in this update.
