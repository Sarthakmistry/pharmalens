## Phase 1/2 Trial of Magrolimab (Hu5F9-G4) in Combination with Rituximab or Rituximab + Chemotherapy for Relapsed/Refractory B-cell Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma (NCT02953509) Terminated

**Date:** 2026-04-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 trial (NCT02953509) investigating Magrolimab (Hu5F9-G4) in combination with Rituximab or Rituximab + Chemotherapy for Relapsed/Refractory B-cell Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma has been terminated. This interventional study, sponsored by Gilead Sciences, aimed to assess the safety, tolerability, and efficacy of these combinations in 178 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this clinical trial for Magrolimab indicates a negative outcome for the drug's development in the Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma indication, potentially impacting Gilead's pipeline valuation.
