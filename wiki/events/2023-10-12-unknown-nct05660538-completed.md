## Vertex completes Phase 2 trial for Suzetrigine (NCT05660538)

**Date:** 2023-10-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05660538) for Suzetrigine, with Pregabalin as a comparator. The trial, which enrolled 194 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 12, 2023, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Vertex's pipeline, with results now available for evaluation.
