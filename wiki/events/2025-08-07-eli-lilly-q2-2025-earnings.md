## Eli Lilly Reports Strong Q2 2025 Financial Results

**Date:** 2025-08-07 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company reported strong second-quarter 2025 financial results with significant increases in net income and earnings per share. Net income for Q2 2025 was $5,660.5 million, up 91% from Q2 2024, and diluted earnings per share increased by 92% to $6.29. The gross margin also improved to 84.3% from 80.8% in the prior year period.

### Market implication
The substantial year-over-year growth in net income and earnings per share indicates strong financial performance and is likely to be viewed positively by investors.
