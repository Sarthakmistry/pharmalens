## Eli Lilly Reports Strong Q2 2025 Financial Results with 91% Net Income Growth

**Date:** 2025-08-07 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company announced strong financial results for the second quarter of 2025, with net income increasing by 91% to $5,660.5 million compared to $2,967.0 million in the second quarter of 2024. Diluted earnings per share were $6.29 for Q2 2025, up 92% from $3.28 in Q2 2024. The company also reported a Gross Margin (as reported) of $13,109.9 million for the quarter.

### Market implication
The significant increase in net income and diluted earnings per share indicates strong financial performance and positive investor sentiment for Eli Lilly.
