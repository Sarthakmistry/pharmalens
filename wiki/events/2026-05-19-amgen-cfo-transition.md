## Amgen announces CFO transition with Peter H. Griffith's retirement and Thomas Dittrich's appointment

**Date:** 2026-05-19 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Amgen announced the retirement of its Chief Financial Officer, Peter H. Griffith, effective August 31, 2026. Thomas Dittrich has been appointed as his successor, effective September 1, 2026, following an initial period as Executive Vice President starting July 1, 2026. Mr. Griffith will remain with the company in a non-executive role until January 31, 2027, to ensure a smooth transition.

### Market implication
The planned and transitional change in Amgen's CFO role is a neutral event for the company's market valuation, as it represents a standard executive succession.
