## Johnson & Johnson Reports Q3 2025 Earnings with Strong Oncology Growth Offset by STELARA Decline

**Date:** 2025-10-22 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported worldwide sales of $69.6 billion for the fiscal nine months of 2025, an operational increase of 4.7%, driven by strong growth in Oncology and Neuroscience. This growth was significantly offset by a 39.5% operational decline in STELARA sales due to biosimilar competition and Medicare Part D redesign.

### Market implication
The company's overall performance reflects a balanced outlook, with robust growth in key therapeutic areas like Oncology and Neuroscience counteracting the significant revenue decline from STELARA due to increased biosimilar competition and policy changes.
