## Roche's Phase 2 Trial NCT04225715 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-07-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04225715, which investigated multiple agents including Nucleos(t)ide (nuc), Cpam (ro7049389), Tlr7 (ro7020531), Sirna (ro7445482), Peg-ifn, and Pd-l1 lna (ro7191863), reached its primary completion date on July 19, 2024. The trial successfully enrolled 281 participants and has reported results, marking a significant milestone in the development of these compounds.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Roche's pipeline, with further analysis of the reported results expected to inform future development decisions.
