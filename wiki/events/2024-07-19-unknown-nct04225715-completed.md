## Roche's Phase 2 Trial NCT04225715 for Chronic Hepatitis B Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2024-07-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 2, multicenter, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study (NCT04225715) evaluating the efficacy and safety of multiple investigational compounds (Cpam (ro7049389), Tlr7 (ro7020531), Sirna (ro7445482), and Pd-l1 lna (ro7191863)) in combination with Nucleos(t)ide analogs and Peg-ifn for patients with chronic Hepatitis B infection, completed its primary phase on July 19, 2024. The trial enrolled 281 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural update and has a neutral market implication for Roche, pending the release and analysis of the study results.
