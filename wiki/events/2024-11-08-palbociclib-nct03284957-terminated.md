## Phase 1/2 trial of palbociclib (NCT03284957) terminated

**Date:** 2024-11-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03284957) investigating palbociclib in combination with Amcenestrant, Alpelisib, or Everolimus for advanced breast cancer, sponsored by Sanofi, was terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 136 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 8, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a setback for the specific combination therapies being investigated, indicating a potential lack of efficacy or safety signals that prevented further development in this context.
