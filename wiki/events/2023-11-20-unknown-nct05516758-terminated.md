## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial NCT05516758 for Peresolimab

**Date:** 2023-11-20 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05516758) for the drug Peresolimab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 491 participants, reached its primary completion on November 20, 2023, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Peresolimab could indicate a setback for Eli Lilly's pipeline in the targeted indication, potentially impacting future revenue projections for the drug.
