## Gilead's Bulevirtide Phase 3 Trial (NCT03852719) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-11-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03852719) for Bulevirtide, sponsored by [[gilead]], reached its primary completion date on November 26, 2020. The trial enrolled 150 participants and has reported results. This milestone indicates the completion of data collection for the primary outcome.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step in the development of Bulevirtide, moving it closer to potential regulatory submission, though specific results are needed for full market assessment.
