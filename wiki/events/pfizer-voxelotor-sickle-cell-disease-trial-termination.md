## Pfizer terminates Phase 2 trial of Voxelotor in pediatric Sickle Cell Disease

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 study (NCT02850406) evaluating Voxelotor (GBT440) in pediatric patients with Sickle Cell Disease has been terminated. The study aimed to evaluate the effect of Voxelotor (GBT440) in pediatric participants with Sickle Cell Disease across various age groups (6 months to 17 years) and had an actual enrollment of 147 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Voxelotor in pediatric Sickle Cell Disease is a negative signal for Pfizer's pipeline in this indication.
