## Bayer's Finerenone (NCT04435626) Phase 3 Trial in Heart Failure Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-05-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04435626, investigating finerenone for heart failure, reached its primary completion date on May 15, 2024. The trial enrolled 6016 participants and has reported results, marking a significant milestone in the drug's development for the hf-htn indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial moves finerenone closer to potential regulatory filings or label expansion, with future data readouts expected to influence its market position.
