## Finerenone Phase 3 Trial (NCT04435626) for HF-HTN Completed

**Date:** 2024-05-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04435626) investigating finerenone for heart failure and hypertension has reached its primary completion date on May 15, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 6016 participants, has results available, marking a significant milestone in the drug's development. This completion moves finerenone closer to potential regulatory review for this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial for finerenone indicates progress in Bayer's pipeline for HF-HTN, with future market impact dependent on the upcoming release of trial results.
