## AbbVie Updates 2025 Earnings Guidance Due to Significant IPR&D Expense

**Date:** 2026-01-07 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. updated its Q4 2025 and full-year 2025 adjusted diluted earnings per share guidance to reflect an unexpected $1.3 billion pre-tax acquired IPR&D and milestones expense. This expense resulted in an unfavorable impact of $0.71 to both GAAP and adjusted non-GAAP diluted earnings per share for Q4 2025.

### Market implication
This update is bearish for AbbVie as the significant, unexpected IPR&D expense of $1.3 billion for Q4 2025, leading to a $0.71 per share reduction, lowers the company's previously announced full-year 2025 earnings guidance.
