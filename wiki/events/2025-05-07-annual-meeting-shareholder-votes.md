## Bristol-Myers Squibb Announces Results of 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders

**Date:** 2025-05-07 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company announced the results of its Annual Meeting of Shareholders held on May 6, 2025. All 11 director nominees were elected, the advisory vote on executive compensation was approved, and Deloitte & Touche LLP was ratified as the independent registered public accounting firm. Two shareholder proposals, including one on Corporate Financial Sustainability, were not approved.

### Market implication
The outcomes reflect standard corporate governance, with management's proposals largely approved and shareholder proposals not approved, indicating no immediate market impact.
