## Nivolumab Phase 2 Trial for Chronic HBV Infection Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-01-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04891770) evaluating nivolumab in combination with Tenofovir alafenamide, Vir-2218, and Selgantolimod for Chronic Hepatitis B Virus (HBV) Infection reached its primary completion date on January 23, 2024. The study, sponsored by Gilead, enrolled 103 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides data for further evaluation of nivolumab in chronic HBV, offering potential insights for Bristol-Myers Squibb and Gilead's future pipeline decisions, though the specific outcomes are not detailed here.
