## Nivolumab Trial NCT04891770 Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-01-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04891770, investigating nivolumab in combination with other agents, has completed its primary endpoint on January 23, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Gilead Sciences, enrolled 103 participants, and results are reported to be available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a development milestone for nivolumab, with results now available for further evaluation.
