## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT03615326 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-03-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03615326, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with chemotherapy, has reached its primary completion date on March 20, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 738 participants, has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural milestone and does not immediately impact market valuation until results are publicly disclosed and analyzed.
