## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT03615326 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-03-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03615326, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy for gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma, reached its primary completion on March 20, 2024. The trial enrolled 738 participants, and results are available.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for [[merck]]'s [[pembrolizumab]] program, with market impact contingent on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
