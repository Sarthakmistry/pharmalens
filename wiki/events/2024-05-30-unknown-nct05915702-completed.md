## Bayer's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05915702) for Gadoquatrane (bay1747846) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05915702) sponsored by [[bayer]] for Gadoquatrane (bay1747846) has reached its primary completion date on May 30, 2024. The study, which enrolled 303 participants, compared Gadoquatrane (bay1747846) to Gadobutrol, Gadoterate meglumine, and Gadoteridol. Results for this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Bayer's Gadoquatrane (bay1747846), with no immediate market impact until results are disclosed.
