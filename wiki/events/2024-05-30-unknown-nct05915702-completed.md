## Bayer's Phase 3 Trial for Gadoquatrane (NCT05915702) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05915702) sponsored by [[bayer]], evaluating Gadoquatrane (bay1747846) against Gadobutrol, Gadoterate meglumine, and Gadoteridol, reached its primary completion on May 30, 2024. The study, which enrolled 303 participants, has not yet released its results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial for Gadoquatrane is a neutral event for Bayer, as no results have been disclosed to indicate efficacy or safety outcomes.
