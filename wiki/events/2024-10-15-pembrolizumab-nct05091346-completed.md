## Phase 1/2 trial NCT05091346 of pembrolizumab for colorectal cancer completed

**Date:** 2024-10-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT05091346, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with E7386 and Lenvatinib for colorectal cancer, reached its primary completion date on October 15, 2024. The study, sponsored by Eisai, enrolled 89 participants and has now completed. Results from this trial are anticipated to provide further insights into the efficacy and safety of this combination therapy.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a step forward in the development process for pembrolizumab in colorectal cancer, with results anticipated to inform future development and potential market positioning.
