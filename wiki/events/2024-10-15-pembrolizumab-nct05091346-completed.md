## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT05091346 Completed for Colorectal Cancer

**Date:** 2024-10-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT05091346, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with E7386 and Lenvatinib for colorectal cancer, reached its primary completion date on October 15, 2024. Sponsored by Eisai, the trial enrolled 89 participants and has reported results. This marks a milestone in the development pathway for this combination therapy.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 1/2 trial provides initial data for future development but has no immediate market impact without disclosed results.
