## Clinical Trial NCT05613088 for Morab-202 Completed

**Date:** 2024-06-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05613088, a Phase 2 study evaluating Morab-202 in combination with other agents (Paclitaxel, Pegylated liposomal doxorubicin (PLD), Topotecan), reached its primary completion date on June 17, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, enrolled 106 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
This completion marks a step in the clinical development process for the involved therapies, with no immediate market implications for Bristol-Myers Squibb given the untracked nature of the primary drug.
