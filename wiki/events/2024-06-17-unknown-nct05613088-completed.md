## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Phase 2 Trial NCT05613088 for Morab-202 Completes with Results

**Date:** 2024-06-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05613088) investigating Morab-202 in combination with Paclitaxel, Pegylated liposomal doxorubicin (PLD), or Topotecan has reached its primary completion date on June 17, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 106 participants, is now complete and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides data that will inform future development decisions for Morab-202, with market implications dependent on the eventual disclosure and nature of these results.
