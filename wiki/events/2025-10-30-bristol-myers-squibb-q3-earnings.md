## Bristol-Myers Squibb Reports Mixed Q3 2025 Financial Results

**Date:** 2025-10-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company announced its financial results for the third quarter of 2025, reporting mixed performance with significant GAAP earnings growth but a decline in Non-GAAP earnings. GAAP diluted EPS increased to $1.08 in Q3 2025 from $0.60 in Q3 2024, while Non-GAAP diluted EPS decreased to $1.63 from $1.80 over the same period. The company also improved its net debt position.

### Market implication
The mixed financial results, with GAAP EPS growth offset by a decline in Non-GAAP EPS, suggest a neutral short-term market reaction for Bristol-Myers Squibb.
