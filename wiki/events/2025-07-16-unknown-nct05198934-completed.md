## Amgen's Phase 3 Colorectal Cancer Trial (NCT05198934) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-07-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

Amgen's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05198934, investigating Sotorasib, Panitumumab, Trifluridine and tipiracil, and Regorafenib for colorectal cancer, reached its primary completion date on July 16, 2025. The study enrolled 160 participants. No results have been reported yet.
