## Nivolumab Trial NCT04704154 for Colorectal Cancer Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2023-03-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04704154, investigating nivolumab in combination with Regorafenib for advanced or metastatic colorectal cancer, reached its primary completion date on March 9, 2023. The trial enrolled 175 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as specific results are not yet available to assess the potential impact on nivolumab's market position or Bristol-Myers Squibb's valuation.
