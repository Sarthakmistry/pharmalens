## Copanlisib and Rituximab iNHL Trial (NCT02367040) Completed

**Date:** 2024-11-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The clinical trial NCT02367040, evaluating Copanlisib and Rituximab in relapsed Indolent B-cell Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma, has been completed. This study compared copanlisib in combination with rituximab against placebo plus rituximab in 458 patients.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides updated data for Bayer's Copanlisib and Rituximab combination in iNHL, which will inform future development or commercial strategies.
