## Sanofi's Pentavalent Meningococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT06128733) Completes Phase 1/2 Study

**Date:** 2025-08-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's clinical trial NCT06128733, investigating a pentavalent meningococcal ABCYW vaccine, has reached its primary completion date for its Phase 1/2 study. The trial, which enrolled 1215 participants, has concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 1/2 trial for Sanofi's meningococcal vaccine is a standard procedural step in drug development and has a neutral market implication at this stage, pending the release of clinical data.
