## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03072238 for Ipatasertib, Abiraterone, and Prednisone/prednisolone Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2020-03-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03072238, investigating Ipatasertib in combination with Abiraterone and Prednisone/prednisolone, reached its primary completion date on March 16, 2020. The study enrolled 1101 participants and is now marked as 'COMPLETED' with results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a progression in Roche's pipeline, moving closer to potential data readout for Ipatasertib in this indication.
