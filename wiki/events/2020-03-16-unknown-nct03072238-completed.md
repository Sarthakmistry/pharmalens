## Roche's Clinical Trial NCT03072238 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-03-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03072238, sponsored by Roche, reached its primary completion on March 16, 2020. This Phase 3 study enrolled 1101 participants and has reported results. The trial investigated Ipatasertib in combination with Abiraterone and Prednisone/prednisolone.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural milestone for Roche, with no immediate market impact without specific results.
