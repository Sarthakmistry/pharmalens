## Johnson & Johnson Reports Q1 Earnings and Announces 64th Consecutive Dividend Increase

**Date:** 2026-04-14 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced its first-quarter 2026 sales and earnings results. Concurrently, the company declared a 3.1% increase in its quarterly dividend, raising it from $1.30 per share to $1.34 per share. This marks the 64th consecutive year that Johnson & Johnson has increased its dividend.

### Market implication
The 3.1% increase in the quarterly dividend, marking 64 consecutive years of increases, signals strong financial health and commitment to shareholder returns for Johnson & Johnson, likely viewed positively by investors.
