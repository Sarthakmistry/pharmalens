## Nivolumab (Bristol-Myers Squibb) Phase 3 Trial NCT02477826 for NSCLC Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-10-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02477826) evaluating nivolumab, developed by Bristol-Myers Squibb, for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on October 25, 2024. The trial enrolled 2747 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial for nivolumab in NSCLC marks a procedural milestone, with market impact dependent on the eventual disclosure of the trial's results.
