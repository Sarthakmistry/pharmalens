## Merck terminates Phase 3 trial NCT04652700

**Date:** 2023-08-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Merck has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04652700, on August 4, 2023. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 494 participants, involved the drug candidates Isl, Ftc/tdf, and Ftc/taf. The primary completion date was also listed as August 4, 2023, and results are available.
