## AstraZeneca completes Phase 3 pharmacokinetic trial for Budesonide/albuterol MDI

**Date:** 2024-06-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca has completed a Phase 3 pharmacokinetic study (NCT06245551) for Budesonide/albuterol metered-dose inhaler 160/180 µg. The trial, which enrolled 64 healthy adult subjects, reached its primary completion date on June 29, 2024, and results are available. This study aimed to evaluate the drug's pharmacokinetic profile.

### Market implication
This trial completion is a standard procedural step for a pharmacokinetic study and is expected to have a neutral market impact.
