## Lademirsen (SAR339375) Alport Syndrome Trial Termination

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 study of lademirsen (SAR339375) in patients with Alport Syndrome, sponsored by Genzyme, a Sanofi Company, has been terminated. The study aimed to assess the efficacy of lademirsen in reducing renal function decline and its safety/tolerability.

### Market implication
The termination of the clinical trial for lademirsen (SAR339375) in Alport Syndrome indicates a halt in its development for this indication, which is a negative signal for Sanofi's pipeline in this area.
