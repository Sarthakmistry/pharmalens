## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05352815) for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-03-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05352815, on March 19, 2024. This study, which enrolled 1291 participants, investigated treatments including Icosema and Insulin icodec for type 2 diabetes. The trial has reached its primary completion, indicating that data collection for the primary outcome measure is finished and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard step in the drug development process for Novo Nordisk, with market impact dependent on future data release.
