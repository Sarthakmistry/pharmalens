## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 trial (NCT05352815) for Insulin Icodec in Type 2 Diabetes completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-03-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk has announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05352815) investigating Insulin Icodec (also referred to as Icosema) for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 1291 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 19, 2024, and results are now available. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for this once-weekly insulin analogue.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial is a necessary step towards potential regulatory submission for Insulin Icodec. While the completion itself is neutral, the availability of results will be crucial for assessing the drug's efficacy and safety profile, which will then determine its market potential and competitive positioning for Novo Nordisk.
