## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial NCT04180488 for Dupilumab sar231893 Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-08-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04180488, investigating Dupilumab sar231893 in combination with non-sedating H1-antihistamine for chronic spontaneous urticaria, reached its primary completion date on August 1, 2024. The study enrolled 397 participants and has reported results, which will be further analyzed.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event for Sanofi, as market impact will depend on the eventual disclosure and interpretation of the trial's results.
