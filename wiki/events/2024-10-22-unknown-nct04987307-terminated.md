## Amgen terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT04987307) for Efavaleukin alfa

**Date:** 2024-10-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Amgen has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04987307) investigating Efavaleukin alfa. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 221 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 22, 2024, before being terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Efavaleukin alfa represents a setback for Amgen's pipeline, potentially impacting future development plans for the drug.
