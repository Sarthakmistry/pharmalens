## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Trial NCT05255601 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-12-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05255601, a Phase 1/2 study investigating nivolumab in combination with Relatlimab, has reached its primary completion date on December 3, 2025. Sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, the trial had an actual enrollment of 5 participants. This milestone indicates that data collection for the primary outcome measures is complete.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this early-phase trial is a neutral event, as it signifies progress in the development pipeline but does not yet provide efficacy or safety data that would impact market valuation.
