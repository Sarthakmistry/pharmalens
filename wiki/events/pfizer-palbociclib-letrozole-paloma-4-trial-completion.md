## Pfizer's PALOMA-4 Trial for Palbociclib + Letrozole in Breast Cancer Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 PALOMA-4 trial (NCT02297438) for palbociclib in combination with Letrozole in ER+/HER2- advanced breast cancer has completed. The trial enrolled 340 patients and evaluated Progression-Free Survival (PFS) based on investigator's assessment as its primary outcome. Results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial represents a standard progression for a clinical study, with results now available for evaluation.
