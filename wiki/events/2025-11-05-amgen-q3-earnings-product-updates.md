## Amgen Q3 Earnings and Product Updates

**Date:** 2025-11-05 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Amgen reported strong financial results for the nine months ended September 30, 2025, with significant revenue and net income growth, alongside key product developments including FDA approvals for Repatha and TEZSPIRE, positive Phase 3 trial results for Repatha, and the termination of a Phase 1b/3 study for bemarituzumab.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, coupled with key FDA approvals and positive clinical trial results for Repatha and TEZSPIRE, indicates a positive outlook for Amgen's stock and valuation, despite some drugs facing Medicare price setting.
