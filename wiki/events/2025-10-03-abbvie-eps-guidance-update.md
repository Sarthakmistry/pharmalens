## AbbVie Revises 2025 EPS Guidance Downward Due to $2.68 Billion Acquired IPR&D Expense

**Date:** 2025-10-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie updated its Q3 and full-year 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance to reflect a $2.68 billion acquired IPR&D and milestones expense incurred in Q3 2025, resulting in an unfavorable $1.50 impact per share. This expense led to a revised Q3 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance range of $1.74 - $1.78 and a full-year 2025 range of $10.38 - $10.58.

### Market implication
This downward revision of EPS guidance is likely to be viewed negatively by investors, reflecting a significant one-time expense impacting profitability.
