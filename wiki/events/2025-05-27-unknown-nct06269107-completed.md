## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 trial (NCT06269107) for Icosema/Insulin glargine in Type 2 Diabetes completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-05-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT06269107, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating Icosema and Insulin glargine for Type 2 Diabetes, completed its primary endpoint on May 27, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 485 participants, has not yet released results.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural step in the drug development process for Novo Nordisk, with no immediate market impact as results are not yet available.
