## Regeneron Reports Q1 2025 Earnings with Increased Net Income and EPS

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals reported Q1 2025 total revenues of $3,028.7 million, a decrease from the prior year. Despite the revenue dip, net income increased to $808.7 million, and diluted earnings per share rose to $7.27, driven by strong performance in key products and recent approvals.

### Market implication
The increase in net income and diluted EPS, coupled with the declaration of the company's first quarterly cash dividend and ongoing share repurchase programs, suggests a positive outlook for Regeneron's financial performance.
