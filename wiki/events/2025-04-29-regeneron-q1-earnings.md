## Regeneron Reports Q1 2025 Financial Results with Increased Net Income and EPS

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. reported Q1 2025 total revenue of $3,028.7 million, a decrease from $3,145.0 million in Q1 2024. Despite the revenue dip, net income increased to $808.7 million from $722.0 million, and diluted EPS rose to $7.27 from $6.27 for the same period.

### Market implication
Despite a slight decrease in total revenue, the increase in net income and diluted EPS indicates a stable financial performance for Regeneron, suggesting a neutral market reaction.
