## Bayer's Phase 4 Trial NCT05036278 for Damoctocog alfa-pegol Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-09-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05036278, investigating Damoctocog alfa-pegol, reached its primary completion date on September 12, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 21 participants, has reported results. Damoctocog alfa-pegol is a recombinant b-domain deleted human coagulation fviii variant.

### Market implication
This routine trial completion for an existing drug is expected to have a neutral market impact.
