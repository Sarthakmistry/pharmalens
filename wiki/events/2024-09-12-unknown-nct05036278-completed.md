## Bayer's Phase 4 Trial NCT05036278 for Damoctocog alfa-pegol Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-09-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05036278) sponsored by Bayer, investigating Damoctocog alfa-pegol, reached its primary completion date on September 12, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 21 participants, has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development timeline for the drug.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides updated data on Damoctocog alfa-pegol, with market implications dependent on the eventual publication and interpretation of its results.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Damoctocog alfa-pegol is a recombinant b-domain deleted human coagulation fviii variant site specifically conjugated with a 60 kda, branched (30 kda each) polyethylene glycol (peg).
