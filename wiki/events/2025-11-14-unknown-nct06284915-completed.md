## Sanofi's Menacyw conjugate vaccine trial (NCT06284915) completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06284915) for Menacyw conjugate vaccine has reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2025. The trial enrolled 840 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
