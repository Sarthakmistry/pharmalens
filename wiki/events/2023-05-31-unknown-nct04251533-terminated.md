## Novartis Terminates Phase 3 Trial NCT04251533 for Alpelisib and Nab-paclitaxel in Breast Cancer

**Date:** 2023-05-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
On May 31, 2023, Novartis terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04251533) investigating Alpelisib in combination with Nab-paclitaxel for breast cancer. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 137 participants, reached its primary completion date on the same day it was terminated, indicating a halt in further development for this specific combination.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline in breast cancer with this specific drug combination, potentially impacting future market opportunities for Alpelisib and Nab-paclitaxel in this indication.
