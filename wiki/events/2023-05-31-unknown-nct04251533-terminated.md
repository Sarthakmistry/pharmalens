## Novartis terminates Phase 3 trial NCT04251533 for Alpelisib and Nab-paclitaxel in breast cancer

**Date:** 2023-05-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04251533) investigating Alpelisib and Nab-paclitaxel for breast cancer. The trial, which enrolled 137 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 31, 2023, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even after primary completion, suggests potential issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic pipeline decisions, which could negatively impact Novartis's oncology pipeline in breast cancer.
