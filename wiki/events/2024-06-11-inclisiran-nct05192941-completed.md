## Novartis' Inclisiran Trial NCT05192941 for Heart Failure Completed

**Date:** 2024-06-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05192941) investigating inclisiran, developed by Novartis, for heart failure has reached its primary completion date on June 11, 2024. The trial enrolled 1770 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial for inclisiran in heart failure indicates progress in its post-market evaluation, with potential for future data publication that could inform clinical practice.
