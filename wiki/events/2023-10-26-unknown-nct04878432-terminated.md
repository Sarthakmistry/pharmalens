## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04878432 for MBG453

**Date:** 2023-10-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04878432) investigating MBG453 in combination with azacitidine or decitabine for patients with myelodysplastic syndromes (MDS) or acute myeloid leukemia (AML). The trial reached its primary completion date on October 26, 2023, with 39 actual participants. The reason for termination was not specified in the provided information.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for an untracked drug like MBG453 is unlikely to have a significant immediate impact on Novartis's overall market valuation.
