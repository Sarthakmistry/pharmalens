## Roche's Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT04471428) for NSCLC Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-09-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04471428) investigating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with Cabozantinib and Docetaxel for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion on September 28, 2022. The trial enrolled 366 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential regulatory submissions and future market availability for [[atezolizumab]] in NSCLC, pending the full disclosure and analysis of the reported results.
