## Roche's Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT04471428) for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04471428), investigating atezolizumab in combination with Cabozantinib and Docetaxel for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on September 28, 2022. The trial successfully enrolled 366 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential regulatory submissions for atezolizumab in NSCLC, with results now available for evaluation.
