## Novartis Terminates Phase 2 Trial NCT04150029

**Date:** 2024-10-25 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04150029, which was investigating the drug MBG453 in combination with venetoclax and azacitidine. The trial had an actual enrollment of 90 participants and reached its primary completion date on October 25, 2024, with results now available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, potentially impacting the future development strategy for MBG453.

- Drug: MBG453, Venetoclax, Azacitidine
