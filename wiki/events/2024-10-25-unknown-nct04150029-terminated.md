## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04150029 for Mbg453, Venetoclax, and Azacitidine

**Date:** 2024-10-25 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04150029) investigating the combination of Mbg453, Venetoclax, and Azacitidine was terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 90 participants and reached its primary completion date on October 25, 2024. The reason for termination was not specified in the provided information.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline in the area targeted by Mbg453, Venetoclax, and Azacitidine, potentially impacting future development plans for this combination.
