## Eli Lilly Reports Strong Q3 2025 Earnings Driven by Mounjaro and Zepbound Growth, Advances Key Pipeline Assets

**Date:** 2025-10-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly reported strong financial results for the nine months ended September 30, 2025, with significant revenue and net income growth driven primarily by Mounjaro and Zepbound. The company also announced numerous positive pipeline advancements and regulatory approvals for other key assets, including tirzepatide, orforglipron, imlunestrant, donanemab, and pirtobrutinib.

### Market implication
The strong revenue growth, particularly from [[tirzepatide]] (Mounjaro and Zepbound), and positive pipeline developments across multiple therapeutic areas suggest a bullish outlook for Eli Lilly's valuation.
