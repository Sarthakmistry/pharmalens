## Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. Announces Results of 2025 Annual Shareholder Meeting

**Date:** 2025-06-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. held its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders, where directors were elected, the independent registered public accounting firm was ratified, executive compensation was approved on an advisory basis, and an amendment to declassify the board was approved. However, two amendments to eliminate supermajority vote requirements were not approved by shareholders.

### Market implication
As the reported corporate governance actions and voting results are routine, this event is not expected to significantly impact Regeneron's market valuation.
