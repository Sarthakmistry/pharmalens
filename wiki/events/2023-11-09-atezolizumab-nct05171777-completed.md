## Atezolizumab (NCT05171777) Phase 2 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2023-11-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05171777) investigating atezolizumab for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on November 9, 2023. The trial, sponsored by Roche, enrolled 179 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development for atezolizumab, with market implications dependent on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
