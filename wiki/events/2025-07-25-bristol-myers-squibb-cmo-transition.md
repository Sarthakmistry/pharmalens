## Bristol Myers Squibb Announces Chief Medical Officer Transition

**Date:** 2025-07-25 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced a leadership transition, with Dr. Samit Hirawat stepping down as Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development, and Dr. Cristian Massacesi appointed to the role, both effective August 1, 2025. Dr. Hirawat will transition to an advisory role before leaving the company by November 1, 2025.

### Market implication
This leadership change is presented as a standard transition, with the company highlighting the extensive experience of the new appointee, suggesting a neutral market impact.
