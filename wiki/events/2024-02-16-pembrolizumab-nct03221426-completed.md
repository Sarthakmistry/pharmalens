## Merck's Pembrolizumab (KEYNOTE-811) Phase 3 Trial for Gastric/GEJ Adenocarcinoma Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-02-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial KEYNOTE-811 (NCT03221426), evaluating Merck's [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy (Cisplatin and 5-fluorouracil or Capecitabine) for participants with gastric or gastroesophageal junction (GEJ) adenocarcinoma, reached its primary completion date on February 16, 2024. The trial enrolled 1007 participants and has reported having results, though specific outcomes are not yet detailed.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential new data for [[pembrolizumab]] in gastric/GEJ adenocarcinoma, which could influence future treatment paradigms and market share for [[merck]].
