## Biogen terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT05265728) for Natalizumab

**Date:** 2024-01-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Biogen has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05265728, which was investigating Natalizumab. The trial reached primary completion on January 18, 2024, with 21 actual participants, and results are available. The termination of a Phase 3 trial suggests a potential setback for the drug's development in the studied indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Natalizumab is a bearish signal for Biogen, indicating a potential loss of future revenue streams or increased R&D costs without a successful outcome.
