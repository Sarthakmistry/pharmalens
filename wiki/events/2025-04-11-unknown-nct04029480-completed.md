## Merck's Phase 3 Trial NCT04029480 for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2025-04-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04029480), investigating Ertugliflozin 5 mg and 15 mg in combination with Insulin and Metformin for Type 2 Diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on April 11, 2025. The study enrolled 166 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides an update on Merck's pipeline in Type 2 Diabetes, with further implications awaiting the release of detailed results.
