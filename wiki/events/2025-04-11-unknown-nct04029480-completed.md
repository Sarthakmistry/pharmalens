## Merck's NCT04029480 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2025-04-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04029480, sponsored by Merck and investigating Ertugliflozin 5 mg and Ertugliflozin 15 mg in combination with Insulin and Metformin for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on April 11, 2025. The trial enrolled 166 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial means that data regarding the efficacy and safety of Ertugliflozin in this patient population is now available for analysis, which will inform future regulatory and commercial decisions.
