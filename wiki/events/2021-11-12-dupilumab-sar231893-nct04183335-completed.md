## Sanofi's Dupilumab sar231893 Phase 3 Trial (NCT04183335) Completes with Positive Results

**Date:** 2021-11-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
A Phase 3 trial (NCT04183335) for Dupilumab sar231893, sponsored by [[sanofi]], has completed, demonstrating nominally significant improvements in signs, symptoms, and quality of life in patients with prurigo nodularis (PN). The trial results support the benefits of continued treatment with Dupilumab sar231893.

### Market implication
This positive trial completion for Dupilumab sar231893 could be a positive indicator for Sanofi's pipeline in dermatology.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Dupilumab sar231893
- Company: [[sanofi]]

### Clinical evidence
- **Trial ID:** NCT04183335
- **Phase:** PHASE3
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 243
- **Comparator:** placebo
- **Primary outcome:** ≥ 9-point reduction from baseline in Dermatology Life Quality Index (DLQI) score
- **Primary result:** 61.1% achieved a ≥ 9-point reduction from baseline in DLQI score (nominal P vs placebo significant)
- **Secondary results:**
    - 55.8% achieved a PGIS score of "none" or "mild"
    - 53.7% achieved PAS-75
    - 45.7% achieved the less stringent itch and skin lesion composite endpoint (nominal P vs placebo significant)
- **Safety:** None
- **Conclusions:** Dupilumab treatment versus placebo resulted in nominally significant improvements in signs, symptoms, and quality of life in patients with PN without optimal response by week 24, supporting the benefits of continued treatment. These results highlight the need to develop a treat-to-target strategy for the long-term management of PN.
- **Journal:** Dermatol Ther (Heidelb), 2026
- **Industry sponsored:** None
