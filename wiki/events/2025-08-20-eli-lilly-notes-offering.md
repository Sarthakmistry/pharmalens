## Eli Lilly Announces $6.75 Billion Notes Offering

**Date:** 2025-08-20 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company entered into an underwriting agreement on August 18, 2025, for the issuance and sale of $750,000,000 aggregate principal amount of Floating Rate Notes due 2028 and $6,000,000,000 aggregate principal amount of various Fixed Rate Notes. The Fixed Rate Notes include several series maturing in 2028, 2031, 2032, 2035, 2055, and 2065. The offering was registered on a Registration Statement on Form S-3.

### Market implication
The capital raise through the issuance of various notes is a standard corporate financing activity and is viewed as a neutral event for the company's valuation.
