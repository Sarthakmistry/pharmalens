## Eli Lilly Announces $6.75 Billion Notes Offering

**Date:** 2025-08-20 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company entered into an underwriting agreement on August 18, 2025, for the issuance and sale of $6.75 billion aggregate principal amount of various notes. This includes $750 million in Floating Rate Notes due 2028 and $6 billion in Fixed Rate Notes with maturities ranging from 2028 to 2065.

### Market implication
