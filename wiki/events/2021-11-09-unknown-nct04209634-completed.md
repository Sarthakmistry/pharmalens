## Regeneron's Phase 2/3 Trial (NCT04209634) for Pozelimab Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-11-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04209634) for Pozelimab, sponsored by Regeneron, reached its primary completion date on November 9, 2021. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 10 participants, has results available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of a clinical trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and typically has a neutral market implication unless specific results are announced.
