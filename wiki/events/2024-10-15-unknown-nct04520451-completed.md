## Sanofi's Phase 2 Trial of Rilzabrutinib (NCT04520451) Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04520451) investigating Rilzabrutinib, with Glucocorticoids as a comparator, reached its primary completion date on October 15, 2024. The trial enrolled 27 participants and is now awaiting results.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released yet to indicate efficacy or safety.
