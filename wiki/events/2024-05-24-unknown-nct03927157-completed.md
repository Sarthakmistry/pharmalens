## AstraZeneca's tezepelumab trial NCT03927157 completes primary phase

**Date:** 2024-05-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03927157, sponsored by AstraZeneca and evaluating tezepelumab, has reached its primary completion date on May 24, 2024. This Phase 3 study, which enrolled 405 participants, has now concluded its primary data collection, indicating that results are expected to be analyzed and reported.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a key development milestone for tezepelumab, moving it closer to potential data readout and subsequent regulatory filings, which could influence AstraZeneca's future pipeline.
