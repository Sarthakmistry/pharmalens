## AbbVie Reports Strong Q1 2025 Results Driven by Skyrizi and Rinvoq Growth, Advances Pipeline

**Date:** 2025-05-09 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported strong first-quarter 2025 financial results with worldwide net revenues of $13.3 billion, an 8% increase on a reported basis, driven by significant growth in Skyrizi and Rinvoq. The company also highlighted key pipeline advancements, including new approvals and BLA submissions, and its entry into the obesity field through a partnership with Gubra.

### Market implication
The strong performance of key growth drivers Skyrizi and Rinvoq, coupled with strategic pipeline advancements and entry into new therapeutic areas like obesity, suggests a positive outlook for AbbVie's future revenue and market position.
