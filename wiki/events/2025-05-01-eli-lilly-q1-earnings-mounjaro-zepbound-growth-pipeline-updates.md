## Eli Lilly Q1 2025 Earnings: Mounjaro & Zepbound Drive Growth, Pipeline Updates

**Date:** 2025-05-01 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly reported strong Q1 2025 financial results with a 45% revenue increase driven primarily by Mounjaro and Zepbound. The company also announced significant pipeline developments, including positive Phase 3 data for oral GLP-1 orforglipron in type 2 diabetes and regulatory approvals for Omvoh and Jaypirca. However, there were setbacks with donanemab receiving a negative CHMP opinion in Europe and the withdrawal of tirzepatide's U.S. application for heart failure with preserved ejection fraction.

### Market implication
The strong performance of key growth drivers Mounjaro and Zepbound, coupled with positive pipeline advancements, suggests continued robust financial growth and a positive outlook for Eli Lilly's valuation despite some regulatory hurdles for other assets.
