## Trial Completion: Acetaminophen/Naproxen Sodium for Postoperative Dental Pain

**Date:** 2024-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05761574, evaluating an acetaminophen/naproxen sodium fixed combination for postoperative dental pain, has completed. The study compared the fixed combination against acetaminophen, naproxen sodium, and placebo. Results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a neutral event as results are not yet available to assess potential market impact for Johnson & Johnson.

### Affected entities
- Drug: acetaminophen, naproxen sodium
- Company: [[johnson-and-johnson]]
- Indication: Pain

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 447
- **Comparator:** acetaminophen, naproxen sodium and placebo
- **Primary outcome:** Time-weighted Sum of Pain Intensity Difference from 0 to 12 Hours After Dosing (SPID 0-12)
- **Primary result:** None
- **Secondary results:** None
- **Safety:** None
- **Conclusions (verbatim):** None
