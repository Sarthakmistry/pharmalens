## AstraZeneca's Baxdrostat Phase 3 Trial for Hypertension Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-05-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06034743) for Baxdrostat, targeting hypertension, has reached its primary completion date on May 21, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 796 participants, is evaluating the drug's efficacy in heart failure and hypertension. Results from this study are not yet available.
