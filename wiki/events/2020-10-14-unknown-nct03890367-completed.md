## Sanofi's Phase 3 Meningococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT03890367) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2020-10-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03890367) sponsored by [[sanofi]], investigating meningococcal polysaccharide conjugate vaccines, reached its primary completion date on October 14, 2020. The study enrolled 707 participants and has reported results. The trial evaluated Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, y, and w) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine, Meningococcal polysaccharide group a, c, w-135 and y conjugate vaccine, and Meningococcal group c polysaccharide conjugate vaccine adsorbed.

### Market implication
This milestone represents a standard progression in Sanofi's vaccine development pipeline, with market impact contingent on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial's results.
