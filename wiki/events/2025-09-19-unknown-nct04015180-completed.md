## Bayer's Trial NCT04015180 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04015180, sponsored by Bayer, reached its primary completion date on September 19, 2025. This Phase 3 study, which enrolled 100 participants, has completed its primary data collection, though results have not yet been posted. The trial investigated Eylea (aflibercept, bay86-5321) and Laser photocoagulation.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for Bayer's trial NCT04015180, with no immediate market impact as results are not yet available.
