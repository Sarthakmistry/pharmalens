## Bayer's Trial NCT04015180 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04015180, which investigated Eylea (aflibercept, bay86-5321) and Laser photocoagulation, reached its primary completion on September 19, 2025. The trial enrolled 100 participants and no results have been posted yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a procedural milestone for Bayer, with market implications pending the release of trial results.
