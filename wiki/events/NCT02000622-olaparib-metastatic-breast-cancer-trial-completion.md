## Olaparib Metastatic Breast Cancer Trial (NCT02000622) Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase III study (NCT02000622) assessing Olaparib monotherapy versus physician's choice chemotherapy for metastatic breast cancer in gBRCA1/2 mutation patients has completed. This open-label, randomised, controlled, multi-centre study compared olaparib against capecitabine, vinorelbine, or eribulin. The primary outcome measure was Progression-free Survival (PFS) using Blinded Independent Central Review (BICR).

### Market implication
This trial completion provides an update on the development timeline for olaparib in metastatic breast cancer, with results pending.

- Sample size: 302 patients
