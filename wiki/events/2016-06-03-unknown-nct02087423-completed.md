## AstraZeneca's Phase 2 Trial for Medi4736 in NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2016-06-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02087423) investigating Medi4736 for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on June 3, 2016. The trial enrolled 446 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on AstraZeneca's pipeline development for Medi4736 in oncology, with results now available for evaluation.
