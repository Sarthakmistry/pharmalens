## Takeda's Pevonedistat Trial NCT04266795 Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04266795), investigating Pevonedistat in combination with Venetoclax and Azacitidine, reached primary completion on September 6, 2022. The trial enrolled 164 participants, and results from this study are now available.
