## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) trial NCT03924856 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2026-01-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03924856, evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with Gemcitabine and Cisplatin, has reached its primary completion date on January 26, 2026. This Phase 3 study, sponsored by Merck, enrolled 907 participants. No results from the trial have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of the primary endpoint is a procedural milestone for the trial and does not carry immediate market implications until the study results are announced.
