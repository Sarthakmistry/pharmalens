**Date:** 2025-12-04 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck & Co., Inc. successfully closed an underwritten public offering of $8 billion aggregate principal amount of various notes on December 4, 2025. This financial transaction included Floating Rate Notes due 2029 and fixed-rate notes with maturities ranging from 2029 to 2065.
