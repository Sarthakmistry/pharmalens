## AstraZeneca's Brazikumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT03961815) Terminated

**Date:** 2023-09-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03961815) investigating Brazikumab induction and maintenance doses, sponsored by AstraZeneca, was terminated on September 19, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 18 participants and has reported results.
