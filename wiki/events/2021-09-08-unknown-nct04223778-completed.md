## Merck's Phase 3 Trial NCT04223778 for Dor/isl and Art Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-09-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04223778, investigating the drugs Dor/isl and Art, reached its primary completion date on September 8, 2021. The trial successfully enrolled 672 participants and has reported results, indicating the conclusion of data collection for its primary endpoints.

### Market implication
This event signifies the completion of the primary data collection phase for the trial, making results available for analysis and informing Merck's future development and regulatory strategies for Dor/isl and Art.
