## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 trial NCT04609904 completed primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-03-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04609904, reached its primary completion date on March 20, 2025. This trial enrolled 2187 participants and investigated Bgf mdi 320/28.8/9.6 µg, Bgf mdi 320/14.4/9.6 µg, Bff mdi 320/9.6 µg, and Bff pmdi 320/9 µg. No results from the trial have been released yet.

### Market implication
Completion of the trial's primary endpoint is a standard procedural step; market impact will depend on future data release.
