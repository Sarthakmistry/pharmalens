## Nilotinib CML Treatment-Free Remission Trial Completion

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 study investigating the safety of suspending nilotinib treatment in CML patients who achieved optimal response, with the primary outcome measuring the percentage of patients in Major Molecular Response (MMR) at 48 weeks post-treatment-free remission. The trial was sponsored by Novartis Pharmaceuticals and completed in 2016.
