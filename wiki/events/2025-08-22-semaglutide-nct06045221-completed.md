## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial (NCT06045221) for Semaglutide in Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06045221) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating semaglutide for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on August 22, 2025. The trial enrolled 1698 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. Results for this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Eli Lilly's development of semaglutide in Type 2 Diabetes, with market impact remaining neutral until results are disclosed.
