## Eli Lilly-Sponsored Phase 3 Trial Involving Semaglutide and Orforglipron for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2025-08-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06045221) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating [[semaglutide]] and Orforglipron for the treatment of type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on August 22, 2025. The trial enrolled 1698 participants and has not yet released results.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a step towards potential future data readout for [[semaglutide]] and Orforglipron in [[type2-diabetes]], with no immediate market impact until results are available.
