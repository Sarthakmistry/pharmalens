## Novartis Completes Phase 2 Trial NCT03663335 for Kidney Transplant Rejection

**Date:** 2021-10-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03663335) investigating Cfz533 in combination with mycophenolate mofetil (MMF) and corticosteroids (CS) for the prevention of acute rejection in adult kidney transplant recipients. The study, which enrolled 418 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 29, 2021, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides data for Novartis's pipeline, with further analysis needed to determine its potential impact on future development and market strategy.
