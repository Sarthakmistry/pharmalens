## Merck Reports Q1 2025 Earnings: Keytruda Drives Oncology Growth Amidst Vaccine Declines and IRA Pressures

**Date:** 2025-05-02 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck reported Q1 2025 worldwide sales of $15.5 billion, a 2% decrease year-over-year, driven by declines in vaccines, virology, and immunology, partially offset by growth in oncology, cardiovascular, and diabetes. The company also engaged in significant business development activities, including the acquisition of HRS-5346 rights and a manufacturing facility, while facing regulatory pressures from the IRA.

### Market implication
Merck's Q1 2025 results show a mixed performance with strong oncology growth, particularly from [[pembrolizumab]], being tempered by declines in other segments and the anticipated impact of the IRA's Drug Price Negotiation Program on diabetes drugs like [[sitagliptin]], leading to a neutral overall market signal.
