## Merck Reports Strong Q4 2025 Earnings with Keytruda Growth and New Product Uptake

**Date:** 2026-02-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Merck announced its fourth-quarter and full-year 2025 financial results, highlighting strong performance in oncology and animal health. The company saw significant uptake for new products WINREVAIR (sotatercept) and CAPVAXIVE (pneumococcal vaccine), while facing declines in older products like LAGEVRIO (molnupiravir) and JANUVIA/JANUMET (sitagliptin) due to reduced demand and generic competition. Worldwide sales for Q4 were $16.4 billion, a 5% increase, driven by strong global demand for [[pembrolizumab]] (KEYTRUDA) which grew 19% to $7.0 billion.

### Market implication
The strong performance in key growth areas, particularly oncology, and successful new product launches are expected to positively impact Merck's valuation, despite some declines in mature products.
