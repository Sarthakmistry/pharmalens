## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial NCT03915951 for Encorafenib and Binimetinib in NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03915951), investigating Encorafenib and Binimetinib for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on September 22, 2022. The trial enrolled 98 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides updated data for Pfizer's pipeline in oncology, with results now available for evaluation.
