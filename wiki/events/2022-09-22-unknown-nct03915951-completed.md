## Pfizer's NCT03915951 Trial for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03915951) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating Encorafenib and Binimetinib for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion on September 22, 2022. The trial enrolled 98 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial for non-tracked drugs by Pfizer is a routine pipeline update and has a neutral market implication without specific efficacy or safety data.
