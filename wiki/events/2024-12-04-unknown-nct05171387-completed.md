## Bayer's Phase 2 Trial NCT05171387 Completed

**Date:** 2024-12-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05171387, which investigated Darolutamide 600 mg twice daily and Androgen deprivation therapy (adt), reached its primary completion on December 4, 2024. The study enrolled 78 participants and is now officially completed, though results have not yet been published.

### Market implication
This event represents a procedural milestone for Bayer's Phase 2 trial, with no immediate market impact as specific clinical results have not been released.
