## Bayer's Phase 2 trial NCT05171387 for prostate cancer completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-12-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05171387, investigating Darolutamide 600 mg twice daily with Androgen deprivation therapy (adt) in prostate cancer, has completed its primary endpoint on December 4, 2024. The trial enrolled 78 participants. No results have been released yet.
