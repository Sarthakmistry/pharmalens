## Johnson & Johnson Annual Meeting Results: Directors Elected, Executive Compensation Approved, Independent Board Chair Proposal Not Approved

**Date:** 2026-04-28 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the results of its 2026 Annual Meeting of Shareholders, where all 12 director nominees were elected, executive compensation was approved on an advisory basis, and the independent auditor was ratified. A shareholder proposal advocating for a policy requiring an independent board chair was not approved by shareholders.

### Market implication
The outcomes reflect a generally supportive stance from shareholders towards current management and governance, with no major changes to the company's strategic direction indicated.
