**Date:** 2025-11-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

AbbVie's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05291234, investigating an undisclosed drug for Alzheimer's disease, reached its primary completion date on November 19, 2025. The trial enrolled 106 participants. No results have been reported yet for this study.
