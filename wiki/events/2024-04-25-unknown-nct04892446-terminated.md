## Gilead's Phase 2 trial NCT04892446 for multiple myeloma terminated

**Date:** 2024-04-25 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04892446) investigating Magrolimab in combination with Daratumumab, Pomalidomide, Dexamethasone, Bortezomib, and Carfilzomib for multiple myeloma was terminated on April 25, 2024. The trial had an actual enrollment of 36 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Gilead's pipeline in multiple myeloma, potentially impacting future revenue projections for the involved compounds.
