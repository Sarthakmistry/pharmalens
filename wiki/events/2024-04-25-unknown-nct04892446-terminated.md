## Gilead terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04892446 for Magrolimab

**Date:** 2024-04-25 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04892446, sponsored by Gilead and investigating Magrolimab in combination therapies (including Daratumumab, Pomalidomide, Dexamethasone, Bortezomib, and Carfilzomib), was terminated. The Phase 2 study, which had an actual enrollment of 36 participants, reached its primary completion on April 25, 2024.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial, especially in Phase 2, represents a setback for Gilead's pipeline, potentially impacting future development plans for Magrolimab.
