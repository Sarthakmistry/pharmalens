## Sanofi Completes Phase 4 Clinical Trial NCT05054387

**Date:** 2023-03-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has completed its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05054387, which investigated Agalsidase beta. The trial reached its primary completion date on March 9, 2023, with an actual enrollment of 22 participants. Results for this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a neutral event for Sanofi, as no results have been disclosed to indicate potential success or failure of the therapy.
