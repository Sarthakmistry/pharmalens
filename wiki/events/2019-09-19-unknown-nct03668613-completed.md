## Novartis's NCT03668613 Trial for Secukinumab Completed

**Date:** 2019-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03668613, which investigated Secukinumab (low and high dose), reached its primary completion date on September 19, 2019. The study enrolled 84 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for Secukinumab.

### Market implication
This event marks the completion of a clinical trial for Novartis, providing data that will inform future development decisions.
