## Novartis's NCT03668613 Phase 3 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2019-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03668613, which investigated Secukinumab (low and high dose), reached its primary completion date on September 19, 2019. The study enrolled 84 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial indicates that data should be available for analysis, which could inform future development or regulatory submissions for Secukinumab.
