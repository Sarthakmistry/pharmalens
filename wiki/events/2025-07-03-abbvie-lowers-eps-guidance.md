## AbbVie Lowers EPS Guidance Due to Unexpected Acquired IPR&D and Milestones Expense

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie updated its Q2 and full-year 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance, lowering it due to an unexpected $823 million acquired IPR&D and milestones expense in the second quarter. This expense had an unfavorable impact of $0.42 per share on both GAAP and adjusted non-GAAP diluted EPS for Q2 2025. The revised Q2 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance is now $2.84 - $2.88, and the full-year 2025 guidance is $11.67 - $11.87, down from the previous $12.09 - $12.29.

### Market implication
The reduction in EPS guidance due to significant unexpected expenses suggests a negative short-term outlook for AbbVie's financial performance, potentially impacting investor sentiment.
