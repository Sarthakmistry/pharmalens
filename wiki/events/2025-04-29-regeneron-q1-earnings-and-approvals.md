## Regeneron Q1 Earnings and Approvals

**Date:** 2025-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals reported strong first quarter 2025 financial results, with global net sales of Dupixent increasing by 19% to $3.67 billion. The company also announced key regulatory approvals, including Dupixent for chronic spontaneous urticaria (CSU) in the U.S. and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) in Japan, and Lynozyfic (linvoseltamab) in the EU for relapsed/refractory multiple myeloma. While EYLEA HD U.S. net sales grew significantly, total EYLEA and EYLEA HD U.S. net sales saw a decline.

### Market implication
The strong performance of Dupixent and new approvals for key products like Dupixent and Lynozyfic indicate positive momentum for Regeneron, despite the overall decline in EYLEA sales, suggesting a bullish outlook for the company's valuation.
