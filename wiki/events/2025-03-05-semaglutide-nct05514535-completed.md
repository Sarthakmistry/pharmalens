## Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT05514535) for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-03-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05514535) investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes and obesity has reached its primary completion date on March 5, 2025. The trial enrolled 568 participants and evaluated semaglutide in combination with insulin glargine U100. Results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for semaglutide, with market impact awaiting the release of study results.
