## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 VZV vaccine trial NCT05703607

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05703607) investigating multiple Varicella Zoster Virus (VZV) vaccine candidates (PF-07915234, PF-07921188, PF-07921186). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 485 participants, reached its primary completion date on September 19, 2025, but was subsequently terminated. No results have been reported.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a setback for Pfizer's VZV vaccine pipeline, potentially impacting its competitive position in the shingles vaccine market.
