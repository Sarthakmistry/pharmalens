## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 VZV vaccine trial (NCT05703607)

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05703607) investigating several Varicella Zoster Virus (VZV) vaccine candidates (PF-07915234, PF-07921188, PF-07921186). The trial had an actual enrollment of 485 participants and was initially expected to reach primary completion on September 19, 2025.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for Pfizer's VZV vaccine pipeline, potentially impacting future market opportunities in this therapeutic area.
