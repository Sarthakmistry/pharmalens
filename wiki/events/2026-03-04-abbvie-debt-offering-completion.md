## AbbVie Completes $8 Billion Senior Notes Offering

**Date:** 2026-03-04 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. completed an underwritten public offering of senior notes totaling $8 billion. This offering included $750 million in senior floating rate notes due 2028 and $7.25 billion in fixed rate senior notes with maturities ranging from 2028 to 2066. The notes are unsecured, unsubordinated obligations of AbbVie and rank equally with all existing and future unsecured, unsubordinated indebtedness.

### Market implication
The completion of this debt offering is a routine financial activity and does not immediately suggest a significant positive or negative impact on AbbVie's market valuation.
