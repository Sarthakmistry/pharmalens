## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03622580 for Aflibercept and Faricimab Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2020-10-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03622580, evaluating Aflibercept and Faricimab, reached its primary completion date on October 20, 2020. The study successfully enrolled 940 participants and has reported results, marking a key milestone in the development of these investigational therapies.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides essential data for the ongoing assessment and potential future regulatory submissions of Aflibercept and Faricimab, with market impact dependent on the disclosed results.
