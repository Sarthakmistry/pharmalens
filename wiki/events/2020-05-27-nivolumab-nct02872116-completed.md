## Nivolumab Trial NCT02872116 Completed

**Date:** 2020-05-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02872116) investigating nivolumab, sponsored by Bristol-Myers Squibb, reached its primary completion date on May 27, 2020. The trial enrolled 2031 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial provides additional data for nivolumab, which will inform its clinical profile and potential market positioning once the results are publicly disclosed.
