## Bristol-Myers Squibb Completes Phase 3 Trial NCT02872116 for Nivolumab

**Date:** 2020-05-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb announced the completion of the Phase 3 clinical trial NCT02872116 for [[nivolumab]] on May 27, 2020. This trial, which enrolled 2031 participants, has reached its primary completion date. The trial investigated nivolumab in combination with other agents, and results are expected to provide further insights into the drug's efficacy and safety profile.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a significant step towards potential regulatory submissions and future market opportunities for nivolumab.
