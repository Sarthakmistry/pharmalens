## Pfizer Revises 2025 Revenue Guidance and Provides 2026 Outlook

**Date:** 2025-12-16 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer reaffirmed its full-year 2025 Adjusted Diluted EPS guidance but revised its full-year 2025 Revenue guidance to approximately $62.0 billion. The company also provided full-year 2026 Revenue guidance of $59.5 to $62.5 billion and Adjusted Diluted EPS guidance of $2.80 to $3.00, indicating a potential decline in revenue for the upcoming year.

### Market implication
The revision of 2025 revenue guidance downwards and the projection of a potential revenue decline in 2026 suggests a bearish outlook for Pfizer's near-term financial performance.
