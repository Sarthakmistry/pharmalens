## Regeneron expects $102 million IPR&D charge in Q1 2026, impacting EPS by $0.81

**Date:** 2026-04-08 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. expects to report an acquired in-process research and development (IPR&D) charge of approximately $102 million pre-tax for the first quarter of 2026. This charge is primarily related to premium on equity securities purchased, as well as development milestone and up-front payments in connection with collaboration and licensing agreements. This is expected to negatively impact diluted EPS by $0.81.

### Market implication
The acquired IPR&D charge is expected to negatively impact both GAAP and non-GAAP net income per diluted share for the first quarter 2026 by approximately $0.81, indicating a bearish financial outlook for the quarter.
