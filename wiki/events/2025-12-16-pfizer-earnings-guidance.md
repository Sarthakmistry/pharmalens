## Pfizer Reaffirms 2025 EPS, Revises 2025 Revenue, and Provides 2026 Financial Guidance

**Date:** 2025-12-16 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer Inc. reaffirmed its full-year 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance while revising its full-year 2025 revenue guidance to approximately $62.0 billion. The company also provided its full-year 2026 revenue guidance ranging from $59.5 billion to $62.5 billion and adjusted diluted EPS guidance between $2.80 and $3.00, indicating continued investment in its pipeline and acquired assets.

### Market implication
The reaffirmation of 2025 EPS guidance and provision of 2026 guidance, alongside a revised 2025 revenue outlook, suggests a stable but evolving financial trajectory for Pfizer.
