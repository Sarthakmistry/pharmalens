## Pfizer's Phase 3 Trial for RSV Prefusion F Subunit Vaccine (NCT06890416) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT06890416, evaluating a Respiratory syncytial virus prefusion f subunit vaccine, has reached its primary completion date on September 30, 2025. The study, sponsored by Pfizer, involved an actual enrollment of 526 participants and is now awaiting results.

### Market implication
The market implication is neutral as the trial has completed its primary phase, but results are not yet available to assess efficacy or safety.
