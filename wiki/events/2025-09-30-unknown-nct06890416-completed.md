## Pfizer's RSV vaccine trial NCT06890416 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06890416) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating a Respiratory syncytial virus prefusion f subunit vaccine, reached its primary completion date on September 30, 2025. The study enrolled 526 participants and is now awaiting results.

### Market implication
The completion of the primary endpoint for this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step, with market impact contingent upon the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
