## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05592275) for Ly3540378 in heart failure

**Date:** 2025-01-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05592275) investigating the drug Ly3540378 for heart failure. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 332 participants, reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2025, and results are available. The reason for termination was not specified in the provided information.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Ly3540378 represents a setback for Eli Lilly's pipeline in the heart failure indication.
