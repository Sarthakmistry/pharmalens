## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial for Alzheimer's Drug (NCT05463731) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-04-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05463731) investigating Remternetug (iv) and Remternetug (sc) for Alzheimer's disease has reached its primary completion date on April 24, 2024. The trial enrolled 1667 participants, and results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this large Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Eli Lilly's Alzheimer's pipeline, with market impact awaiting the release of clinical data.
