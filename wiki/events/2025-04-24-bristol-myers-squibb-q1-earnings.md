## Bristol-Myers Squibb Reports Significant Improvement in Q1 2025 Earnings

**Date:** 2025-04-24 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company announced its first-quarter 2025 financial results, reporting a significant improvement from a net loss in the prior year to positive net earnings and diluted EPS. Q1 2025 GAAP Diluted EPS was $1.20, a significant improvement from a loss of $(5.89) in Q1 2024, with GAAP Net Earnings reaching $2,456 million, up from a net loss of $(11,911) million in the prior year.

### Market implication
This indicates a bullish outlook for Bristol-Myers Squibb, as net earnings attributable to BMS used for diluted EPS calculation improved from a loss of $(11,911) million (GAAP) in Q1 2024 to earnings of $2,456 million (GAAP) in Q1 2025.
