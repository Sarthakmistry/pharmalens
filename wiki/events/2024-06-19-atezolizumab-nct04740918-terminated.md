## Roche's atezolizumab Phase 3 trial (NCT04740918) for breast cancer terminated

**Date:** 2024-06-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04740918) investigating atezolizumab in combination with trastuzumab emtansine for HER2-positive metastatic breast cancer was terminated on June 19, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Roche, had enrolled 96 participants and reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for atezolizumab in a breast cancer indication represents a negative development for Roche's oncology pipeline and the drug's potential label expansion.
