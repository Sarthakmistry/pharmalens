## AstraZeneca's Phase 2 Trial for Cin-107 (NCT05432167) in HF-HTN Completed

**Date:** 2024-05-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca has announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05432167) for the drug Cin-107, targeting heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN). The trial, which enrolled 195 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 2, 2024, and results are now available. This marks a significant step in the development of Cin-107.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a procedural advancement for AstraZeneca's pipeline in HF-HTN, with future market implications dependent on the disclosed results.
