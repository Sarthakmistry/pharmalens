## AstraZeneca's Baxdrostat Phase 3 Trial for HF-HTN Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-08-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06168409) investigating Baxdrostat for heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN) reached its primary completion date on August 17, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 218 participants, has completed its data collection for the primary outcome measures.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as results are not yet available to assess the drug's efficacy or safety.
