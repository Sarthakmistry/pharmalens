## Takeda terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04501614 for Ponatinib

**Date:** 2023-12-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04501614) which was investigating Ponatinib in combination with chemotherapy agents. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 11 participants, reached its primary completion date on December 19, 2023, and has reported results. The specific reason for the termination was not disclosed in the available information.

### Market implication
The termination of an early-phase trial for Ponatinib is a neutral event for Takeda, as it involves an early-stage asset and the reason for termination is not specified.
