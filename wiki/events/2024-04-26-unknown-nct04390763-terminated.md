## Novartis Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT04390763) for Nis793 and Spartalizumab

**Date:** 2024-04-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04390763) investigating Nis793 and Spartalizumab in combination with Gemcitabine and Nab-paclitaxel. The trial had an actual enrollment of 164 participants and reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2024.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, potentially impacting the future development and market prospects of Nis793 and Spartalizumab.
