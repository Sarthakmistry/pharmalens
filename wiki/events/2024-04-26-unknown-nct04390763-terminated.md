## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04390763 for Nis793 and Spartalizumab

**Date:** 2024-04-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04390763, which investigated Nis793 and Spartalizumab in combination with Gemcitabine and Nab-paclitaxel. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 164 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 26, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for these investigational drugs suggests a potential setback for Novartis's pipeline in the targeted indication.
