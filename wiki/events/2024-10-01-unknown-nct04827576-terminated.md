## Gilead Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT04827576) of Magrolimab

**Date:** 2024-10-01 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Gilead has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04827576, which investigated Magrolimab in combination with Docetaxel. The trial, with an actual enrollment of 106 participants, reached its primary completion on October 1, 2024, and results are available. The termination indicates a halt in further development for this specific study.

### Market implication
This trial termination represents a bearish signal for Gilead, as it removes a potential pipeline asset from further development.
