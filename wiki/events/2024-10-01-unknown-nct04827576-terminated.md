## Gilead's Phase 2 Trial (NCT04827576) for Magrolimab in Combination with Docetaxel Terminated

**Date:** 2024-10-01 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04827576, investigating Magrolimab in combination with Docetaxel, has been terminated. The study, which had an actual enrollment of 106 participants, reached its primary completion on October 1, 2024, and results are available.
