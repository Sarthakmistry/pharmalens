## Pembrolizumab (NCT04221945) Phase 3 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2025-01-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04221945, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] for cervical cancer, reached its primary completion date on January 7, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 1060 participants, has now completed its primary data collection, and results are expected to follow.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone for [[pembrolizumab]], moving it closer to potential regulatory submission for cervical cancer, though the market impact awaits the release of clinical data.
