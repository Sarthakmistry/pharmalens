## Pembrolizumab Phase 3 Trial NCT04221945 for Cervical Cancer Completes Primary Endpoint

Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04221945, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with chemoradiotherapy for cervical cancer, reached its primary completion date on January 7, 2025. The trial enrolled 1060 participants and has indicated that results are available.
