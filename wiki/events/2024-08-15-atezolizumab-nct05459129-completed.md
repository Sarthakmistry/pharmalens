## Roche's Atezolizumab Combination Trial for Alzheimer's Disease Completes Primary Phase 1/2 Study

**Date:** 2024-08-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05459129) investigating atezolizumab in combination with Tiragolumab, Carboplatin, and Paclitaxel for Alzheimer's disease, completed its primary study on August 15, 2024. The trial enrolled 12 participants and has reported results, though specific findings are not detailed in this completion notice.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial is a neutral event for Roche, as specific results are not yet publicly available to assess potential impact.
