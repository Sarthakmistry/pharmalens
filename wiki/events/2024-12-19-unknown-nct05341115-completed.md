## Takeda's Leuprorelin acetate depot 3m trial (NCT05341115) completes primary phase

A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05341115) sponsored by Takeda, investigating Leuprorelin acetate depot 3m, reached its primary completion date on December 19, 2024. The trial enrolled 80 participants and has reported results.

The completion of this Phase 4 trial for Leuprorelin acetate depot 3m is a standard procedural update and is not expected to have an immediate market impact for Takeda.
