## Eli Lilly Reports Strong Q1 2025 Earnings with Mounjaro and Zepbound Driving Growth, Updates Key Pipeline Assets

**Date:** 2025-05-01 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly reported strong Q1 2025 financial results with significant revenue growth driven primarily by sales of [[tirzepatide]] (Mounjaro and Zepbound). The company also provided updates on its clinical pipeline, including positive Phase 3 results for Orforglipron in type 2 diabetes, approval of Mirikizumab in Japan for Crohn's disease, and European approval for Pirtobrutinib in chronic lymphocytic leukemia. However, there were regulatory setbacks with the withdrawal of the U.S. application for [[tirzepatide]] in heart failure with preserved ejection fraction and a negative EMA opinion for [[donanemab]] in Alzheimer's disease.

### Market implication
The robust sales performance of [[tirzepatide]] (Mounjaro and Zepbound) significantly boosted Eli Lilly's Q1 2025 revenue and net income, indicating a strong financial position and positive market outlook despite some pipeline challenges and the impact of the Inflation Reduction Act on a competitor's drug, [[empagliflozin]].
