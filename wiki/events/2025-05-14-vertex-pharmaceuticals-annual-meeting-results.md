## Vertex Pharmaceuticals Reports Annual Meeting Results, Including Director Elections and Executive Compensation Vote

**Date:** 2025-05-14 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated held its annual meeting of shareholders on May 14, 2025. Shareholders elected 11 directors to the Board of Directors, ratified Ernst & Young LLP as the independent registered public accounting firm for 2025, and approved, on an advisory basis, the 2024 compensation program for the company's named executive officers. A shareholder proposal regarding excessive golden parachutes was not approved.

### Market implication
As these are routine corporate governance matters, the event is expected to have a neutral impact on Vertex Pharmaceuticals' market valuation.
