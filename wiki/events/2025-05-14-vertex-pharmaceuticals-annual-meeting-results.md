## Vertex Pharmaceuticals Annual Meeting Results

**Date:** 2025-05-14 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated held its annual meeting of shareholders on May 14, 2025, where shareholders voted on the election of directors, ratification of auditors, executive compensation, and a shareholder proposal. Shareholders elected the proposed slate of directors, ratified the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP, and approved the 2024 compensation program for named executive officers. A shareholder proposal regarding excessive golden parachutes was not approved.

### Market implication
This event reflects routine corporate governance activities and is not expected to have a direct impact on the company's valuation or stock performance.
