## Nivolumab Phase 2 trial (NCT02060188) for Colorectal Cancer reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2024-10-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02060188) evaluating [[nivolumab]] for colorectal cancer has reached its primary completion on October 22, 2024. The trial, sponsored by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], enrolled 385 participants and has results available. This marks a significant milestone in the drug's development for the oncology-crc indication.

The completion of this Phase 2 trial for nivolumab in colorectal cancer provides new data that could inform future development and market positioning for Bristol-Myers Squibb.
