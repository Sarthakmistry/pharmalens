## AstraZeneca's Benralizumab trial NCT04053634 completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-07-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04053634, investigating Benralizumab, has reached its primary completion date on July 28, 2025. The trial enrolled 689 participants and is now completed, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
This event is neutral as it signifies the completion of the trial's primary phase, but no results have been released to indicate efficacy or safety, thus no immediate market impact is expected.
