**Date:** 2024-11-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03573882) for Voxelotor, which had an actual enrollment of 179 participants. The trial reached its primary completion on November 12, 2024, and results are available.
