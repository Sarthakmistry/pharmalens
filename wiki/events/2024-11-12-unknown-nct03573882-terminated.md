## Pfizer's Voxelotor Trial (NCT03573882) Terminated

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03573882) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating Voxelotor, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 179 participants and reached its primary completion date on November 12, 2024, with results available. The termination of a Phase 3 trial typically indicates issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic business decisions.
