## Roche's Breast Cancer Trial NCT04024462 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-12-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04024462, sponsored by Roche, investigating various treatments including Pertuzumab, Trastuzumab, Doxorubicin, Cyclophosphamide, and Docetaxel for breast cancer, reached its primary completion on December 13, 2021. The Phase 3 study enrolled 200 participants and has reported having results.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a standard procedural step, with market impact dependent on the eventual release and nature of the study results.
