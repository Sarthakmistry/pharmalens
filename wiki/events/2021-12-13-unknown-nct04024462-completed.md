## Roche's Phase 3 Breast Cancer Trial NCT04024462 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-12-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04024462, investigating various treatments including Pertuzumab iv, Trastuzumab iv, Pertuzumab and trastuzumab fdc sc, Doxorubicin, Cyclophosphamide, Docetaxel, Post-operative radiotherapy, and Hormone therapy for breast cancer, reached its primary completion on December 13, 2021. The trial enrolled 200 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for Roche's breast cancer pipeline, with the market awaiting the release and analysis of the trial results.
