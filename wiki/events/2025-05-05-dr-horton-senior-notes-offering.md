## D.R. Horton, Inc. Completes $500 Million Senior Notes Offering

**Date:** 2025-05-05 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
D.R. Horton, Inc. completed a public offering of $500 million aggregate principal amount of 4.850% Senior Notes due 2030, receiving net proceeds of $496.7 million after underwriting discount. Interest on the Notes will accrue at 4.850% per annum, payable semi-annually starting October 15, 2025, with maturity on October 15, 2030. The Notes are general unsecured obligations of the company and are guaranteed by substantially all of its current homebuilding subsidiaries.

### Market implication
The completion of this senior notes offering provides D.R. Horton, Inc. with additional capital, representing a routine financing activity for the company.
