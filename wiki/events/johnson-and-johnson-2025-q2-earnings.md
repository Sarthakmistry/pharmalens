## Johnson & Johnson reports strong Q2 2025 results, raises full-year outlook

**Date:** 2025-07-16 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported strong Q2 2025 results with sales growth of 5.8% and raised its full-year 2025 outlook, driven by key growth products and pipeline advancements. Q2 2025 reported sales grew 5.8% to $23.7 billion, with adjusted operational growth of 3.0%, and adjusted EPS was $2.77. The company raised its full-year 2025 adjusted operational sales growth outlook to 3.0% - 3.5% and adjusted EPS outlook to $11.00 - $11.15. Significant new product pipeline progress includes the FDA approval of IMAAVY for generalized myasthenia gravis and priority review for TAR-200.

### Market implication
The raised full-year guidance and significant pipeline progress, including an FDA approval and priority review, indicate a bullish outlook for Johnson & Johnson's financial performance.
