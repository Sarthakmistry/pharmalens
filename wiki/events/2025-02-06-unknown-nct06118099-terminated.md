## Sanofi terminates Phase 2 trial NCT06118099 for Amlitelimab in Alzheimer's disease

**Date:** 2025-02-06 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06118099) for Amlitelimab, an investigational drug for Alzheimer's disease. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 90 participants, reached its primary completion date on February 6, 2025, but was subsequently terminated. No results have been reported.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an Alzheimer's drug represents a setback for [[sanofi]]'s pipeline in this high-risk indication, potentially impacting future revenue expectations from this asset.
