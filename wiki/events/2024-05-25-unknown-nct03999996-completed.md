## Takeda's Dengue Vaccine Trial NCT03999996 Reaches Primary Completion

Clinical trial NCT03999996, evaluating Takeda's dengue tetravalent vaccine (live, attenuated) (tdv), reached its primary completion date on May 25, 2024. The Phase 3 study enrolled 365 participants and is now marked as completed with results available.
