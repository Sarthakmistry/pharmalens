## Biogen's Phase 2 Trial NCT02255422 for Omaveloxolone Completed

**Date:** 2017-11-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02255422) for omaveloxolone capsules across various dosages (2.5 mg, 5 mg, 10 mg, 20 mg, 40 mg, 80 mg, 160 mg) reached its primary completion date on November 2, 2017. The study enrolled 53 participants and has reported results, marking the conclusion of this phase of development for the drug.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides foundational data for Biogen's omaveloxolone program, with potential future implications depending on the disclosed results.
