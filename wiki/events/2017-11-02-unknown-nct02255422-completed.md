## Biogen's Omaveloxolone Phase 2 Trial (NCT02255422) Completes Primary Study

**Date:** 2017-11-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT02255422, investigating various doses of Omaveloxolone capsules, reached its primary completion on November 2, 2017. The study enrolled 53 participants and has reported results, which will inform the drug's future development path.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides Biogen with data to assess Omaveloxolone's efficacy and safety, with market impact contingent on the disclosed results and subsequent development decisions.
