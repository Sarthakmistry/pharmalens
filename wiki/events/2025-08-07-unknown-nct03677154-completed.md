## Roche's Mosunetuzumab (NCT03677154) Phase 1/2 Trial Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-08-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03677154) investigating Mosunetuzumab (intravenous and subcutaneous), Polatuzumab vedotin, and Tocilizumab has reached its primary completion date on August 7, 2025. The study enrolled 188 participants and was designed to evaluate these agents, though specific results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication until results are released.
