## Roche's Trial NCT03677154 Completed

**Date:** 2025-08-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03677154, sponsored by Roche, reached its primary completion date on August 7, 2025. This Phase 1/2 study, which investigated Mosunetuzumab intravenous (iv), Mosunetuzumab subcutaneous (sc), Polatuzumab vedotin, and Tocilizumab, enrolled 188 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication as no results have been released to indicate success or failure.
