## Regeneron Reports Mixed Q1 2026 Results with Revenue Growth and Decreased Net Income

**Date:** 2026-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron reported Q1 2026 total revenue of $3,605.4 million, an increase from the prior year, indicating strong sales performance. However, net income and diluted EPS decreased compared to Q1 2025, and gross margin was negatively impacted by a temporary manufacturing interruption at its Limerick, Ireland facility.

### Market implication
The mixed financial results, with strong revenue growth offset by a decline in net income and EPS due to manufacturing issues, suggest a neutral short-term market reaction for Regeneron.
