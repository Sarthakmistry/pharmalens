## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial NCT05144945 for Influenza Vaccines Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-09-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05144945, reached its primary completion on September 1, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 301 participants, investigated Quadrivalent recombinant influenza vaccine (riv4) and Quadrivalent inactivated influenza vaccine (iiv4). Results for this completed trial are available.

### Market implication
This event has a neutral market implication as it represents a routine completion of a Phase 3 trial for influenza vaccines, with no specific results or significant market-moving information provided at this time.
