## Regeneron Shareholders Approve Board Declassification, Elect Directors, Ratify Auditor, and Executive Compensation

**Date:** 2025-06-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. held its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders, where directors were elected, the independent registered public accounting firm was ratified, executive compensation was approved on an advisory basis, and an amendment to declassify the Board of Directors was approved. However, two amendments to eliminate supermajority vote requirements were not approved.

### Market implication
Shareholders approved several key proposals including director elections, auditor ratification, executive compensation, and board declassification, but did not approve two proposals aimed at eliminating supermajority vote requirements, indicating a mixed outcome on corporate governance changes.
