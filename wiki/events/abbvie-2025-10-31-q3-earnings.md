## AbbVie Reports Q3 2025 Net Revenues Up 9.1% to $15.776 Billion, Driven by Skyrizi and Rinvoq, Despite EPS Decline from IPR&D Expense

**Date:** 2025-10-31 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie reported third-quarter 2025 net revenues of $15.776 billion, an increase of 9.1% on a reported basis. The growth was primarily driven by strong performance from immunology drugs risankizumab (Skyrizi) and upadacitinib (Rinvoq), and neuroscience products like cariprazine (Vraylar) and onabotulinumtoxinA (Botox Therapeutic). However, diluted EPS decreased significantly by 88.6% on a GAAP basis and 38.0% on an adjusted basis, primarily due to a $1.50 per share impact from acquired IPR&D and milestones expense.

### Market implication
The mixed financial results, with strong revenue growth offset by a substantial decline in EPS due to specific expenses, suggest a neutral short-term market reaction as investors weigh underlying product strength against one-time costs.
