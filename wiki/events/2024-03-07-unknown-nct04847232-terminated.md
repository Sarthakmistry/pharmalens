## AstraZeneca terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT04847232) for Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate

**Date:** 2024-03-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AstraZeneca has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04847232, which was investigating Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate (SZC). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 2690 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 7, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a late-stage Phase 3 trial for Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate represents a negative development for AstraZeneca's pipeline in this area.
