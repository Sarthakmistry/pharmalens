## Novo Nordisk Completes Primary Completion for Phase 3 Trial NCT04760626 in Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2022-08-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04760626), a study investigating treatments for type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on August 12, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 1085 participants, evaluated drugs including Insulin icodec, Insulin glargine 100u/ml, Insulin degludec, and Insulin glargine 300u/ml. Results for this completed study are now available.

### Market implication
This completion marks a standard progression in the clinical development of Novo Nordisk's diabetes pipeline, with results now available for further evaluation.
