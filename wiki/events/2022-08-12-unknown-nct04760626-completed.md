## Novo Nordisk Completes Phase 3 Trial (NCT04760626) for Insulin Icodec in Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2022-08-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk has completed its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04760626) comparing insulin icodec with insulin glargine 100 U/mL in patients with type 2 diabetes. The study, which enrolled 1085 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 12, 2022, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides data for Novo Nordisk's insulin icodec, which will inform its development and potential market positioning in the type 2 diabetes landscape.
