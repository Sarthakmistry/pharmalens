## Novo Nordisk completes Phase 2 trial NCT04992065

**Date:** 2022-04-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On April 26, 2022, Novo Nordisk completed its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04992065) investigating Nnc0385-0434 at various doses (15 mg, 40 mg, 100 mg) and Evolocumab 140 mg/ml, Repatha®. The trial enrolled 267 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard step in drug development and has a neutral market implication without specific results or further context.
