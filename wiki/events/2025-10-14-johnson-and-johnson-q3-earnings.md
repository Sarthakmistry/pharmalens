## Johnson & Johnson Reports Strong Q3 2025 Sales Growth and Raises Full-Year Outlook

**Date:** 2025-10-14 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported its third-quarter 2025 financial results, showing sales growth of 6.8% to $24.0 Billion. The company also raised its full-year sales outlook for 2025 and announced its intent to separate its Orthopaedics business.

### Market implication
The reported sales growth and increased full-year sales outlook indicate strong financial performance, which is likely to have a positive impact on Johnson & Johnson's stock valuation.
