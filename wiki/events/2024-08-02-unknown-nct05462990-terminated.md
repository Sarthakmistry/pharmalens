## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05462990) for Quc398

**Date:** 2024-08-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05462990) investigating the drug Quc398. The trial, which enrolled 101 participants, reached primary completion on August 2, 2024, and results are available. The specific reason for the termination was not disclosed.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Quc398 represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, indicating the drug will not progress further in development.
