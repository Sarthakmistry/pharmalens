## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05462990) for Quc398

**Date:** 2024-08-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05462990) investigating the drug Quc398. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 101 participants, reached its primary completion on August 2, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Quc398 could be seen negatively, indicating potential issues with the drug's efficacy or safety, or a strategic pipeline reprioritization by Novartis.
