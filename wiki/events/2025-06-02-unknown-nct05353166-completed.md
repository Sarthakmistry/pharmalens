## Regeneron's Regn5381 Phase 2 Trial for HF-HTN Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2 trial (NCT05353166) investigating Regn5381 for heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN) reached its primary completion date on June 2, 2025. The trial enrolled 59 participants, and results have not yet been published.
