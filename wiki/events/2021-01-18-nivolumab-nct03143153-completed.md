## Nivolumab (NCT03143153) Phase 3 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2021-01-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03143153) for [[nivolumab]], sponsored by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], reached its primary completion date on January 18, 2021. The trial, which involved 970 participants, investigated nivolumab in the context of [[alzheimers]] disease and has reported results. Other drugs mentioned in the trial include Ipilimumab, Cisplatin, and Fluorouracil.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for nivolumab in Alzheimer's disease marks a development milestone, with market implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
