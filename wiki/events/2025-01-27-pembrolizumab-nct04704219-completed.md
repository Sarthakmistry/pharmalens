## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2 Trial NCT04704219 Completed

**Date:** 2025-01-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04704219) evaluating pembrolizumab, sponsored by Merck, has reached its primary completion date on January 27, 2025. The trial enrolled 160 participants and has results available. This trial investigated pembrolizumab in combination with Lenvatinib.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Merck's pipeline development for pembrolizumab.
