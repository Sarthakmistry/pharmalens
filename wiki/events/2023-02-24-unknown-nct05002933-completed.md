## Sanofi completes Phase 4 trial for Insulin glargine 300 u/ml in Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2023-02-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has announced the primary completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05002933) investigating Insulin glargine 300 u/ml for Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 570 participants, reached its primary completion date on February 24, 2023. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a standard procedural step for Sanofi's Insulin glargine 300 u/ml, with no immediate market impact as results are not yet available.
