## Amgen Reports Annual Meeting Results, Stockholders Approve Directors and Executive Compensation

**Date:** 2025-05-27 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Amgen Inc. reported the results of its Annual Meeting held on May 23, 2025. Stockholders elected twelve nominees for director to serve a one-year term, approved the advisory vote on executive compensation, and ratified Ernst & Young LLP as the Company’s independent registered public accountants for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2025.

### Market implication
This event is a routine corporate governance update and does not present a direct financial or operational signal for the company's valuation.
