## Johnson & Johnson Reports Strong Q4 2025 Earnings, Exceeding Expectations with Key Drug Growth

**Date:** 2026-01-21 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported strong fourth-quarter and full-year 2025 results, with reported sales growth of 9.1% and 6.0% respectively, driven by key pharmaceutical products like Skyrizi, Stelara, and Darzalex, and robust performance in MedTech. Q4 2025 sales reached $24.6 billion, and full-year sales were $94.2 billion, with adjusted EPS of $2.46 for the quarter.

### Market implication
The strong performance across pharmaceutical and MedTech segments, particularly the significant growth of key drugs, indicates a positive outlook for Johnson & Johnson's valuation.
