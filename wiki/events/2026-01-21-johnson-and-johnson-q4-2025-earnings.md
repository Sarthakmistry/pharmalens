## Johnson & Johnson Reports Strong Q4 and Full-Year 2025 Results, Exceeding Expectations

**Date:** 2026-01-21 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced robust Q4 and Full-Year 2025 results, with Q4 reported sales growing 9.1% to $24.6 billion and adjusted EPS increasing 10.0% to $2.46. The pharmaceutical segment saw a 12.5% sales increase, driven by strong performance in oncology and immunology, while the MedTech segment also contributed to growth. The company exceeded expectations and provided an optimistic 2026 full-year guidance, reflecting a positive outlook.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance and positive 2026 guidance are likely to be viewed favorably by investors, indicating a bullish outlook for Johnson & Johnson's stock.
