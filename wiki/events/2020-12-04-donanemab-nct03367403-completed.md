## Eli Lilly's Phase 2 Trial for Donanemab (NCT03367403) in Alzheimer's Disease Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-12-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03367403) investigating [[donanemab]] (also known as Ly3202626) for the treatment of early symptomatic [[alzheimers]] disease reached its primary completion date on December 4, 2020. The study, which enrolled 272 participants, has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a key development milestone for Eli Lilly's [[donanemab]] in the [[alzheimers]] therapeutic area, providing data to inform its ongoing clinical program.
