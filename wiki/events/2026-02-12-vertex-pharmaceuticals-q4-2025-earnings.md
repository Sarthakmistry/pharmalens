## Vertex Pharmaceuticals Reports Strong Q4 and Full Year 2025 Results, Provides Optimistic 2026 Guidance

**Date:** 2026-02-12 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals reported strong fourth quarter and full year 2025 financial results, with total revenue reaching $12.0 billion for the full year, marking a 9% increase over 2024. The company also provided optimistic revenue guidance for 2026, projecting $12.95 billion to $13.1 billion. Key pipeline advancements include being on track to complete the BLA filing for U.S. accelerated approval of povetacicept in IgAN in the first half of 2026, alongside continued commercialization efforts for CASGEVY and JOURNAVX.

### Market implication
The strong financial performance, positive 2026 revenue guidance, and pipeline progress suggest continued growth and investor confidence in Vertex's commercial products and future therapeutic offerings.
