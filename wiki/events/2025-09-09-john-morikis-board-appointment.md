## Johnson & Johnson announced the election of John Morikis to its Board of Directors, who will also serve on the Audit Committee and Compensation & Benefits Committee.

**Date:** 2025-09-09 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the election of John Morikis to its Board of Directors, effective September 8, 2025. Mr. Morikis will also serve on the Audit Committee and Compensation & Benefits Committee. The Board has determined that he satisfies the applicable independence requirements of the NYSE Listed Company Manual.

### Market implication
This board appointment is a standard corporate governance update for Johnson & Johnson, indicating no immediate direct market impact.
