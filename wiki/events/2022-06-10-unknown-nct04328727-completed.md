## Novartis's Phase 2 trial NCT04328727 investigating Eltrombopag, r-ATG, and CSA completed primary phase

**Date:** 2022-06-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04328727, sponsored by Novartis, reached its primary completion on June 10, 2022. This Phase 2 study, which enrolled 36 participants, investigated Eltrombopag, Rabbit anti-thymocyte globulin (r-atg), and Cyclosporine a (csa). Results from the trial are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides data for Novartis to evaluate the next steps for the studied compounds, with results now available for analysis.
