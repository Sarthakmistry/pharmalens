## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04305041 for Advanced Solid Tumors Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04305041) evaluating [[pembrolizumab]], in combination with other agents, for advanced solid tumors, completed its primary endpoint on August 25, 2025. The study, sponsored by [[merck]], enrolled 100 participants and marks a significant step in the drug's development pipeline.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides an update on the development timeline for [[pembrolizumab]] in advanced solid tumors, with further data expected to inform future clinical strategies.
