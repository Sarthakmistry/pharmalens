## Sanofi's Phase 2 Trial NCT04398706 for Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine Formulations Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-08-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04398706) evaluating three different pneumococcal conjugate vaccine formulations in healthy infants has reached its primary completion date on August 10, 2023. The study enrolled 852 participants and has results available, though not detailed in this update. This trial aimed to assess the safety and immunogenicity of the vaccine candidates, comparing them against existing vaccines like Pneumococcal 13-valent conjugate vaccine and other routine childhood immunizations.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard development step for Sanofi's vaccine pipeline; market impact will depend on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial results.
