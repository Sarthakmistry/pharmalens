## Merck's Phase 2 Trial NCT04447755 for Lenvatinib Completed

**Date:** 2022-09-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04447755, investigating Lenvatinib, reached its primary completion on September 16, 2022. The trial successfully enrolled 127 participants and has reported results. This milestone indicates the conclusion of the primary data collection phase for this study.

The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and does not immediately imply a change in market valuation without further results.
