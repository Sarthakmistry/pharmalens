## Lapatinib HER2 Gastric Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The LOGiC study, a Phase III global trial evaluating lapatinib with chemotherapy in HER2-positive gastric, esophageal, or gastro-esophageal junction cancer, has completed, with results indicating its completion. The study enrolled 545 patients and investigated lapatinib in combination with capecitabine plus oxaliplatin (CapeOx) for locally advanced, unresectable, or metastatic disease.
