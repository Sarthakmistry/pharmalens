## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05649137) for Semaglutide in Obesity and Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05649137, investigating [[semaglutide]] for the treatment of GLP-1 obesity and type 2 diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 512 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 28, 2024, and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a key step towards potential regulatory submissions, with market impact dependent on the forthcoming results.
