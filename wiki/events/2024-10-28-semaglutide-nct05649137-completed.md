## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial NCT05649137 Completed for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity

**Date:** 2024-10-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 trial NCT05649137, investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes and GLP-1 obesity, has reached primary completion on October 28, 2024. The trial enrolled 512 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development timeline for semaglutide, with market implications pending the release of detailed results.
