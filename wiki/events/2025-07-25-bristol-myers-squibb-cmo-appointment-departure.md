## Bristol Myers Squibb Announces Leadership Change in R&D with New Chief Medical Officer Appointment and Departure

**Date:** 2025-07-25 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced a leadership change in its R&D division, with Dr. Samit Hirawat departing as Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development, and Dr. Cristian Massacesi appointed to the role. Dr. Hirawat will transition to an advisory role effective August 1, 2025, before leaving the company by November 1, 2025. Dr. Massacesi, previously CMO of AstraZeneca and Alexion, will assume the position on August 1, 2025, reporting directly to CEO Christopher Boerner.

### Market implication
The appointment of a highly experienced new Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development, coupled with a smooth transition plan for the departing officer, suggests a stable outlook for the company's R&D pipeline and strategy.
