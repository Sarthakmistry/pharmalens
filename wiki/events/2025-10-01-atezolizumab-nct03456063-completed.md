## Atezolizumab Phase 3 Trial NCT03456063 for Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-10-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03456063) evaluating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with Nab-paclitaxel for previously untreated locally advanced or metastatic triple-negative breast cancer has reached its primary completion date on October 1, 2025. The trial, sponsored by [[roche]], enrolled 453 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a step towards potential data readout, but without results, the immediate market impact is neutral.
