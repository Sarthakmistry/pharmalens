## Atezolizumab (Tecentriq) Phase 3 Trial NCT03456063 for Non-squamous NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-10-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03456063) investigating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with nab-paclitaxel compared to chemotherapy for patients with Stage IIIB/IV non-squamous non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on October 1, 2025. The trial, sponsored by [[roche]], enrolled 453 participants and has not yet released results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step towards potential new data for [[atezolizumab]] in NSCLC, with future results anticipated to inform its market position.
