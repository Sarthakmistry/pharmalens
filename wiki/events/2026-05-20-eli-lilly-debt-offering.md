## Eli Lilly Completes $8.94 Billion Debt Offering

**Date:** 2026-05-20 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company completed a debt offering on May 20, 2026, raising approximately $8.94 billion in net proceeds. The offering included the issuance of Floating Rate Notes due in 2028 and 2029, as well as Fixed Rate Notes with maturities ranging from 2029 to 2066.

### Market implication
This debt offering represents a standard financing activity for Eli Lilly, providing capital for general corporate purposes without a direct immediate impact on valuation.
