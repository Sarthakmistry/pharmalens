## Pfizer's Phase 3 RSV Vaccine Candidate Trial (NCT06473519) Reaches Primary Completion

Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT06473519, investigating an RSV vaccine candidate (Rsvpref mdv/sdv), reached its primary completion date on September 20, 2024. The study enrolled 453 participants and has reported results, marking a significant step in the vaccine's development.
