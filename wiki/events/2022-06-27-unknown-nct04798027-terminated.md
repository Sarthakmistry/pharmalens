## Sanofi terminates Phase 1/2 trial for SARS-CoV-2 mRNA vaccine formulations

**Date:** 2022-06-27 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04798027) investigating three SARS-CoV-2 mRNA vaccine formulations (Sars-cov-2 mrna vaccine formulation 1, Sars-cov-2 mrna vaccine formulation 2, Sars-cov-2 mrna vaccine formulation 3). The trial, which enrolled 182 participants, reached its primary completion date on June 27, 2022. This termination indicates the discontinuation of development for these specific vaccine candidates by Sanofi.

### Market implication
The termination of this vaccine trial suggests Sanofi is deprioritizing or discontinuing these specific SARS-CoV-2 vaccine candidates, potentially impacting its infectious disease pipeline in this area.
