## Novo Nordisk's Somapacitan Growth Hormone Deficiency Trial Completes Primary Observation Period

**Date:** 2021-11-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03811535, comparing somapacitan to Norditropin® for Growth Hormone Deficiency in Children, has completed its primary observation period. This study is an interventional trial with an actual enrollment of 200 participants, comparing once-weekly somapacitan to once-daily Norditropin®.
