## AbbVie's AGN-151597 Retinitis Pigmentosa Trial Completes with No Efficacy Demonstrated

**Date:** 2020-06-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie's Phase I/II trial for AGN-151597 in Advanced Retinitis Pigmentosa completed, but efficacy was not demonstrated. The trial, which enrolled 14 participants, concluded without showing the desired therapeutic effect for the investigational drug.

### Market implication
The lack of demonstrated efficacy for AGN-151597 in this trial represents a negative outcome for AbbVie's pipeline in ophthalmology.
