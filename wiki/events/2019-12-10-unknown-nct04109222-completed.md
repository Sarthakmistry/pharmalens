## Sanofi's NCT04109222 Trial Completed

**Date:** 2019-12-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04109222) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating Fluzone quadrivalent vaccine and Fluzone high-dose vaccine, reached its primary completion date on December 10, 2019. The study enrolled 90 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial for established vaccines is a routine operational update for Sanofi and is not expected to have a significant immediate market impact.
