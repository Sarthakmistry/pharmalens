## AbbVie Announces Board Changes and Stockholder Meeting Results

**Date:** 2025-05-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. announced changes to its Board of Directors, including the appointment of Thomas J. Falk as a Class III director and a planned future reduction in board size. The company also reported the results of its 2025 Annual Meeting of Stockholders, which included the election of directors, ratification of auditors, and advisory approval of executive compensation. Stockholders did not approve proposals related to supermajority voting.

### Market implication
These corporate governance updates and stockholder voting results are factual and do not present an immediate positive or negative financial or clinical impact for AbbVie.
