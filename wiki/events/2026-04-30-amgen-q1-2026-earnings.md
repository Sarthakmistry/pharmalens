## Amgen Reports Strong Q1 2026 Financial Results with Increased Operating Cash Flow

**Date:** 2026-04-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Amgen Inc. reported its first quarter 2026 financial results, including increased net cash provided by operating activities and free cash flow, and provided full-year non-GAAP EPS and tax rate guidance. Net cash from operations rose to $2,189 million from $1,391 million in Q1 2025, and free cash flow increased to $1,477 million from $980 million in the same period. The company also issued full-year non-GAAP diluted EPS guidance of $21.70 to $23.10 and a non-GAAP tax rate of 15.0% to 16.5%.

### Market implication
The significant increase in operating cash flow and free cash flow, coupled with positive full-year guidance, indicates strong financial health and operational performance for Amgen.
