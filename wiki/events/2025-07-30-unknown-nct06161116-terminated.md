## Merck Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT06161116) for Mk-6194

**Date:** 2025-07-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Merck has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06161116) for the drug candidate Mk-6194. The trial had an actual enrollment of 149 participants and was originally scheduled for primary completion on July 30, 2025. The termination of a Phase 2 trial typically indicates issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic changes in the development program.
