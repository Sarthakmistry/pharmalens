## Merck terminates Phase 2 trial NCT06161116 for Mk-6194

**Date:** 2025-07-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Merck has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06161116) for the investigational drug Mk-6194. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 149 participants, was originally set to reach primary completion on July 30, 2025, but was terminated prior to that date.
