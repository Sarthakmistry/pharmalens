## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 Trial NCT04609878 for Respiratory Drugs Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-03-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04609878, evaluating respiratory drugs such as Bgf mdi 320/28.8/9.6 µg, Bgf mdi 320/14.4/9.6 µg, Bff mdi 320/9.6 µg, and Bff pmdi 320/9 µg, reached its primary completion date on March 21, 2025. The trial enrolled 2274 participants and is now completed, though results have not yet been published.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural milestone for AstraZeneca, with no immediate market impact until results are released.
