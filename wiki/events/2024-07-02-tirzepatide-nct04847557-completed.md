## Tirzepatide's NCT04847557 Trial for Obesity and Heart Failure Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-07-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04847557, investigating tirzepatide for glp1-obesity and hf-htn, has reached its primary completion date on July 2, 2024. The trial enrolled 731 participants and has reported that results are available. This marks a key milestone in the drug's development for these indications.

### Market implication
The successful completion of this Phase 3 trial with results available is a positive development for Eli Lilly, indicating progress towards potential regulatory submissions and market expansion for tirzepatide in obesity and heart failure.
