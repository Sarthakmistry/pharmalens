## Merck's Phase 2 Trial NCT05974267 for M5717 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-12-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05974267) investigating M5717 (60 mg, 200 mg, 660 mg), Pyronaridine, and Atovaquone-proguanil has reached its primary completion date on December 29, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 192 participants, has completed its data collection for the primary outcome measures. Results are now available.
