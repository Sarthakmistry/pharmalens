## Merck's Phase 2 Trial NCT05974267 for M5717 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-12-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05974267), which investigated various doses of M5717 (60 mg, 200 mg, 660 mg) alongside comparators Pyronaridine and Atovaquone-proguanil, reached its primary completion date on December 29, 2024. The trial successfully enrolled 192 participants and has completed its primary data collection, indicating a standard progression in the development pipeline.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a routine pipeline update for Merck, with any significant market implications contingent upon the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
