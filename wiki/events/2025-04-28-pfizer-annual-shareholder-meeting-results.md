## Pfizer Announces Results of 2025 Annual Shareholder Meeting

**Date:** 2025-04-28 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer Inc. held its Annual Meeting of Shareholders on April 24, 2025. During the meeting, shareholders voted on several key items, including the election of directors, the ratification of KPMG LLP as the independent registered public accounting firm for 2025, and the advisory approval of executive compensation. Two shareholder proposals, one concerning 'Golden Parachutes' and another on 'Religious Discrimination Against Employees', were not approved.

### Market implication
The factual reporting of shareholder voting results from the annual meeting provides no specific commentary or forward-looking statements that would indicate a particular sentiment regarding Pfizer's future performance or valuation.
