## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04770532) for Insulin Icodec in Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-01-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04770532) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating Insulin Icodec for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on January 27, 2022. The trial, which also involved Insulin degludec, enrolled 526 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural step forward for Novo Nordisk's Insulin Icodec, with market implications dependent on the forthcoming results.
