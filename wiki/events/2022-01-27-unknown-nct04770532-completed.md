## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04770532) for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-01-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04770532), investigating Insulin degludec and Insulin icodec for the treatment of type 2 diabetes, reached its primary completion date on January 27, 2022. The study enrolled 526 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Novo Nordisk in its diabetes pipeline, with further market implications dependent on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial results.
