## AstraZeneca completes Phase 2 trial (NCT02442349) for Azd9291 in NSCLC

**Date:** 2016-03-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca announced the completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT02442349) for Azd9291, an investigational drug for oncology-nsclc. The trial, which enrolled 171 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 4, 2016, and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a progression in AstraZeneca's oncology pipeline, providing data for potential further development of Azd9291.
