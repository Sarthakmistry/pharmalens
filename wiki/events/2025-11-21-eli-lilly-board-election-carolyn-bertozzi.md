## Eli Lilly Elects Carolyn R. Bertozzi to Board of Directors

**Date:** 2025-11-21 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company announced the election of Carolyn R. Bertozzi, Ph. D, to its Board of Directors, effective December 8, 2025. Dr. Bertozzi, a professor at Stanford University and an Investigator of the Howard Hughes Medical Institute, will serve on the Board's Science and Technology Committee and the Ethics and Compliance Committee.

### Market implication
This corporate governance update is a standard procedure and is not expected to have a direct impact on Eli Lilly's market valuation or drug pipeline.
