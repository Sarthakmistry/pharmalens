## Novartis's Crizanlizumab Phase 2 Trial (NCT03474965) Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis announced the primary completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03474965) for Crizanlizumab. The study, which enrolled 117 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 6, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a step forward in the development of Crizanlizumab, with results now available for evaluation by Novartis.
