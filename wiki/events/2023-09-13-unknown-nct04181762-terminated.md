## Novartis terminates Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04181762) for Secukinumab

**Date:** 2023-09-13 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04181762) for Secukinumab, which had reached primary completion on September 13, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 275 participants and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial represents a setback for Novartis's Secukinumab pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue expectations for the drug.
