## Amgen terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04631601

**Date:** 2023-10-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Amgen has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04631601), which was investigating Acapatamab, Enzalutamide, Abiraterone, and Amg 404. The trial reached primary completion on October 23, 2023, with 65 actual participants, and no results have been reported.

### Market implication
The termination of an early-phase trial represents a bearish signal for the pipeline assets involved, as it indicates a halt in their development.
