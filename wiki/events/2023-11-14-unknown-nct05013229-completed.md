## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05013229) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating Icosema, Insulin glargine, and Insulin aspart for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2023. The trial enrolled 679 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a neutral event, as results have not yet been released to indicate efficacy or safety.
