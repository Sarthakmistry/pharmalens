## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial (NCT05013229) for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05013229) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating a combination of Insulin glargine, Insulin aspart, and Icosema for Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2023. The study enrolled 679 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as no efficacy or safety data has been released to inform market expectations.
