## Sanofi Terminates Phase 3 RSV Vaccine Trial (NCT06705140)

**Date:** 2025-06-11 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06705140) investigating low dose, standard dose, and high dose RSVt vaccines. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 947 participants, reached its primary completion date on June 11, 2025, but was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for an RSV vaccine candidate represents a setback for Sanofi's pipeline in this therapeutic area.
