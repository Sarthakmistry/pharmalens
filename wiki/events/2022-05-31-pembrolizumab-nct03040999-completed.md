## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT03040999 for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2022-05-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03040999, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with concurrent chemoradiation for locally advanced non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on May 31, 2022. The study enrolled 804 participants and has reported results. This completion marks a significant milestone in the development pathway for pembrolizumab in this indication.
