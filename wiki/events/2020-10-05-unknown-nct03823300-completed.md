## Roche's Phase 3 Trial (NCT03823300) for Faricimab and Aflibercept Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-10-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03823300) sponsored by [[roche]], investigating Faricimab and Aflibercept, reached its primary completion on October 5, 2020. The trial enrolled 658 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development for [[roche]], with no immediate market impact until detailed results are publicly disclosed.
