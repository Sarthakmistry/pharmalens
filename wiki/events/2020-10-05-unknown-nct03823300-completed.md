## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03823300 for Faricimab and Aflibercept Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-10-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03823300, investigating Faricimab and Aflibercept, reached its primary completion date on October 5, 2020. The trial enrolled 658 participants and has reported results.
