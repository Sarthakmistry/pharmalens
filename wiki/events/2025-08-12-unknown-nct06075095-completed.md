## AstraZeneca's Phase 3 trial NCT06075095 for Bgf mdi hfo/hfa completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-08-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT06075095, evaluating Bgf mdi hfo 320/14.4/9.6µg and Bgf mdi hfa 320/14.4/9.6 µg, reached its primary completion date on August 12, 2025. The trial enrolled 297 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of the primary endpoint is a neutral event as no results have been released to indicate efficacy or safety, thus no immediate market impact is expected.
