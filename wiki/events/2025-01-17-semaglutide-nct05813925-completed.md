## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial NCT05813925 for Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-01-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05813925) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for obesity, has reached its primary completion date on January 17, 2025. The trial enrolled 331 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial marks a step forward in the development of semaglutide for obesity, with market impact pending the release of trial results.
