## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial of Semaglutide (NCT05813925) for Obesity Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-01-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05813925) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for the treatment of obesity, has reached its primary completion date on January 17, 2025. The trial enrolled 331 participants and is evaluating semaglutide, a GLP-1 receptor agonist, in combination with Cagrilintide.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development and is considered neutral until results are announced, which could then impact market sentiment for Novo Nordisk and semaglutide.
