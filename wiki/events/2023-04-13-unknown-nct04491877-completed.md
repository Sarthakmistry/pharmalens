## Sanofi's RSV Vaccine Formulation Trial NCT04491877 Completed

**Date:** 2023-04-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04491877) sponsored by Sanofi, investigating RSV vaccine formulations (Rsv vaccine formulation 1 and Rsv vaccine formulation 2), reached its primary completion on April 13, 2023. The study enrolled 259 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides foundational data for Sanofi's RSV vaccine development, with future implications dependent on the released results.
