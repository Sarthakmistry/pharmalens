## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial (KEYNOTE-866) for Muscle-Invasive Bladder Carcinoma Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-12-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, KEYNOTE-866 (NCT04003636), investigating [[pembrolizumab]] with or without chemotherapy (Gemcitabine and Cisplatin) in participants with muscle-invasive bladder carcinoma, reached its primary completion date on December 15, 2022. The trial enrolled 1069 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Merck, with further implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the study results.
