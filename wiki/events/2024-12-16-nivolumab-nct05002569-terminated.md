## Bristol Myers Squibb terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT05002569) for nivolumab

**Date:** 2024-12-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05002569, which investigated [[nivolumab]] (Opdivo) in combination with relatlimab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 1093 participants, reached its primary completion date on December 16, 2024. The termination of a late-stage trial, particularly after primary completion, often indicates that the study did not meet its primary endpoints or faced other significant challenges.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for [[nivolumab]] could lead to a reassessment of [[bristol-myers-squibb]]'s pipeline value and future growth prospects, potentially exerting downward pressure on its stock price due to the loss of a potential revenue stream or increased R&D costs.
