## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Phase 3 Nivolumab Trial (NCT05002569) Terminated

**Date:** 2024-12-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05002569, which was investigating [[nivolumab]]. The trial, with an actual enrollment of 1093 participants, reached its primary completion date on December 16, 2024, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a late-stage clinical trial for [[nivolumab]] could negatively impact Bristol-Myers Squibb's pipeline and future revenue projections for the drug.
