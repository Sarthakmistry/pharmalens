## Phase 3 Trial (NCT04478266) for Palbociclib in Breast Cancer Terminated

**Date:** 2022-06-28 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04478266) investigating palbociclib for oncology-breast, sponsored by Sanofi, was terminated on June 28, 2022. The trial had an actual enrollment of 1068 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even with results available, is generally a bearish signal for the drug's development in the specified indication, potentially indicating futility, safety concerns, or strategic pipeline shifts.
