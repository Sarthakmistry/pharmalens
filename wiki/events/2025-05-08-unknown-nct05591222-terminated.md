## Amgen terminates Phase 2 trial of Daxdilimab (NCT05591222)

**Date:** 2025-05-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Amgen has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05591222) for Daxdilimab, which had an actual enrollment of 72 participants. The trial's primary completion date was listed as May 8, 2025, but it was terminated before results were reported.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Daxdilimab is a negative development for Amgen, indicating a potential setback in its pipeline for this asset.
