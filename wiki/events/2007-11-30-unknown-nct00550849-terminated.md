## Biogen terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT00550849

**Date:** 2007-11-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Biogen has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT00550849. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 16 participants, reached its primary completion on November 30, 2007, before being terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial, even an early-phase one, can be a minor negative signal for Biogen's pipeline development, indicating a potential setback in a research program.
