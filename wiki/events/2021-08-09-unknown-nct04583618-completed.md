## Sanofi's Phase 2 Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine Trial (NCT04583618) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-08-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On August 9, 2021, Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04583618) for pneumococcal conjugate vaccines reached its primary completion. The trial, which enrolled 750 participants, evaluated different formulations of pneumococcal conjugate vaccines (formulation 1-sp0202-iib, formulation 2-sp0202-vi, formulation 3-sp0202-vii) against comparators like Pneumococcal 13-valent conjugate vaccine (Prevnar 13) and Pneumococcal vaccine polyvalent (Pneumovax 23). The trial status is COMPLETED and results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard development in the drug development process for Sanofi's vaccine pipeline, with no immediate market impact until results are disclosed.
