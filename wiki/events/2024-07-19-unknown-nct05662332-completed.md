## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial for Insulin Efsitora Alfa in Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-07-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05662332) evaluating Insulin efsitora alfa, a novel once-weekly basal insulin, for the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes, reached its primary completion date on July 19, 2024. The study enrolled 795 participants and has reported results, marking a significant milestone in its development.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for Insulin efsitora alfa advances Eli Lilly's diabetes pipeline, with future market reaction dependent on the detailed clinical outcomes.
