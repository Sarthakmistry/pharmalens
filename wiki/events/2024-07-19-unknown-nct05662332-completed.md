## Eli Lilly's Insulin Efsitora Alfa Phase 3 Trial (NCT05662332) Completes Primary Completion for Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2024-07-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05662332) evaluating Insulin efsitora alfa against Insulin glargine in participants with type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on July 19, 2024. The study enrolled 795 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development of Insulin efsitora alfa for the treatment of type 2 diabetes.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as specific efficacy and safety results have not yet been publicly disclosed, which would determine the market's reaction.
