## AstraZeneca's NCT03528681 Trial for Sodium Zirconium Cyclosilicate Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-09-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03528681, sponsored by AstraZeneca, reached its primary completion date on September 15, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 270 participants, investigated Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate 5g and Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate 10g. Results for this trial are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural update for AstraZeneca, with no immediate market impact given the absence of specific results in this notification.
