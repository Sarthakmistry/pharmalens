## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Semaglutide Trial (NCT05064735) for GLP-1 Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2023-07-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05064735) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating semaglutide for GLP-1 obesity, reached its primary completion date on July 24, 2023. The trial enrolled 407 participants and has results available, indicating the study has concluded its main data collection phase.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a procedural milestone for Novo Nordisk's semaglutide program in obesity, with market impact dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
