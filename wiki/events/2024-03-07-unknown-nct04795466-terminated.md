## Novartis terminates Phase 2 Alzheimer's trial NCT04795466

**Date:** 2024-03-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04795466) investigating Canakinumab for Alzheimer's disease. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 34 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 7, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
Given that Canakinumab is not a tracked drug in our system and the trial was in Phase 2, this termination is expected to have a neutral market impact on Novartis.
