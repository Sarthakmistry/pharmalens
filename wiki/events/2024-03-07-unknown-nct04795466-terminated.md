## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial of Canakinumab (NCT04795466) for Alzheimer's disease

**Date:** 2024-03-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04795466) investigating Canakinumab for Alzheimer's disease. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 34 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 7, 2024, and results are available. The specific reason for termination was not provided in the source document.
