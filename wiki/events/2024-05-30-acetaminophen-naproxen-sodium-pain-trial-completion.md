## Johnson & Johnson Completes Phase 3 Trial for Acetaminophen/Naproxen Sodium in Postoperative Pain

**Date:** 2024-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 study evaluating a fixed combination of acetaminophen/naproxen sodium for acute postoperative pain following bunionectomy has completed. The trial, sponsored by Johnson & Johnson Consumer Inc., involved 122 participants and assessed the Time-weighted Sum of Pain Intensity Difference Score from 0 to 24 Hours After Dosing (SPID 0-24) as its primary outcome.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial without immediate results leads to a neutral market reaction, awaiting future data disclosures.
