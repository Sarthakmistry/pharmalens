## Durvalumab (NCT04774380) Phase 3 Trial Completed for NSCLC

**Date:** 2023-06-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04774380) investigating durvalumab in combination with Cisplatin, Carboplatin, and Etoposide for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on June 12, 2023. The trial enrolled 152 participants and has reported having results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event for AstraZeneca, as no specific results or outcomes have been disclosed yet.
