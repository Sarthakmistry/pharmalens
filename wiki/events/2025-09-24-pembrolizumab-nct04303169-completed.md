## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04303169 for Advanced Solid Tumors Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-09-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04303169) sponsored by Merck, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with vibostolimab (MK-7684) for advanced solid tumors, has reached its primary completion date on September 24, 2025. The study enrolled 146 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 1/2 trial for pembrolizumab is a neutral event, as no efficacy or safety results have been released at this stage.
