## Amgen's Phase 2 Trial NCT04781543 for Hzn-825 Terminated

**Date:** 2025-02-24 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Amgen's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04781543), which was investigating Hzn-825 bid and Hzn-825 qd, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 301 participants and reached its primary completion date on February 24, 2025, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Hzn-825 is a bearish signal for Amgen's pipeline, indicating a potential setback for the development of this drug.
