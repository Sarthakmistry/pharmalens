## Roche terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT04732286) of atezolizumab and bevacizumab in Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer

**Date:** 2024-04-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Roche has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04732286) evaluating [[atezolizumab]] and [[bevacizumab]] for Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 100 participants, reached its primary completion on April 26, 2024, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 3 trial could be seen as a minor negative signal for Roche's oncology pipeline, particularly for atezolizumab and bevacizumab in NSCLC, despite results being available.
