A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03037385) investigating Pralsetinib (BLU-667) for advanced RET-fusion positive solid tumors, sponsored by [[roche]], reached its primary completion on March 21, 2024. The trial enrolled 590 participants and has reported results, providing new data for this investigational drug.
