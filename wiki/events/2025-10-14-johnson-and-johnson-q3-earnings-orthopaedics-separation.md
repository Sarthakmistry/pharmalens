## Johnson & Johnson Q3 2025 Earnings and Orthopaedics Separation

**Date:** 2025-10-14 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson reported strong third-quarter 2025 sales and earnings, raising its full-year sales and adjusted EPS outlook, and announced its intent to separate its Orthopaedics business. Q3 2025 reported sales grew 6.8% to $24.0 Billion, with adjusted diluted earnings per share (EPS) increasing 10.5% to $2.85. The company also raised its full-year 2025 sales outlook to $97.0-$97.5 Billion and adjusted diluted EPS outlook to $11.00-$11.10.

### Market implication
Johnson & Johnson's Q3 2025 results, coupled with raised full-year guidance and the strategic separation of its Orthopaedics business, signal a positive outlook for the company's financial performance and strategic focus.
