## Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 Trial NCT04848480 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-04-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04848480, investigating Insulin icodec, Insulin degludec, and Insulin aspart, reached its primary completion date on April 28, 2022. The study, which enrolled 582 participants, has now completed its main data collection phase.

### Market implication
This event marks a procedural milestone for Novo Nordisk's clinical development pipeline, with market impact pending the release of trial results.
