## Bristol-Myers Squibb terminates Phase 2 trial of Morab-202 (NCT05577715) in NSCLC

**Date:** 2024-08-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05577715) investigating Morab-202 for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 31 participants, reached its primary completion on August 12, 2024, and results are available.
