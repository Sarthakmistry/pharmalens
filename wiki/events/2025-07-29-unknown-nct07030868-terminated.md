## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial NCT07030868 for Ly6249492

**Date:** 2025-07-29 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT07030868) for the investigational drug Ly6249492, which was being studied for glp1-obesity and type2-diabetes. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of only 1 participant, reached its primary completion date on July 29, 2025, and was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Ly6249492 represents a bearish signal for Eli Lilly's pipeline in glp1-obesity and type2-diabetes, indicating a potential setback in its development.
