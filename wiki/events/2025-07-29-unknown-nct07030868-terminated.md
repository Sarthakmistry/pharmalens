## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial NCT07030868 for investigational drug Ly6249492

**Date:** 2025-07-29 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT07030868) for the investigational drug Ly6249492, which was exploring treatments for glp1-obesity and type2-diabetes. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 1 participant, was terminated on its primary completion date of July 29, 2025. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Ly6249492 for these indications.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an investigational drug represents a bearish signal for Eli Lilly's pipeline in the GLP-1 and type 2 diabetes spaces, indicating a setback in its development efforts.
