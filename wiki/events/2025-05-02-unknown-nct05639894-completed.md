## Sanofi's RSV Vaccine Candidate Trial NCT05639894 Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2025-05-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05639894) sponsored by [[sanofi]], evaluating two RSV vaccine candidate formulations (Rsv vaccine candiate formulation 1 and Rsv vaccine candidate formulation 2), has reached its primary completion date on May 2, 2025. The trial enrolled 865 participants.
