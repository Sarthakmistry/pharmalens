## Efanesoctocog alfa (bivv001) Trial NCT04161495 Completed

**Date:** 2022-02-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04161495) for Efanesoctocog alfa (bivv001) sponsored by Sanofi reached its primary completion date on February 3, 2022. The trial, which enrolled 159 participants, focused on assessing the psychometric properties of the Haem-A-QoL PH and clinically meaningful change thresholds. The results indicate that the Haem-A-QoL PH is a reliable, valid, and responsive measure for assessing interventions in haemophilia A treatment.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial and the positive validation of the Haem-A-QoL PH instrument provide valuable data for future research and clinical assessment in haemophilia A, with a neutral immediate market impact for Sanofi.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 126
- **Comparator:** null
- **Primary outcome:** psychometric properties of Haem-A-QoL PH and clinically meaningful change thresholds
- **Primary result:** Haem-A-QoL PH and Haem-A-QoL total scores demonstrated high internal consistency reliability, construct validity, acceptable test-retest reliability, responsiveness, and discriminated between known groups at baseline and Week 52.
- **Secondary results:**
    - The meaningful within-patient change (MWPC) threshold estimate was -10.0 (range -15.0 to -8.7) for Haem-A-QoL PH
    - The meaningful within-patient change (MWPC) threshold estimate was -8.5 (-8.5 to -4.6) for Haem-A-QoL total scores (using PGIS-Joint)
    - Respective proposed meaningful within-group improvement thresholds were -6.8 (range -4.8 to -6.8) for Haem-A-QoL PH
    - Respective proposed meaningful within-group improvement thresholds were -5.8 (-5.8 to -3.5) for Haem-A-QoL total scores
- **Safety:** null
- **Conclusions:** The Haem-A-QoL PH is a reliable, valid, and responsive measure to assess the impact of interventions in the treatment of haemophilia A.
- **Journal:** Haemophilia, 2026
- **Industry sponsored:** True
