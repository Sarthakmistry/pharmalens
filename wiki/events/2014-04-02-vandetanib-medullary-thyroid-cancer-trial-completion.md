## Vandetanib Medullary Thyroid Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2014-04-02 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT01496313, sponsored by [[sanofi]], comparing two doses of vandetanib in patients with advanced medullary thyroid cancer, has completed. The study compared vandetanib (300mg/day vs 150mg/day) with Overall Response Rate (ORR) as the primary outcome measure. Primary completion occurred on 2014-04-02.
