## Sanofi's Phase 3 Meningococcal Vaccine Trial (NCT06228586) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-03-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06228586) for a Meningococcal polysaccharide (serogroups a, c, w, and y) tetanus toxoid conjugate vaccine reached its primary completion date on March 31, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 447 participants, has now completed data collection for its primary endpoint, indicating a significant milestone in its development.

This trial completion is a neutral signal, as the specific results and their implications for market entry or competitive positioning are not yet available.
