## AbbVie updates 2025 financial guidance due to acquired IPR&D and milestones expense

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie updated its 2025 financial guidance to include an unfavorable impact from acquired IPR&D and milestones expense incurred in the second quarter of 2025. This expense, totaling $823 million pre-tax for Q2 2025, led to a reduction in both GAAP and adjusted non-GAAP diluted earnings per share guidance for the quarter and the full year.

### Market implication
The updated guidance reflects an 'unfavorable impact of $0.42' to both GAAP and adjusted non-GAAP diluted earnings per share due to acquired IPR&D and milestones expense, resulting in a lower full-year EPS guidance range than previously announced, which is a bearish signal for the company's financial outlook.
