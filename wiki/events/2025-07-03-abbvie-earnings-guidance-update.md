## AbbVie lowers 2025 EPS guidance due to unexpected acquired IPR&D expense

**Date:** 2025-07-03 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie updated its 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance, lowering it due to an unexpected $823 million acquired IPR&D and milestones expense incurred in the second quarter of 2025. This expense represents an unfavorable impact of $0.42 to both GAAP and adjusted non-GAAP diluted earnings per share.

### Market implication
The company's full-year 2025 adjusted diluted EPS guidance range was lowered from $12.09 - $12.29 to $11.67 - $11.87, and Q2 2025 guidance was lowered from $3.26 - $3.30 to $2.84 - $2.88, indicating a negative financial impact for investors.
