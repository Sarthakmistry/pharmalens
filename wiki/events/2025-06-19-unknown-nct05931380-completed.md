## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial (NCT05931380) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05931380) for Orforglipron, investigating its efficacy in glp1-obesity, reached its primary completion on June 19, 2025. The trial successfully enrolled 238 participants. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a procedural milestone for Orforglipron, with market implications contingent upon the eventual release and interpretation of its clinical results.
