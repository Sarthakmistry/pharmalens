## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial (NCT05931380) for GLP-1 Obesity Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05931380) investigating Orforglipron for GLP-1 obesity has reached its primary completion date on June 19, 2025. The trial enrolled 238 participants, and while primary completion has been achieved, results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The market will await the release of the trial results to assess the potential efficacy and safety profile of Orforglipron, which could impact Eli Lilly's competitive position in the GLP-1 obesity market.
