## Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Drug Nnc0519-0130 Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06326047) evaluating Nnc0519-0130 for Type 2 Diabetes has completed its primary endpoint. The study, which enrolled 299 participants, reached primary completion on September 19, 2025. Results from this trial are not yet publicly available.

### Market implication
This trial completion is a neutral event for Novo Nordisk, as the market awaits the release of study results to assess the drug's potential in the Type 2 Diabetes landscape.
