## Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 trial for Nnc0519-0130 and Trizepatide in Type 2 Diabetes completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06326047) investigating Nnc0519-0130 and Trizepatide for Type 2 Diabetes has reached its primary completion date on September 19, 2025. The trial enrolled 299 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial's primary endpoint is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication until results are released.
