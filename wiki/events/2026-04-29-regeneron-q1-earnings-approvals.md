## Regeneron Reports Strong Q1 2026 Results with Key Approvals

**Date:** 2026-04-29 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron reported robust first quarter 2026 financial results, with overall revenue increasing by 19% to $3.6 billion. This growth was primarily fueled by strong sales of Dupixent (dupilumab) and EYLEA HD (aflibercept), alongside significant regulatory approvals for Otarmeni (lunsotogene parvec) for genetic hearing loss, EYLEA HD for extended dosing in wAMD and DME, and Dupixent for chronic spontaneous urticaria and allergic fungal rhinosinusitis.

### Market implication
The strong revenue growth, particularly from key products like Dupixent, and multiple new drug approvals indicate a positive outlook for Regeneron's financial performance and pipeline, suggesting a bullish market response.
