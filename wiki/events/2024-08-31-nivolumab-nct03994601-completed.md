## Bristol Myers Squibb Completes Phase 1/2 Trial (NCT03994601) for Nivolumab in Colorectal Cancer

**Date:** 2024-08-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has announced the primary completion of its Phase 1/2 clinical trial, NCT03994601, investigating nivolumab in combination with BMS-986288 or Regorafenib for advanced or metastatic colorectal cancer. The trial enrolled 219 participants and reached its primary completion date on August 31, 2024, with results now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural update for Bristol Myers Squibb's oncology pipeline, with market impact dependent on the eventual disclosure of trial results.
