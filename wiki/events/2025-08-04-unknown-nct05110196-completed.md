## Novartis's Capmatinib Phase 4 Trial (NCT05110196) Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2025-08-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05110196, investigating Capmatinib (150 mg and 200 mg doses), has reached its primary completion date on August 4, 2025. The trial enrolled 50 participants and has not yet reported results. This marks a procedural milestone in the study.
