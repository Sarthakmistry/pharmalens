## Novartis completes Phase 4 trial NCT05110196 for Capmatinib

**Date:** 2025-08-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05110196, on August 4, 2025. The study, which investigated Capmatinib 150 mg and Capmatinib 200 mg, enrolled 50 participants. No results have been released yet for this trial.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a procedural step, with market implications pending the release of study results.
