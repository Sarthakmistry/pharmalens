## Tirzepatide (NCT04255433) Phase 3 Trial Completed for Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2025-06-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04255433) investigating tirzepatide for type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on June 12, 2025. The study enrolled 13,299 participants and compared tirzepatide against dulaglutide. Results from this large-scale trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step. Market impact is neutral until the trial results are published and analyzed, which will provide insights into tirzepatide's efficacy and safety profile in this indication.
