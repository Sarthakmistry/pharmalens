## Eli Lilly's Tirzepatide (NCT04255433) Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-12 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04255433) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating tirzepatide for the treatment of type 2 diabetes, has reached its primary completion date on June 12, 2025. The study enrolled 13,299 participants and compared tirzepatide against dulaglutide and placebo. No results from the trial have been released yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step; market implications will depend on the eventual release and interpretation of the study's results.
