## Eli Lilly Q1 2026 Earnings: Orforglipron FDA Approval and Strong Growth

**Date:** 2026-04-30 | **Type:** fda_approval | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Eli Lilly reported a 56% increase in total revenue for Q1 2026, reaching $19,799 million, primarily driven by strong sales of [[tirzepatide]] (Mounjaro and Zepbound). Concurrently, the FDA approved orforglipron (Foundayo) for the treatment of obesity in April 2026, marking a significant pipeline advancement. The company also provided updates on other pipeline assets and the impact of the Inflation Reduction Act, including the selection of [[abemaciclib]] (Verzenio) and [[dulaglutide]] (Trulicity) for government-set prices.

### Market implication
Eli Lilly's stock is expected to react bullishly due to the substantial revenue and net income growth, exceeding expectations, and the significant FDA approval of Orforglipron, which expands its market presence in obesity.
