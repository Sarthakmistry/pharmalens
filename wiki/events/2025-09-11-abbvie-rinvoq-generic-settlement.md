## AbbVie Announces Settlement Delaying RINVOQ Generic Entry Until April 2037

**Date:** 2025-09-11 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie announced a settlement of litigation with generic manufacturers that had filed abbreviated new drug applications for generic versions of upadacitinib (RINVOQ). This agreement delays generic entry for upadacitinib tablets in the United States until April 2037, assuming pediatric exclusivity is granted. The settlement and license agreements are subject to standard acceleration provisions.

### Market implication
This settlement is bullish for AbbVie as it extends the market exclusivity for RINVOQ, a key product, significantly bolstering its long-term revenue outlook.
