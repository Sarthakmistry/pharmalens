## Dapagliflozin Phase 4 Trial (NCT06327815) for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-06-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT06327815) investigating dapagliflozin for Type 2 Diabetes has reached its primary completion date on June 30, 2025. The trial enrolled 633 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a neutral event, as no results have been released to indicate efficacy or safety changes for dapagliflozin.
