## Eli Lilly Terminates Phase 3 Trial of Lasmiditan (NCT04396236)

**Date:** 2025-11-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04396236) investigating Lasmiditan. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 851 participants, was originally expected to reach primary completion on November 12, 2025. The termination of a late-stage trial typically indicates issues with efficacy, safety, or business strategy.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Lasmiditan is a bearish signal for Eli Lilly, as it removes a potential future revenue stream or indicates a setback in its pipeline.
