## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT04396236) for Lasmiditan

**Date:** 2025-11-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04396236) for the drug Lasmiditan. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 851 participants, was originally set to reach primary completion on November 12, 2025. The termination of a Phase 3 trial typically indicates issues with efficacy, safety, or a strategic business decision.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Lasmiditan represents a negative development for Eli Lilly's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue streams and R&D investment returns for the drug.
