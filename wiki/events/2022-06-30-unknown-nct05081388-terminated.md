## Regeneron terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT05081388 involving Regn14256 and Imdevimab

**Date:** 2022-06-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05081388) on June 30, 2022. The study, which had an actual enrollment of 25 participants, was investigating Regn14256 and Casirivimab + imdevimab. The trial reached its primary completion date on the same day it was terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial for Regn14256 and Casirivimab + imdevimab represents a setback for Regeneron's pipeline in this specific area.
