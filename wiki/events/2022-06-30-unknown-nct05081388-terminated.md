## Regeneron terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT05081388) for Regn14256, Imdevimab, and Casirivimab + imdevimab

**Date:** 2022-06-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
On June 30, 2022, Regeneron terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial NCT05081388, which was investigating Regn14256, Imdevimab, and Casirivimab + imdevimab. The trial had an actual enrollment of 25 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day it was terminated. The termination of an early-phase trial can indicate issues with safety, efficacy, or strategic reprioritization.

### Market implication
The termination of an early-phase trial is generally a bearish signal for the involved drugs and company, as it removes a potential pipeline asset.
