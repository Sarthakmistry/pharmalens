## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT05523323 for Advanced Solid Tumors Completes Primary Endpoint

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05523323) investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Lenvatinib for advanced solid tumors reached its primary completion date on August 25, 2023. The trial, which enrolled 112 participants, has completed its primary data collection, indicating a standard progression in the development pipeline for oncology treatments.
