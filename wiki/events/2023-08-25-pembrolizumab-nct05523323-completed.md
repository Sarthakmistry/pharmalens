## Merck's Pembrolizumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT05523323) for Alzheimer's Disease Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-08-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05523323) sponsored by Merck, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with Lenvatinib for Alzheimer's Disease, reached its primary completion date on August 25, 2023. The trial enrolled 112 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial for pembrolizumab in Alzheimer's Disease is a neutral event, as specific efficacy or safety results have not yet been disclosed.
