## Pembrolizumab Phase 2 Trial (NCT04740307) for Alzheimer's Disease Completed

**Date:** 2025-07-29 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04740307) investigating [[pembrolizumab]] for Alzheimer's disease has reached its primary completion date on July 29, 2025. The trial, which also involved Lenvatinib, enrolled 116 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step, with market impact pending the release of clinical data.
