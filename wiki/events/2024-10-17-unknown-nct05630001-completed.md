## Novartis's Phase 3 Trial NCT05630001 for Iptacopan Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-10-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05630001, investigating Iptacopan, has reached its primary completion date on October 17, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 52 participants, has now concluded its primary data collection phase. Results are expected to be available in the future.
