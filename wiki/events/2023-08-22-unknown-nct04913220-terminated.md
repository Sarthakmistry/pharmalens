## Sanofi terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04913220 for Thor-707 and Cemiplimab

**Date:** 2023-08-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04913220) investigating Thor-707 in combination with Cemiplimab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 46 participants, reached its primary completion on August 22, 2023, and results are available. The termination of a clinical trial typically indicates a strategic shift or issues with efficacy or safety.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial represents a setback for Sanofi's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue streams from the combination therapy.
