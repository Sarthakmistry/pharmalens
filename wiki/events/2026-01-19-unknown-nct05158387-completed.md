## AstraZeneca's Tozorakimab Phase 3 Trial (NCT05158387) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2026-01-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05158387) for the drug Tozorakimab reached its primary completion date on January 19, 2026. The trial successfully enrolled 1172 participants. As of this event, no results from the trial have been publicly reported.

### Market implication
The completion of this large Phase 3 trial represents a significant step forward in AstraZeneca's development pipeline for Tozorakimab, with future market impact dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial's results.
