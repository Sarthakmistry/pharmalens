## AstraZeneca's Tozorakimab Phase 3 Trial (NCT05158387) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-01-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05158387) for the investigational drug Tozorakimab has reached its primary completion date on January 19, 2026. This trial enrolled 1172 participants and marks a significant procedural milestone, indicating that data collection for the primary outcome measures is complete.
