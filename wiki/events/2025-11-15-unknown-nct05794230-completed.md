## Sanofi's Phase 3 Vaccine Trial NCT05794230 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05794230, which investigated various vaccines including Meningococcal, Pneumococcal, MMR, DTaP-IPV-Hib-HepB, Hepatitis A, Rotavirus, Typhoid, and Varicella vaccines, has reached its primary completion date on November 15, 2025. The study successfully enrolled 1528 participants. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
This event is neutral as the trial has completed but no results have been reported yet, thus providing no immediate new data for market valuation.
