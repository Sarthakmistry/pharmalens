## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03622593 for Aflibercept and Faricimab Completed

**Date:** 2020-10-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03622593) sponsored by Roche, investigating Aflibercept and Faricimab, reached its primary completion date on October 19, 2020. The trial successfully enrolled 951 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial makes the data available for analysis, which could inform future development or regulatory submissions for Roche's involved therapies.
