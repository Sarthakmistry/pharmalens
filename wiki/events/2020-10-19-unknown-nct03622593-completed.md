## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03622593 for Aflibercept and Faricimab Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-10-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03622593, investigating Aflibercept and Faricimab, achieved its primary completion on October 19, 2020. The trial enrolled 951 participants, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial allows for the analysis and potential publication of data for Aflibercept and Faricimab, which could inform future development decisions.
