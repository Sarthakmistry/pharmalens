## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04199104 for Advanced Solid Tumors Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-05-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04199104, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with Lenvatinib for advanced solid tumors, reached its primary completion date on May 30, 2023. The trial enrolled 511 participants, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of a clinical trial is a standard procedural step and is generally neutral for market valuation, pending the release and analysis of the trial results.
