## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial NCT04440176 for Meningococcal Vaccine Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-01-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04440176), which evaluated the safety, tolerability, and immunogenicity of a meningococcal ABCWY vaccine in healthy infants and toddlers, completed its primary endpoint on January 5, 2024. The trial successfully enrolled 309 participants.

### Market implication
This completion marks a procedural milestone for Pfizer's vaccine pipeline, with further implications dependent on the eventual release of trial results.
