## Sanofi's Phase 2/3 Trial NCT04074187 Completed

**Date:** 2021-05-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's clinical trial NCT04074187, investigating Caplacizumab (alx-0081) in combination with standard of care (including plasma exchange, corticosteroid treatment, and immunosuppressive treatment) for patients with acquired thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura (aTTP), reached its primary completion on May 19, 2021. This Phase 2/3 study enrolled 21 participants. No results have been posted yet.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial is a procedural step; market impact is neutral until results are announced.
