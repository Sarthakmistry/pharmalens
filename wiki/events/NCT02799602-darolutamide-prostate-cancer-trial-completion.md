## Darolutamide Trial NCT02799602 for Prostate Cancer Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT02799602, investigating Darolutamide in combination with standard ADT and docetaxel for metastatic hormone-sensitive prostate cancer, has completed with results available. The study enrolled 1306 participants and was sponsored by Bayer, with collaborators Orion Corporation and Orion Pharma. The primary outcome measure was Overall Survival (OS) from randomization until death from any cause.
