## Pfizer's Phase 3 Trial NCT05348915 for Inclacumab Terminated

**Date:** 2025-11-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05348915, which was investigating Inclacumab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 242 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 7, 2025, but was subsequently terminated without results being reported.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Inclacumab represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue expectations for the drug.
