## Pfizer's Inclacumab Phase 3 Trial (NCT05348915) Terminated

**Date:** 2025-11-07 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05348915 for the drug Inclacumab has been officially terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 242 participants and was originally slated for primary completion on November 7, 2025. This termination signifies a halt in the development of Inclacumab.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 clinical trial for Inclacumab represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue projections associated with this investigational asset.
