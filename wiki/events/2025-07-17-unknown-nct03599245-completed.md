## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT03599245 for Ocrelizumab Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-07-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03599245, investigating Ocrelizumab, has reached its primary completion date on July 17, 2025. The trial successfully enrolled 1055 participants and has now completed the primary data collection phase. This milestone indicates the trial is progressing as planned towards data analysis and eventual results.

### Market implication
This event is a neutral development, as trial completion is a standard step in the drug development process and results are not yet available to assess efficacy or safety.
