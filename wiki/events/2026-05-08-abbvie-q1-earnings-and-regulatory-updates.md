## AbbVie Q1 2026 Earnings Show Strong Growth for Skyrizi and Rinvoq Amidst Regulatory Updates

**Date:** 2026-05-08 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
AbbVie reported strong Q1 2026 financial results with 12% revenue growth, driven by risankizumab (Skyrizi) and upadacitinib (Rinvoq). Key regulatory updates included FDA approval for venetoclax (Venclexta) in combination with acalabrutinib for CLL, multiple new indication submissions for Skyrizi and Rinvoq, and a Complete Response Letter for trenibotulinumtoxinE (TrenibotE). The company also announced significant R&D and capital investments.

### Market implication
The strong performance of key growth drivers like risankizumab (Skyrizi) and upadacitinib (Rinvoq), coupled with strategic regulatory advancements and significant investment plans, suggests a positive outlook for AbbVie's valuation despite some product declines and a regulatory setback for TrenibotE.
