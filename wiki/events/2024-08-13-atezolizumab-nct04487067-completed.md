## Roche's Atezolizumab Trial NCT04487067 Completed

**Date:** 2024-08-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04487067, investigating atezolizumab (and bevacizumab) has reached its primary completion date on August 13, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 152 participants, is now complete and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of a Phase 3 trial is a standard development milestone; market implications will depend on the eventual publication and analysis of the trial results.
