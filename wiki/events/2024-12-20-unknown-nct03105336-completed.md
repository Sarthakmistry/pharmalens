## Gilead's Phase 2 Trial NCT03105336 for Axicabtagene Ciloleucel Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-12-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03105336), investigating Axicabtagene ciloleucel in combination with Cyclophosphamide and Fludarabine, has reached its primary completion date on December 20, 2024. The trial enrolled 159 participants and results are now available, marking a key milestone in the development of the therapy.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial indicates progress in Gilead's clinical pipeline, moving the therapy closer to potential further development or regulatory consideration.
