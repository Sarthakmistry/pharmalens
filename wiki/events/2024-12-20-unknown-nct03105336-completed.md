## Gilead's Phase 2 Trial NCT03105336 for Axicabtagene Ciloleucel Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-12-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Gilead's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03105336), investigating Axicabtagene Ciloleucel in combination with Cyclophosphamide and Fludarabine, reached its primary completion date on December 20, 2024. The trial successfully enrolled 159 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a pipeline progression milestone for Gilead, with future market implications dependent on the eventual disclosure and nature of the trial results.
