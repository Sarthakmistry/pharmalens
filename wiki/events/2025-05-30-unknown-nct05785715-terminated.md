## AbbVie terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT05785715) for Nx-13

**Date:** 2025-05-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05785715) investigating Nx-13 250mg and Nx-13 750mg. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 81 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 30, 2025, but was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Nx-13 could be seen as a minor negative signal for [[abbvie]]'s pipeline, indicating a potential setback in its development efforts for this compound.
