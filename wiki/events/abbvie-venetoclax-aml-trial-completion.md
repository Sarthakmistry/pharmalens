## AbbVie's Phase 3 Venetoclax Trial for AML Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03069352) sponsored by AbbVie, studying venetoclax in combination with low dose cytarabine for treatment-naive acute myeloid leukemia patients, has completed. The trial aimed to evaluate if venetoclax with LDAC improves overall survival (OS) versus LDAC and placebo in patients ineligible for intensive chemotherapy.
