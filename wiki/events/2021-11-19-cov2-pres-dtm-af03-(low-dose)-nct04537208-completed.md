## Sanofi's CoV2-PreS-dTM Vaccine Candidate Phase 1/2 Trial NCT04537208 Completes with Immunogenicity Findings

**Date:** 2021-11-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 1/2 trial (NCT04537208) for its CoV2-PreS-dTM vaccine candidate, including formulations like Cov2 pres dtm-af03 (low-dose) and Cov2 pres dtm-as03 (high-dose), reached primary completion on November 19, 2021. The study investigated the cellular immunogenicity profile, finding similar vaccine-induced responses across doses and a Th1 bias for most treatment groups.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides initial immunogenicity data for Sanofi's vaccine candidate, with neutral immediate market implications.

### Affected entities
- Drug: Cov2 pres dtm-af03 (low-dose), Cov2 pres dtm-af03 (high-dose), Cov2 pres dtm-as03 (low-dose), Cov2 pres dtm-as03 (high-dose), Cov2 pres dtm (high-dose) without adjuvant
- Company: [[sanofi]]
- Indication: None

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 90 patients
- **Comparator:** saline placebo
- **Primary outcome:** cellular immunogenicity profile of CoV2-PreS-dTM vaccine candidate
- **Primary result:** Both methods detected similar vaccine-induced responses after the first and second doses. A Th1 bias (Th1/Th2 ratio > 1.0) was observed for most treatment groups when analysed in whole blood, mainly characterised by increased IFN-γ, IL-2 and TNF-α secretion.
- **Secondary results:**
    - Among participants aged ≥ 50 years, the Th1/Th2 ratio was higher for those who received vaccine candidate with AS03 versus AF03 adjuvant.
    - ICS revealed that this higher Th1/Th2 ratio resulted from higher levels of IFN-γ expression and that the vaccine induced polyfunctional CD4+ T cells.
- **Safety:** None
- **Conclusions:** The whole-blood cytokine secretion assay is a high-throughput alternative for assessing the quantity and character of vaccine-induced cellular responses.
- **Journal:** Clin Transl Immunology, 2022
- **Industry sponsored:** True
