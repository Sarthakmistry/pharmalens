## Eli Lilly and Company Elects Carolyn R. Bertozzi to Board of Directors

**Date:** 2025-11-18 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company elected Carolyn R. Bertozzi, Ph. D as a new independent member of its Board of Directors, effective December 8, 2025. Dr. Bertozzi, a Nobel laureate and professor at Stanford University, will serve on the Board's Science and Technology Committee and the Ethics and Compliance Committee.

### Market implication
This event is a standard corporate governance update and is not expected to have a direct impact on Eli Lilly's market valuation.
