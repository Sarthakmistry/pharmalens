## Gilead's Obeldesivir Trial (NCT06585150) Terminated

**Date:** 2025-05-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06585150) for the drug Obeldesivir, sponsored by Gilead, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 150 participants and was originally set to complete its primary phase on May 8, 2025. The termination indicates a halt in the development of Obeldesivir.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Obeldesivir is a negative development for Gilead's pipeline, suggesting potential issues with the drug's efficacy, safety, or strategic fit, which could impact future revenue projections.
