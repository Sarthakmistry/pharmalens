## Sanofi terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04659603 for Tusamitamab ravtansine in breast cancer

**Date:** 2024-02-16 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04659603) investigating Tusamitamab ravtansine in combination with Gemcitabine for breast cancer. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 50 participants, reached its primary completion on February 16, 2024, and results are available. Trial termination often indicates issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic business decisions.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an investigational drug is generally a negative signal for the developing company, potentially impacting future pipeline expectations.
