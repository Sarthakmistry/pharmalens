## Merck's Phase 3 TECOS Trial (NCT00092534) for Sitagliptin in Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2007-07-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck announced the completion of its Phase 3 TECOS (Trial Evaluating Cardiovascular Outcomes with Sitagliptin) study (NCT00092534) for sitagliptin. This randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial, which enrolled 12,167 participants, investigated the cardiovascular safety of sitagliptin in patients with type 2 diabetes. The trial reached its primary completion date on July 31, 2007.

### Market implication
The completion of this large cardiovascular outcomes trial provides important safety data for sitagliptin, a key drug in the type 2 diabetes market.
