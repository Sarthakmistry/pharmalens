## Takeda's Tetravalent Dengue Vaccine (TDV) Phase 3 Trial (NCT02747927) Completes

**Date:** 2018-07-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Takeda's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT02747927) for its Tetravalent dengue vaccine (tdv) reached its primary completion date on July 11, 2018. This large-scale study enrolled 20099 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this significant Phase 3 trial indicates progress in the development of Takeda's dengue vaccine candidate, moving it closer to potential regulatory submission and market entry.
