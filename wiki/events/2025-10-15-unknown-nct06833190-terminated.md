## Regeneron Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT06833190) for Regn5381 in Heart Failure and Hypertension

**Date:** 2025-10-15 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06833190) for the investigational drug Regn5381. The trial, which was studying Regn5381 for heart failure and hypertension, reached its primary completion date on October 15, 2025, with an actual enrollment of 34 participants.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an investigational drug typically represents a setback for the company's pipeline in the affected indication.
