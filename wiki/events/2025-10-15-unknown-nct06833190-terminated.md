## Regeneron terminates Phase 2 trial NCT06833190 for Regn5381 in heart failure

**Date:** 2025-10-15 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Regeneron has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06833190, which was investigating the drug Regn5381 for heart failure (HF-HTN). The trial had an actual enrollment of 34 participants and was originally set for primary completion on October 15, 2025, but was terminated prior to reaching this milestone.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Regn5381 is a negative signal for Regeneron's pipeline in heart failure, potentially impacting future revenue projections for this specific asset.
