## Novartis's Phase 3 Trial NCT03867201 for Erenumab Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2021-08-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT03867201, investigating Erenumab, reached its primary completion date on August 11, 2021. The study, which enrolled 557 participants, has now concluded its main data collection phase, with results available. This milestone indicates progress in the development of Erenumab.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides Novartis with critical data for Erenumab, which will inform its future development and potential regulatory submissions.
