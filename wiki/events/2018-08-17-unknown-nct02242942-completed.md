## Roche completes Phase 3 clinical trial NCT022242942

**Date:** 2018-08-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT02242942, sponsored by Roche, reached its primary completion on August 17, 2018. This Phase 3 study, which enrolled 445 participants, investigated Chlorambucil, Venetoclax, and Obinutuzumab. Results for this trial are available.

### Market implication
The completion of a Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step; market implications will depend on the published results and their clinical significance.
