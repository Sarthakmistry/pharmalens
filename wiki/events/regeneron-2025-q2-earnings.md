## Regeneron Reports Strong Q2 2025 Financial Results with Increased Revenue and EPS

**Date:** 2025-08-01 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals reported total revenue of $6,704.3 million, net income of $2,200.3 million, and diluted EPS of $20.02 for the six months ended June 30, 2025. Combined sales of EYLEA HD and EYLEA reached $2,190.3 million, contributing to the overall positive financial performance.

### Market implication
The increase in total revenue, net income, and diluted EPS compared to the same period in 2024 indicates strong financial health and positive operational momentum for Regeneron.
