## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2 Trial (NCT05007106) for Breast Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2025-08-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05007106) evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with other therapies for breast cancer has reached its primary completion date on August 5, 2025. The trial enrolled 613 participants and has completed its primary data collection, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step, and without results, it has a neutral market implication.
