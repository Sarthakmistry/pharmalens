## Novartis completes Phase 3 trial of sacubitril-valsartan for hypertension

**Date:** 2025-05-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has completed a Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06236061) investigating sacubitril-valsartan (referred to as LCZ/AML in the trial) for hypertension. The trial, which enrolled 718 participants, reached its primary completion date on May 28, 2025. Results from this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard development for Novartis's sacubitril-valsartan program, with market impact pending the release of clinical results.
