## Novartis's Phase 3 Trial (NCT06236061) for Hypertension and Heart Failure Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2025-05-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06236061), investigating Lcz/aml 200 mg/2.5 mg, Lcz/aml 200 mg/5 mg, and Lcz/aml 200 mg/10 mg for the treatment of hypertension and heart failure, reached its primary completion date on May 28, 2025. The study enrolled 718 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
This completion marks a step forward in the development process for Novartis's Lcz/aml, with market impact pending the release of trial results.
