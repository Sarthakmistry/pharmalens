## AstraZeneca's Azd5462 Phase 2 Trial for HF-HTN Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-02-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06299826) for the drug Azd5462, targeting heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN), reached its primary completion date on February 10, 2026. The trial enrolled 375 participants. This milestone indicates the completion of data collection for the primary outcome, though results have not yet been reported.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral event, as results are not yet available to assess the drug's efficacy or safety, and therefore, its potential market impact.
