## Merck's Phase 2 Trial (NCT06664801) for Sotatercept in Heart Failure Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-11-20 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06664801, investigating Sotatercept for heart failure (HF-HTN) has reached its primary completion date on November 20, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 164 participants, has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. Results are not yet available.
