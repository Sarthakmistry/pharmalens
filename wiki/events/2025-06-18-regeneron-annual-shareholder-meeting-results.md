## Regeneron Annual Shareholder Meeting Results

**Date:** 2025-06-18 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. announced the results of its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Key outcomes included the election of Class I directors (Bonnie L. Bassler, Michael S. Brown, Leonard S. Schleifer, and George D. Yancopoulos) to serve until 2028, ratification of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as independent auditors, and advisory approval of executive compensation. Shareholders also approved an amendment to declassify the board of directors, but proposals to amend Article IV, Section 2(e)(8) relating to mandatory conversion of Class A Stock and Article VI relating to director removal for cause were not approved.

### Market implication
The reported outcomes of the shareholder meeting reflect standard corporate governance procedures and do not indicate a direct positive or negative impact on Regeneron's market valuation or operational performance.
