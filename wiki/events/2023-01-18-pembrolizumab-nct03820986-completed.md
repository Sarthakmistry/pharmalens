## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT03820986 Completed

**Date:** 2023-01-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03820986, evaluating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Lenvatinib, has reached primary completion on January 18, 2023. This trial, sponsored by [[merck]], enrolled 674 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development of [[pembrolizumab]], with results now available for evaluation.
