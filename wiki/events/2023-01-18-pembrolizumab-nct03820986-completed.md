## Merck's Pembrolizumab Trial NCT03820986 for Advanced Solid Tumors Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2023-01-18 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03820986, evaluating [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda) in combination with Lenvatinib for advanced solid tumors, including [[oncology-nsclc]] and [[oncology-crc]], has reached its primary completion date on January 18, 2023. The Phase 3 study, sponsored by [[merck]], enrolled 674 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial for [[pembrolizumab]] in advanced solid tumors is a standard procedural step, indicating progress in its development pipeline for [[merck]].
