## AstraZeneca's Tezepelumab Trial NCT05274815 Reaches Primary Completion

AstraZeneca's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05274815, investigating Tezepelumab, reached its primary completion date on September 9, 2024. The trial enrolled 305 participants and has reported having results. This marks a significant milestone in the development of Tezepelumab.
