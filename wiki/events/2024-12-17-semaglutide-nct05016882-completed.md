## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT05016882) for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2024-12-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05016882), investigating semaglutide for the treatment of type 2 diabetes and obesity, reached its primary completion date on December 17, 2024. The study, which enrolled 698 participants, aimed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of semaglutide in individuals with type 2 diabetes who are also overweight or obese. This milestone signifies the conclusion of the primary data collection phase for the trial.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step in drug development, with market implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial's results.
