## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT04777201 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-09-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04777201, investigating Faricimab and anti-VEGF therapy for a retinal condition, reached its primary completion on September 3, 2024. The trial successfully enrolled 1036 participants and has reported results.
