## AstraZeneca's AZD4573 and Acalabrutinib Trial (NCT04630756) for Alzheimer's Disease Completed

**Date:** 2023-09-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04630756) sponsored by AstraZeneca, investigating AZD4573 and Acalabrutinib for Alzheimer's Disease, reached its primary completion on September 8, 2023. The trial enrolled 40 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides an update on AstraZeneca's pipeline in Alzheimer's disease, with further implications dependent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
