## Bristol-Myers Squibb Q3 2025 Earnings Show Significant Increase in Net Earnings and EPS

**Date:** 2025-10-30 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Bristol-Myers Squibb Company announced its financial results for the third quarter of 2025, reporting increased net earnings and diluted EPS compared to the prior year. Net earnings attributable to BMS for diluted EPS calculation rose to $2,201 million for Q3 2025 from $1,211 million for Q3 2024, with diluted earnings per share increasing to $1.08 from $0.60.

### Market implication
The substantial year-over-year growth in net earnings and diluted EPS indicates strong financial performance and is expected to generate positive investor sentiment for Bristol-Myers Squibb.
