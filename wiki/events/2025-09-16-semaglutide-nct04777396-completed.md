## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial for Alzheimer's Disease (NCT04777396) Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2025-09-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04777396) investigating semaglutide for Alzheimer's disease completed its primary data collection on September 16, 2025. The trial enrolled an actual total of 1840 participants. This milestone signifies the completion of the data collection phase for the primary outcome measures, moving the drug closer to potential results disclosure and regulatory evaluation for this indication.
