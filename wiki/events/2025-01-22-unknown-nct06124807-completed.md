## Eli Lilly's Mazdutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT06124807) for GLP-1 Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2025-01-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06124807) investigating Mazdutide for GLP-1 obesity has reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2025. The trial enrolled 179 participants and has indicated that results are available. This marks a significant procedural milestone in the drug's development pipeline.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard development step for Eli Lilly's Mazdutide, with market attention now turning to the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
