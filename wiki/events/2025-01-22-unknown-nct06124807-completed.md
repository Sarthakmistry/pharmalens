## Eli Lilly's Phase 2 Trial for Mazdutide in Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-01-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06124807) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating Mazdutide for glp1-obesity, reached its primary completion date on January 22, 2025. The trial enrolled 179 participants and has indicated that results are available, though not yet published.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a step forward in Mazdutide's development, with future data readouts expected to inform its market potential.
