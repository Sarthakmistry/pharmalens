## Novartis terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05722522 for Secukinumab

**Date:** 2024-10-17 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05722522) for Secukinumab. The trial, which had enrolled 33 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 17, 2024, and results are available, indicating the termination occurred after data collection.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even with results available, typically signals a setback for the drug's development program and could negatively impact Novartis's pipeline valuation.
