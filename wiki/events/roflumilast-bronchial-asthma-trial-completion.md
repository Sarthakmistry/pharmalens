## Roflumilast Bronchial Asthma Trial Completion

**Date:** 2007-10-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A long-term Phase 3 study (APTA-2217-07) investigating the safety and efficacy of Roflumilast in Japanese patients with Bronchial Asthma completed on 2007-10-01. The study was an extension of a previous 24-week study, adding 28 weeks of treatment, and enrolled 150 patients.
