## AstraZeneca's Phase 1/2 Trial NCT02499328 for Alzheimer's Disease Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2020-02-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's clinical trial NCT02499328, a Phase 1/2 study investigating multiple drugs including Azd9150, Medi4736, Azd5069, and Tremelimumab for Alzheimer's disease, reached its primary completion date on February 28, 2020. The trial enrolled 340 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial provides an update on AstraZeneca's early-stage pipeline in Alzheimer's disease, with results now available for further evaluation.
