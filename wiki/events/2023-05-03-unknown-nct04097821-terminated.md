## Novartis terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04097821

**Date:** 2023-05-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT04097821) which had an actual enrollment of 45 participants. The trial, involving drugs such as Ruxolitinib, Siremadlin, Crizanlizumab, Sabatolimab, Rineterkib, and Nis793, reached its primary completion date on May 3, 2023, and results are available. The termination of an early-phase trial often suggests issues with safety, efficacy, or a strategic shift in the company's pipeline.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 1/2 trial could be viewed as a bearish signal for Novartis, indicating potential setbacks in its early-stage pipeline or a reprioritization of its development efforts.
