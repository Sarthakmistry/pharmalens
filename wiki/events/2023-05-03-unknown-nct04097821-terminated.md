## Novartis terminates Phase 1/2 trial NCT04097821

**Date:** 2023-05-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial, NCT04097821, which had an actual enrollment of 45 participants. The trial, which reached its primary completion on May 3, 2023, involved several investigational drugs including Ruxolitinib, Siremadlin, Crizanlizumab, Sabatolimab, Rineterkib, and Nis793. Results from the terminated study are available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline, potentially impacting future development in the therapeutic areas targeted by the investigational drugs.
