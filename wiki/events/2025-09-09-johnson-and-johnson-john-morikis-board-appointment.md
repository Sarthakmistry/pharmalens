## Johnson & Johnson Appoints John Morikis to Board of Directors

**Date:** 2025-09-09 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the election of John Morikis, former CEO of The Sherwin-Williams Company, to its Board of Directors, where he will serve on the Audit Committee and Compensation & Benefits Committee. Mr. Morikis's appointment was effective September 8, 2025, and the Board determined he meets NYSE independence requirements.

### Market implication
This announcement concerns a routine corporate governance matter, the election of a new independent director to the board, and is therefore expected to have a neutral market impact.
