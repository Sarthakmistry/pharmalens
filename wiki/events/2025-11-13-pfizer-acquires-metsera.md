## Pfizer Acquires Metsera for $7.0 Billion, Expanding GLP-1 Pipeline

**Date:** 2025-11-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Pfizer Inc. has completed the acquisition of Metsera, Inc. for an enterprise value of approximately $7.0 billion. The deal includes potential additional payments of up to $20.65 per share in Contingent Value Rights (CVRs) tied to clinical and regulatory milestones for Metsera's GLP-1 receptor antagonist (MET-097i) and amylin analog (MET-233i) pipeline, which targets obesity and diabetes.

### Market implication
This acquisition significantly strengthens Pfizer's pipeline in the lucrative GLP-1 and obesity/diabetes markets, indicating a strong belief in the future value of Metsera's assets and potentially boosting Pfizer's long-term growth prospects.
