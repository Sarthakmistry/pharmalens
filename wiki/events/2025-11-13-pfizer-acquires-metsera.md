## Pfizer acquires Metsera, Inc. for $7.0 billion, expanding GLP-1 and amylin analog pipeline

**Date:** 2025-11-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Pfizer Inc. completed the acquisition of Metsera, Inc. for an enterprise value of approximately $7.0 billion in cash at closing. The deal includes potential additional payments tied to clinical and regulatory milestones for Metsera's pipeline, which features GLP-1 receptor antagonist MET-097i and amylin analog MET-233i, targeting obesity and type 2 diabetes.

### Market implication
This strategic acquisition by Pfizer, involving significant upfront and potential milestone payments for Metsera's GLP-1 and amylin analog pipeline, signals a strong commitment to a high-growth therapeutic area and is expected to positively impact Pfizer's long-term growth prospects.
