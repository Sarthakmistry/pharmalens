## Bristol Myers Squibb terminates Phase 4 trial NCT05369832 for Ozanimod

**Date:** 2025-01-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial NCT05369832, which was studying Ozanimod and had an actual enrollment of 139 participants. The trial reached its primary completion date on January 21, 2025, and results are available.

### Market implication
