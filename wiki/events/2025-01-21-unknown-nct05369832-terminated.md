## Bristol Myers Squibb terminates Phase 4 trial NCT05369832 for Ozanimod

**Date:** 2025-01-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05369832, which involved the drug Ozanimod. The trial had an actual enrollment of 139 participants and reached its primary completion on January 21, 2025, with results now available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 4 trial, even with results available, generally represents a bearish signal for Bristol Myers Squibb, as it could indicate issues with the drug's profile or strategic repositioning.
