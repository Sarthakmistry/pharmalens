## Vertex Pharmaceuticals VX-661 Ivacaftor Cystic Fibrosis Trial Completion

**Date:** 2016-05-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Vertex Pharmaceuticals completed a Phase 2 study (NCT02070744) evaluating the safety and efficacy of VX-661 in combination with ivacaftor for cystic fibrosis patients homozygous for the F508del-CFTR mutation.

### Market implication
The completion of the trial indicates progress in the development pipeline for Vertex Pharmaceuticals, but without specific efficacy or safety results, the market implication remains neutral.
