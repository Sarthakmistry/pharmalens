## AstraZeneca's Phase 2 trial NCT05516498 for dapagliflozin terminated

**Date:** 2025-05-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05516498) investigating dapagliflozin in combination with zibotentan, which had an actual enrollment of 205 participants, has been terminated. The trial's primary completion date was 2025-05-22, and no results have been reported.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for dapagliflozin, even in combination with another drug, represents a negative signal for AstraZeneca's pipeline, as it indicates a halt in development for this specific therapeutic approach.
