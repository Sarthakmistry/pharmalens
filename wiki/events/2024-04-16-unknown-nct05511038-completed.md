## Bayer's Phase 4 Aflibercept Trial (NCT05511038) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-04-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bayer's Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05511038), investigating Aflibercept (eylea, vegf trap-eye, bay86-5321), has reached its primary completion date on April 16, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 100 participants, is now complete and has results available. This marks a significant milestone in the development timeline for Aflibercept.

The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a standard procedural step and its market implication remains neutral until the trial results are publicly disclosed.
