## Merck's Phase 3 Trial NCT04223791 for HIV Treatment Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2021-08-26 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04223791, investigating the safety and efficacy of Dor/isl and Bic/ftc/taf for HIV treatment, reached its primary completion date on August 26, 2021. The trial enrolled 643 participants and has reported results.
