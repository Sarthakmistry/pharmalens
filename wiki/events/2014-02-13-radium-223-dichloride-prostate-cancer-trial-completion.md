## Radium-223 Dichloride Prostate Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2014-02-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase III study of Radium-223 Dichloride (ALpharadin) in patients with symptomatic hormone refractory prostate cancer with skeletal metastases has completed. The trial, NCT00699751, was sponsored by Bayer and enrolled 921 patients, with Overall Survival as the primary outcome.
