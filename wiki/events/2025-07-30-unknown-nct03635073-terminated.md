## Takeda Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT03635073) for Soticlestat

**Date:** 2025-07-30 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03635073) investigating Soticlestat. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 156 participants, reached its primary completion on July 30, 2025. The termination indicates a halt in the development of Soticlestat for its intended indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Soticlestat represents a setback for Takeda's pipeline, potentially impacting its long-term development strategy.
