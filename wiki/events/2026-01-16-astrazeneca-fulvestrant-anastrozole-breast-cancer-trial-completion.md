## AstraZeneca Completes Phase 3 Trial of Fulvestrant vs. Anastrozole in Breast Cancer

**Date:** 2026-01-16 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 3 study comparing Fulvestrant and Anastrozole in hormone receptor-positive breast cancer patients has completed. This interventional study, sponsored by AstraZeneca, enrolled 462 patients and aimed to compare Progression-Free Survival (PFS) between the two treatments.

### Market implication
The market implication is neutral as no trial results have been released to indicate efficacy or safety outcomes.
