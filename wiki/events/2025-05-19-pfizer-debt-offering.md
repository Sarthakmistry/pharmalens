## Pfizer Netherlands International Finance B.V. Completes €3.3 Billion Public Debt Offering

**Date:** 2025-05-19 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer Netherlands International Finance B.V., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Pfizer Inc., completed a public offering of €3.3 billion aggregate principal amount of notes due 2029, 2032, 2037, and 2045. These notes are fully and unconditionally guaranteed by Pfizer Inc.
