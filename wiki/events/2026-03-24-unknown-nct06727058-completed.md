## Sanofi's Pandemic Flu H5 mRNA Vaccine Trial (NCT06727058) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2026-03-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT06727058) for Sanofi's Pandemic flu h5 mRNA vaccine has reached its primary completion date on March 24, 2026. The trial, which enrolled 276 participants, has completed its data collection for the primary outcome measures. Results for this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
This event is neutral as it signifies a procedural milestone in the clinical development of the vaccine without the release of efficacy or safety data.
