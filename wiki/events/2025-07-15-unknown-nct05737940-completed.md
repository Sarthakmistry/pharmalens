## AstraZeneca's Azd3427 Phase 2 Trial (NCT05737940) for Heart Failure and Hypertension Completes Primary Completion

AstraZeneca's Phase 2 trial (NCT05737940) for Azd3427, targeting heart failure and hypertension, reached its primary completion on July 15, 2025. The trial enrolled 260 participants. Results from this study are not yet available.
