## Nivolumab and Atezolizumab Trial NCT05116202 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-09-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT05116202, a Phase 1/2 study investigating the combination of [[nivolumab]], [[atezolizumab]], Ipilimumab, Tiragolumab, Ro7247669 2100 mg, and Ro7247669 600 mg in participants with advanced solid tumors, has reached its primary completion date on September 22, 2023. The trial enrolled 110 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this early-phase oncology trial provides an update on the development timeline for these assets, with full results anticipated to be published in 2025.
