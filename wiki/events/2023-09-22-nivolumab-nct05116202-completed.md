## Trial NCT05116202 for Nivolumab and Atezolizumab in Advanced Solid Tumors Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-09-22 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05116202) evaluating the combination of [[nivolumab]] (Bristol-Myers Squibb) and [[atezolizumab]] (Roche) for advanced solid tumors reached its primary completion on September 22, 2023. The trial enrolled 110 participants and has reported results. This event marks a procedural milestone in the development of these immunotherapies for oncology.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural step and its market impact is neutral until specific results are released and analyzed.
