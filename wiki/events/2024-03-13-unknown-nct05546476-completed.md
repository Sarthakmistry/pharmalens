## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial (NCT05546476) for Ponsegromab in Oncology Completed

**Date:** 2024-03-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer has announced the completion of its Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT05546476, investigating Ponsegromab for oncology indications, specifically non-small cell lung cancer and colorectal cancer. The trial, which enrolled 187 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 13, 2024, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Pfizer's oncology pipeline, with further market impact dependent on the disclosed results.
