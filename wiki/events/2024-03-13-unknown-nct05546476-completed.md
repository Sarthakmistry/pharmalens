## Pfizer's Phase 2 trial (NCT05546476) for Ponsegromab in oncology indications completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-03-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05546476) investigating Ponsegromab for oncology indications, including non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) and colorectal cancer (CRC), reached its primary completion date on March 13, 2024. The study successfully enrolled 187 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard development in the drug development process, and the neutral signal reflects the absence of specific efficacy or safety data in this update.
