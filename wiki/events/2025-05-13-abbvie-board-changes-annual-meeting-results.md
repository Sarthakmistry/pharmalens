## AbbVie Announces Board Changes and Annual Meeting Results

**Date:** 2025-05-13 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AbbVie Inc. announced changes to its Board of Directors, including the appointment of Thomas J. Falk and upcoming retirements, and reported the results of its 2025 Annual Meeting of Stockholders. Key outcomes included director elections, ratification of the independent registered public accounting firm, and advisory approval of executive compensation. Stockholders did not approve proposals regarding supermajority voting or simple majority voting.

### Market implication
The routine corporate governance updates and annual meeting results are not expected to have a significant impact on AbbVie's market valuation.
