## Semaglutide (NCT04560998) Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completed

**Date:** 2024-06-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04560998) investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes has reached its primary completion date on June 5, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, enrolled 792 participants and has indicated that results are available. This marks a significant step towards potential data readout and publication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step for Novo Nordisk's semaglutide program. Market implications are neutral until the trial results are publicly disclosed and analyzed.
