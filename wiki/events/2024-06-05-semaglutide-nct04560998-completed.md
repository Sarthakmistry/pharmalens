## Semaglutide (NCT04560998) Phase 3 Trial Completed for Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2024-06-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04560998) evaluating semaglutide for the treatment of type 2 diabetes, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, has reached its primary completion date on June 5, 2024. The trial enrolled 792 participants and is expected to have results available in the future.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard step in drug development, paving the way for future data readouts that could impact market perception of semaglutide's efficacy and safety profile in type 2 diabetes.
