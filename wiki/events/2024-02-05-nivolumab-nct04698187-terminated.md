## Nivolumab Phase 2 trial for Alzheimer's disease (NCT04698187) terminated

**Date:** 2024-02-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04698187) investigating [[nivolumab]] for Alzheimer's disease, sponsored by [[regeneron]], was terminated on February 5, 2024. The trial had enrolled 44 participants and reached its primary completion date on the day of termination.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for [[nivolumab]] in Alzheimer's disease is a bearish signal for its potential expansion into this indication for both [[regeneron]] and [[bristol-myers-squibb]].
