## Nivolumab trial NCT04698187 for Alzheimer's disease terminated

**Date:** 2024-02-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04698187) investigating nivolumab for Alzheimer's disease, sponsored by Regeneron, was terminated on February 5, 2024. The trial had an actual enrollment of 44 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day as its termination.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a negative development for nivolumab's potential expansion into the Alzheimer's disease indication, removing a potential future revenue stream for Regeneron.
