## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 Trial for Type 2 Diabetes Completes Primary Endpoint Collection

**Date:** 2025-09-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06109311) for Orforglipron in Type 2 Diabetes has reached its primary completion date on September 15, 2025. The trial, which enrolled 546 participants, has completed data collection for its primary endpoint. Results from this study are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as results are not yet available, but it moves Orforglipron closer to potential data readout and future regulatory considerations.
