## Eli Lilly's Orforglipron Phase 3 trial for Type 2 Diabetes completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06109311) for Orforglipron in Type 2 Diabetes has reached its primary completion date on September 15, 2025. The trial enrolled 546 participants and has not yet reported results.
