## Sanofi's Trial NCT05878093 for Dupilumab and Budesonide Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-09-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05878093, investigating Dupilumab and Budesonide, reached its primary completion on September 6, 2024. The trial enrolled 63 participants and has reported that results are available.
