## AbbVie terminates Phase 2 trial (NCT06032546) for Budigalimab/Abbv-382

**Date:** 2025-09-22 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
AbbVie has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06032546) investigating Budigalimab and Abbv-382. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 163 participants, reached its primary completion date on September 22, 2025, but was subsequently terminated. No results have been posted.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for Budigalimab/Abbv-382 is a negative signal for AbbVie's pipeline in this area, indicating potential issues with efficacy, safety, or strategic priorities.
