## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2 trial for investigational drug Ly3549492

**Date:** 2025-08-14 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT07085468) for the investigational drug Ly3549492. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of only one participant, reached its primary completion on the same date as its termination.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial, especially with minimal enrollment, suggests potential issues with the drug's development or strategic reprioritization, which is a bearish signal for Eli Lilly's pipeline in this area.
