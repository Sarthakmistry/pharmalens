## Eli Lilly's Phase 2 trial for Ly3549492 (NCT07085468) terminated

**Date:** 2025-08-14 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT07085468) for the drug Ly3549492 was officially terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of only one participant, was originally slated for primary completion on August 14, 2025. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Ly3549492.
