## Eli Lilly Reports Results of 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders

**Date:** 2025-05-05 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly and Company reported the results of its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Shareholders approved the election of directors, executive compensation on an advisory basis, and the ratification of Ernst & Young LLP as the independent auditor for 2025. However, two proposals to amend the Articles of Incorporation, one to eliminate the classified board structure and another to eliminate supermajority voting provisions, failed to receive the required 80% vote of outstanding shares.

### Market implication
This event reflects routine corporate governance matters and is expected to have a neutral impact on Eli Lilly's market valuation.
