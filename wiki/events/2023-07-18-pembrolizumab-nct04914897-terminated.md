## Phase 2 trial of pembrolizumab (NCT04914897) in NSCLC terminated

**Date:** 2023-07-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04914897, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with Thor-707 for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), was terminated on July 18, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 106 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day. Results are available, but the specific reason for termination was not provided in the source document.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for pembrolizumab in combination with an investigational agent may have a neutral implication for the specific combination, pending the release of trial results and the reason for termination.
