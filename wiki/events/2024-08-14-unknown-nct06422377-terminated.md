## Takeda terminates Phase 3 trial (NCT06422377) for Soticlestat

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06422377) for Soticlestat, a drug in development. The trial, which aimed to assess Soticlestat, was terminated after enrolling only one participant, indicating an early cessation of the study. This termination suggests a significant change in the development path for Soticlestat.
