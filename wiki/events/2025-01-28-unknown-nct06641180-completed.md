## Sanofi's Phase 3 Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT06641180) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-01-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06641180) investigating high-dose and standard-dose trivalent influenza vaccines has reached its primary completion date on January 28, 2025. The study enrolled 1180 participants to evaluate these vaccines, though results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step for Sanofi, with market implications pending the release of study results.
