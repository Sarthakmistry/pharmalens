## Semaglutide Phase 3 Trial (NCT05040971) for Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2023-01-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05040971) investigating semaglutide for obesity, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, reached its primary completion date on January 6, 2023. The trial enrolled 207 participants and has reported having results. This marks a significant milestone in the development of semaglutide for this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial provides further data for semaglutide in the obesity indication, potentially supporting future regulatory submissions or label expansions.
