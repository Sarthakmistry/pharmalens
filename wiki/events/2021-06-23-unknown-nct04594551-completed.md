## Sanofi's Phase 3 Rabies Vaccine Trial (NCT04594551) Completed

**Date:** 2021-06-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04594551, investigating a purified vero rabies vaccine in combination with human rabies immunoglobulins, reached its primary completion date on June 23, 2021. The trial enrolled 201 participants to evaluate the vaccine's efficacy and safety. Results from this completed study are not yet publicly available.

### Market implication
This routine trial completion is a standard step in drug development and has a neutral market implication for Sanofi, pending the release of trial results.
