## Sanofi's Phase 2 Trial NCT05305989 for Belumosudil Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2024-06-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05305989), which investigated Belumosudil at doses of 200 mg qd, 200 mg bid, and 400 mg qd, completed its primary data collection on June 6, 2024. The trial enrolled 23 participants and has reported that results are available. This milestone indicates the conclusion of the data collection phase for this study.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides an update on Sanofi's pipeline, with further analysis of the results expected to inform future development.
