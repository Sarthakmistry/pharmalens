## Pfizer's Phase 2 Trial (NCT06465368) for Breast Cancer Completes Primary Data Collection

**Date:** 2025-07-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06465368, investigating the drug Pf-07220060 in combination with Letrozole for breast cancer, reached its primary completion date on July 10, 2025. The trial enrolled 121 participants and has completed data collection for its primary outcome measures. Results are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of primary data collection is a standard procedural step in clinical development, and market implications will depend on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
