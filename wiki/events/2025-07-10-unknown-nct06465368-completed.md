## Pfizer's Phase 2 trial for Pf-07220060 in breast cancer reaches primary completion

**Date:** 2025-07-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06465368) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating Pf-07220060 in combination with Letrozole for breast cancer, reached its primary completion date on July 10, 2025. The trial enrolled 121 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural milestone and does not immediately impact Pfizer's valuation or the breast cancer market until results are released.
