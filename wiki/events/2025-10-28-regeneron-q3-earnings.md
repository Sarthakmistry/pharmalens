## Regeneron Reports Strong Q3 2025 Earnings with Growth in Dupixent and EYLEA HD, and Libtayo FDA Approval

**Date:** 2025-10-28 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
Regeneron Pharmaceuticals announced its third quarter 2025 financial results, with total revenues increasing 1% to $3.75 billion. This growth was primarily driven by strong sales of Dupixent (up 27%), Libtayo (up 27%), and EYLEA HD (up 31.35%), alongside the FDA approval of Libtayo for high-risk adjuvant cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma (CSCC). Despite these positive developments, sales of EYLEA decreased by 34.93%.

### Market implication
The robust performance of key growth drivers like Dupixent, Libtayo, and EYLEA HD, coupled with a significant FDA approval for Libtayo and positive Phase 3 trial results, suggests a bullish outlook for Regeneron, despite the decline in EYLEA sales.
