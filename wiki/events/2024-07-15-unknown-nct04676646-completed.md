## AstraZeneca's Phase 4 Trial NCT04676646 for HF-HTN Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-07-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
AstraZeneca's Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04676646), investigating Sodium zirconium cyclosilicate and Spironolactone for heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN), reached its primary completion date on July 15, 2024. The trial enrolled 366 participants, and results are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides additional data for AstraZeneca's research in heart failure and hypertension, with results now available for analysis.
