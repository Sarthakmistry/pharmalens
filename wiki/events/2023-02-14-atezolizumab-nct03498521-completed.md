### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT03498521, investigating [[atezolizumab]] in combination with other anticancer therapies for solid tumors, has reached its primary completion date on February 14, 2023. The trial, sponsored by [[roche]], enrolled 529 participants and has reported results.
