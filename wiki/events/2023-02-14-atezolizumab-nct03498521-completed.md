## Atezolizumab (NCT03498521) Phase 2 Trial for Solid Tumors Completed

**Date:** 2023-02-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03498521) investigating atezolizumab in combination with other anticancer therapies for locally advanced or metastatic solid tumors has reached primary completion on February 14, 2023. The trial enrolled 529 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a development milestone for atezolizumab, with results now available for further analysis.
