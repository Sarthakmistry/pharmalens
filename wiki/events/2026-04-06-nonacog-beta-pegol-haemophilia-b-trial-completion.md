## Nonacog Beta Pegol Haemophilia B Trial Completion

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT02141074 for nonacog beta pegol in previously untreated patients with Haemophilia B has completed. The trial, sponsored by Novo Nordisk A/S, enrolled 54 patients and focused on the incidence of inhibitory antibodies against Coagulation Factor IX (FIX).

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides an update on the development pipeline for Novo Nordisk in Haemophilia B, with further implications pending the release of detailed results.
