## Novartis terminates clinical trial NCT04300309 for Artemether:lumefantrine

**Date:** 2023-07-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04300309, sponsored by [[novartis]], investigating Artemether:lumefantrine (2.5 mg:30 mg) in Phase 2/3, was officially terminated on July 2, 2023. The trial had an actual enrollment of 28 participants and has reported results. This termination signifies a halt in the development pathway for this specific drug and indication within Novartis's pipeline.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial, even for an untracked drug, can be seen as a minor bearish signal for Novartis, indicating a potential re-prioritization of its pipeline or unfavorable outcomes that led to the decision.
