## Novartis terminates Phase 2/3 trial NCT04300309 for Artemether:lumefantrine

**Date:** 2023-07-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04300309) investigating Artemether:lumefantrine (2.5 mg:30 mg). The trial, which enrolled 28 participants, reached its primary completion date on July 2, 2023. The reason for termination was not specified in the available information.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial, particularly without a stated positive outcome or strategic shift, typically represents a bearish signal for the company's pipeline and future prospects in the related therapeutic area.
