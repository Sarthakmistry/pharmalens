## Merck's Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT04716933 in NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-08-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck has announced the primary completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04716933, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] (Keytruda) for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). The trial, which enrolled 201 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 11, 2023, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step towards potential new data for [[pembrolizumab]] in NSCLC, with market implications dependent on the forthcoming results.
