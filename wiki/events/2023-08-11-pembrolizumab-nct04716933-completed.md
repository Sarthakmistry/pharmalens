## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT04716933 in NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-08-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04716933, investigating pembrolizumab in combination with chemotherapy for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on August 11, 2023. The study, sponsored by Merck, enrolled 201 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural step, and its market impact will depend on the eventual release and interpretation of the results.
