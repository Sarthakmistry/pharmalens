## AstraZeneca's Phase 2 Trial (NCT06269484) for Dapagliflozin Combination Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-12-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06269484) sponsored by AstraZeneca, investigating a combination therapy including dapagliflozin, has completed its primary endpoint. The trial, which enrolled 73 participants, reached primary completion on December 11, 2024, and has reported results. This milestone provides an update on the development of dapagliflozin in a new combination.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a development milestone for AstraZeneca's dapagliflozin, with market impact contingent on the eventual release and interpretation of the trial results.
