## Bristol Myers Squibb Announces Cash Tender Offers for Up to $7 Billion in Notes

**Date:** 2025-11-03 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced cash tender offers to purchase certain of its outstanding notes for an aggregate purchase price of up to $7 billion. These offers are subject to specific Pool 1 Maximum and Pool 2 Maximum limits, as detailed in the Company’s Offer to Purchase dated November 3, 2025.

### Market implication
