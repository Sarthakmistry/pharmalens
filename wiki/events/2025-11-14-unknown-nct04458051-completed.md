## Sanofi's Tolebrutinib Phase 3 Trial (NCT04458051) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04458051) for Tolebrutinib, investigating its efficacy and safety, has reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2025. The trial enrolled 767 participants and has not yet reported results.
