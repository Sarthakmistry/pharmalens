## Bristol Myers Squibb Announces Chief Medical Officer Transition

**Date:** 2025-07-25 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb announced a leadership change with Dr. Samit Hirawat departing as Chief Medical Officer and Head of Development, and Dr. Cristian Massacesi joining in the same role. Dr. Hirawat will transition to an advisor role effective August 1, 2025, before leaving the company by November 1, 2025. Dr. Massacesi, previously CMO at AstraZeneca and Alexion, will assume the role on August 1, 2025, reporting directly to CEO Christopher Boerner.

### Market implication
The appointment of Dr. Cristian Massacesi, with his extensive biopharmaceutical experience and track record, is presented by the company as a strategic move to advance its pipeline.
