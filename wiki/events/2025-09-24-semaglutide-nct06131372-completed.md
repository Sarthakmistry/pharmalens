## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT06131372) for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-09-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06131372), investigating semaglutide and Cagrilintide for the treatment of type 2 diabetes and obesity, reached its primary completion date on September 24, 2025. This study enrolled 626 participants. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial represents a procedural advancement in Novo Nordisk's pipeline for semaglutide, with market implications awaiting the release and analysis of the study's results.
