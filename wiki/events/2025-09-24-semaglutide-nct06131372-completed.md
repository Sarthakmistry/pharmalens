## Semaglutide Phase 2 Trial (NCT06131372) for Type 2 Diabetes and Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2025-09-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT06131372, investigating semaglutide for type 2 diabetes and obesity, has reached primary completion on September 24, 2025. This trial, sponsored by Novo Nordisk, enrolled 626 participants and evaluated semaglutide in combination with Cagrilintide. The completion marks a significant step in the drug's development, with results pending.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a neutral development, as it signifies progress in the development pipeline for semaglutide, with market impact awaiting the release of trial results.
