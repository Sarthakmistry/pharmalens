## Merck Reports Q1 2025 Earnings with $15.5 Billion in Sales, Driven by KEYTRUDA Growth

**Date:** 2025-04-24 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck announced its first-quarter 2025 financial results, reporting total worldwide sales of $15.5 billion, a 2% decrease from the prior year but a 1% increase excluding foreign exchange impact. This performance was primarily driven by strong growth in [[pembrolizumab]] (KEYTRUDA), which saw sales of $7.2 billion, and Animal Health sales. New product WINREVAIR also contributed with $280 million in sales.

### Market implication
The overall neutral sentiment for Merck's Q1 2025 earnings reflects a slight decrease in total worldwide sales, balanced by growth in key products like [[pembrolizumab]] and Animal Health, and new product launches, indicating a stable but not significantly accelerating financial position.
