## Nivolumab (Opdivo) Phase 1/2 Trial (NCT03369223) in Alzheimer's Disease Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-11-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03369223) investigating [[nivolumab]], an immunotherapy developed by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], for [[alzheimers]] Disease has reached its primary completion date on November 7, 2024. The trial enrolled 356 participants and has reported having results.

### Market implication
The completion of this early-phase trial provides initial data for nivolumab in Alzheimer's, but its market impact is neutral pending the release and interpretation of results, especially given nivolumab's primary use in oncology.
