## Bristol Myers Squibb's Nivolumab Trial NCT03369223 for Alzheimer's Disease Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03369223) sponsored by Bristol Myers Squibb, investigating nivolumab, BMS-986249, and ipilimumab in Alzheimer's disease, has reached its primary completion date on November 7, 2024. The study enrolled 356 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial allows Bristol Myers Squibb to analyze the collected data, which will be crucial for determining the future development strategy for nivolumab and other agents in Alzheimer's disease.
