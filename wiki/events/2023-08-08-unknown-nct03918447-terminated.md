## Biogen's Phase 3 Trial (NCT03918447) for Alzheimer's Terminated

**Date:** 2023-08-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03918447, investigating Bardoxolone methyl oral capsule for Alzheimer's disease, was officially terminated on August 8, 2023. The trial had enrolled 667 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a late-stage clinical trial represents a setback for Biogen's pipeline in Alzheimer's disease, potentially impacting future revenue projections in this indication.
