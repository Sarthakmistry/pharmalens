## Biogen's Phase 3 Trial of Bardoxolone Methyl for Alzheimer's Disease Terminated

**Date:** 2023-08-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Biogen's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03918447) investigating Bardoxolone methyl oral capsule for Alzheimer's disease was terminated on August 8, 2023. The trial had enrolled 667 participants and had reached its primary completion date, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial represents a setback for Biogen's pipeline in Alzheimer's disease and the development of Bardoxolone methyl for this indication, likely having a negative impact on future revenue expectations for the drug.
