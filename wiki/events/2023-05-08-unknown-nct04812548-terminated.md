## Novartis terminates Phase 2 trial NCT04812548 for Sabatolimab in combination with Azacitidine and Venetoclax

**Date:** 2023-05-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04812548) investigating Sabatolimab in combination with Azacitidine and Venetoclax. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 20 participants, reached its primary completion on May 8, 2023, and results are available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Sabatolimab is a bearish signal for Novartis's pipeline, indicating a halt in the development of this specific combination therapy.
