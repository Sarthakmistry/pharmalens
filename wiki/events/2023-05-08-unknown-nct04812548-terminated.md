## Novartis Terminates Phase 2 Trial (NCT04812548) for Sabatolimab

**Date:** 2023-05-08 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Novartis has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04812548) investigating Sabatolimab in combination with Azacitidine or Venetoclax. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 20 participants, reached its primary completion on May 8, 2023, the same day it was terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for Sabatolimab represents a setback for Novartis's pipeline in this specific therapeutic area, potentially impacting future development plans for the drug.
