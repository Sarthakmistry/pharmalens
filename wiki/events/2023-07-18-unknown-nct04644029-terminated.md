## Merck's Phase 3 Trial NCT04644029 for Islatravir and Ftc/tdf Terminated

**Date:** 2023-07-18 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04644029), which was investigating Islatravir and Ftc/tdf, has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 730 participants and reached its primary completion on July 18, 2023, with results now available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Islatravir and Ftc/tdf represents a significant setback for Merck's pipeline in the therapeutic area targeted by these drugs.
