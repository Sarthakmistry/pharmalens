## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT05896163) for Maplirpacept (pf-07901801)

**Date:** 2026-04-09 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05896163) investigating Maplirpacept (pf-07901801) in combination with Glofitamab and Obinutuzumab. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 40 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 9, 2026, before being terminated.

### Market implication
This termination represents a setback for Pfizer's pipeline in this therapeutic area, potentially impacting future development plans for Maplirpacept.
