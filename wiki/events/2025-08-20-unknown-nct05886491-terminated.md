## Takeda terminates Phase 1/2 trial of Gdx012 (NCT05886491)

**Date:** 2025-08-20 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Takeda has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05886491) investigating Gdx012 in combination with chemotherapy agents. The study, which had an actual enrollment of 16 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 20, 2025, before being terminated. The termination halts further development of Gdx012 in this context.

### Market implication
The termination of an early-phase clinical trial for an investigational drug is generally a bearish signal, as it removes a potential pipeline asset and future revenue stream for the company.
