## Sanofi's Phase 3 Rabies Vaccine Trial (NCT04127786) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-03-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On March 25, 2020, Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04127786, evaluating Purified vero rabies vaccine - serum free - vrvg-2, Purified inactivated rabies vaccine - verorab®, and Purified inactivated rabies vaccine - imovax® rabies, reached its primary completion. The trial enrolled 1708 participants and has results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides data for Sanofi's rabies vaccine candidates, representing a neutral development for the company.
