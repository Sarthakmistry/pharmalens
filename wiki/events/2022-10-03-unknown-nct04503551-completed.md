## Roche's Phase 3 trial NCT04503551 for Pds implant pre-filled with 100 mg/ml ranibizumab completed

**Date:** 2022-10-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04503551, investigating Pds implant pre-filled with 100 mg/ml ranibizumab and Intravitreal ranibizumab 0.5 mg injection, reached its primary completion date on October 3, 2022. The study enrolled 174 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a development milestone for Roche, with the market impact dependent on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial results.
