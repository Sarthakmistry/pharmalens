## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT04503551 for Ranibizumab Delivery System Completed Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2022-10-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Roche's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04503551, evaluating a Pds implant pre-filled with 100 mg/ml ranibizumab against intravitreal ranibizumab 0.5 mg injection, completed its primary endpoint on October 3, 2022. The study enrolled 174 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial indicates that data analysis will follow, which could inform future development or regulatory submissions for Roche's ranibizumab delivery system.
