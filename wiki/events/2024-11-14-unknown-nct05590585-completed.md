## Regeneron's Phase 4 Trial NCT05590585 for Dupilumab Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05590585, which investigated Dupilumab and Topical emollient (moisturizer), reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2024. The study enrolled 124 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides updated data for Dupilumab, which will be further evaluated.
