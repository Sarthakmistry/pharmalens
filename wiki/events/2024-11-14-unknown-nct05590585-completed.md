## Regeneron's Phase 4 Trial NCT05590585 for Dupilumab and Topical Emollient Completed

**Date:** 2024-11-14 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 4 clinical trial, NCT05590585, investigating Dupilumab and Topical emollient (moisturizer), reached its primary completion date on November 14, 2024. The study enrolled 124 participants and has reported that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial is a standard procedural update and does not immediately convey new information regarding the efficacy or safety of the studied treatments, thus having a neutral market implication.
