## Bayer Completes Phase 2 Trial of Riociguat for ILD-Associated Pulmonary Hypertension

**Date:** 2026-04-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 interventional study (NCT00694850) evaluating multiple doses of BAY63-2521 (Riociguat/Adempas) for safety and tolerability in patients with Interstitial Lung Disease (ILD) Associated Pulmonary Hypertension (PH) has been completed by Bayer. The study assessed multiple ascending doses of Riociguat in patients with ILD-Associated PH, with the primary objective to evaluate the safety and tolerability of the drug over 12 weeks of treatment. The trial enrolled 22 patients and was sponsored by Bayer. There is currently no approved medication specifically for pulmonary hypertension associated with interstitial lung disease.

### Market implication
The trial has been completed, but results are not yet available to assess the drug's efficacy or safety profile, leading to a neutral market implication.
