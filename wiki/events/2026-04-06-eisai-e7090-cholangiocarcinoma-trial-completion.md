## Eisai's E7090 Phase 2 Trial for Cholangiocarcinoma Completes

**Date:** 2026-04-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

Eisai Co., Ltd. has completed a Phase 2 study of E7090 in participants with unresectable advanced or metastatic cholangiocarcinoma with FGFR2 gene fusion. The trial reached its completion status, marking a procedural milestone for the investigational drug.
