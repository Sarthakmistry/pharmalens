## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04418661 Terminated

**Date:** 2024-04-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT04418661, investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with Vociprotafib and Adagrasib for advanced solid tumors, was officially terminated on April 4, 2024. The Phase 1/2 study, sponsored by Sanofi, had enrolled 65 participants and reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
The termination of this early-phase trial for [[pembrolizumab]] in a specific combination may slightly reduce the long-term pipeline potential for [[merck]] in advanced solid tumors, though its impact on overall revenue is likely minimal given the drug's broad approval.
