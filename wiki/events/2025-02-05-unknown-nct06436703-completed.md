## Pfizer's Phase 2 Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT06436703) Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-02-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06436703) sponsored by Pfizer, evaluating an Influenza modRNA vaccine, has reached its primary completion date on February 5, 2025. The study enrolled 1202 participants to assess the vaccine's efficacy and safety. No results have been published yet.

### Market implication
This event represents a standard progression in Pfizer's vaccine development pipeline, with further data expected to inform future development.
