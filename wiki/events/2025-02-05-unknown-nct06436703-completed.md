## Pfizer's Phase 2 Influenza Vaccine Trial (NCT06436703) Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-02-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06436703) sponsored by Pfizer, investigating an Influenza modRNA vaccine, has reached its primary completion date on February 5, 2025. The trial enrolled 1202 participants and compared the modRNA vaccine against a Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine (QIV). No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a development milestone for Pfizer's influenza vaccine pipeline, with future market implications dependent on the trial's results.
