## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT03829319 for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2023-08-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03829319, evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with chemotherapy for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), has reached its primary completion date on August 11, 2023. This trial, sponsored by Merck, enrolled 761 participants and has reported having results available. The completion of this pivotal trial is a key milestone in the development pathway for pembrolizumab in this indication.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 3 trial for pembrolizumab in NSCLC indicates progress towards potential data readout and subsequent regulatory submissions, which could reinforce Merck's market leadership in oncology.
