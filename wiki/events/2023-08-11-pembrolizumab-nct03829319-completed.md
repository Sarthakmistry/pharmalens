## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 3 Trial NCT03829319 for NSCLC Completed

**Date:** 2023-08-11 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT03829319) investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in combination with chemotherapy for non-small cell lung cancer ([[oncology-nsclc/_index]]) has reached its primary completion date on August 11, 2023. Sponsored by [[merck]], the trial enrolled 761 participants and has indicated that results are now available.

The completion of this Phase 3 trial for [[pembrolizumab]] in NSCLC is a standard procedural update, with market impact dependent on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
