## Eli Lilly's Tirzepatide Phase 2 Trial (NCT06373146) for GLP-1 Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2025-12-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT06373146) investigating tirzepatide for GLP-1 obesity has reached its primary completion date on December 15, 2025. The study, which enrolled 392 participants, has concluded its data collection for the primary outcome measures. Results from this trial are not yet available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial for tirzepatide in obesity is a neutral event, as it signifies progress in the development pipeline but does not yet provide efficacy or safety data to impact market valuation.
