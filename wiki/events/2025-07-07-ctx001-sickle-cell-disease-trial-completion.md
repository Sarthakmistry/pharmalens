## CTX001 Sickle Cell Disease Trial (NCT03745287) Completes

**Date:** 2025-07-07 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2/3 study (NCT03745287) evaluating CTX001 in subjects with severe sickle cell disease has completed. The study is a single-arm, open-label, multi-site, single-dose trial evaluating autologous CRISPR-Cas9 Modified CD34+ Human Hematopoietic Stem and Progenitor Cells (hHSPCs) using CTX001. Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated is the lead sponsor, with CRISPR Therapeutics as a collaborator.

### Market implication
The completion of the study is a procedural milestone, with market implications pending the release of results.
