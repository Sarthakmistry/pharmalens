## Merck Reports Results of 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders

**Date:** 2025-05-29 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck & Co., Inc. announced the outcomes of its Annual Meeting of Shareholders held on May 27, 2025. Key results included the election of directors to the Company's Board, shareholder approval of executive compensation on an advisory basis, and the ratification of the company's independent registered public accounting firm. Several shareholder proposals, such as those concerning a human rights impact assessment, a tax transparency report, and revisiting DEI goals in executive pay incentives, failed to pass.

### Market implication
The announcement of routine shareholder meeting results, without any unexpected outcomes or forward-looking guidance, is expected to have a neutral impact on Merck's market valuation.
