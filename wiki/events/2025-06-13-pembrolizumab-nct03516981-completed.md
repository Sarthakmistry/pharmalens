## Pembrolizumab (Keytruda) Phase 2 Trial NCT03516981 for NSCLC Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-06-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT03516981) investigating pembrolizumab in combination with other agents for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) reached its primary completion date on June 13, 2025. The trial enrolled 245 participants and has not yet reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step, and without results, there is no immediate market implication for Merck or pembrolizumab. Investors will await future data releases.
