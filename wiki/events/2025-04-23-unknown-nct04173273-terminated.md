## Pfizer Terminates Phase 3 Trial (NCT04173273) for Etrasimod

**Date:** 2025-04-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04173273, which was investigating Etrasimod. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 375 participants, reached its primary completion date on April 23, 2025, before being terminated. This termination indicates a halt in the development of Etrasimod for its intended indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Etrasimod is a bearish signal for Pfizer, as it removes a potential future revenue stream and indicates a setback in its pipeline.
