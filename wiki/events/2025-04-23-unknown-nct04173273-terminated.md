## Pfizer Terminates Phase 3 Clinical Trial NCT04173273

**Date:** 2025-04-23 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04173273, which had an actual enrollment of 375 participants and was primarily completed on April 23, 2025. The trial involved the drug Etrasimod. The termination of a late-stage trial indicates a halt in the development pathway for this specific investigation.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial is generally a negative signal for the company's pipeline and future revenue prospects, especially for the drug Etrasimod.
