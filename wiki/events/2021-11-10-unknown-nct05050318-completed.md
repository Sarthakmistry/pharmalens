## Sanofi completes Phase 4 trial NCT05050318 for influenza vaccines

**Date:** 2021-11-10 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi announced the primary completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial NCT05050318 on November 10, 2021. This trial, which enrolled 90 participants, investigated Fluzone quadrivalent vaccine, no preservative (0.5-ml dose), 2021-2022 formulation and Fluzone high-dose quadrivalent vaccine (0.7-ml dose), 2021-2022 formulation. Results from this completed trial are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a standard procedural step for Sanofi's influenza vaccines, with neutral immediate market implications.
