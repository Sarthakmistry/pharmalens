## Sanofi completes Phase 4 trial (NCT05114590) for Insulin glargine/lixisenatide in Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2023-03-28 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi has completed a Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05114590) for Insulin glargine/lixisenatide in patients with Type 2 Diabetes. The trial, which enrolled 124 participants, reached its primary completion date on March 28, 2023, and has reported having results available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial represents a standard progression in the clinical development and post-market surveillance of Sanofi's combination therapy for Type 2 Diabetes.
