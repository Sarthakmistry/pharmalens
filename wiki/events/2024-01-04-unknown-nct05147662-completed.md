## Bayer's Phase 3 trial (NCT05147662) for Damoctocog alfa pegol (Jivi) completes primary endpoint

**Date:** 2024-01-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
On January 4, 2024, Bayer's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05147662, investigating Damoctocog alfa pegol (Jivi, BAY94-9027) reached its primary completion date. The trial, which enrolled 36 participants, has results available. This marks a significant milestone in the development of the drug.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a neutral event, as results are not yet publicly available to assess efficacy or safety.
