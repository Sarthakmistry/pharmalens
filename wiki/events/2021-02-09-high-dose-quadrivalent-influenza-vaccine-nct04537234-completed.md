## Sanofi's High-Dose Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine (NCT04537234) Demonstrates High Immunogenicity and Acceptable Safety in Older Adults

**Date:** 2021-02-09 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
A Phase 3 randomized controlled trial (NCT04537234) evaluating Sanofi's High-dose quadrivalent influenza vaccine (QIV-HD) against standard-dose QIV-SD in 165 older adults aged ≥ 65 years in Taiwan has completed. The study found QIV-HD to be highly immunogenic for all four influenza strains with a similar safety profile to the standard dose.

### Market implication
This positive trial completion strengthens Sanofi's position in the influenza vaccine market, particularly for the older adult demographic.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 165 patients
- **Comparator:** standard of care
- **Primary outcome:** geometric means titers (GMTs) of hemagglutination inhibition
- **Primary result:** GMT ratios (95 % CI) for QIV-HD vs QIV-SD: 2.65 (1.87-3.75) for A/H1N1; 1.76 (1.31-2.38) for A/H3N2; 2.60 (1.90-3.56) for B/Victoria; and 2.01 (1.57-2.56) for B/Yamagata.
- **Secondary results:** The seroconversion was higher for QIV-HD than QIV-SD
- **Safety:** similar safety profiles across both groups
- **Conclusions:** QIV-HD was highly immunogenic for four influenza strains and have acceptable safety profile in older adults aged ≥ 65 years in Taiwan.
- **Journal:** Vaccine, 2022
- **Industry sponsored:** None
