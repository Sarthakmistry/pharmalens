## Johnson & Johnson Director Retirement and Annual Meeting Results

**Date:** 2025-04-30 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the retirement of D. Adamczyk from its Board of Directors effective May 31, 2025, and reported the results of its 2025 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. At the meeting, directors were elected, executive compensation was approved on an advisory basis, and two shareholder proposals regarding excessive golden parachutes and human rights impact assessment were not approved.

### Market implication
This event reflects routine corporate governance activities and is not expected to have a material impact on Johnson & Johnson's market valuation or operations.
