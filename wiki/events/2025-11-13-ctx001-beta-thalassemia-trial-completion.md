## CTX001 Beta-Thalassemia Trial Completion

**Date:** 2025-11-13 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2/3 study (NCT03655678) evaluating CTX001 in participants with transfusion-dependent β-thalassemia has been completed. The study aimed to assess the safety and efficacy of autologous CRISPR-Cas9 Modified CD34+ Human Hematopoietic Stem and Progenitor Cells (hHSPCs) using CTX001.

### Market implication
The trial has completed, but no results have been released yet, leading to a neutral market implication.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** single-arm, open-label, multi-site, single-dose Phase 1/2/3 study
- **Sample size:** 59 participants
- **Comparator:** None
- **Primary outcome:** Proportion of participants achieving transfusion independence for at least 12 consecutive months (TI12)
- **Primary result:** None
- **Secondary results:** []
- **Safety:** None
