## Vandetanib Medullary Thyroid Cancer Trial Completion

**Date:** 2009-07-31 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 efficacy study comparing ZD6474 (Vandetanib) to placebo in medullary thyroid cancer, sponsored by Genzyme (a Sanofi Company), has been completed. The study investigated ZD6474 (Vandetanib) for Medullary Thyroid Cancer, was an interventional study comparing the drug to placebo, and enrolled 331 participants. The primary outcome measure was Progression-Free Survival (PFS). The trial's overall status is 'COMPLETED'.
