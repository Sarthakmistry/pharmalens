## Roche's Phase 3 Trial NCT06774859 Completed with Results

**Date:** 2025-05-08 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT06774859) sponsored by Roche, investigating Baloxavir marboxil and Oseltamivir, reached primary completion on May 8, 2025. The trial enrolled 100 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development process for these influenza treatments.

### Market implication
The completion and availability of results from this Phase 3 trial provide new data for Roche's influenza portfolio, potentially influencing future development strategies or market positioning.
