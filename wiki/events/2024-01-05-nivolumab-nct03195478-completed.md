## Bristol-Myers Squibb's Nivolumab Trial NCT03195478 Completes Primary Phase

**Date:** 2024-01-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT03195478) evaluating [[nivolumab]], developed by [[bristol-myers-squibb]], in combination with Ipilimumab for patients with advanced solid tumors, completed its primary phase on January 5, 2024. The study enrolled 37 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides new data for Bristol-Myers Squibb regarding the combination therapy of nivolumab and Ipilimumab in advanced solid tumors, which could influence future clinical strategies and market positioning for the drug.
