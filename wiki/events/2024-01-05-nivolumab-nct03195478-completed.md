## Nivolumab (Opdivo) trial NCT03195478 completed

**Date:** 2024-01-05 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Clinical trial NCT03195478, a Phase 1/2 study evaluating nivolumab (Opdivo) in combination with ipilimumab, reached primary completion on January 5, 2024. The trial enrolled 37 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this trial provides further data for Bristol-Myers Squibb's nivolumab, contributing to its clinical profile.
