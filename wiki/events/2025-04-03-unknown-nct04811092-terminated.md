## Eli Lilly's Phase 3 Trial (NCT04811092) for Sotatercept in HF-HTN Terminated

**Date:** 2025-04-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT04811092) sponsored by Eli Lilly, investigating Sotatercept for heart failure and hypertension (HF-HTN), has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 321 participants and reached its primary completion date on April 3, 2025, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Sotatercept represents a negative development for Eli Lilly's pipeline in the HF-HTN indication, potentially impacting future revenue projections for the drug.
