## Clinical Trial NCT04811092 for Sotatercept Terminated

**Date:** 2025-04-03 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
The Phase 3 clinical trial NCT04811092, sponsored by [[eli-lilly]], investigating Sotatercept for heart failure (hf-htn), has been terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 321 participants and reached its primary completion date on April 3, 2025, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, even with results available, typically represents a negative development for the drug's future prospects and the sponsoring company's pipeline in the affected indication.
