## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05074420 for Baricitinib

**Date:** 2024-09-05 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Eli Lilly has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial NCT05074420, which was investigating Baricitinib. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of only 6 participants, reached its primary completion date on September 5, 2024, but was subsequently terminated.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial, especially with very low enrollment, is a negative signal for Eli Lilly's pipeline and the future prospects of Baricitinib in this specific indication.
