## D.R. Horton Reports 10% Revenue Decrease and 30% Net Income Drop in Q4 2025 Earnings

**Date:** 2026-01-22 | **Type:** earnings_signal | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
D.R. Horton reported a 10% decrease in consolidated revenues to $6.9 billion and a 30% decrease in net income to $594.8 million for the three months ended December 31, 2025, compared to the prior year period. This decline was attributed to ongoing affordability constraints and cautious consumer sentiment in the housing market.

### Market implication
The substantial decrease in D.R. Horton's revenues and net income, driven by affordability constraints and cautious consumer sentiment, suggests a negative outlook for the company's short-term financial performance.
