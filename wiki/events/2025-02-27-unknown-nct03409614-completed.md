## Regeneron's Phase 3 Trial NCT03409614 for NSCLC Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-02-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 3 clinical trial NCT03409614, investigating Regn2810/chemo/ipi for non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), reached its primary completion date on February 27, 2025. The trial enrolled 789 participants and has reported results.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a significant milestone for Regeneron, moving the program closer to potential regulatory submission, with market reaction pending the release and interpretation of the trial results.
