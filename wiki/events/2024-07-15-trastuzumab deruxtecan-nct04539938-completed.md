## Trial NCT04539938 for Trastuzumab Deruxtecan in Breast Cancer Completed

**Date:** 2024-07-15 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 clinical trial NCT04539938, investigating trastuzumab deruxtecan for the treatment of breast cancer, has reached its primary completion date on July 15, 2024. The study successfully enrolled 70 participants, and results from the trial are now available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides new data for trastuzumab deruxtecan in breast cancer, which will be important for its ongoing clinical development and potential future regulatory submissions.
