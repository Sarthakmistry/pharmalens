## Sanofi terminates Phase 3 trial NCT05132569 for Tolebrutininb

**Date:** 2023-02-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi has terminated its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05132569, which was investigating Tolebrutininb. The trial reached its primary completion date on February 21, 2023, with an actual enrollment of 6 participants before termination. This event indicates a halt in the development of Tolebrutininb for the conditions studied in this particular trial.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial for Tolebrutininb is a negative signal for Sanofi's pipeline, potentially impacting future revenue expectations for the drug.
