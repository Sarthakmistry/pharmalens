## Johnson & Johnson Announces Election of Daniel Pinto to Board of Directors

**Date:** 2025-06-10 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Johnson & Johnson announced the election of Daniel Pinto to its Board of Directors, effective July 1, 2025. Mr. Pinto will serve on the Audit Committee and the Compensation & Benefits Committee. He currently serves as President of JPMorganChase and will transition to Vice Chairman from July 1, 2025, through his retirement at the end of 2026.
