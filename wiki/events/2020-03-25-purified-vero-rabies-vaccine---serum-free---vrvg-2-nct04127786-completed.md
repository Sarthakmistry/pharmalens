## Sanofi's Purified Vero Rabies Vaccine - Serum Free - VRVG-2 Phase 3 Trial (NCT04127786) Completes with Positive Noninferiority Results

**Date:** 2020-03-25 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
A Phase 3 randomized controlled trial (NCT04127786) for Purified vero rabies vaccine - serum free - vrvg-2, sponsored by Sanofi, completed on March 25, 2020. The study, with an actual enrollment of 1708 participants, demonstrated noninferiority of Purified vero rabies vaccine - serum free - vrvg-2 to existing vaccines for rabies pre-exposure prophylaxis, with no safety concerns identified.

### Market implication
This positive trial completion supports the potential future use of Purified vero rabies vaccine - serum free - vrvg-2, strengthening Sanofi's vaccine pipeline.

### Clinical evidence
- **Study design:** RCT
- **Sample size:** 1708
- **Comparator:** PVRV and HDCV
- **Primary outcome:** noninferiority of PVRV-NG2 to PVRV and HDCV as 3-dose PrEP, based on the proportion of participants with rabies virus-neutralizing antibody titer ≥0.5 IU/mL at D42
- **Primary result:** All participants had rabies virus-neutralizing antibody titers ≥0.5 IU/mL after 3-dose PVRV-NG2 (D42), with noninferiority to PVRV and HDCV demonstrated.
- **Secondary results:**
    - noninferiority of 2-dose PVRV-NG2 versus 2-dose PVRV and HDCV (D28)
    - noninferiority of 2-dose PVRV-NG2 versus 3-dose HDCV (D42)
    - noninferiority of 2-dose HDCV versus 3-dose HDCV
- **Safety:** The safety profile of PVRV-NG2 was comparable to those of PVRV and HDCV; no safety concerns identified.
- **Conclusions:** This study supports the use of PVRV-NG2 in 2- or 3-dose PrEP regimens, with no safety concerns identified.
- **Journal:** Clin Infect Dis, 2025
- **Industry sponsored:** True
