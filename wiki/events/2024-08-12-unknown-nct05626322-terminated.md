## Pfizer terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT05626322) involving Maplirpacept, Tafasitamab, and Lenalidomide

**Date:** 2024-08-12 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Pfizer has terminated its Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05626322) investigating Maplirpacept, Tafasitamab, and Lenalidomide. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 6 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 12, 2024, and has reported results.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 1/2 trial, particularly with low enrollment, suggests potential issues with the drug candidates or a strategic pipeline reprioritization, which is a negative signal for Pfizer's early-stage oncology assets.
