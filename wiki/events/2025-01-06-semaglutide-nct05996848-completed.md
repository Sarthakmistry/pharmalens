## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial (NCT05996848) for GLP-1 Obesity Completed

**Date:** 2025-01-06 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk announced the completion of its Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT05996848, evaluating semaglutide for GLP-1 obesity. The trial, which enrolled 300 participants, reached its primary completion date on January 6, 2025.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial marks a step forward in the development of semaglutide for GLP-1 obesity, with future results expected to inform its market potential.
