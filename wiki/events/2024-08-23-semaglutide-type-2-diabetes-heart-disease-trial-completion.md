## Semaglutide Type 2 Diabetes Heart Disease Trial Completion

**Date:** 2024-08-23 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 3 study (NCT03914326) investigating semaglutide's effect on heart disease in patients with Type 2 Diabetes has completed. The trial, which enrolled 9651 participants, aimed to assess the number of participants from randomization to the first occurrence of a Major Adverse Cardiovascular Event (MACE). The study duration was approximately 3.5-5 years.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial for semaglutide in heart disease for Type 2 Diabetes patients marks a significant milestone, though results are not yet available in this document, leading to a neutral market sentiment.
