## Biogen terminates Phase 3 trial NCT01351675 for Type 2 Diabetes

**Date:** 2012-10-31 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT01351675) sponsored by Biogen, investigating a treatment for Type 2 Diabetes, was terminated on October 31, 2012. The trial had an actual enrollment of 2185 participants. The termination of a late-stage trial indicates a halt in the development of the investigational treatment for this indication.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 3 trial represents a setback for Biogen's pipeline in Type 2 Diabetes, potentially impacting future revenue projections in this therapeutic area.
