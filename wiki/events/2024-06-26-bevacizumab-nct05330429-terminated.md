## Phase 2 Trial of Bevacizumab in Colorectal Cancer Terminated

**Date:** 2024-06-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05330429) investigating bevacizumab in combination with Magrolimab and FOLFIRI (Irinotecan, Fluorouracil, Leucovorin) for metastatic colorectal cancer was terminated on June 26, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Gilead, had an actual enrollment of 77 participants and reached its primary completion date.

### Market implication
The termination of this trial represents a minor setback for bevacizumab's potential expansion in combination therapies for colorectal cancer, though its established uses remain unaffected.
