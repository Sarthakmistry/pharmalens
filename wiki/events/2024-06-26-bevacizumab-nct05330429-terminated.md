## Phase 2 Trial of Bevacizumab (NCT05330429) in Colorectal Cancer Terminated

**Date:** 2024-06-26 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05330429) evaluating [[bevacizumab]] in combination with Magrolimab, Irinotecan, Fluorouracil, and Leucovorin for metastatic colorectal cancer was terminated on June 26, 2024. The trial, sponsored by Gilead, had an actual enrollment of 77 participants and reached its primary completion date on the same day as its termination. The reason for termination is not specified in the available data.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial represents a setback for the combination therapy involving bevacizumab in metastatic colorectal cancer, potentially impacting future development plans for this specific regimen by Gilead and indirectly affecting [[roche]] as the owner of bevacizumab.
