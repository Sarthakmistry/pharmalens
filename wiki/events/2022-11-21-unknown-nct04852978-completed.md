## Regeneron's Phase 2 Trial NCT04852978 Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2022-11-21 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT04852978, reached its primary completion date on November 21, 2022. The trial, which investigated Casirivimab+imdevimab and Moderna mrna-1273 vaccine, enrolled 295 participants and has reported results. This milestone signifies the conclusion of the primary data collection for the study.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this trial is a neutral event, as it marks a procedural milestone without immediate disclosure of detailed efficacy or safety outcomes.
