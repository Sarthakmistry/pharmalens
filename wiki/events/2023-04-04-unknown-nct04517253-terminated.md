## Eli Lilly terminates Phase 2/3 trial (NCT04517253) for Baricitinib

### Summary
Eli Lilly's Phase 2/3 clinical trial (NCT04517253) for Baricitinib was terminated on April 4, 2023. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 10 participants, reached its primary completion date on the same day it was terminated.
