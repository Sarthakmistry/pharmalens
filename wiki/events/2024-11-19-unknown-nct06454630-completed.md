## Regeneron's Trial NCT06454630 Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-19 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Regeneron's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06454630, reached its primary completion date on November 19, 2024. The study, which investigated Regn7508 and Enoxaparin, enrolled 179 participants and has indicated that results are available.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial marks a procedural milestone for Regeneron, with market impact awaiting the release of study results.
