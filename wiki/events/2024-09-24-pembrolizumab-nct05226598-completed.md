## Pembrolizumab Phase 3 Trial NCT05226598 for NSCLC Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-09-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Merck's Phase 3 clinical trial (NCT05226598) investigating [[pembrolizumab]] in non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) has reached its primary completion date on September 24, 2024. The trial enrolled 739 participants and has reported that results are available. This marks a significant milestone in the trial's progression.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step; market impact will depend on the eventual release and nature of the trial results.
