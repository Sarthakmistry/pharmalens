## Sanofi's Phase 2 Trial NCT06015308 for Amlitelimab Completes Primary Completion

**Date:** 2025-10-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 2 clinical trial, NCT06015308, investigating Amlitelimab, Tdap vaccine, and Pps vaccine, reached its primary completion on October 1, 2025. The study enrolled 224 participants. No results from this trial have been publicly reported yet.

### Market implication
The primary completion of this Phase 2 trial is a standard procedural step and does not immediately impact Sanofi's market valuation without the disclosure of trial results.
