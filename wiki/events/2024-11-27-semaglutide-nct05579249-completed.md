## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Phase 4 Trial (NCT05579249) for Obesity Reaches Primary Completion

**Date:** 2024-11-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05579249) sponsored by Novo Nordisk, investigating [[semaglutide]] and [[liraglutide]] for obesity, has reached its primary completion date on November 27, 2024. The trial enrolled 500 participants and has results available, marking a significant milestone in the study of these GLP-1 receptor agonists for weight management. The study compared semaglutide and liraglutide against other obesity treatments like Orlistat, Phentermine/topiramate, and Naltrexone/bupropion.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial provides further data for [[semaglutide]] and [[liraglutide]] in obesity, potentially reinforcing their market position and informing future clinical practice.
