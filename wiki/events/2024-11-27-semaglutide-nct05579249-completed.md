## Novo Nordisk's Semaglutide Trial NCT05579249 for Obesity Completes Primary Endpoint

**Date:** 2024-11-27 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novo Nordisk's Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05579249) investigating semaglutide and liraglutide for glp1-obesity has reached its primary completion date on November 27, 2024. The trial, which enrolled 500 participants, has indicated that results are available. This marks a significant milestone in the study of these GLP-1 receptor agonists for weight management.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a neutral event, as the market impact will depend on the eventual publication and interpretation of the trial results for semaglutide and liraglutide in obesity.
