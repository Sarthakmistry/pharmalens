## Pfizer's Phase 3 LGX818/MEK162 Melanoma Trial Reaches Completion

**Date:** 2024-09-03 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Pfizer's Phase 3 study (NCT01909453) comparing LGX818 plus MEK162 versus Vemurafenib and LGX818 monotherapy in BRAF mutant melanoma has been completed. The study was a 2-part, randomized, open label, multi-center, parallel group trial involving 921 patients.

### Market implication
As no results have been released, the market implication is neutral, awaiting further data.
