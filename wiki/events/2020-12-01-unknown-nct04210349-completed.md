## Sanofi's Phase 3 Trial NCT04210349 for Influenza Vaccines Completed Primary Completion

**Date:** 2020-12-01 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Sanofi's Phase 3 clinical trial, NCT04210349, investigating Quadrivalent influenza vaccine, Trivalent influenza vaccine 1 sp shz tiv1, and Trivalent influenza vaccine 2 sp shz tiv2, reached its primary completion date on December 1, 2020. The study enrolled 7106 participants and was sponsored by Sanofi. No results have been reported yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 3 trial is a standard procedural step and has a neutral market implication without disclosed results.
