## Pembrolizumab Trial NCT04854499 Terminated

**Date:** 2024-10-02 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04854499) evaluating pembrolizumab in combination with Magrolimab, Docetaxel, 5-fu, Cisplatin, and Carboplatin for various oncology indications has been terminated. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 193 participants, reached its primary completion date on October 2, 2024, with results available.

### Market implication
The termination of this Phase 2 trial for pembrolizumab, despite having results, represents a minor setback for Merck's pipeline, indicating a potential discontinuation of this specific combination or indication path.
