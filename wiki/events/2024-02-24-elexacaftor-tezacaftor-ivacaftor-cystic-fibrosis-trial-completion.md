## Elexacaftor, Tezacaftor, and Ivacaftor Triple Combination Trial for Cystic Fibrosis Completes

**Date:** 2024-02-24 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 3 clinical trial evaluating the long-term safety and efficacy of elexacaftor, tezacaftor, and ivacaftor triple combination therapy in participants with Cystic Fibrosis aged 6 years and older has been completed. The study, sponsored by Vertex Pharmaceuticals, focused on assessing safety and tolerability as its primary outcome.

### Market implication
As the trial completion does not include specific efficacy or safety outcomes, the immediate market implication for Vertex Pharmaceuticals and the drugs elexacaftor, tezacaftor, and ivacaftor is neutral.
