## Nivolumab (NCT04785820) Phase 2 Trial Completed for Alzheimer's Disease

**Date:** 2025-01-30 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
A Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT04785820) investigating nivolumab for Alzheimer's disease, sponsored by Roche, has reached its primary completion date on January 30, 2025. The trial enrolled 190 participants and has reported results. This marks a significant milestone in the development of nivolumab for this indication.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 2 trial provides new data for nivolumab in Alzheimer's disease, which will be evaluated by Bristol-Myers Squibb and Roche for future development decisions.
