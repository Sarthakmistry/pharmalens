## Roche terminates Phase 1/2 trial (NCT05251948) investigating atezolizumab

**Date:** 2025-09-04 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
A Phase 1/2 clinical trial (NCT05251948) sponsored by Roche, investigating atezolizumab, was terminated. The trial had an actual enrollment of 40 participants and reached its primary completion on September 4, 2025. The termination halts further development or investigation of atezolizumab within the scope of this specific trial.

### Market implication
The termination of a clinical trial is generally a bearish signal for the involved drug and company, as it indicates a halt in a specific development pathway, potentially due to futility, safety concerns, or strategic business decisions.
