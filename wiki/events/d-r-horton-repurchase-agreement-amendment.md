## DHI Mortgage Company, a subsidiary of D.R. Horton, Inc., amended its Master Repurchase Agreement, increasing the maximum commitment to $1.925 billion and extending the maturity date to May 4, 2029.

**Date:** 2026-05-12 | **Type:** news | **Signal:** bullish

### Summary
DHI Mortgage Company, a subsidiary of D.R. Horton, Inc., amended its Master Repurchase Agreement, increasing the maximum commitment to $1.925 billion and extending the maturity date to May 4, 2029. This Fifth Amendment became effective on May 6, 2026, and also modified certain pricing terms, fees, and financial covenants.

### Market implication
The amendment increases the maximum aggregate commitment and extends the maturity date of a financing facility, providing DHI Mortgage with enhanced financing and liquidity.

- Company: D.R. Horton, Inc., DHI Mortgage Company, Ltd., U.S. Bank National Association
