## Sanofi terminates Phase 4 trial NCT04003818 for Teicoplanin

**Date:** 2021-01-21 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Sanofi terminated its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT04003818) for Teicoplanin on January 21, 2021. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 50 participants, reached its primary completion on the same date but was subsequently terminated. No results have been reported for this study.
