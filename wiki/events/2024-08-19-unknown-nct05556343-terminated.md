## Bristol Myers Squibb terminates Phase 2 trial of Myk-224 (NCT05556343)

**Date:** 2024-08-19 | **Type:** trial_termination | **Signal:** bearish

### Summary
Bristol Myers Squibb has terminated its Phase 2 clinical trial (NCT05556343) for the investigational drug Myk-224. The trial, which had an actual enrollment of 18 participants, reached its primary completion date on August 19, 2024. The termination indicates that the development of Myk-224 will not proceed further, removing it from the company's pipeline.

### Market implication
The termination of a Phase 2 trial for an investigational drug like Myk-224 is a negative signal for Bristol Myers Squibb's pipeline, as it removes a potential future revenue stream, though the impact is limited given the drug was not widely tracked.
