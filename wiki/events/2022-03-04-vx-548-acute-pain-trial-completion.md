## VX-548 Acute Pain Trial Completion

**Date:** 2022-03-04 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
The Phase 2 study evaluating the efficacy and safety of VX-548 for acute pain after a bunionectomy has completed. This interventional trial, sponsored by Vertex Pharmaceuticals Incorporated, enrolled 274 participants. The primary outcome measure was Time-Weighted Sum of Pain Intensity Difference (SPID) 0 to 48 Hours after the first dose.

### Market implication
The clinical trial has completed, marking a development milestone, but results are not yet available to determine efficacy or safety outcomes, leading to a neutral market signal.
