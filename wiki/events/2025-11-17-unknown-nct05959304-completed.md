## Novartis completes Phase 4 trial for Brolucizumab (NCT05959304)

**Date:** 2025-11-17 | **Type:** trial_completion | **Signal:** neutral

### Summary
Novartis has announced the primary completion of its Phase 4 clinical trial (NCT05959304) for Brolucizumab. The trial, which enrolled 66 participants, reached its primary completion date on November 17, 2025. No results have been posted yet.

### Market implication
The completion of this Phase 4 trial is a standard procedural step, and without results, it has a neutral market implication for Novartis.
