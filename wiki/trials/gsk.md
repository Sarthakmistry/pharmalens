## A Study to Evaluate the Efficacy and Safety of BLU-5937 in Participants With Refractory Chronic Cough (RCC)

**Phase:** 3 | **Status:** active | **Sponsor:** [[gsk]]

### Design
This is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-arm study designed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of BLU-5937 in participants with Refractory Chronic Cough (RCC). The study aims to compare BLU-5937 against placebo in this patient population.

### Primary endpoint
