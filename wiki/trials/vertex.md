## Phase 2 Trial of ALPN-101 (Acazicolcept) in Systemic Lupus Erythematosus

**Phase:** 2 | **Status:** completed | **Sponsor:** [[vertex]]

### Design
This Phase 2 randomized controlled trial (RCT) investigated ALPN-101 (Acazicolcept) in 76 participants with Systemic Lupus Erythematosus, comparing it against placebo. The trial was sponsored by Alpine Immune Sciences Inc, a subsidiary of Vertex.

### Primary endpoint
Safety and Tolerability as Assessed by Number of Participants With Treatment- Emergent Adverse Events (TEAEs) and Serious Adverse Events (SAEs) | Percentage of Participants Achieving a Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE) Responder Index (SRI)-4 | Percentage of Participants Achieving a British Isles Lupus Assessment Group-based Composite Lupus Assessment (BICLA) Response

### Results summary
