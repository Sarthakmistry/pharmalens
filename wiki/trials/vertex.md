## A Study of VX-147 in Patients With APOL1-Mediated Focal Segmental Glomerulosclerosis (FSGS)

**Phase:** 2 | **Status:** completed | **Sponsor:** [[vertex]]

### Design
This Phase 2 study investigated VX-147 (inaxaplin) in 16 patients with APOL1-mediated focal segmental glomerulosclerosis (FSGS).

### Primary endpoint
percent change from the baseline urinary protein-to-creatinine ratio at week 13 in participants who had at least 80% adherence to inaxaplin therapy. Result: mean change from the baseline urinary protein-to-creatinine ratio at week 13 was -47.6% (95% confidence interval, -60.0 to -31.3)

### Results summary
Targeted inhibition of APOL1 channel function with inaxaplin reduced proteinuria in participants with two APOL1 variants and focal segmental glomerulosclerosis. Adverse events were mild or moderate in severity; none led to study discontinuation.
