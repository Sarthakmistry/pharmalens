### NCT03875235: A Study of Durvalumab Plus Chemotherapy in Advanced Biliary Tract Cancer

**Phase:** 3 | **Status:** Completed | **Sponsor:** [[astrazeneca]]

### Design
This Phase 3, Randomised Controlled Trial (RCT) enrolled 685 participants to study [[durvalumab]] plus gemcitabine and cisplatin (GemCis) compared to placebo plus GemCis in patients with advanced biliary tract cancer.

### Primary endpoint
overall survival (OS)

### Results summary
Median OS for [[durvalumab]]+GemCis was 12.9 months (95% CI 11.6-14.1) vs. 11.3 months (10.1-12.5) for placebo+GemCis (HR, 0.74 [95% CI 0.63-0.87]) after a median 41.3 months' follow-up. Survival benefit and manageable safety continued with [[durvalumab]]+GemCis vs. placebo+GemCis approximately 3 years after the last participant was randomised.

### Clinical evidence
- Study design: RCT
- Sample size: 685 participants
- Comparator: placebo+GemCis
- Primary outcome: overall survival (OS)
- Primary result: median OS (95% CI) for durvalumab+GemCis vs. placebo+GemCis was 12.9 (11.6-14.1) vs. 11.3 (10.1-12.5) months (hazard ratio, 0.74 [95% CI 0.63-0.87])
