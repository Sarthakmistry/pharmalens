## A Phase 2 Study of Lecanemab in Early Alzheimer's Disease

**Phase:** 2 | **Status:** completed | **Sponsor:** [[eisai]]

### Design
This was an RCT with placebo as a comparator. Multinational, multicenter, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel-group study using a Bayesian design. Evaluated [[lecanemab]] at various doses (2.5, 5, 10 mg/kg biweekly; 5, 10 mg/kg monthly) in 5 active arms. Targeted participants with mild cognitive impairment (MCI) due to Alzheimer's disease (AD) or mild Alzheimer's disease dementia. The study enrolled 856 participants.

### Primary endpoint
Core Study Phase: Change From Baseline in Alzheimer's Disease Composite Score (ADCOMS) at Month 12 | Core Study Phase: Number of Participants With All Treatment Emergent Adverse Events (TEAEs) and Serious Adverse Events (SAEs) | OLE Phase: Number of Participants With All TEAEs and SAEs

### Results summary
Safety was assessed by monitoring the number of participants with all treatment emergent adverse events (TEAEs) and SAEs.
