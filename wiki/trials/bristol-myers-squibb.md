### NCT00777036: A Study of BMS-790052 in Patients With Chronic Hepatitis C Virus Infection

**Phase:** 2 | **Status:** Completed | **Sponsor:** [[bristol-myers-squibb]]

#### Design
This Phase 2a, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study evaluated the antiviral activity, safety, and pharmacokinetics of BMS-790052 in combination with pegylated interferon alfa-2a and ribavirin in treatment-naive patients with chronic hepatitis C virus infection.

#### Primary endpoint
To evaluate the antiviral activity, safety, and pharmacokinetics of BMS-790052 in patients with chronic hepatitis C virus infection.

#### Results summary
