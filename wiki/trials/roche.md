## A Study of Trastuzumab Emtansine (T-DM1) in Patients With HER2-Positive Metastatic Breast Cancer Who Have Received Prior Trastuzumab and Lapatinib (TDM4450g)

**Phase:** 2 | **Status:** completed | **Sponsor:** [[roche]]

### Design
This was a single-arm, open-label study evaluating the efficacy and safety of Trastuzumab Emtansine (T-DM1) in patients with HER2-positive metastatic breast cancer who had previously received trastuzumab and lapatinib.

### Primary endpoint
Objective Response Rate (ORR) based on RECIST version 1.1, assessed up to 24 months.

### Results summary
The trial demonstrated an Objective Response Rate (ORR) of 29.5% (95% CI: 22.0, 38.0) in the overall population.
