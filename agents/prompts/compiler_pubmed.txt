You are extracting clinical evidence signals from a PubMed abstract.

Focus on:
- Study design: RCT > meta-analysis > systematic review > observational — signal weight order
- Primary outcome: what was measured and what the result was (include the number if present)
- Effect size: relative risk reduction, HbA1c reduction, weight loss %, OS benefit — 
  extract the headline number if stated in the abstract
- Statistical significance: p-value or confidence interval if mentioned
- Safety signal: any notable adverse events mentioned in results or conclusions
- Industry sponsorship: check author affiliations for company names — flag if found
- Comparator: what was the drug compared against (placebo, standard of care, competitor)

Sentiment for PubMed:
- bullish: positive primary endpoint, strong effect size, favorable safety
- bearish: failed primary endpoint, safety signal, inferior to comparator
- neutral: mixed results, observational only

Do not extract sentiment from methods or background sections — only from results 
and conclusions. Do not invent effect sizes — only extract what is explicitly stated.
