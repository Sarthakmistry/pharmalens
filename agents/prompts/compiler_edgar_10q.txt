You are extracting pharma intelligence from an SEC 10-Q quarterly report.

The company slug, filing date, and period of report are in the YAML frontmatter —
use these directly. The period_of_report field tells you which quarter this covers.

10-Q files have two distinct sections — extract from both separately:

SECTION 1 — XBRL Revenue Block (appears at the top of the document):
This is structured product revenue data extracted directly from XBRL tags.
- Extract revenue per product with exact dollar amount as listed
- Extract total revenue and key P&L lines (net income, R&D expense, SG&A, operating income)
- These are factual numbers — do not interpret or summarize, extract exactly as stated
- Only extract products that appear in the tracked drugs reference list

SECTION 2 — MD&A Narrative:
This is management's discussion — higher signal for forward-looking intelligence.

Extract pipeline updates:
- Drug name, phase, indication, milestone reached (approval | trial start |
  trial result | submission | discontinuation)
- Include exact phase and trial name if stated

Extract risk factors per drug:
- IRA Medicare price setting: flag which drugs are selected and effective year
- Biosimilar entry: drug name, biosimilar entrant if named, timing
- Patent expiry: drug name and year if stated
- Tariff exposure: flag as company-level risk, not drug-level

Extract management commentary:
- Any product-specific forward guidance or demand language
- Macro risks (tariffs, IRA, pricing pressure) flag as company_level_risk: true —
  these apply across the portfolio, not to individual drugs

Sentiment rules:
- bullish: strong volume growth, positive trial result, new approval, pipeline
  advancement, guidance raised
- bearish: net price decline, IRA selection, biosimilar pressure, failed trial,
  guidance lowered, safety concern
- neutral: stable performance, no directional signal, macro uncertainty only
- Assign sentiment per drug based only on what is stated about that drug
- Do not assign drug-level sentiment based on company-level macro risks alone

requires_new_event_page: true for:
- New FDA approval disclosed
- Phase 3 trial result disclosed (positive or negative)
- Drug selected for IRA Medicare price setting
- Guidance raised or lowered
- Any drug discontinued from pipeline

requires_new_event_page: false for:
- Revenue updates with no discrete event
- Routine macro risk factor language
- Pipeline drugs with no status change
